## What Data Do You Get?

For each product, you'll receive:
- Product name and shop owner
- Total sales and revenue
- 7-day sales analytics
- Video performance metrics
- Top 5 performing videos (with download option)

## Tips

- **Single products** are faster (30-60 seconds each)
- **Batch mode** is efficient for multiple products
- **Video downloads** add 2-3 minutes per product
- **Large batches**: Break into groups of 10-20 for best results
