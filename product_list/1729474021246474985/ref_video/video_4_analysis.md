## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show a Real Person:** Introduce a human host to build trust. German consumers often skeptical of faceless "gadget" ads. (展示真人出镜，建立信任)
2. **Localize Context:** Show the tool cleaning a specifically "German" context like a messy car boot after a hike or a balcony in a recognizable housing style. (场景本土化)
3. **Test Sound ASMR:** Emphasize the *sound* of the powerful suction/blowing to create a sensory trigger. (增强听觉ASMR体验)

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Generic TTS:** Use a native German speaker if possible to sound less "robotic" and more authentic. (避免生硬的机器配音)
2. **Overcrowd the End Screen:** Too many offers (50%, Free Gift, More Discounts) can sound desperate or scammy. Focus on one strong "Bundle Offer". (避免结尾促销信息过载)
3. **Ignore the "Why":** Explain *why* it's 50% off (e.g., "New Year Clearance" or "Launch Sale") to justify the low price. (解释降价原因以增加可信度)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Car Cleaning" Angle:** Position it specifically as a "Car Detailer" tool rather than just a leaf blower. This often appeals to a younger, male demographic on TikTok. (主打"车载吸尘/清洁"场景)
2. **Comparison Video:** Split screen with a standard heavy vacuum vs. this lightweight tool. (对比视频)
3. **Reply to Comment:** Create a follow-up video answering a specific question like "How long does the battery last?" to drive engagement. (回复评论视频)
