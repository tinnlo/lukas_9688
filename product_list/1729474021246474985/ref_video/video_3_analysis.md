## 3. Hook/Opening Strategy (First 3 Seconds) | 开场策略(前3秒)

- **Hook Type | 钩子类型:** Unboxing/Feature-Led (开箱/功能导向)
- **Hook Description | 策略描述:** The video starts immediately with the unboxed product on a table and the creator announcing "I got this leaf blower." It's a very direct, low-energy introduction showing the core product immediately.
- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:** Visuals show a cluttered table with the device components. Audio is a calm, male voice simply stating what the item is. No music, no sound effects, no fast cuts.
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** **Low/Ineffective**. It lacks a "pattern interrupt." For TikTok, it's too slow and visually "messy." There is no immediate "wow" factor or problem demonstration to grab attention. The lack of visual contrast makes it blend in with generic content.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Key features like "2x Akkus inkl." (2x Batteries inc.) and "Saugen & Blasen" (Vacuum & Blow) must appear on screen. German viewers like to read specs.
2.  **Start with the Action:** Move the outdoor leaf-blowing shot to the **first 3 seconds**. Show the result *first*, then explain how it works.
3.  **Improve Lighting:** The indoor scenes are too dark. Move the table near a window for natural light.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't leave the beginning silent/slow:** The first 3 seconds are just panning over parts. This kills retention.
2.  **Don't rely on audio only:** Many users watch with sound off. The lack of subtitles means they miss the "2-in-1" explanation.
3.  **Don't ignore the "Dirt Bag":** The "Schmutzsäckchen" is mentioned but barely shown. Show it *filling up* with leaves to prove the vacuum power.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of crunching dry leaves is satisfying. A version focusing on the sound of the vacuum sucking up crunchy leaves could work well.
2.  **"Mess Test":** Create a deliberate mess (flour, cereal, or fake snow) in a garage to show it works for more than just leaves (as the title mentions "Garage/Pet").
3.  **Price Comparison:** Visually compare it to a big brand brand (Stihl/Bosch) to highlight the massive price difference for similar utility.
