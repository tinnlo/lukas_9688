### 7. Replication Checklist for DE Creators | 德国创作者复刻清单
*   **Socket Type:** Ensure the video shows the device plugging into a **European (Schuko)** socket. Using a US/UK plug in a DE ad kills conversion immediately.
*   **Terminology:** Use "WLAN" instead of "WiFi" in speech (Germans use both, but "WLAN" is very common for home networks).
*   **Building Material:** Mention "Dicke Wände" (Thick walls). German homes are often brick/concrete, which kills signals. This is a specific local pain point.
*   **Legal/Trust:** Avoid "Increases Speed" (Verstärkt Geschwindigkeit) claims unless true. Stick to "Increases Range" (Erweitert Reichweite).
*   **Tone:** Be "Sachlich" (factual) but enthusiastic. Germans love technical specs that prove value.
