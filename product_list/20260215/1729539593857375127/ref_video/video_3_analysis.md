## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add a Voiceover:** Even a TTS voice saying "Transform your room in seconds" adds immense value. | **添加旁白：** 即使是文字转语音说“几秒钟内改变你的房间”也能增加巨大的价值。
2. **Show the Product Being Used:** Show a hand pressing the remote to demonstrate control and reality. | **展示产品使用过程：** 展示一只手按下遥控器，以演示控制功能和真实性。
3. **Use German Text Overlays:** "Beste Zimmer-Deko" (Best Room Decor) or "50% Rabatt" (50% Off). | **使用德语文字叠加：** “Beste Zimmer-Deko”（最佳房间装饰）或“50% Rabatt”（五折优惠）。

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on "Vibes" Alone:** 7 seconds of blinking lights is not a sales pitch; it's a screensaver. | **不要仅依赖“氛围”：** 7秒钟的闪烁灯光不是推销；它是屏保。
2. **Leave Audio Empty:** TikTok is an audio-first platform. Silence kills reach. | **不要让音频留白：** TikTok 是一个音频优先的平台。静音会扼杀覆盖率。
3. **Hide the "Mechanism":** Show *how* it works (plugging it in, turning it on) to build trust. | **不要隐藏“机制”：** 展示它是*如何*工作的（插上电源，打开开关）以建立信任。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Gamer Setup" Angle:** Target German gamers ("Zocker") showing this as a setup essential. | **“游戏玩家设置”角度：** 针对德国游戏玩家（"Zocker"），将其展示为设置必备品。
2. **"Relaxation" Angle:** Position it as a stress-relief tool after work/school ("Entspannung pur"). | **“放松”角度：** 将其定位为工作/放学后的减压工具（"Entspannung pur"）。
3. **Reply to Comment Strategy:** Use a video reply format "Does it have blue?" to drive engagement. | **回复评论策略：** 使用视频回复格式“它有蓝色吗？”来推动互动。
