## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Extend Duration:** Aim for at least 7-10 seconds to allow the algorithm to register engagement.
2. **Show the Transformation:** Start with lights OFF (boring room) -> Snap fingers -> Lights ON (cool room). This "before/after" is a classic, effective hook.
3. **Add Text Overlay:** Use German text like "Mach dein Zimmer krass" (Make your room cool) or "Must-Have für Gamer" to give context.

**DON'T:**
1. **Post <3s Videos:** Unless it's a specific "sound sync" trend, extremely short videos often perform poorly for sales.
2. **Ignore Audio:** Even a simple trending song can boost reach significantly.
3. **Rely Solely on Visuals:** Without a CTA or text, viewers don't know where to buy or what the product is called.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Unboxing:** Show the box, the remote, and the click of the button to turn it on.
2. **Remote Control Demo:** Show changing colors with the remote to demonstrate versatility (red -> blue -> fade).
3. **Gaming Setup Integration:** Place the light behind a monitor or TV to target the gaming niche specifically.
