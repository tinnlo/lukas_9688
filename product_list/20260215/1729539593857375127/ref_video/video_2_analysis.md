## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会点)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Text Overlays:** Use "Muss man haben" (Must-have) or "Dein Zimmer wird es lieben" (Your room will love it). | **添加德语文字叠加：** 使用“必备神器”或“你的房间会爱上它”之类的文案。
2. **Use a Problem/Solution Hook:** Start with a boring, dimly lit room before the "magic" happens. | **使用“问题-解决”式开头：** 先展示一个普通单调的房间，然后开启“魔法”变换。
3. **Include a Voiceover:** Even a simple "Look at this vibe..." (Schau dir diesen Vibe an...) would increase engagement. | **增加旁白：** 即使是简单的“看这氛围感……”也能显著提升互动率。

**DON'T:**
1. **Stay Silent:** Music-only videos often get scrolled past unless the visual is world-class. | **不要只是静默：** 纯音乐视频很容易被刷走，除非视觉效果极其惊艳。
2. **Ignore the CTA:** Don't assume users will click the link without being told to. | **不要忽视行动号召：** 不要指望观众会在没有提示的情况下主动点击购买链接。
3. **Oversaturate:** Ensure the colors look realistic so customers don't feel misled. | **不要过度调色：** 确保颜色看起来真实，以免让顾客觉得货不对板。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the remote click and the hum of the light. | **ASMR切入点：** 放大遥控器的按键音和灯光的轻微嗡鸣声。
2. **Gift Idea:** Frame it as "Das perfekte Geschenk" (The perfect gift) for Valentine's or birthdays. | **礼品策略：** 将其包装成情人节或生日的“完美礼物”。
3. **Comparison:** Compare a cheap lamp vs. this RGB projector. | **对比评测：** 展示普通灯泡与这款RGB投影仪的效果差异。
