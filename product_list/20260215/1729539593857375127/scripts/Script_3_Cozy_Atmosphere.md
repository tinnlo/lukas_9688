## Scripts

Emotional wellness angle focusing on creating cozy sanctuary atmosphere during dark German winters.

### Structure (45s)
- Hook: Soft whisper about dark winter evenings and mood (0-3s)
- Problem/Reveal: Missing warmth and light in winter months (3-10s)
- Solution/Demo: Sunset lamp creating golden hour anytime (10-30s)
- Benefits: Mood boost, relaxation, home sanctuary feeling (30-40s)
- CTA: Bring warmth home, link below (40-45s)

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Person looking out dark window in the evening, looking tired/stressed | Hook: Emotional resonance with winter blues |
| 03-07 | Same person walks to desk, clicks remote | Problem: Need for mood boost |
| 07-12 | Room slowly fills with warm sunset glow, person's face lights up with smile | Solution: Quick emotional shift |

| 42-45 | Peaceful final frame with text overlay | Conversion: Gentle CTA |
