## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Replicate the "Secret Feature" Structure:** Always save a unique, "wow" feature (like the phone stand) for the end to boost watch time.
2.  **Emphasize "Anti-Scald" Visually:** Continue to show holding the bowl with boiling water inside; this is the strongest visual proof of quality.
3.  **Use Colloquial Language:** Words like "Pfoten", "Mega", "Geil" resonate with the younger TikTok demographic in Germany.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Oversell the Taste:** Focus on the *convenience* of the bowl, not the quality of the instant noodles themselves (which aren't the product).
2.  **Forget the "Sieve" Close-up:** Ensure the draining shot is clear; if people don't see the holes, they won't understand how the water gets out without losing noodles.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Dorm Room Angle:** Create a version specifically targeting students ("Studentenfutter") who need to cook in limited spaces.
2.  **Recipe Variations:** Show making different things in it (e.g., porridge, soup) to broaden the utility beyond just noodles.
3.  **ASMR Version:** A version focusing on the sounds of preparation (water pouring, slurping, lid snapping) could appeal to the sensory audience.
