**DO:**
1. **Shorten the Intro:** Cut the "struggle" section down to 5-8 seconds to get to the solution faster. 114s is very long for a simple gadget.
2. **Show Variety:** Show the bowl being used for other hot foods (soup, porridge) to increase perceived value.
3. **Add Text Overlay:** Add "Nie wieder Finger verbrennen!" (Never burn fingers again!) as a persistent text hook.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Over-Explain:** The segment about "Is it a pot? Microwave?" (00:39) is a bit dragging. Show, don't just tell.
2. **Don't Hide the Lid:** Ensure the "draining" feature (if it has holes for dry noodles) is clearly shown in a close-up.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of pouring water and the "clack" of the lid sealing for a sensory hook.
2. **Gifting Angle:** Explicitly mention "Perfect for students/dorm life" or "Father's Day" (as per title) in the voiceover to drive gift purchases.
3. **Bundle Deal:** Suggest buying two (one for office, one for home) to increase AOV.
