## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (执行建议):**
1. **Add Text Overlays:** If no voiceover, ensure key benefits ("1200ml", "Keep Warm", "Safe Handle") appear as text on screen.
2. **Shorten the Loop:** Create a 15-30s cutdown version of this 137s video for paid ads (Spark Ads). The current length is good for organic/community but too long for cold traffic.
3. **Reply to Comments:** Engage with fans asking "Where to buy?" to boost the algorithm.

**DON'T (避免事项):**
1. **Rely only on Caption:** Many users watch without reading the caption. Ensure the visual story is self-explanatory.
2. **Hide the Product:** Ensure the bowl is visible in the first 1 second (don't just show the creator's face).

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1. **ASMR Version:** If the original track is music, try a version with *just* the noodle slurping/soup pouring sounds (ASMR is huge in the noodle niche).
2. **"Office Lunch" Angle:** Frame it specifically for workplace lunches (saving money vs eating out) – "Das läppert sich" (That adds up) angle for savings.
3. **Bundle Deal:** Sell with a specific noodle brand (cross-promotion).
