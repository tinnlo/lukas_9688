## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Critical Filming Requirements (关键拍摄要求)

1. **The Bare-Hand Hold (徒手抓拿)**  
   MUST show creator holding bowl with bare hands immediately after pouring boiling water. This is THE trust signal for German buyers.  
   **Timing:** Within 5 seconds of pouring  
   **Camera Angle:** Close-up on hands gripping exterior, with visible steam from vent  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 3

2. **The Shake Test (摇晃测试)**  
   Flip bowl upside down and shake vigorously to prove leak-proof seal.  
   **Safety:** Ensure lid is properly sealed before filming  
   **Visual:** Use clear background so viewers see no leakage  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 3, Table

3. **Steam Release Moment (蒸汽释放)**  
   Capture dramatic steam cloud when opening lid after steeping.  
   **Lighting:** Backlight or side light to maximize steam visibility  
   **Sound:** Amplify the "hiss" of steam in ASMR mix  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 7

4. **Phone Holder Reveal (手机支架揭秘)**  
   Use suspense build: pause, look at camera, then slowly flip lid to reveal handle slot.  
   **Prop:** Use recognizable streaming app (Netflix, YouTube) on phone screen  
   **Timing:** This should be the 28-38s "showstopper" moment  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 7

### Props \u0026 Ingredients (道具与食材)

**Required:**
- Instant noodle brands: Buldak (火鸡面), Shin Ramyun (辛拉面), or YumYum (营多面)
