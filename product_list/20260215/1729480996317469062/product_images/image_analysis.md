    *   12-15s: Add sauce, mix in the big bowl. Eat happy.
*   **Visual Key:** Emphasis on `product_image_5.webp` (vent holes).

*   **Visual Key:** High contrast macro shots of the steel texture vs. flimsy plastic cups.
