## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Add ASMR/Sound Effects:** Record the *sound* of the tapping. It adds tactile satisfaction. (增加ASMR音效：触碰灯的声音)
2.  **Use a Voiceover (TTS is fine):** "This is why you need this lamp..." or "Stop waking up your partner with big lights..." (Solve a problem). (使用旁白：解释使用场景，如"不再打扰伴侣睡眠")
3.  **Show "Before & After":** Show a boring dark room, then *tap* -> Cozy Vibe. Contrast sells. (展示"使用前/使用后"对比：从黑暗无聊到温馨氛围)

**DON'T (避免做法):**
1.  **Don't use Silence:** Background music alone is weak on TikTok. (不要只放背景音乐)
2.  **Don't List Specs Only:** "13 Colors" is a spec. "Perfect for Movie Night" is a benefit. Focus on benefits. (不要只列参数，要讲场景)
3.  **Don't overexpose:** Ensure the camera exposure is locked to the *lamp* so the colors pop, rather than washing out. (不要过曝：锁定曝光以展示色彩)

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **"Gaming Setup" Angle:** Pitch it as a budget RGB addition to a gaming desk. (切入"电竞房"场景：作为廉价RGB配件)
2.  **"Gift for Her/Him" Angle:** "The perfect little gift under €20". (切入"礼物"场景：20欧以下的完美礼物)
3.  **Comparisons:** "My old lamp vs. This lamp". (对比视频：旧灯泡 vs 新潮灯)
