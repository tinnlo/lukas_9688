## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

*   **Angle 1: The "Parent Hack" (Problem/Solution)**
    *   **Concept**: Focus on the pain of waking up fully when turning on a big light for baby/bathroom.
        *   **ZH**: 关注半夜为了婴儿/上厕所开大灯而彻底惊醒的痛苦。
    *   **Hook**: "Eltern, ihr braucht das!" (Parents, you need this!) showing the soft dim light.
    *   **Flow**: Nighttime scene -> Baby cries -> Tap lamp (soft light) -> Baby stays asleep -> Parent happy.
    *   **Key Feature**: Dimmable + Portable.

*   **Angle 2: The "Vibe Creator" (Aesthetic/Gen Z)**
    *   **Concept**: Visual-heavy montage of changing room aesthetics with colors.
        *   **ZH**: 以视觉为主的剪辑，展示用颜色改变房间美学。
    *   **Hook**: "Wie ich mein Zimmer für unter 25€ verwandelt habe." (How I transformed my room for under €25.)
    *   **Flow**: Boring room -> Place lamp -> Tap -> RED (sexy) -> Tap -> BLUE (chill) -> Tap -> PARTY MODE.
    *   **Key Feature**: 13 RGB Colors + Touch.

*   **Angle 3: The "Magic Touch" (ASMR/Satisfaction)**
    *   **Concept**: Focus on the satisfying sound and response of the touch control. Minimal talking.
        *   **ZH**: 专注于触控令人满意的声音和反应。极少说话。
    *   **Hook**: Close-up finger tapping -> Light bursts on. "Satisfying."
    *   **Flow**: Tap on, tap off. Tap change color. Tap dim. Show wireless portability by sliding it across the table.
    *   **Key Feature**: Touch Control + Wireless.
