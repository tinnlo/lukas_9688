## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO | 建议做:**
1.  **Enhance the CTA:** Change "Check if it's there" to "This size sells out fast" or "Grab it for your evening routine." (增加紧迫感).
2.  **Visual Text:** Add text overlays for key specs like "150ml vs Standard" or "3-Hour Timer" to catch scrollers without audio. (添加关键卖点字幕).
3.  **ASMR Elements:** Amplify the sound of the water bubbling or the button click to increase sensory satisfaction. (增加ASMR音效).

**DON'T | 建议不做:**
1.  **Don't hide the product:** Ensure the mist is visible against a dark background. White mist on a white wall is invisible. (不要让背景吞没烟雾效果).
2.  **Don't be too passive:** "It helps me relax" is good, but "You need this for your anxiety" is a stronger (though riskier) hook. (不要太佛系，直击痛点).

**OPPORTUNITY | 潜在机会:**
1.  **Scenario Usage:** Show it in a bedroom setting (night routine) vs. an office setting (stress relief) to widen the use case. (展示不同场景：卧室助眠 vs 办公室解压).
2.  **Oil Recipes:** "My favorite recipe for sleep: Lavender + Chamomile." Educational content drives retention better than just product features. (分享精油配方，增加教育属性).
