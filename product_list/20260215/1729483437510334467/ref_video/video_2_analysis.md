## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (红黑榜/机会点)

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Fix the Audio (修正音频):** Use a native German speaker or high-quality AI (ElevenLabs) to ensure "Schlafzimmer" is pronounced correctly, not "Schlachtzimmer".
2.  **Show the "Vibe" (展示氛围):** Instead of just the machine, show a person reading a book next to it with a cup of tea. Sell the *relaxation*, not the plastic.
3.  **Add a CTA (增加行动号召):** End with "Hol dir Entspannung nach Hause - Link unten!" (Get relaxation home - Link below!).

**DON'T (避雷):**
1.  **Don't rely on unverified translation:** "Schlachtzimmer" (Slaughter room) in a wellness ad is a catastrophic mistake.
2.  **Don't be boring:** 12 seconds of static product shots is not engaging on TikTok.
3.  **Don't list technical specs first:** "Ultrasonic technology" is boring. Start with "Make your room smell like a spa in seconds."

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **ASMR Route:** Focus on the sound of the water bubbling and the visual of the mist for a sensory "satisfying" video.
2.  **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for your stressed girlfriend" (Das perfekte Geschenk für deine gestresste Freundin).
3.  **Aesthetic/Cozy Home:** Jump on the #cozyhome or #roomdecor trends with better lighting and set design.
