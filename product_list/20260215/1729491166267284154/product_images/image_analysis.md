## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

### Angle 1: The "Fruit Fly" Problem Solver (Seasonal/Pain-Point)
*   **Core Concept**: Summer is coming (or heating is on), and fruit flies are the enemy. This bin is the fortress.
*   **Hook**: "Hast du auch diese kleinen Fliegen in der Küche?" (Do you also have these little flies in the kitchen?)
*   **Visual Flow**: Show fruit flies buzzing around an old open bowl -> Cut to EcoBio closing with a satisfying snap (`product_image_5.webp`) -> Show the seal -> Show the happy user.
*   **Why**: Directly targets the #1 visceral hatred of bio waste.

### Angle 2: The "Bin Juice" Educator (Feature-Focused)
*   **Core Concept**: Explaining *why* bio waste smells (moisture). The double-basket system (`product_image_3.webp`) solves the root cause.
*   **Hook**: "Weißt du, warum dein Müll stinkt? Wegen der Nässe!" (Do you know why your trash smells? Because of the wetness!)
*   **Visual Flow**: Lift the inner basket out -> Show how it allows air in and water out -> Show the bottom reservoir -> Dump it out cleanly.
*   **Why**: Positions the product as a "smart" scientific solution, not just a plastic bucket.

### Angle 3: The "Aesthetic Kitchen" Upgrade (Lifestyle/ASMR)
*   **Core Concept**: Bio bins are usually ugly. This one is décor.
*   **Hook**: "Ich dachte, alle Biomülleimer müssen hässlich sein. Falsch." (I thought all compost bins had to be ugly. Wrong.)
*   **Visual Flow**: Slow pans of the matte finish (`product_image_1.webp`) -> ASMR sounds of scraping food (`product_image_2.webp`) -> Placing it next to a fancy coffee machine.
*   **Why**: Appeals to the TikTok demographic that values "Aesthetic" and "Clean Girl/Boy" vibes.
