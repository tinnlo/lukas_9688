## Scripts

Lifestyle-focused ASMR narrative targeting aesthetic-conscious Gen Z and minimalist kitchen enthusiasts. Reframes the bio bin as a design piece, not just a utility item. Emphasizes visual harmony and tactile satisfaction.

### Structure (40s)
- Hook: "I thought all bio bins had to be ugly" (0-3s)
- Problem: Show typical ugly green/orange bins (3-6s)
- Reveal: Introduce EcoBio's matte modern design (6-10s)
- Sensory: ASMR scraping, lid clicking, smooth surfaces (10-25s)
- Lifestyle: Product integrated into beautiful kitchen setup (25-35s)
