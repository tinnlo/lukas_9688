## Scripts

Educational counter-intuitive narrative that explains WHY bio waste smells (moisture) and positions the double-basket drainage system as the scientific solution, not just a container. Appeals to logic-driven German market.

### Structure (45s)
- Hook: Question - "Why does bio waste smell?" (0-3s)
- Agitation: Show wet, leaking bio bag - the moisture problem (3-8s)
- Solution: Introduce EcoBio's sieve system concept (8-12s)
- Demo: Lift inner basket, show separated liquid below (12-25s)
- Education: Explain airflow + drainage = dry waste = no smell (25-37s)
- CTA: "Smart solution for smart kitchens" (37-45s)
