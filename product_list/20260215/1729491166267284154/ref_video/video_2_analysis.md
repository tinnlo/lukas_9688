## 3. Hook/Opening Strategy (First 3 Seconds) | 开场策略(前3秒)

- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:**
    - **Audio:** Enthusiastic "good buy" statement.
    - **Visual:** Establishing shot of the bin in a clean kitchen environment.
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** **Weak (弱)**.
    - *Reason:* "Good buy" is generic. Without a "pattern interrupt" (e.g., showing a gross, leaking old bin first) or a visually satisfying moment immediately, users scroll past. The stakes aren't established early enough.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO | 建议:**
1.  **Visual Hook:** Start with a "Gross" visual – e.g., an old plastic bag leaking "Müllsaft" (bin juice) on the counter, then cut to the clean EcoBio bin. (开头先展示痛点：漏水的旧垃圾袋).
2.  **Text Overlays:** Add "Nie wieder Stinken" (Never stinks again) or "Spülmaschinenfest" (Dishwasher safe) as text bubbles on screen during relevant points. (添加核心卖点字幕).
3.  **Stronger CTA:** End with "Link unten links" (Link bottom left) and a reason to buy NOW (e.g., "Perfekt für den Sommer" - Perfect for summer). (强化购买引导).

**DON'T | 禁忌:**
1.  **Don't rely on audio only:** Many watch without sound. If you don't *show* the seal is tight (e.g., fill with water and turn upside down), the claim is weak. (不要只靠嘴说，要演示).
2.  **Don't be generic:** "Good buy" is boring. Use "The kitchen hack that saved my marriage" or "Why I stopped using plastic bags." (不要用通用的开场白).

**OPPORTUNITY | 机会:**
1.  **Fruit Fly Angle:** Summer is coming. Position this as the "Anti-Fruchtfliegen-Bunker" (Anti-fruit-fly bunker). This is a massive pain point in Germany. (主打"防果蝇"痛点).
2.  **Comparison:** Split screen: Old smelly bio-bin vs. New EcoBio bin. (分屏对比：旧垃圾桶 vs 新产品).
3.  **Paper Bag Test:** Show how standard paper bags get wet and tear, while this bin protects the bag (if used with one) or replaces it. (展示纸袋容易破的痛点).
