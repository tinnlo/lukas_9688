## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Start with the PAIN:** Don't start with "I haven't shown this in a while." Start with: "Hass ihr auch Fruchtfliegen in der Küche?" (Do you also hate fruit flies?) or "Der einzige Biomülleimer, der nicht stinkt" (The only bio bin that doesn't stink).
2.  **Cut the Duration:** Compress this info into 15-25 seconds. 73 seconds is a documentary for a trash can.
3.  **Show "The Soup":** Visually emphasize the gross liquid at the bottom of *other* bins vs. the dry state of this one to trigger a disgust/relief reaction.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Assume Familiarity:** Never assume the viewer knows who you are or has seen the product before (unless you are a mega-celebrity). Treat every video like a first impression.
2.  **Don't Linger on Features:** The explanation of the lid mechanism took 15 seconds; it can be shown in 2 seconds.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of peeling or the "click" of the lid sealing could be used for a sensory hook.
2.  **Comparison:** "Standard Bio Bin vs. Eco-Bio" split screen. Show the standard one attracting flies and leaking, show Eco-Bio clean and sealed.
3.  **Summer Seasonal Push:** Repost this with a "Summer Essential" angle when fruit flies are at their peak (warm months).
