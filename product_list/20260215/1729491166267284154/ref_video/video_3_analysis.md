## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. Focus on the "No more smell" benefit early.
2. Show the "Sieve" in action with actual liquid to visualize the benefit.
3. Use the "Dishwasher Safe" claim as a major trust signal.

**DON'T:**
1. Don't spend too much time on the "summer test" backstory; keep it to 3 seconds.
2. Avoid showing the bin empty for too long; show it "in use."

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. Add a visual comparison (Split screen) with a "Standard" bin that leaks/smells.
2. Feature a "How to setup" 5-second montage.
3. Offer a "Kitchen Hack" angle: How to keep the bin fresh for 7 days.
