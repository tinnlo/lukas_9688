## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Critical Filming Requirements (关键拍摄要求)

1. **The Chaos Intro (混乱开场)**  
   Start with authentic messy shoe pile (10+ pairs scattered). German viewers relate to this daily frustration.  
   **Timing:** 0-3s  
   **Emotional Note:** Frustration should be understated, not over-acted  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 1

2. **The Assembly Time-Lapse (组装延时)**  
   Show EVERY click of tube connections (ASMR audio boost). This proves "tool-free" claim.  
   **Speed:** 200-300% time-lapse  
   **Sound:** Amplify "click" sounds for satisfying rhythm  
   **Source:** Analysis Section 6

3. **The Zipper Close (拉链关闭)**  
   Slow-motion or real-time zipper closure from bottom to top. This is THE satisfying moment.  
   **Camera Angle:** Close-up on zipper teeth, then pull back to show full rack  
   **Sound:** Crisp zipper ASMR  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 3

4. **The Before/After Split (前后对比)**  
   Use split-screen: messy pile (top) vs. organized rack (bottom). Germans love visual efficiency proof.  
   **Timing:** Can be used as transition or final proof shot  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 7

### Props & Setting (道具与场景)

**Required:**
- Variety of shoes: Heavy boots (bottom tiers stability test), sneakers, heels
- Real German hallway or bedroom setting (NOT studio)
- Clean floor space to show assembly process
- Natural lighting (Germans distrust overly-produced content)

**Source:** Synthesis Section 3 (Echter Kontext)

### Editing Techniques (剪辑技巧)

- **Cut Frequency:** Every 2-3 seconds for retention
- **Jump Cuts:** During assembly to create dynamic energy
- **Close-ups:** Macro shots of zipper, metal tubes, fabric quality
- **Split-Screen:** Chaos vs. Order for instant comparison

**Source:** Synthesis Section 7
