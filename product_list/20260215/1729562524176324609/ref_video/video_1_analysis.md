## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add "Nie mehr Staub!" (No more dust!) or "Schuh-Chaos beendet" (Shoe chaos ended) as text on screen to catch silent scrollers.
2.  **Show "Before" State:** Start with a messy pile of shoes for 0.5s to contrast with the clean rack.
3.  **Explicit CTA:** End with a text overlay: "Jetzt 50% Rabatt sichern" (Secure 50% off now) to drive urgency.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Oversell Quality:** Keep it realistic. This is a fabric rack, not solid wood. Promoting it as "luxury" would hurt trust.
2.  **Don't Rush the Zipper:** Make sure the zipper action looks smooth. If it gets stuck, cut that take.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Amplify the sound of the zipper and the shoes being placed for a sensory "satisfaction" video.
2.  **"Assemble with Me":** A fast-motion timelapse of the assembly process can prove it's easy to build (a common fear for furniture).
3.  **Capacity Test:** "How many pairs fit?" video where you count out loud as you fill it up (e.g., "10... 20... 30 pairs!").
