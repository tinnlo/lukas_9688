## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text like "Endlich Platz für alle Schuhe!" (Finally room for all shoes!) and "Staubdicht" (Dust-proof) to reinforce benefits.
    (添加德语文字叠加，如“终于能放下所有鞋子了！”和“防尘”，以强化卖点。)
2.  **Speed Up:** Use a timelapse of the assembly or filling the rack to add energy.
    (使用组装或填满鞋架的延时摄影来增加活力。)
3.  **Voiceover:** Add a simple TTS (Text-to-Speech) voiceover: "Hier ist die beste Lösung für dein Schuh-Chaos." (Here is the best solution for your shoe chaos.)
    (添加简单的TTS旁白：“这是解决你鞋子混乱的最佳方案。”)

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't leave it silent:** Silence is a missed opportunity for engagement and algorithm ranking.
    (不要留白：静音错失了互动和算法排名的机会。)
2.  **Don't use poor lighting:** Ensure the shoe rack is well-lit so it doesn't look cheap or dirty.
    (不要使用糟糕的灯光：确保鞋架光线充足，以免看起来廉价或脏乱。)
3.  **Don't forget the CTA:** Don't assume people know to click. Point to the basket.
    (不要忘记CTA：别以为人们知道要点击，要指引他们点击购物篮。)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Assembly:** Focus on the sounds of clicking the pipes together and zipping the cover.
    (ASMR组装：专注于管道拼接和拉链拉动的声音。)
2.  **"Before & After" Split Screen:** Show the pile of shoes on top and the organized rack on the bottom simultaneously.
    (“前后对比”分屏：上方显示鞋堆，下方显示整洁的鞋架。)
3.  **Localized Pain Point:** Mention "Small German Apartments" (Kleine Wohnung? Kein Problem!) to resonate with specific housing struggles.
    (本地化痛点：提及“德国小公寓”（房子小？没问题！）以引起特定住房难题的共鸣。)
