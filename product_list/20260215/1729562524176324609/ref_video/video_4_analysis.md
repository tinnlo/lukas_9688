## 13. Recommendations | 建议 (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Emphasize "Before/After":** Start with a pile of messy shoes on the floor, then cut to the organized rack. The contrast creates immediate desire.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add text like "Nie wieder Chaos!" (No more chaos!) or "Platzwunder" (Space miracle) to reinforce the message for viewers watching without sound.
3.  **Strengthen CTA:** Add a visual arrow pointing to the cart or mention a price/deal (e.g., "Unter 25€!" if applicable) to drive the click.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't linger on empty shelves:** Keep the rack full to show capacity.
2.  **Don't use complex language:** Keep the "kumpelhaft" (buddy-like) tone; formal German would kill the vibe.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Version:** Focus on the sounds of zippers and shoes being placed for a sensory "clean up" video.
2.  **"Assemble with me" Timelapse:** Speed up the assembly to 5 seconds to visually prove the "super fast" claim.
3.  **Capacity Challenge:** "How many pairs fit?" – Count them out loud to demonstrate value (e.g., "Fits 30 pairs!").
