**DO:**
1. Keep the native German voiceover as it builds immense trust in the DE market. | 保留德语母语旁白，因为它能极大地建立德国市场的信任度。
2. Focus on the "cold for 34h" claim with a visual test (e.g., showing ice after several hours). | 重点突出“34小时保冷”，并配合视觉测试（例如：展示几小时后的冰块）。
3. Use text overlays to highlight key specs (40oz, Stainless Steel). | 使用文字叠加来强调核心参数（40盎司，不锈钢材质）。

**DON'T:**
1. Don't rely solely on a "soft" recommendation; the low conversion suggests a need for more urgency. | 不要仅仅依赖“温和”的推荐；极低的转化率表明需要更多的紧迫感。
2. Avoid low-contrast backgrounds; make the product pop more against the setting. | 避免低对比度的背景；要让产品在场景中更加突出。
3. Don't forget a clear verbal CTA like "Click the link below to get yours". | 不要忘记清晰的口头行动引导，如“点击下方链接购买”。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Price Comparison:** Compare the cost of this tumbler to the famous "Stanley" brand to show value. | **价格对比**：将此款水杯与著名的“史丹利(Stanley)”品牌对比，突出性价比。

3. **Limited Offer:** Add a "Flash Sale" or "TikTok Shop Coupon" overlay to fix the low conversion rate. | **限时优惠**：添加“闪购”或“TikTok小店优惠券”遮罩，以解决转化率低的问题。
