## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议做的):**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add "40oz Kapazität" (40oz Capacity) and "Hält 34 Std. Kalt" (Keeps cold 34h) on screen to reinforce the garbled audio.
    *   *中文:* 添加文字贴纸强调“40oz大容量”和“34小时保冷”。
2.  **Clear CTA:** End with "Jetzt unten bestellen!" (Order below now!) and point to the basket.
    *   *中文:* 结尾明确口播“点击左下角购买”，并配合手势指引。
3.  **Ice Test:** Show ice being put in, or shaking the cup to hear the ice, to prove the "vacuum insulated" claim.
    *   *中文:* 展示放入冰块或摇晃杯子听到冰块声，证明“真空保温”功能。

**DON'T (不要做的):**
1.  **Mumble:** Ensure voiceover is crisp and clear. Use text-to-speech (TTS) if recording environment is bad.
    *   *中文:* 避免口齿不清。如果录音环境不好，直接使用TikTok自带的TTS语音。
2.  **Ignore the Lid:** Don't just show the side; show the lid/straw mechanism to prove it's leak-resistant.
    *   *中文:* 不要只展示侧面，要特写杯盖和吸管，证明其防漏功能。

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **ASMR:** The sound of ice hitting the metal, the lid screwing on, and the "clack" of the straw could be a strong sensory hook.
    *   *中文:* **ASMR方向**：冰块撞击金属的声音、拧盖子声、吸管声都可以作为很好的感官钩子。
2.  **Office/Car Context:** Filming this *in a car cup holder* would immediately validate the "Travel Mug" use case.
    *   *中文:* **场景化**：在车内杯架拍摄，直接验证“车载杯”的使用场景，转化率通常更高。
