## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show Ice Retention:** "Keeps ice for 24h" is a standard flask selling point. Show ice still being there after the "work day".
2.  **Fix the Script:** Instead of repeating "I drink a lot of water," say *why* the flask helps. "Passt nicht in den Getränkehalter? Doch!" (Doesn't fit in cupholder? Yes it does!).
3.  **Add a CTA:** "Sicher dir deine jetzt unten links" (Get yours now bottom left).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Repeat Phrases:** Avoid saying "deswegen nutze ich diese große Flasche" twice in 15 seconds. It sounds scripted.
2.  **Don't Ignore Features:** Don't just show it sitting there. Open the lid, show the straw, turn it upside down (leak test).
3.  **Don't Use Bad Audio:** The mumbled middle section must be re-recorded or cut.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Energy Drink" Angle:** Since it's a Red Bull design, fill it with an energy drink (or mock one) and pour it over ice. Lean into the branding.
2.  **ASMR:** The sound of ice hitting the metal, the straw suction, the lid closing.
3.  **Comparison:** Put it next to a tiny plastic water bottle. "Reicht für den ganzen Tag vs. nach 1 Stunde leer" (Lasts all day vs. empty after 1 hour).
