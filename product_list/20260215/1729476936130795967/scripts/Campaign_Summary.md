## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Critical Filming Requirements (关键拍摄要求)

1. **The Transparent Handle Shot (透明手柄镜头)**  
   MUST show water flowing through filter beads in transparent handle. This is THE trust signal.  
   **Timing:** Macro close-up for 3-5 seconds  
   **Lighting:** Backlight to make beads/water visible  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 3

2. **The Installation Demo (安装演示)**  
   Real-time, uncut footage of hand-tightening installation. No edits or cuts to maintain credibility.  
   **Duration:** Full 20-30 second sequence  
   **Angle:** Over-shoulder POV showing hands only  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 3

3. **The Power Wash Effect (强力冲洗效果)**  
   Show before/after of limescale stain removal on tile or glass (oddly satisfying content).  
   **Setup:** Create or find existing limescale buildup  
   **Timing:** 5-10 second blast, then reveal clean surface  
   **Source:** Analysis Section 6, Image 4

4. **The Skeptical Admission (怀疑式承认)**  
   Creator should admit initial skepticism ("Ich war zuerst skeptisch, aber..."). Germans trust honest reviews over hype.  
   **Delivery:** Understated tone, not enthusiastic oversell  
   **Source:** Synthesis Section 3

### Props & Setting (道具与场景)

**Required:**
- Old shower head with visible limescale buildup
- Clean tiles with one limescale stain (for Power Wash demo)
- Real German bathroom (NOT studio)
- Timer app on phone (for installation script)
- Natural lighting (Germans distrust over-produced content)

**Source:** Synthesis Section 3 (Authentisches UGC-Material)

- **ASMR Audio:** Boost water flow sounds, installation clicks
