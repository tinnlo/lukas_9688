## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Pain Points:** If targeting German speakers, dub this video or recreate it in German focusing on "Kalk" (Limescale).
2.  **Keep the "Trash" Scene:** Visually discarding the old unit conveys strong confidence.
3.  **Zoom in on the Filter:** Show a "dirty vs. clean" filter comparison if possible (after 1 month usage) to prove it catches dirt.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Overproduce:** Do not use studio lighting or heavy editing. The bathroom echo and handheld shake add necessary credibility.
2.  **Don't Skip Installation:** Showing how easy it is to fit (universal fit) removes the biggest barrier to purchase for non-handy people.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Dirty Filter Update:** Create a follow-up video showing the filter brown/yellow after 2 weeks. This visualizes the "invisible enemy" in the water.
2.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of the "Stop" button and the water spray modes could be highlighted more for sensory satisfaction.
3.  **German Dub:** Use an AI dubbing tool to translate this exact high-performing script into German to reach the broader market (95% of the population).
