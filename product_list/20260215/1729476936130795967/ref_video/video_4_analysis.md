## 13. Recommendations | 优化建议

**DO (务必做):**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add dynamic text like "⚠️ Low Pressure?" at 00:00 and "✅ 300% Boost" at 00:09. German users read while watching.
2.  **Highlight "Anti-Kalk" (Anti-Limescale):** In Germany, "Kalk" is a massive pain point. Explicitly mention the filter removes limescale, not just "bacteria".
3.  **Visual ASMR:** Enhance the sound of the water spraying to make it feel powerful.

**DON'T (千万别做):**
1.  **Don't hide the price:** If it's "preiswert" (cheap/good value), overlay the price tag (e.g., "Nur 15€").
2.  **Avoid generic bacteria claims:** "Removes all bacteria" is a high-risk claim that can get ads flagged. Switch to "Removes impurities" (Entfernt Verunreinigungen) or "Clean water" (Sauberes Wasser).

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **"Skin/Hair" Angle:** Create a variation focusing on how hard water damages hair (frizzy hair) and how this filter fixes it. This often converts better than just "water pressure".
2.  **Installation Demo:** Show how easy it is to install (screw off, screw on). "In 30 Sekunden montiert" (Installed in 30s) is a huge selling point for non-handy people.
