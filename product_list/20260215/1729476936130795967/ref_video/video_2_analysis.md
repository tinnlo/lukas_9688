## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Emphasize Hard Water:** Explicitly mention "Kalk" (Limescale) in the text overlay. It's the #1 enemy in German bathrooms.
2.  **Shorten for Ads:** Cut a 30-45 second version focusing ONLY on the "Dirty Filter vs Clean Water" visual or the "Power Wash" mode for paid scaling.
3.  **ASMR Elements:** Amplify the sound of the water spray in the first 3 seconds for a sensory hook.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Over-promise Medical Benefits:** Be careful with claims about curing skin conditions; stick to "softer skin" (weicher) rather than medical cures.
2.  **Hide the Installation:** Never skip the installation part for bathroom fixtures; people fear plumbing work. Keep showing how easy it is.
3.  **Use Robotic TTS:** Stick to the human-sounding voice; a robotic voice would kill the "personal recommendation" vibe.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Filter Change Video:** A follow-up video showing a dirty filter after 1 month vs a new one would be viral gold ("Look what I was bathing in!").
2.  **Pet Washing Angle:** Create a separate creative focused entirely on how the "Stop" button and "Massage" mode make washing dogs easier.
3.  **Renters Angle:** Explicitly label it "The #1 Must-Have for Renters" (Mietwohnung Must-Have) to target that specific demographic.
