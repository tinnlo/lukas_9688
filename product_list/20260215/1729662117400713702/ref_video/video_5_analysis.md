## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Voiceover (German):** Use a TTS or real voice saying "Mein Kind hatte Angst im Dunkeln, bis wir das gefunden haben..." (My child was scared of the dark until we found this...).
2. **Shorten to 15-20s:** 36 seconds is too long. Compress the demo: Tap -> Squish -> Glow -> CTA.
3. **Add Text Overlay:** "3 Helligkeitsstufen" (3 Brightness Levels) and "Super weich" (Super soft) to emphasize features for sound-off users.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** A music-only video rarely builds enough trust or urgency for a purchase in 2026.
2. **Ignore the "Problem":** Don't just show the light; show the *need* (e.g., waking up for baby feeding, scary dark room).
3. **Forget the CTA:** Always tell the viewer where to buy (e.g., "Jetzt im Shop" arrow pointing to the basket).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus heavily on the sound of the silicone being squished or the "click" of the tap for a sensory video.
2. **"Gift Idea" Angle:** Frame it specifically as a birthday gift ("Das perfekte Geschenk für...").
3. **Emotional Hook:** Start with a crying baby sound (audio hook) then silence when the light turns on (visual solution).
