## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Text Overlays | 添加德语字幕:** Use text like "Perfekt zum Einschlafen" (Perfect for falling asleep) or "So weich!" (So soft!) to engage the local audience.
2. **Improve Lighting/Cozy Vibe | 改善灯光/温馨氛围:** Film in a stylized bedroom setting with warm blankets to sell the *feeling*, not just the lamp.
3. **Include a CTA | 加入行动号召:** End with "Jetzt sichern!" (Get it now!) or a pointer to the shop.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence/Music Only | 不要仅依赖音乐:** Add a TTS voiceover explaining the features (e.g., "3 Helligkeitsstufen für dein Zimmer").
2. **Film in Messy Backgrounds | 避免杂乱背景:** Ensure the background is clean and aesthetic (aesthetic decor).
3. **Forget the "Why" | 别忘了"为什么":** Don't just show it lights up; show it solving a problem (e.g., "Scared of the dark?").

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Gift Angle | 礼物角度:** Position it explicitly as a "Geschenkidee für Freundin" (Gift idea for girlfriend) – high performing niche.
2. **ASMR Potential | ASMR 潜力:** Use the "click" sound of the tap and the "squish" sound for a sensory ASMR video.
3. **Reaction Video | 反应视频:** Show a child or person reacting with "Awww" when they see it light up.
