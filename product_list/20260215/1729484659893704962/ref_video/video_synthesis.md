## 6. German Market Psychology | å¾·å›½å¸‚åœºå¿ƒç†

**Cultural Triggers (æ–‡åŒ–è§¦å‘å™¨):**
1.  **"GemÃ¼tlichkeit" (Coziness):**
    *   *Observation:* Winning videos show warm, enclosed spaces.
    *   *Production Choice:* Film in small corners, not empty halls. Use blankets and pillows in the frame.
    *   *Why:* Germans value the home as a private retreat/sanctuary ("RÃ¼ckzugsort").

2.  **Price-Performance Ratio ("Preis-Leistungs-VerhÃ¤ltnis"):**
    *   *Observation:* â‚¬4.86 is an impulse buy, but they fear "Schrott" (junk).
    *   *Production Choice:* Close-ups of the crystal cut material. It must look like glass, not cheap plastic.
    *   *Why:* Even for cheap items, Germans hate feeling ripped off.

3.  **Efficiency & Logic:**
    *   *Observation:* Short videos (6s) worked best.
    *   *Production Choice:* Don't over-explain feelings. Show the function immediately.
    *   *Why:* "Don't waste my time."

4.  **Skepticism of Dropshipping:**
    *   *Observation:* Generic ads fail.
    *   *Production Choice:* Add specific German text overlays. Avoid generic English "Buy Now" stickers.
    *   *Why:* English-only ads signal "China dropshipper." German text signals "Local relevance."

5.  **Lighting as "Feierabend" Ritual:**
    *   *Observation:* Darkness is key.
    *   *Production Choice:* Frame the product as the transition from "Work Mode" to "Relax Mode."
    *   *Why:* The separation of work and private life is culturally strict in Germany.

## 12. Risk / Compliance Notes | é£é™©ä¸åˆè§„æé†’

*   **Epilepsy Warning:** Do not use the "Strobe" mode with extremely fast flashing lights without a warning.
*   **Music Copyright:** Use TikTok's "Commercial Music Library" if running paid ads. For organic, use trending sounds but be aware of takedowns.

*   **Product Safety:** Ensure the CE mark is visible on the product if showing the bottom/label, as Germans look for safety certifications.
