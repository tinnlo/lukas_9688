## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Use German text hooks like *"Dein Zimmer braucht das"* (Your room needs this) or *"POV: Du hast das schönste Zimmer"* (POV: You have the nicest room).
2.  **Show the Remote:** Briefly show the hand clicking the remote to change colors. This proves control and functionality.
3.  **Sync to Beat:** Edit the color changes specifically to match the beat drop of a trending German TikTok song.

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Avoid Ambient Light:** Don't film during the day. Any sunlight washes out the effect and makes the product look cheap/weak.
2.  **Don't Ignore Audio:** Even without a voiceover, use a "Click" sound effect when the color changes to add tactile satisfaction.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **Gift Angle (Gifting Season):** Position it as *"Die beste Geschenkidee unter 20€"* (Best gift idea under €20).
2.  **"Vibe Check" Trend:** Compare "Room before" (boring, dark) vs. "Room after" (Magical aurora) to show the transformation/problem-solution dynamic.
    *   **"Vibe Check" 趋势：** 对比“使用前”（无聊、黑暗）与“使用后”（神奇极光），展示“问题-解决”的动态变化。
