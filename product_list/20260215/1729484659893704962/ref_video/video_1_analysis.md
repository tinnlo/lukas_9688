## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. Use trending "emotional" or "philosophical" audio to create a lifestyle connection.
2. Ensure the room is completely dark to let the product shine.
3. Keep the video under 15 seconds for maximum TikTok algorithm favor.

**DON'T:**
1. Don't use generic "salesy" background music.
2. Avoid showing the product in bright daylight where the effect is lost.
3. Don't over-explain features; let the visuals do the talking.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Split Test:** Try a version with German voiceover to see if it increases the conversion rate (CVR) for the local market.
2. **Bundle Offer:** Mention "Buy 2 for the full room effect" to increase Average Order Value (AOV).
3. **Influencer Whitelisting:** This content style is perfect for Spark Ads through creator accounts.
