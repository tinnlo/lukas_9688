- **Reproducible Elements:**

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show the Unit:** Ensure at least one flash showing the physical crystal lamp so people know what the device looks like, not just the light on the wall. (展示产品实体)
2.  **Add Text Overlay:** A simple German text like "Dein Zimmer Update" (Your room update) or "Entspannung pur" (Pure relaxation) could increase context. (添加德语文字说明)
3.  **Loop Perfectly:** Make the video a seamless loop to encourage multiple watches. (制作完美循环)

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't use bright room lighting:** It will wash out the projector effect and make the product look weak. (不要在明亮环境下拍摄)
2.  **Don't make it too long:** The effect is simple; 6-10 seconds is the sweet spot. (不要太长)
3.  **Don't use chaotic music:** The light moves slowly/fluidly; fast music would clash. (不要使用嘈杂音乐)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Record the *sound* of the button click or a quiet room ambience to increase immersion. (ASMR 角度)
2.  **Comparison:** "With vs. Without" split screen (Normal boring room vs. Magical Aurora room). (对比视角)
3.  **Gift Angle:** Caption it "Geschenkidee für Sie/Ihn" (Gift idea for her/him) to target gift buyers. (礼物角度)
