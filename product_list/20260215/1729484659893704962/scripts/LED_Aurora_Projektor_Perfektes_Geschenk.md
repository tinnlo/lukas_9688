## Scripts

Gift-giving solution for budget-conscious buyers who want to appear thoughtful. Frames the projector as a "looks expensive, actually affordable" present that creates memorable emotional reactions. Targets couples, friends, students.

### Structure (40s)
- Hook: Holding wrapped box with text "Didn't know what to get her/him" (0-3s)
- Setup: Unwrapping scene - recipient's curious face (3-8s)
- Reveal: Opening box, pulling out crystal lamp (8-12s)
- Demo: Plugging it in together, turning it on - reaction shot of amazement (12-22s)
- Payoff: Cozy couple scene under purple/pink waves - "perfect date night" vibe (22-32s)
- CTA: "Affordable thoughtful gift" + TikTok cart (32-40s)
