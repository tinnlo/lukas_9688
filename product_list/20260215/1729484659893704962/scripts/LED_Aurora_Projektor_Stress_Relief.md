## Scripts

Stressed-out person seeks mental decompression through visual escape. Positions the projector as a "Feierabend" (end-of-workday) ritual tool that transforms anxiety into calm through immersive ocean-like light therapy.

### Structure (45s)
- Hook: POV lying in bed, staring at boring white ceiling after stressful day (0-3s)
- Problem: Voice acknowledges daily stress, tight shoulders, racing thoughts (3-8s)
- Reveal: Hand reaches for remote, clicks - ceiling explodes into blue water ripples (8-12s)
- Demo: Slow pan of rippling light waves covering walls and ceiling, close-up of crystal refraction (12-25s)
- Benefits: Deep breath reaction, shoulders relax, mind quiets (25-35s)
- CTA: "Your daily escape ritual" + TikTok cart direction (35-45s)
