## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Critical Filming Requirements | 关键拍摄要求

**Environment Setup (环境设置):**
- ✅ **Darkest possible room** — Projector looks weak in bright spaces (per `image_analysis.md` Section 8 Risk note)
- ✅ **Plain white walls/ceiling** — Maximizes projection clarity and contrast
- ✅ **Clear nightstand/corner** — Product must be visible, not cluttered

**Equipment (设备):**
- Blackout curtains or night shooting (evening 7-10pm)
- White USB adapter visible in setup shots (proves "easy plug-and-play")
- Remote control must be clearly shown in hand (per `video_synthesis.md` Section 3 — "Remote Control Action" builds trust)
