## Scripts

Room transformation narrative targeting aesthetic-seeking Gen Z. Shows dramatic before/after contrast - boring white walls to immersive Aurora effect. Focuses on affordability and quick visual impact for TikTok-ready room content.

### Structure (42s)
- Hook: Split screen or fast cut - left side boring white wall, right side Aurora explosion (0-3s)
- Problem: "My room was so boring" voiceover over generic white space (3-7s)
- Unboxing: Fast unboxing sequence showing crystal lamp, remote, cable (7-12s)
- Setup: Plug in USB → Touch button press → Light reveal (12-17s)
- Demo: Fast montage of 4-5 different colors transforming the space (17-30s)
- Social Proof: "Everyone asks where I got this" reaction shots (30-37s)
- CTA: Price anchor + TikTok cart direction (37-42s)
