## Scripts

### Structure

| Section | Time | Visual | Audio | Purpose |
|---------|------|--------|-------|---------|
| Hook | 0-4s | Close-up foggy glasses, person wiping them frustrated. Cut to clear glasses with mask on. | VO: "Stop suffering this winter." | Problem statement + immediate solution |
| Proof | 4-12s | Extreme close-up on eyes. Person breathes heavily. Glasses stay completely clear. | VO: Anti-fog explanation | Visual proof of airflow system |
| Feature | 12-20s | Finger points to red vents on mask. Cut to side profile showing airflow. | VO: Breathability tech | Show the "killer feature" |
| Payoff | 20-25s | Pull mask down to show steam breath. Snap it back up. | VO: Warmth + seal | Demonstrate full protection |
| CTA | 25-28s | Text overlay: "Neu im Sale" + red arrow pointing down. | VO: Limited time offer | Urgency + action |

### Visual Strategy

**Environment:** Outdoor winter setting, visible steam breath required

**Props:**
- Glasses or sunglasses (essential for this angle)
- Winter coat/jacket
- Optional: High-viz vest for worker aesthetic

**Camera:** Selfie-style close-up (0.5x zoom) on eyes/glasses area

**Key Visual Hooks:**
1. **Foggy to Clear transition** - Split screen effect if possible
2. **Heavy breathing demonstration** - Show air moving through vents
3. **Steam breath when mask pulled down** - Proves it's actually cold
