## Scripts

### Structure

| Section | Time | Visual | Audio | Purpose |
|---------|------|--------|-------|---------|
| Hook | 0-6s | Selfie in snow, person looks tired but comfortable. Visible breath steam. | VO: "6 hours outside. Minus 10." | Credibility through endurance |
| Proof | 6-14s | Pull down neck part to show massive steam breath. Snap it back. | VO: Only face not freezing | Visual proof of thermal seal |
| Feature | 14-22s | Rub mask fabric. Cut to inside-out view showing fleece lining. | VO: No wet fabric, lined interior | Comfort + breathability |
| Payoff | 22-27s | Stretch mask wide to show elasticity. Fit over head easily. | VO: Fits any head, no pressure | Universal fit demonstration |
| CTA | 27-32s | Continue working (shoveling/loading). Text: "Kostenloser Versand" | VO: Sale details | Back-to-work credibility |

### Visual Strategy

**Environment:** Deep winter, snow on ground, overcast sky

**Props:**
- High-visibility safety vest (yellow/orange)
- Work gloves
- Contextual tool: Shovel, delivery bag, or work equipment

**Camera:** Handheld selfie mode, slight shake acceptable (adds realism)

**Key Visual Hooks:**
1. **The "Steam Release"** - Pull down neck, show massive vapor cloud
2. **Snap-back test** - Proves elasticity + seal
3. **Worker aesthetic** - Vest implies durability/utility

**Sound Design:**
- Ambient winter wind (subtle)
- "Crunchy" snow footsteps (ASMR texture)
- Fabric snap sound when elastic rebounds
