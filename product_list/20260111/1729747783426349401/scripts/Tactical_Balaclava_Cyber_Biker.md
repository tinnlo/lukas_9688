## Scripts

### Structure

| Section | Time | Visual | Audio | Purpose |
|---------|------|--------|-------|---------|
| Hook | 0-5s | Slow-motion mask application. Person adjusts straps, transforms into "tactical" look. | VO: "Upgrade your winter outfit." | Style + transformation appeal |
| Proof | 5-12s | Get on bike/scooter. Wind test using hair dryer OR actual riding shot. | VO: Wind protection claim | Functional demonstration |
| Feature | 12-18s | Close-up on red vents. Cut to riding POV with clear visibility. | VO: Airflow + visibility | Tech feature showcase |
| Payoff | 18-22s | Snap-back elasticity test. Cool pose with arms crossed. | VO: Fit + warmth | Comfort + style balance |
| CTA | 22-25s | Ride off into frame. Text overlay with arrow. | VO: Call to action | Dynamic exit |

### Visual Strategy

**Environment:** Urban street, bike lane, or garage with bike visible

**Props:**
- Motorcycle, e-bike, or scooter
- Riding gloves (adds to tactical aesthetic)
- Helmet (visible but not worn for mask showcase)

**Camera:**
- Hook: Slow-motion (60fps) for transformation
- Riding shots: POV from handlebar or follow shot

**Key Visual Hooks:**
1. **Tactical transformation** - Ordinary person → "ready for mission"
2. **Wind test** - Hair dryer or actual riding proves windproof
3. **Red vent close-up** - Aggressive, sporty detail shot

**Style:** Faster cuts, upbeat match, "cool" aesthetic over utility
