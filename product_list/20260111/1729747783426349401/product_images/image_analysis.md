## Customer Pain Points & Solutions | 客户痛点与解决方案

*   **Pain Point:** Regular masks get wet and cold from breathing.
    *   **Solution:** The PVC mask separates breath from fabric.
*   **Pain Point:** Glasses fog up constantly.
    *   **Solution:** Directed airflow vents push breath away from eyes.
*   **Pain Point:** Balaclavas are too tight and cause headaches.
    *   **Solution:** High-elastic "wool" fabric adapts to head size comfortably.
*   **痛点：** 普通面罩因呼吸而变湿变冷。
    *   **解决方案：** PVC 面罩将呼吸与织物隔开。
*   **痛点：** 眼镜经常起雾。
    *   **解决方案：** 定向气流通风口将呼吸推离眼部。
*   **痛点：** 头套太紧导致头痛。
    *   **解决方案：** 高弹力“羊毛”面料舒适地适应头围尺寸。
