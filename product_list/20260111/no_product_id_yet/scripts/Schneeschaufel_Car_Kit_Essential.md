## Scripts

### Structure

| Section | Time | Visual | Audio | Purpose |
|---------|------|--------|-------|---------|
| Hook | 0-4s | Person trying to dig out car tire with hands (failing), looking frustrated | VO: "Don't be the person stuck in the parking lot." | Problem/Fear |
| Solution | 4-8s | Open trunk, pull out extendable shovel, snap it open | VO: The tool you need | Solution reveal |
| Feature | 8-13s | Clearing snow around tire efficiently with large shovel surface | VO: Large surface, clears fast | Benefit demo |
| Payoff | 13-16s | Collapse shovel, fits easily back in trunk | VO: Always in your car | Portability |
| CTA | 16-18s | Trunk closes. Text overlay | VO: Get yours before next storm | Urgency |

### Visual Strategy

**Environment:** Parking lot or driveway with fresh snow

**Props:**
- Car (essential for this angle)
- Winter gloves (authenticity)
- The extendable shovel

**Camera:** Selfie-style + Tripod for trunk shot

**Key Visual Hooks:**
1. **Hand vs Shovel** - Show futility of trying to clear with bare hands
2. **The Extension Snap** - Mechanical satisfaction of opening the shovel
3. **Tetris Win** - Showing it fit perfectly in trunk
