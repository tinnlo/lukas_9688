## Scripts

### Structure

| Section | Time | Visual | Audio | Purpose |
|---------|------|--------|-------|---------|
| Hook | 0-5s | Person using heavy old shovel, stops to rub lower back in pain | VO: "Your back hates winter mornings." | Pain point |
| Transition | 5-8s | Throw old shovel aside. Pick up new lightweight shovel | VO: There's a better way | Solution intro |
| Feature | 8-15s | Effortless shoveling. One hand, show how light it is | VO: Lightweight, extendable, easy | Benefit demo |
| Payoff | 15-19s | Continue clearing without stopping, smiling now | VO: No more back pain | Relief |
| CTA | 19-22s | Finished clean driveway. Collapse and carry with one finger | VO: Treat your back better | Final appeal |

### Visual Strategy

**Environment:** Home driveway or walkway

**Props:**
- Old heavy shovel (for contrast)
- New extendable shovel (hero)
- Winter jacket, gloves

**Camera:** Follow the action, emphasize the weight difference

**Key Visual Hooks:**
1. **The Back Rub** - Universal sign of pain/discomfort
2. **One-Finger Carry** - Prove how light it is
3. **Smile Return** - Transformation from suffering to relief
