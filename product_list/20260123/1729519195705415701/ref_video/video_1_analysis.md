## 3. Hook/Opening Strategy (First 3 Seconds) | 开场策略(前3秒)

- **Hook Type | 钩子类型:** Problem-Solution (Pain Point) | 问题-解决方案（痛点切入）
- **Hook Description | 策略描述:** Immediately addresses a common frustration for power tool users: batteries dying mid-job. It validates the user's struggle ("always empty too fast") before promising an immediate resolution ("That ends now").
- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:** The audio asks a relatable rhetorical question while the visual likely shows a tool stopping or a low battery indicator, creating instant recognition of the problem.
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** **High.** It targets a specific, high-frequency pain point for the target demographic (Makita users) immediately, ensuring they listen to the solution.

## 13. Recommendations | 建议与机会

**DO (执行):**
1.  **Add Human Validation:** Show a real person *actually* using it on a job site, not just B-roll. A "stress test" (e.g., "We drilled 500 holes on one charge") would build massive trust.
2.  **Verify Pronunciation:** Fix the TTS pronunciation of "Makita" (not "Magieter") to sound professional and native.
3.  **Highlight the LED:** The product title mentions an "LED-Anzeige" (Display). Show a close-up of this feature working; it's a premium touch that builds perceived value.

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't Over-promise Capacity:** 8000mAh in a standard form factor can sound "too good to be true" (fake). If it's physically larger, show that. If it's standard size, explain *how* it has more density, or users will assume it's a scam.
2.  **Avoid Generic TTS:** The robotic/mispronounced voice lowers trust for technical products. Use a native German speaker recording.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **Comparison Video:** A split-screen timelapse of this battery vs. an original Makita battery running a fan or light until one dies. This provides irrefutable visual proof.
2.  **ASMR/Unboxing:** A "Satisfying Click" video of the battery sliding into the charger and tool can trigger a sensory response for tool lovers.
3.  **German "TÜV" Style Test:** Mimic a technical review style (measuring voltage/weight) to appeal to the analytical German mindset.
