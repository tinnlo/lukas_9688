## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Fix the Voiceover (修正配音):** Use a native German speaker or a high-end AI (like ElevenLabs) that correctly pronounces "Makita", "mAh", and "Rabatt".
2. **Show Real Comparison (展示真实对比):** Instead of just stating "2-3x runtime", show a split screen of a Makita battery dying while this one keeps going.
3. **Verify Grammar (校对语法):** "Ratt sich an" is gibberish. Use "Sichern Sie sich den Rabatt".

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Bad ASR/Translation (不要依赖劣质翻译):** Direct translation errors like "Liter rischerm" (likely misread from "Lithium" or "Ladezyklen") make the brand look like a scam.
2. **Overload the Script (不要塞太多内容):** 12 selling points in 36 seconds is too fast. Focus on the top 3: 8000mAh, TÜV, Price.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **The "Winter" Angle (冬季切入点):** The "-20°C" feature is excellent for January. Make the *entire* video about "Is your battery dying in the cold?" instead of burying it at 00:23.
2. **Visual ASMR (视觉ASMR):** Show the battery *clicking* satisfyingly into a tool. This proves fit/compatibility visually without needing words.
