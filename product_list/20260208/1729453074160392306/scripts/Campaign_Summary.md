## Compliance & Risk Notes | 合规提醒

### AVOID:
- ❌ "Hält ewig" (lasts forever) - plastic degrades
- ❌ "Tötet Motten" (kills moths) - only prevents, doesn't kill
- ❌ "Komplett geruchsdicht" (completely odor-proof) - not proven

### USE:
- ✅ "Luftdicht versiegelt" (airtight sealed)
- ✅ "Schützt vor Feuchtigkeit und Schädlingen" (protects from moisture and pests)
- ✅ "BPA-frei" (BPA-free)
- ✅ "Von -20°C bis +120°C geeignet" (suitable from -20°C to +120°C)
- ✅ "Mit Etiketten und Marker" (with labels and marker)
