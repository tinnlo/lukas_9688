## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a Voiceover (German):** Even a simple AI TTS saying "This cart saved my messy kitchen!" will double retention. Germans need context. (增加德语配音：即使是简单的AI语音说“这个推车拯救了我的脏厨房！”也能让留存率翻倍。)
2.  **Use Heavy Text Overlays:** If VO is impossible, use large text bubbles: "POV: You found the perfect organizer" or "50% OFF TODAY". (使用大量文字叠加：如果没有配音，必须使用大号文字气泡。)
3.  **Shorten to 15s:** For a silent video, 24s is dragging. Cut it to 12-15s of fast-paced highlights. (缩短至15秒：对于静音视频，24秒太拖沓了。)

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on Captions:** Users don't read captions before watching. The value must be *in* the video (audio or overlay). (不要依赖标题：用户看视频前不看标题。)
2.  **Don't have "Dead Air":** Ensure every 3-second segment changes angle or shows a new use case. (不要有“死气沉沉”的片段：确保每3秒切换角度。)
3.  **Don't ignore Audio Trends:** If using music only, use a "Trending" sound that boosts reach, not generic stock music. (不要忽视音频趋势：如果只用音乐，请使用能增加流量的“热门”音频。)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"ASMR Organization":** Lean fully into the silence by amplifying the *sounds* of the product (wheels rolling, bins snapping in, items being placed). This is a huge niche (#CleanTok). (ASMR整理：通过放大产品的声音——轮子滚动、箱子卡入、物品放置的声音——完全投入静音模式。)
2.  **Stop Motion:** Use the silent format to create a stop-motion video of the cart "building itself" or filling itself up. (定格动画：利用静音形式制作一个推车“自动组装”或自动填满的定格动画。)
3.  **Speed Up:** Speed the video up by 2x to make it chaotic and energetic, matching a fast phonk beat. (加速：将视频加速2倍，使其混乱而充满活力，配合快节奏的Phonk音乐。)
