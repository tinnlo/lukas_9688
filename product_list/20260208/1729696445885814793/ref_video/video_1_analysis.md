## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlay:** Ensure "50% OFF" or the price is visually huge at the end.
2.  **Show "Before" State:** Emphasize the *mess* more at the start to make the *clean* result more satisfying.
3.  **Reply to Comments:** If there are comments asking "Where to buy?", reply with video to boost engagement.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't hide the product:** Ensure the cart is fully visible in the first 1 second.
2.  **Don't use robotic TTS:** Keep the human, enthusiastic voice.
3.  **Don't forget the link:** Make sure the anchor link is active and points to the exact variant shown.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Version:** A version with just the sounds of organizing (clicking, rolling) could go viral as "Satisfying" content.
2.  **"What's in my cart":** A specific niche version (e.g., "Baby Station" or "Snack Cart") to target specific sub-audiences.
3.  **Stitch/Duet:** React to a "Messy Room" video with this product as the solution.
