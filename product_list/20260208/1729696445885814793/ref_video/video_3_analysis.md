## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show "Before" State:** Briefly flash a messy counter before showing the organized rack to heighten the dopamine hit of the solution.
2. **Add Text Overlay:** Place "Nur 15€" (or actual price) and "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) as text overlays to drive action.
3. **Loop the Video:** Ensure the end matches the beginning perfectly to encourage replays.

**DON'T:**
1. **Omit Dimensions:** For a "niche" cart, users need to know if it fits *their* gap. Add text like "Nur 15cm breit" (Only 15cm wide).
2. **Rush the Bathroom Shot:** Ensure the "humidity proof" claim is backed by the cart looking sturdy/plastic/rust-free.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Record the sound of sliding the rack out or placing items to increase sensory engagement.
2. **"Hack" Angle:** Position it not just as storage, but as a "Secret Pantry" for snacks.
