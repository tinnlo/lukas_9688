## Compliance & Risk Notes | 合规提醒

### AVOID:
- ❌ "Tötet 99.9% der Bakterien" (kills 99.9% bacteria) - not a disinfectant wipe
- ❌ "Ersetzt Reiniger" (replaces cleaner) - overselling
- ❌ Medical claims (e.g., "hypoallergenic" without proof)

### USE:
- ✅ "Für Gesicht und Hände geeignet" (suitable for face and hands)
- ✅ "Rückstandsfreie Formel" (residue-free formula)
- ✅ "Sanft zur Haut" (gentle on skin)
- ✅ "Mit natürlichem Duft" (with natural scent)
