## Visual Guidelines | 视觉指南

### Key Shots (Must-Have):
1. **Sensor Trigger** - Hand/cat approaches, water starts (proof of smart feature)
2. **Wireless Placement** - Fountain on unusual spot (coffee table, hallway) to prove "no cables"
3. **Silence Test** - Microphone close to pump, no noise
4. **Cat Drinking** - Eye-level shot, peaceful drinking moment
5. **Filter Closeup** - 5-layer system showing cleanliness
6. **Capacity Demo** - Pouring water into 2.2L tank

### Editing:
- Start fast (0.5s cuts) for hook
- Slow down (2-3s shots) for "cat drinking" proof
- Text overlays: Clean sans-serif, top center or middle left
- Color correction: Bright, water must look blue/clear
- Music: Lo-fi beat, volume 10-15% (never overpower VO)

## Compliance \u0026 Risk Notes | 合规提醒

### AVOID:
- ❌ "Heilt Nierenkrankheiten" (cures kidney disease)
- ❌ "30 Tage Akkulaufzeit" without "Bis zu" (up to)
- ❌ Specific price in VO (TikTok Shop prices change)
- ❌ "Spülmaschinenfest" for pump/battery unit (only tank!)

### USE:
- ✅ "Unterstützt die Flüssigkeitsaufnahme" (supports fluid intake)
- ✅ "Bis zu 30 Tage Akkulaufzeit" (up to 30 days)
- ✅ "Günstiger als du denkst" (cheaper than you think)
- ✅ "Tank ist leicht zu reinigen" (tank is easy to clean)
