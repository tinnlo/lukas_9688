## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the Sensor:** Always capture the exact moment the water starts as the pet approaches.
2. **Stress 'Wireless':** The freedom from cables is a massive selling point for pet owners tired of chew-risks.
3. **Use Native VO:** The colloquial German adds immense trust compared to AI voices.

**DON'T:**
1. **Ignore the Filter:** Don't forget to show the internal filter; hygiene is as important as the gadget factor.
2. **Make it too 'Ad-y':** Keep the "messy house" or "real home" background to maintain UGC authenticity.
3. **Muffle the Sound:** Don't over-edit the background music; let the sound of flowing water be heard.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Health Angle:** Mention vet recommendations for increased water intake in cats.
2. **Multi-Pet Demo:** Show two cats using it to emphasize the 2.2L capacity.
3. **Cleaning Tutorial:** A 5-second "how easy to clean" segment would remove the final barrier to purchase.
