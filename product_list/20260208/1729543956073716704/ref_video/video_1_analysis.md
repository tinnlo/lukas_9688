## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议做的):**
1.  **Emphasize "Silent":** If the pump is silent, add a "shhh" sound effect or mention "Super leise" (Super quiet), as noise is a major objection for fountains.
2.  **Show "Cleaning":** Briefly show how easy it is to take apart and clean (another major pain point for fountain owners).
3.  **Add Text Overlay:** Add "Nie wieder nasse Socken!" (No more wet socks!) as a hook text overlay to catch viewers watching without sound.

**DON'T (不要做的):**
1.  **Don't Fake the Cat:** Do not use stock footage of a cat. It must look like the creator's actual pet interacting with the specific product.
2.  **Don't Drag the Intro:** Keep the "Albtraum" section under 3 seconds (as done here). Don't explain *why* it's bad, just show it's bad.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **Battery Life Angle:** Test a variation focusing solely on "How long does the battery last?" (e.g., "30 Tage ohne Laden?").
2.  **Motion Sensor Demo:** If it has a motion sensor (activates when cat approaches), film a specific shot of the water turning on as the cat walks up. This feels "high tech" and justifies the price.
3.  **Bundle Deal:** Suggest buying filters immediately to increase AOV (Average Order Value).
