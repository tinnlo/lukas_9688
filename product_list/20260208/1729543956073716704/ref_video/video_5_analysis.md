## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1. **Highlight the "Cordless" aspect** in the first 3 seconds visually—it's the strongest differentiator.
2. **Use a split-screen** showing a dirty standard bowl vs. the clean fountain for a stronger visual hook.
3. **Keep the 15-second length;** it's perfect for this product's complexity level.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't hide the price** or the shop link; with a 0.28% conversion, viewers need more "frictionless" ways to buy.
2. **Avoid long technical explanations;** the "Sensor" demo should speak for itself.
3. **Don't use generic stock music;** the current "calm" vibe works, but ensure it doesn't become "boring."

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **A/B Test a "Fear" Hook:** "Is your cat dehydrated? Most cats hate standing water..."
2. **Include a "Unboxing" or "Setup" clip** to show how easy it is to get started (building more trust).
3. **Add a limited-time discount code** overlay to boost the low conversion rate (0.28% -> 1%).
