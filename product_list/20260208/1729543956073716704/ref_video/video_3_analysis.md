## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localized Pain Points:** In Germany, lead with "Energy Saving" or "Order/Cleanliness" (Kein Kabelsalat). In other markets (like US), lead with "Cat Health" or "Convenience".
2.  **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Demonstrate the sensor activating. The visual "off... then on" is the "Aha!" moment.
3.  **Engagement Question:** Always end with a question unrelated to the purchase (e.g., "What is your cat's name?") to boost comment metrics.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't hide the cable:** In the "after" shots, ensure it looks truly wireless. If there is a cable, explain it honestly (e.g., "Battery lasts 30 days").
2.  **Don't use loud music:** For a "Silent" product, the video audio should be clean and calm.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** A version focused entirely on the silence/water sounds could work well for a different audience segment.
2.  **Battery Life Test:** A follow-up video showing "Day 1 vs Day 30" on a single charge would address the #1 objection for battery devices.
3.  **Cleaning Demo:** Germans love hygiene. A video showing how easy it is to take apart and clean (dishwasher safe?) would be a strong conversion driver.
