## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Localize Language:** Dub or caption this video in German (DE). The visual demonstration is good, but the audio limits the audience to <1% of the population.
2. **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Instead of just holding it indoors, actually attach it to a frame or window to demonstrate the "gazebo" use case.
3. **Shorten the Intro:** Cut the "I don't know what it is" segment down to 5 seconds to get to the product reveal faster.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't use foreign languages without subtitles:** For the German TikTok Shop, German audio or hardcoded German subtitles are essential for mass appeal.
2. **Don't linger on folding:** The middle section of unfolding the tarp is too slow; speed this up.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Weather Test:** Pour water on it to prove it is "Waterproof" (as per product title). This visual proof converts very well.
2. **"Before/After":** Show a wet balcony vs. a dry balcony protected by this tarp.
