## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO | 建议做:**
1.  **Focus on Specific Scenarios:** Like "Protecting outdoor furniture" or "Keeping pets dry on the balcony". Specific use cases sell generic products.
2.  **Show "Before/After":** A split second of the windy/wet balcony vs. the calm/warm enclosed space would heighten the contrast.
3.  **Mention Price:** If the price is competitive (e.g., "Under €15"), mention it to highlight the contrast with a real glass Wintergarden (€10,000+).

**DON'T | 建议不做:**
1.  **Don't Over-polish:** A studio shot of a folded tarp is boring. It needs to be seen *working* against the elements.
2.  **Don't Ignore Audio:** The explanation of *why* (warmth) is as important as the visual. Don't use generic music without voiceover.
3.  **Don't Forget Technical Details:** German buyers care about "Ösen" (eyelets) and material thickness. Don't skip these details.

**OPPORTUNITY | 机会:**
1.  **ASMR/Sensory Angle:** Record the sound of rain hitting the tarp from the *inside* to emphasize the cozy/protected feeling.
2.  **Durability Test:** Throw a bucket of water against it or poke it to show it doesn't tear easily.
3.  **Installation Guide:** A fast-forward (timelapse) of how easy it is to hang up using zip ties (Kabelbinder).
