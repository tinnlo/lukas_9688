## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1.  **Add a Verbal CTA:** End with "Get yours 50% off link in bio" or "Click to weatherproof your terrace." (添加口头行动号召)
2.  **Show Installation:** Germans love to see *how* it attaches (grommets, ropes). Prove it is easy to install. (展示安装过程)
3.  **Visual Text Overlays:** Add text like "100% Waterproof" or "Windproof Level 5" on screen to reinforce the VO. (添加视觉文字叠加)

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on TTS alone:** If possible, use a real German voice or a more emotive AI voice. Current one might sound too robotic. (不要仅依赖机械的TTS)
2.  **Forget the "After" Shot:** Show someone actually sitting comfortably *while* it is raining outside. Frame 5 implies calm, but a person enjoying a coffee behind the tarp is stronger social proof. (不要忘记展示使用场景)
3.  **Leave the Ending Open:** Don't just fade out on the product. Use the last 2 seconds for a "Shop Now" card. (不要以开放式结尾)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Seasonal Pivot:** If it's winter, change the script to "Protect your outdoor furniture from snow/frost" instead of "Grilling." (季节性调整：针对冬季防雪/防霜)
2.  **"Invisible" Angle:** Market it as "The protection you don't see" – emphasizing the clarity/view preservation. (强调“隐形”/透明度优势)
3.  **Reply to Comment:** Use this video to reply to a comment asking "Does it work in storms?" to build trust. (回复评论建立信任)
