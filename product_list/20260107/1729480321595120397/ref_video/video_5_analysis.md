## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO:**
1.  **Fix the Script/Voiceover:** Use native German speakers or high-quality AI (ElevenLabs) with verified grammar ("Wasserdichte Plane" not "Planet").
2.  **Add a Visual Hook:** Start with a "Fail" scene (e.g., something getting soaked by rain) then show the solution.
3.  **Add Text Overlays:** Key features (e.g., "100% Wasserdicht", "Inkl. Seil") should appear on screen for silent viewers.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Use Raw TTS Errors:** Never publish an ad where the product name is mispronounced ("Planet" vs "Plane").
2.  **Forget the CTA:** Always end with "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) or a visual arrow to the shop link.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Seasonal Angle:** Pitch it specifically for current German weather (e.g., "Protect your balcony furniture from Winter rain").
2.  **ASMR Style:** Focus on the sound of rain hitting the tarp to create a sensory "protection" feeling.
