## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议做的):**
1.  **Add a specific Call-to-Action:** End with "Get yours now on TikTok Shop" or "Link below" (Jetzt bestellen).
2.  **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Instead of saying "Waterproof," show a heavy bucket of water being thrown at it.
3.  **Address "Wind":** German weather is often windy. Show the tarp staying secure in a breeze to back up the "installation" claim.

**DON'T (不要做的):**
1.  **Over-hype the intro:** Avoid "Oh my god" unless the visual is truly shocking. Try a problem-led intro like "Is your balcony always wet?" (Ist dein Balkon immer nass?).
2.  **Ignore Audio Sync:** Ensure the cut to the "transparency" view happens exactly when the word "Durchblick" is spoken.
3.  **Forget Text Overlays:** Add "50% OFF" or "Free Shipping" stickers if applicable to drive that first sale.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of rain hitting the tarp could be a satisfying ASMR hook.
2.  **Before/After:** Split screen showing a wet, unusable balcony vs. a dry, cozy balcony with the tarp.
3.  **Pet Angle:** Show a dog/cat sitting on the balcony protected from the rain. "Keep your pets dry" is a huge emotional hook.
