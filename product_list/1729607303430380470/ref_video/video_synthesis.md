## Common Winning Patterns

### 1. Hook Types | 钩子类型

**Most Effective Hooks:**

1.  **Negative Pattern Interrupt (负面模式打断)** - Video 2
    *   **Action:** Aggressively throwing an old adapter into the trash.
    *   **Audio:** "No entiendo... realmente inseguros" (I don't understand... really unsafe).
    *   **Why it works:** Stops the scroll by challenging the viewer's current behavior and appealing to safety fears.

2.  **Problem-Solution / Agitation (问题-解决方案/激惹)** - Video 1 & 4
    *   **Action:** Showing a cluttered outlet or inability to plug in a coffee maker.
    *   **Audio:** "Problema con tu enchufe..." (Problem with your plug...) or "Minus one problem in the house."
    *   **Why it works:** Instantly validates a universal daily annoyance.

3.  **Feature-Led "All-Rounder" (功能导向“全能型”)** - Video 3 & 5
    *   **Action:** Hero shot of the device fully utilized.
    *   **Audio:** "All-Round Talent" or "Really perfect."
    *   **Why it works:** Appeals to efficiency-minded buyers (strong for German market).

### 2. Visual Strategy | 视觉策略

**Critical Visual Elements:**
*   **The "Shelf" Hack:** Placing the phone on top of the device. This transforms it from a boring electrical part to a "lifestyle gadget."
*   **Dark Mode Demo:** Physically turning off the room lights to demonstrate the motion-sensor night light. This creates a "wow" moment.
*   **The "Click":** Showing the individual switches being toggled. For the German market, this visualizes "Energy Saving" (Stromsparen).
*   **Mess vs. Order:** Explicitly showing the "Before" (tangled cables) and "After" (neat setup).

### 3. Key Selling Points (Ranked) | 核心卖点

1.  **Organization (Shelf/Holder):** "Keine Kabel auf dem Boden" (No cables on the floor).
2.  **Versatility (6-in-1):** USB-C + USB-A + AC Sockets. Covers all modern device needs.
3.  **Night Light (Ambience):** Motion sensor feature adds perceived value beyond a standard strip.
4.  **Safety & Efficiency:** **Individual Switches** and **CE Certification** are critical trust builders for the German audience.

### 4. Language & Voice Strategy | 语言与声音策略

**Market Requirement:** Germany (DE)
*   **Current State:** Dataset includes Spanish, Russian, and German.
*   **Action:** **Must dub/localize to German.** Non-German audio (Video 1, 2, 4) is a conversion killer in DACH region despite good visuals.

**Winning German Keywords:**
*   "Ordnung" (Order/Tidiness)
*   "Stromsparend" (Energy saving)
*   "Sicher/CE-Zertifiziert" (Safe/CE Certified)
*   "Platzsparend" (Space-saving)
*   "Genial" (Genius/Great)

## Replication Strategy for New Scripts | 新脚本复制策略

### Angle 1: The "Kitchen Chaos" Solver (Problem-Solution)
*   **Hook:** "Nervt dich dieser Kabelsalat in der Küche?" (Does this cable clutter in the kitchen annoy you?) showing a messy counter.
*   **Body:** Plug in the device. Show coffee machine + toaster + phone charging all at once.
*   **Closer:** "Endlich Ordnung." (Finally organized.)

### Angle 2: The "Safety/Trash" Hook (Aggressive)
*   **Hook:** Hold up a cheap white adapter. "Wirf das sofort weg." (Throw this away immediately.) Throw it in a bin.
*   **Body:** "Das hier ist gefährlich. Nimm lieber das..." (This is dangerous. Better take this...) Show the Safety Shutters, CE mark, and robust build.
*   **Closer:** "Sicherheit geht vor." (Safety first.)

### Angle 3: The "Nightstand Upgrade" (Feature-Focus)
*   **Hook:** Dark room, motion light turns on. "Dein Nachttisch braucht dieses Upgrade." (Your nightstand needs this upgrade.)
*   **Body:** Show charging phone on the **Shelf** (no cables on floor). Show the soft night light.
*   **Closer:** "Perfekt fürs Schlafzimmer." (Perfect for the bedroom.)

## Recommendations for Script Generation | 脚本生成建议

### DO's (必须做)
✅ **Localize to German:** Use native speakers or high-quality AI (ElevenLabs) with a "helpful neighbor" tone.
✅ **Show the "Click":** Audio-visual feedback of flipping the switches is satisfying and proves "control."
✅ **Dark Room Shot:** You MUST show the night light in the dark. It's the "delight" feature.
✅ **Verify Plug Standard:** Ensure the video visual matches the **Type F (Schuko)** German standard.
✅ **Mention "CE Certified":** Essential for trust in electronics in Germany.

### DON'Ts (禁止做)
❌ **Don't use US/UK plug visuals:** Immediate scroll for German viewers.
❌ **Don't forget the Shelf:** It's the unique differentiator vs. normal power strips.
❌ **Don't be boring:** Start with a specific problem (Mess, Fear, Darkness), not just "Here is a product."
