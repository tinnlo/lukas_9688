# Script 1: The Kitchen Chaos Solver

## Visual Storyboard

| Time | Visual Action |
|:-----|:--------------|
| **00:00-00:03** | **[HOOK]** POV shot of a kitchen counter. A coffee maker, toaster, and phone charger are fighting for one wall outlet. It's a tangled mess ("Kabelsalat"). |
| **00:03-00:05** | Hand tries to plug in the toaster but there's no space. Frustrated sigh gesture. |
| **00:05-00:10** | **[SOLUTION]** Fast cut. Hand plugs in the **Multi-Function Extender**. It looks sleek and solid. |
| **00:10-00:15** | Rapid fire: Coffee machine plugged in. Toaster plugged in. Phone plugged into the USB-C port. |
| **00:15-00:20** | **[WOW MOMENT]** Hand places the phone on the **top shelf**. No phone lying in possible kitchen spills. |
| **00:20-00:25** | Close up on the **Individual Switches** being clicked off. Visual cue: Green/Blue light goes off. |
| **00:25-00:30** | **[CTA]** Split screen: Left side (Messy Old Way) vs Right side (New Organized Way). Finger points to the orange cart icon below. |
