## 12. Risk Assessment | 风险评估

*   **Compatibility:** Biggest risk is customers buying it for the wrong wall socket type (e.g., trying to use it in Switzerland or UK without adapters). **Mitigation:** Clear close-ups of the plug pins.
*   **Weight/Stability:** If the wall socket is loose, the device might wobble when unplugging cables. **Mitigation:** Show holding the device with one hand while unplugging.
