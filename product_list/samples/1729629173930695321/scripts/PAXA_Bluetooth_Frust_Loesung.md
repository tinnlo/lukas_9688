## Scripts

Split-screen Problem/Solution hook showcasing the Bluetooth pairing frustration vs. instant magnetic induction solution. Shows relatable pain point then demonstrates zero-configuration magic.

### Structure (45s)
- Hook: Bluetooth pairing struggle (frustrated user) (0-3s)
- Problem Amplification: Multiple failed connection attempts (3-10s)
- Solution Reveal: Drop phone on stand → instant sound (10-18s)
- How It Works: Magnetic induction explanation with visuals (18-30s)
- Benefits Summary: No Bluetooth, universal compatibility, dual function (30-40s)
- CTA: Strong call to action (40-45s)
