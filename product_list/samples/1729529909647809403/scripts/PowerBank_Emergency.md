## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Emergency/Battery Anxiety" - trigger universal fear of dying phone battery, then provide instant relief. Targets commuters and heavy phone users. Emotional arc from panic to rescue creates strong conversion motivation.

### Structure (30s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** Red battery panic - universal fear trigger
- **Problem (3-10s):** Scenarios where low battery is critical
- **Solution (10-23s):** Magnetic power bank as instant救命稻草
- **CTA (23-30s):** Prevention positioning - "never again"

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Screen recording: iPhone showing red "1%" battery with low battery warning popup | INSTANT ANXIETY - universal trigger |
| 03-06 | Panic sequence: trying to find cable in messy bag, frustrated expression | AGITATION - amplify the pain point |
| 06-10 | Multiple quick scenarios: train arriving (need ticket), urgent call incoming, GPS navigation failing | URGENCY STACK - when battery death hurts most |
| 10-13 | Hand reaching into pocket, pulling out slim power bank like a superhero reveal | HERO MOMENT - the救命稻草 |
| 13-16 | Magnetic SNAP onto phone back (satisfying sound), immediate green charging bolt icon | INSTANT RELIEF - problem solved in 1 second |
| 16-19 | Battery percentage rapidly increasing: 1% → 5% → 12% in timelapse (5 seconds compressed to 2) | SPEED PROOF - fast charging validation |
| 19-22 | Using phone normally while power bank attached: scrolling, texting, no cables restricting movement | LIFESTYLE FREEDOM - wireless advantage |
| 22-23 | LED display showing "100%" on power bank, confident face | CAPACITY REASSURANCE - won't run out soon |
| 23-27 | Red "BEFORE" text fading to green "AFTER", creator relaxed vs. stressed earlier | EMOTIONAL TRANSFORMATION |
| 27-30 | Text: "Dein Retter für 2026" with product shot + pointing to cart | CTA with positioning as essential safety tool |
