## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Subtitles:** To capture the broader German market while keeping the authentic Persian VO for the niche.
2. **Show Charging Speed:** Visually show the battery percentage going up to prove "Fast Charging".
3. **Improve Lighting:** Ensure the "LED Display" on the power bank is clearly legible.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely solely on Persian:** Without subtitles, you lose 99% of the German market.
2. **Hide the Product Thickness:** Be transparent about size so customers aren't surprised.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the magnetic snap without music.
2. **"Shake Test":** Vigorously shake the phone to prove the magnet is strong (trust builder).
3. **Battery Low Anxiety:** Start the video with a "1% Battery" red icon anxiety trigger.
