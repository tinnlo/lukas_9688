## 3. Hook/Opening Strategy (First 3 Seconds) | 开场策略(前3秒)

- **Hook Type | 钩子类型:** Pricing-FOMO (价格/错失恐惧)
- **Hook Description | 策略描述:** Immediate mention of the product capacity (10,000mAh) followed instantly by a discount offer ("5% Rabatt").
- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:** The product is held clearly in frame while the voiceover announces the deal. Simple, direct visual confirmation of the product being spoken about.
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** **Low/Medium**. While direct, a "5% discount" is a very weak hook for TikTok users accustomed to "Flash Sales" or bigger drops. It doesn't create massive excitement.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Demonstrate the MagSafe/Magnetic feature:** Actually stick it to an iPhone to show the snap and charging animation. Visual proof sells better than holding a black brick.
2.  **Increase the perceived value:** If the discount is only 5%, focus on the *utility* first ("Never run out of battery at New Year's Eve") rather than the small discount.
3.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add "FLASH SALE" or the price (e.g., "€29.99") on screen. Many users watch without sound.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't lead with a weak discount:** "5% off" sounds like a joke in the world of TikTok Shop. If the discount is small, hide it behind the features first.
2.  **Don't use static shots:** Do not just hold the object still for 20 seconds. Move it, show ports, show the LED display turning on.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Problem/Solution Angle:** "Handy leer an Silvester?" (Phone empty on New Year's?) - Connect the product to the immediate event (Year End) mentioned in the transcript.
2.  **Bundle Deal:** If 5% is the max discount, suggest buying 2 for a partner to increase AOV (Average Order Value).
