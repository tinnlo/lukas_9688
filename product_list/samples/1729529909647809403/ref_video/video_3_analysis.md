**DO:**
1.  **Hire a Native German Speaker:** Re-record the voiceover. The current script has grammar issues that scream "cheap foreign dropshipper."
2.  **Cut the Duration:** Slash the video from 81s to **15-25s**. 81s is an eternity for a power bank.
3.  **Show Speed:** Visually demonstrate "Fast Charging" (e.g., show battery % going up fast).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Use Bad AI Translation:** Never rely on direct translation for scripts. "Hier sind das Leib" is nonsensical.
2.  **Bury the Lead:** Don't wait 40 seconds to show the "cool" magnetic feature. It should be in the first 5 seconds.
3.  **Over-explain:** Users know how a power bank works. Focus on what makes *this* one special (Magnetic + Built-in cables).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Unboxing:** Focus on the "snap" sound of the magnet attaching.
2.  **"What's in my bag" Angle:** Position it as an essential item for students/commuters (more relatable than generic "adventure").
3.  **Visual Hooks:** Start with a dead phone screen (black screen) then *SNAP* -> Charging symbol. Immediate visual gratification.
