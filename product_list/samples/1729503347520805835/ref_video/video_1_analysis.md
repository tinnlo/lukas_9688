## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a "Why":** Show a specific problem (e.g., walking dog in pitch black, power outage) that this solves. / **增加“为什么”：** 展示这个产品解决的具体问题（例如，漆黑中遛狗，停电）。
2.  **Include Voiceover:** A German narrator explaining "Nie wieder Batterien kaufen" (Never buy batteries again) adds value. / **包含旁白：** 德语解说员解释“Nie wieder Batterien kaufen”（再也不用买电池）增加价值。
3.  **Show Scale:** Compare the brightness to a phone light to show dominance. / **展示规模：** 将亮度与手机灯光进行比较以显示优势。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Silence:** Background music alone is passive. / **依赖静音：** 仅有背景音乐是被动的。
2.  **Just Show Hardware:** Don't just show the lamp; show what it *illuminates*. / **只展示硬件：** 不要只展示灯；展示它*照亮*了什么。
3.  **Forget the CTA:** Explicitly tell viewers to check the link for the discount. / **忘记CTA：** 明确告诉观众查看链接以获取折扣。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Safety Angle:** Market it as a car emergency tool (seatbelt cutter/glass breaker if applicable, or just SOS light). / **安全角度：** 将其作为汽车应急工具进行营销（如果适用，安全带切割器/玻璃破碎器，或仅作为SOS灯）。
2.  **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for Dad." / **礼物角度：** “送给爸爸的完美礼物。”
3.  **ASMR:** Clicky buttons and satisfying focus adjustment sounds. / **ASMR：** 点击按钮和令人满意的焦距调节声音。
