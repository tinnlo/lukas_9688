## 11. Risk / Compliance Notes | È£éÈô©‰∏éÂêàËßÑÊèêÈÜí

*   **False Claims:** Do not claim specific lumen counts (e.g., "10,000 Lumens") unless verified by the manufacturer. Stick to subjective terms like "Superhell" (Super bright) or "Taghell" (Day bright).
*   **Safety:** Do not show shining the light directly into people's eyes or at moving vehicles (cars/planes). This violates safety guidelines and promotes dangerous behavior.
*   **Music Rights:** Ensure background music is commercial-use licensed (use TikTok Commercial Music Library) to avoid muting or takedowns.
*   **Ad Disclosure:** Even if organic-style, ensure appropriate ad disclosures if required by German law (e.g., "Werbung" or "Anzeige" in caption) if this is a sponsored post.
*   **Shipping Promises:** Do not promise "Next Day Delivery" unless the logistics (Twinkle Home) guarantees it. Stick to "Schneller Versand" (Fast shipping).
