## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Safety First" - tap into fear/safety anxiety around walking alone or with dogs in dark German winters (early sunset at 16:30). The strobe mode positioning as a self-defense tool creates emotional urgency. Target dog owners, women, and safety-conscious commuters.

### Structure (35s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** POV dark park walk - "Do you feel safe at night?"
- **Agitation (3-10s):** Build tension with scary shadows/sounds
- **Solution (10-22s):** Light turns on, strobe mode demo
- **Benefits (22-30s):** Safety features + visibility advantages
- **CTA (30-35s):** Peace of mind positioning

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | POV from eye level: walking down dark tree-lined path, barely visible, ominous shadows moving | FEAR TRIGGER - establish vulnerability |
| 03-06 | Audio: rustling leaves sound effect, footsteps behind, quick pan to darkness (nothing visible) | TENSION BUILD - paranoia amplification |
| 06-10 | Text overlay: "Dunkle Wege sind gefährlich" fading in slowly with heartbeat sound | PROBLEM AGITATION - verbalize fear |
| 10-13 | Hand reaching into pocket (shaky cam for nervousness), pulling out flashlight | PREPARATION - hero object introduction |
| 13-16 | CLICK! 4-core LED blasts forward, entire path illuminated to bright daylight, shadows disappear | RELIEF MOMENT - problem solved instantly |
| 16-19 | Quick button press → STROBE MODE activates (rapid flashing), camera shakes slightly (disorienting effect) | DEFENSE PROOF - tactical capability demonstration |
| 19-22 | Text: "Stroboskop zur Abwehr" with defensive posture silhouette overlay | SAFETY FEATURE emphasis |
| 22-25 | Dog on leash walking confidently, owner holding flashlight, both clearly visible from car perspective | USE CASE - dog walking safety |
| 25-28 | Side COB light mode: illuminates entire sidewalk width (not just spot), finding keys on ground demo | PRACTICAL BENEFIT - visibility breadth |
| 28-30 | Confident face in flashlight glow, relaxed expression (vs scared opening) | EMOTIONAL TRANSFORMATION |
| 30-35 | Product on table with house key, leash, creating "safety kit" vibe, text: "Hol dir Sicherheit hier" | CTA with lifestyle positioning |
