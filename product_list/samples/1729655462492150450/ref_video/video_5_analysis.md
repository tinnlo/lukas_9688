## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text overlays (e.g., "Wie echte Hände" - Like real hands) to better target the DE market. | 添加德语文字叠加（如“Wie echte Hände”），以更好地定位德国市场。
2. **Show Reaction:** Include a shot of the user's face showing genuine relief/pleasure to humanize the benefit. | 加入用户面部表现出真正放松/愉悦的镜头，使利益人性化。
3. **Use TTS:** Add a German Text-to-Speech voiceover explaining the "4D Kneading" technology. | 添加德语TTS旁白，解释“4D揉捏”技术。

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Music Only:** Don't rely solely on vague music tracks; explain *why* it helps. | 不要仅依赖模糊的音乐曲目；解释*为什么*它有用。
2. **Dark Lighting:** Avoid shadows on the device; the mechanism needs to be clearly visible. | 避免设备上有阴影；机制需要清晰可见。
3. **Ignore Sound:** Don't use audio that overpowers the visual or feels disconnected. | 不要使用压倒视觉或感觉不相关的音频。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the machine (if quiet) and the "squish" of the massage for an ASMR-style ad. | 专注于机器的声音（如果安静）和按摩的“挤压感”，制作ASMR风格的广告。
2. **Gift Angle:** Frame it explicitly as a gift for parents ("Geschenk für Eltern") which performs well in DE. | 明确将其包装为给父母的礼物（“Geschenk für Eltern”），这在德国表现良好。
3. **Comparison:** Split screen with a standard massage pillow to show why "Bionic" is better. | 与普通按摩枕分屏对比，展示为何“仿生”更好。
