## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. Use the "Comparison to human hands" angle; it's very effective for this product category.
2. Maintain the casual, unscripted feel of the dialogue.
3. Show the device's mechanical movement in macro close-ups.

**DON'T:**
1. Over-edit with flashy transitions; it ruins the "authentic" vibe.
2. Use generic AI voices; the real human emotion in this video is its strongest asset.
3. Hide the straps; showing how it's worn is important for size context.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. Add a "Flash Sale" or "Limited Offer" text overlay in the last 5 seconds to boost immediate conversion.
2. Test a variant where the "partner" tries it and reacts even more dramatically.
3. Use a split screen showing the massager vs. a manual massage to emphasize the "uniformity" point mentioned in the transcript.
