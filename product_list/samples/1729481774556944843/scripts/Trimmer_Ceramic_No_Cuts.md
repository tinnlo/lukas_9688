## Scripts

"Problem-Agitation" hook addressing physical pain of shaving. Targets visceral reaction to "cuts," "burns," "ingrown hairs." Showcases ceramic blade safety advantage over traditional razors. German men value practicality over aesthetics.

### Structure (33s)
- Hook: Relatable shaving pain problem (0-3s)
- Agitation: Show old razor damage (3-8s)
- Solution: Ceramic blade reveal (8-16s)
- Demo: Safe live shave test (16-26s)
- CTA: No more fear (26-33s)
