- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:**

    - **Visual:** Likely shows the "problem" state (stubble/irritation) or the generic "bad" tool to establish context instantly.
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** **High**. It validates the user's struggle before selling the solution. The phrase "shave my wood" (slang) in the following seconds acts as a secondary "Pattern Interrupt" to keep engagement high.
