## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize for Germany:** Re-record the voiceover in **German** (or use an AI German dub). The visual content is universal, but the audio friction is likely killing conversions.
2.  **Match Currency:** Change the script to reference Euros (€) instead of Pounds (£) to align with the TikTok Shop DE interface.
3.  **Subtitle Strategy:** Add German captions (burned in) if keeping English audio is necessary for the creator's brand.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Ignore Regional Context:** Do not mention "Pounds" in a Euro market; it confuses the value proposition.
2.  **Rely solely on English:** While catchy, English-only ads in DACH (Germany/Austria/Switzerland) regions often have lower conversion rates than localized ones.
3.  **Over-polish:** Keep the "messy/real" bathroom vibe; it adds to the authenticity of a grooming routine.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The "shaving" sound (buzzing) vs "silence" (smoothness) could be emphasized for a sensory hook.
2.  **Partner/Reaction:** Have a partner react to the "Smell better" claim (e.g., "My girlfriend loves it").
3.  **Gift Angle:** Position as "The only gift he actually needs" if targeting female buyers (gifting market).
