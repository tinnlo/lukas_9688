## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Focus on Visual "Pop":** Ensure the first frame is extremely high-quality. Use a "Hero Shot" of the product with the ceramic blade visible.
2.  **Sync to Trending Audio:** Use TikTok’s commercial library to find beats that are currently "hot" in Germany.
3.  **Highlight the "3-in-1":** Use text overlays like "3 Tools - 1 Preis" (3 tools - 1 price) to emphasize value in German.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Add Fluff:** Avoid long intros or "Hey guys" greetings. In a 9s format, every frame must sell.
2.  **Don't Use Poor Lighting:** This format relies 100% on visual appeal. If the lighting is dull, the product looks cheap.
3.  **Don't Forget Localization:** Even with no voiceover, ensure the on-screen text (if any) is in German to maximize trust in the DE market.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Split Test ASMR:** Try a version with no music, only the "buzz" of the trimmer and the "click" of the attachments.
2.  **Comparison Shot:** A 1-second split screen showing the 3 heads working on 3 different areas (Face/Body/Nose).
3.  **Discount Countdown:** Add a small timer or "Last 24 Hours" overlay to increase the FOMO of the "Winter Deals".
