- **Equipment Needed:** Smartphone with good macro lens, ring light, spray bottle (for rain), key/knife (for scratch test).
- **Key Success Factor:** The **Destruction/Durability Test**. Showing the watch survive "abuse" builds instant trust in the build quality. | **破坏/耐用性测试**。展示手表经受“虐待”后依然完好，能瞬间建立对制造质量的信任。
- **Reproducible Elements:** The 3-part structure: Feature Scroll -> Scratch Test -> Water Test. This is a standard template for rugged smartwatches. | 三段式结构：功能滚动 -> 刮擦测试 -> 防水测试。这是耐用智能手表的标准模板。

1. **Add a Verbal CTA:** End with "Jetzt 50% Rabatt sichern" (Secure 50% off now) or "Link in Bio". | 结尾加上“现在享受50%折扣”或“链接在主页”。

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't ignore the "BT Calling" feature:** It's a high-value feature for cheap watches; omitting it leaves value on the table. | 不要忽略“蓝牙通话”功能：这是廉价手表的高价值功能；省略它会降低感知价值。
