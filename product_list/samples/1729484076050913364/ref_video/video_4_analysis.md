## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Emphasize "German Language":** Continue to highlight this; it reduces purchase anxiety for DACH region buyers.
2.  **Show the Swap:** Actually film the process of changing the strap from rubber to metal to prove how easy it is.
3.  **Price Anchor:** Mention a specific competitor price (e.g., "Costs 1/10th of a brand name watch") to make the savings concrete.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Overcrowd the text:** Avoid covering the watch face with too much text overlay; let the HD display speak for itself.
2.  **Ignore the "Tool":** You mentioned a tool is included; show a close-up of it to emphasize the "DIY friendly" aspect.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Waterproof Test:** If the watch supports it, a 2-second clip of it being splashed or dunked adds massive trust.
2.  **Gift Angle:** "Perfect gift for Dad/Boyfriend" - the metal strap makes it look like a substantial gift.
3.  **Notification Demo:** Show a WhatsApp message in German appearing on the watch face to prove the integration works.
