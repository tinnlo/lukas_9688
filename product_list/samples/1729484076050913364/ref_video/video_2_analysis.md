## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the Watch in Action:** Include a shot of someone selecting a workout mode to prove the "100+ Modes" claim.
   **展示手表使用中：** 包含有人选择锻炼模式的镜头，以证明“100+模式”的宣称。
2. **Add Text Overlays:** Explicitly list "Dual Band" or "7-Day Battery" on screen in German.
   **添加文字叠加：** 在屏幕上用德语明确列出“双频”或“7天电池”。
3. **Use a Voiceover:** Even a simple TTS voiceover reading the key features improves retention.
   **使用旁白：** 即使是简单的TTS旁白朗读关键功能也能提高留存率。

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** Silent videos lose users who are listening; always include trending audio or voice.
   **依赖静音：** 静音视频会失去正在收听的用户；始终包含流行音频 or 语音。
2. **Film in Dark Environments:** Ensure the screen brightness doesn't blow out the camera exposure.
   **在黑暗环境中拍摄：** 确保屏幕亮度不会导致相机曝光过度。
3. **Forget the 'Problem':** Don't just show the watch; hint at why they need it (tracking steps, missing calls).
   **忘记“痛点”：** 不要只展示手表；暗示他们为什么需要它（追踪步数，漏接电话）。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Unboxing" Angle:** A quick unboxing to show the "Back to School" gift potential.
   **“开箱”角度：** 快速开箱以展示“返校”礼物的潜力。
2. **Strap Swap:** Show how easy it is to change styles/options.
   **更换表带：** 展示改变风格/选项是多么容易。
3. **Notification Demo:** Show a message popping up to demonstrate the BT Calling/Notification feature.
   **通知演示：** 展示弹出消息以演示蓝牙通话/通知功能。
