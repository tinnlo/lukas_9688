## 6. Visual Editing Playbook | è§†è§‰å‰ªè¾‘æ‰“æ³•

### Pacing Strategy
*   **0-2s:** Visual Shock (Scratch/Water) or Face-to-Camera Hook.
*   **2-5s:** The "Problem/Solution" setup (e.g., "Need a watch but broke?").
*   **5-12s:** Rapid Feature Demo (Swipe, Tap, Scroll). *Must be sped up 1.5x*.
*   **12-18s:** The "Value Stack" (Show box contents: Straps, Charger, Manual).
*   **18-20s:** CTA with on-screen arrow pointing to cart.

### Text Overlay Rules
*   **Font:** San Francisco (iOS style) or Classic TikTok Font.
*   **Color:** White text with Black background (High contrast) or Red background for "Sale/Price".
*   **Placement:** Center for Hook, Top-Center for Features, Bottom-Center for CTA. *Never cover the cart icon area (bottom left).*
*   **Language:** ALWAYS in German.

### Sound Design
*   **Music:** Upbeat, instrumental tech/lo-fi. No distracting vocals.
*   **SFX:** Add subtle "woosh" sounds on transitions. Add "ding" sound when showing price/value.
*   **Voiceover:** Use "Narrator" (Male) or "Jessie" (Female) TTS if real voice is unavailable, but real German voice is 10x better.

## 12. Risk / Compliance Notes | é£é™©ä¸åˆè§„æé†’

*   **"Price Error" Claims:** Be careful using terms like "Preisfehler" (Price Error). Platforms may flag this as misleading if it's just a regular sale. Use "Blitzangebot" (Flash Deal) instead.
*   **Medical Claims:** Do NOT claim the Heart Rate/Blood Pressure features are "medical grade" or can "diagnose" anything. Always add a disclaimer or visual text: "Not a medical device" (Kein Medizinprodukt).
*   **Waterproof vs. Water Resistant:** Do not show diving/swimming if it's only IP67 (splash/rain proof). Stick to rain/washing hands scenarios.
*   **Brand Comparison:** Do not show the Apple Logo or mention "Apple Watch" explicitly. Use "Teure Marken" (Expensive brands).
*   **Music Rights:** Use TikTok Commercial Library music only to avoid muting.
