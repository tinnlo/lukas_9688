## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Price Gap" hook - reveal the premium look vs. the shockingly low price, then overwhelm with value in a rapid feature speedrun. Target the "Value Hunter" audience who wants Samsung/Garmin quality at 1/10th the price.

### Structure (35s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** Price gap shock - "Looks expensive, costs super little"
- **Proof (3-18s):** Rapid feature showcase - display, battery, sports modes, dual straps
- **Benefits (18-30s):** Health tracking, waterproof, Bluetooth calling
- **CTA (30-35s):** Direct command with urgency

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Close-up of metal strap watch on wrist, slow rotation to catch light, then SNAP ZOOM to "super günstig" price tag | Creates immediate VALUE DISCREPANCY - premium aesthetic contradicts budget price |
| 03-08 | Ultra-fast cuts (0.5s each): HD screen lighting up → 710mAh battery icon → scrolling through 100+ sports modes → dual strap reveal | Overwhelming FEATURE DENSITY proves "not cheap garbage" |
| 08-13 | Split screen: expensive branded watch (blurred logo) on left vs. this watch on right with checkmarks appearing | Visual COMPARISON validates "why pay more?" positioning |
| 13-18 | Wrist roll showing metal strap, then SNAP finger transition to silicone strap on same wrist | Demonstrates VERSATILITY and 2-in-1 value |
| 18-23 | Real-time Bluetooth call demo: phone ringing in bag → tap watch → talking into wrist | Proves SOFTWARE INTEGRATION works (eliminates "it's fake" fear) |
| 23-28 | Water splash test under tap + close-up of IP67 label on packaging | DURABILITY validation for skeptical buyers |
| 28-30 | Top-down shot: watch + 2 straps + charging cable + tool + box laid out | Reinforces COMPLETE BUNDLE value |
| 30-35 | Direct POV: finger pointing at bottom-left TikTok cart icon, then creator's confident face | DIRECT COMMAND with social proof (creator approval) |
