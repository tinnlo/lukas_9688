## Scripts

**Structure: Stranger Question + Feature Introduction (Coffee Shop)**

1. **Hook (0-3s):** Woman compliments: "I notice your watch is stylish!"
2. **Price Question (3-5s):** Woman asks: "Is it expensive?"
3. **Value Response (5-10s):** Man answers: "Not really. Good design, built solid, very good price"
4. **Feature Showcase (10-35s):** Rapid feature introduction - display, battery, sports modes, dual straps, waterproof, health tracking
5. **Purchase Question (35-37s):** Woman asks: "Where to buy?"
6. **CTA (37-40s):** TikTok Shop, click the link easily
