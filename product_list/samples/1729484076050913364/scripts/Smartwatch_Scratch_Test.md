## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Skeptic Turned Believer" format - start with doubt about cheap quality, then systematically destroy objections through aggressive stress tests. Position as the "Fragile Watch Killer" for the blue-collar "Doer" audience who needs rugged reliability.

### Structure (38s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** Skeptical question - "For this price, this must be trash, right?"
- **Test 1 (3-10s):** Scratch resistance with keys - sparks flying
- **Test 2 (10-18s):** Water submersion under tap (IP67 validation)
- **Revelation (18-30s):** Premium features reveal (HD screen, battery, calling)
- **CTA (30-38s):** Urgency-driven purchase command

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Creator holding watch skeptically, eyebrow raised, turning it over examining cheap-looking silicone strap | Establishes DOUBT - mirrors viewer's skepticism about low price |
| 03-06 | AGGRESSIVE close-up: metal key scratching screen surface hard (shot from 2 angles), sparks/light reflection effect | VISUAL PROOF of scratch resistance - overrides "cheap = fragile" assumption |
| 06-10 | Slow-mo wipe with cloth → screen is PRISTINE, no scratches visible, creator's shocked face reaction | Validates durability claim with BEFORE/AFTER evidence |
| 10-13 | Plunge watch under running tap water, water splashing aggressively on screen, close-up of droplets | STRESS TEST for IP67 claim - shows it's not just marketing |
| 13-18 | Pull watch from water, shake off water droplets, screen LIGHTS UP instantly showing time | Proves WATERPROOF functionality - screen still works perfectly |
| 18-21 | Rapid UI scroll through menus (sped up 1.5x), highlighting Sports Modes, Heart Rate, Sleep icons | Demonstrates SMOOTH PERFORMANCE - "cheap but not laggy" |
| 21-25 | Battery info screen showing 90% charge + "Day 4" text overlay | BATTERY PROOF contradicts "dies in 1 day" objection |
| 25-30 | Incoming call demo: phone in pocket, watch vibrates, tap to answer, talking clearly into wrist speaker | INTEGRATION PROOF - Bluetooth calling actually works |
| 30-33 | Quick swap: metal strap to silicone strap using included tool (satisfying CLICK sound) | BONUS VALUE reveal - 2 straps increase perceived worth |
| 33-38 | Creator's confident nod, pointing to TikTok cart, text overlay: "Nur noch wenige verfügbar!" | URGENCY CTA with scarcity trigger |
