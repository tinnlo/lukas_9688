## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Business to Gym Transformer" angle - solve the "boring watches don't fit every outfit" pain point by showcasing the dual-strap versatility. Target the "Gift Buyer" and style-conscious "Budget Techie" who wants one device for all occasions.
