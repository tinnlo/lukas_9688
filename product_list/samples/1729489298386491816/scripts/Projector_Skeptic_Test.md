## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Skeptic Turned Believer" format - start with extreme doubt about cheap projectors, then systematically prove quality through pixel-peeping, brightness tests, and app functionality. Counter the "you get what you pay for" objection. Target value-conscious buyers who need convincing.

### Structure (38s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** Skeptical question - "Can a cheap projector actually be good?"
- **Test 1 (3-12s):** Walk camera to wall, show text sharpness (pixel peep)
- **Test 2 (12-20s):** Lights ON vs OFF brightness comparison
- **Test 3 (20-30s):** App demo - Netflix actually works smoothly
- **Revelation (30-38s):** Price reveal and purchase justification

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
