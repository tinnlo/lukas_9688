## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Gamer's Dream" angle - appeal to console gamers (Switch, PS5, Xbox) who want massive immersion without spending thousands on a TV. Show actual gameplay with vibrant racing visuals to prove low latency and vivid colors. Target the "Budget Gamer" and "Gadget Lover" segments.

### Structure (33s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** Gameplay shock - massive Mario Kart on wall
- **Reveal (3-10s):** Pan back to show tiny projector creating huge image
- **Setup (10-20s):** HDMI connection demo, size comparison
- **Proof (20-28s):** Low latency button press test
- **CTA (28-33s):** Price comparison with gaming monitors/TVs
