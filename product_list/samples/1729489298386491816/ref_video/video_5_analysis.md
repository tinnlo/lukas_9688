## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Show the projection FIRST:** Start the video with the massive, bright screen on the wall, *then* cut to the tiny device. This creates visual surprise.
2.  **Improve Audio/Subtitles:** Ensure the voiceover is crisp. Add large, dynamic captions (captions are missing or static in many low-effort UGC).
3.  **Specific Use Case:** Instead of "consoles generally", show *Mario Kart* or *Call of Duty* specifically being played to target a niche.

**DON'T (避免错误):**
1.  **Don't start with just the box:** Unboxing is slow. Start with the *result*.
2.  **Don't mumble key terms:** Words like "Beamer" (Projector) and "Store" need to be clear.
3.  **Don't ignore the lighting:** Ensure the "dark room" shots are pitch black so the projector brightness pops.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **"Bedroom Cinema" Angle:** Frame it specifically as "The ultimate upgrade for your bedroom" rather than just a gadget.
2.  **Ceiling Projection:** Show the projector pointing at the ceiling for watching while lying in bed (a huge trend for these rotatable projectors like the HY300).
3.  **ASMR:** Use the sound of the fan (if quiet) or the clicking of the interface to add sensory texture.
