## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize for Germany:** Use German TTS ("Warte bis zum Ende" instead of "Let the end comes out") or German trending music.
2.  **Add a Clear CTA:** Add a text overlay at the end: "Jetzt kaufen" (Buy now) or "Link in Bio".
3.  **Show the Price:** If it's affordable, overlay the price (€60-€80 range) to trigger impulse buys.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Use Broken English:** "Let the end comes out" sounds unprofessional. Use correct grammar or just music.
2.  **Make it too Dark:** Ensure the product itself is visible at some point, not just the projection.
3.  **Leave the Ending Hanging:** 11 seconds is short; ensure the final frame lingers long enough to see the product quality.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Unboxing:** Focus on the sounds of the fan (if quiet) or the button clicks to add sensory depth.
2.  **Ceiling Projection:** Show projecting onto the ceiling while lying in bed – a huge selling point for this specific rotatable projector.
3.  **Gaming Test:** Show a popular game (Mario Kart, COD) being played on it to attract gamers.
