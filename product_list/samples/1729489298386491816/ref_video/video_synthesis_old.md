## 6. Visual Editing Playbook | è§†è§‰å‰ªè¾‘æ‰“æ³•

*   **Pacing:**
    *   **0-2s:** Cut every 0.5s (Fast cuts to grab attention).
    *   **2-10s:** Slower pans (1-2s clips) to show features clearly.
    *   **10-end:** Match cuts to the beat of the music.
*   **Text Overlays:**
    *   Use the "Classic" or "Typewriter" font in TikTok editor.
    *   **Color:** White text with Black background (high contrast) or Yellow/Black for "Deal" vibes.
    *   **Placement:** Center screen for hooks, bottom center for info. *Never* cover the product.
*   **Lighting Strategy:**
    *   Always start with **Ambient Light** (Golden hour or lamp) to show the device looks nice.
    *   Hard cut to **Darkness** when the projection starts. This enhances the perceived brightness.
*   **Audio Mixing:**
    *   Voiceover: 100% volume.
    *   Music: 15-20% volume (Background).
    *   Original Sound (SFX): Boost clicking sounds/fan whir (if pleasant) for ASMR effect.
