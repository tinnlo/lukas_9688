## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Voiceover (German):** Explain *what* it is. "Das ist das beste Kino für unter 50€!" (This is the best cinema for under €50!).
2. **Show the Hardware:** The HY300's rotating stand is its "Cool Factor". Spin it, tilt it to the ceiling.
3. **Add Text Overlays:** "Android 11 Built-in", "Netflix Included", "Link in Bio".

**DON'T:**
1. **Silent Videos:** Never post silent product demos for gadgets; they look like spam.
2. **Dark Only:** Start with the light ON to show the device, *then* turn it off to show the brightness (the "before/after" effect).
3. **Ignore CTA:** Always tell them where to buy.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Ceiling Cinema" Angle:** Position the projector to play movies on the ceiling while lying in bed. This is the #1 viral use case for this specific model.
2. **Unboxing ASMR:** Tap the box, peel the plastic, plug it in. High sensory engagement.
3. **Price Anchor:** "Why buy a €500 TV when you can have this?" (Cost comparison).
