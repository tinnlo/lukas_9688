## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Show Usage:** Actually turn the toothbrush on. Show water vibrating or brush teeth. Static objects don't sell "performance" devices well.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add a sticker saying "8x Refills Included" or "€XX.XX Price". Visualizing the price makes the "Almost free" claim real.
3.  **Improve Hook Visual:** Don't just hold the brush. Start with the box opening or dumping the 8 heads onto the table to create visual chaos/interest.

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't Rely on Audio Only:** Many users watch on mute. Without text overlays, the "8 heads" value might be missed if they just see a box.
2.  **Avoid "Salesy" Adjectives:** "Mega mega günstig" can sound like a scam. Use specific numbers instead (e.g., "Under €15").
3.  **Don't Hide the Product:** Ensure the lighting highlights the texture/quality of the brush so it doesn't look like cheap plastic.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of the toothbrush vibrating or the "click" of the heads snapping on could be a sensory hook.
2.  **Comparison:** Split screen with a famous brand toothbrush showing "Their replacement heads = €20" vs "Our whole set + 8 heads = €15".
3.  **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for your boyfriend who never changes his toothbrush."
