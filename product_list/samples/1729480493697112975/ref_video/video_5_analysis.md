## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Visual Hook Upgrade:** Start with a close-up of the dirty old toothbrush vs. this new one, or dump all 8 heads onto the table to visually demonstrate "abundance" in the first second.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add energetic text like "⚠️ 2 JAHRE RUHE" (2 Years Peace of Mind) or "Billiger als Drogerie" (Cheaper than drugstores) to catch sound-off users.
3.  **Direct CTA:** Point to the bottom left corner at the end. Say "Click the yellow basket before the sale ends."

**DON'T (避免错误):**
1.  **Don't rely on auto-captions:** The transcript errors suggest poor audio clarity or bad captioning. Ensure subtitles are accurate.
2.  **Don't be too passive:** "Check the reviews" allows the user to leave the video. Keep them *in* the purchase flow.
3.  **Don't ignore lighting:** If claiming "solid build quality," show it under crisp light so the plastic doesn't look cheap.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of the vibration modes or the "click" of the head snapping on could be satisfying.
2.  **Cost Comparison:** Visually compare the price of this set vs. 2 years of branded replacement heads (e.g., "This set = €15 vs Brand = €80"). This appeals strongly to the German "Sparfuchs" (saver) mentality.
3.  **Travel Angle:** Since it has USB-C and is "simple", market it as the perfect "Zweit-Zahnbürste" (secondary toothbrush) for travel or gym bags.
