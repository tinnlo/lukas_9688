- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:** Direct eye contact (implied) + Rhetorical Question. The audio challenges a norm, while the visual likely sets up the "old vs. new" contrast.
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** Highly effective for this demographic. It feels like a friend giving advice rather than a corporate ad. The specific mention of "traditional toothbrushes" sets up the solution perfectly.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Count the heads visually:** Literally count "One, two, three..." on camera to emphasize value.
2.  **Use the "Shipping Hack":** If your shop has a free shipping threshold, *always* teach the user how to reach it as a "money-saving tip."
3.  **Target Diaspora:** If selling in US/UK/DE, try creating ads in Polish, Arabic, Spanish, or Turkish. Lower competition, higher trust.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't over-polish:** A studio-quality ad would look like a corporate lie here. Keep it raw.
2.  **Don't hide the price:** The €10 price point is the star. Mention it early (within 15s).
3.  **Don't ignore the box:** Show the packaging to prove it's a "real" brand (Deep Clean) and not loose junk.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Gift Angle:** "Buy one for you and one for your spouse/friend" aligns perfectly with the "Buy 2" strategy.
2.  **Sound Test:** Hold the brush to the mic to show the ultrasonic vibration power (ASMR element).
3.  **Visual Comparison:** Use a disclosing tablet (shows plaque) to demonstrate the cleaning power vs manual brush (more production effort, high payoff).
