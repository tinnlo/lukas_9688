## Scripts

**Concept**: "Skeptic's Test" - address doubt about cheap winter gloves being effective. Prove through water test, wind test, touchscreen test. Trust-building through transparency.

### Structure (32s)
- **Hook (0-3s):** Price skepticism
- **Test 1 (3-12s):** Water resistance
- **Test 2 (12-20s):** Touchscreen functionality
- **Test 3 (20-28s):** Wind/cold protection
- **CTA (28-32s):** Value proven

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Price tag "mega günstig", skeptical face, text: "So günstig? Das kann nicht gut sein!" | DOUBT establishment |
| 03-06 | Pouring water on gloves, camera close-up watching droplets | WATER TEST setup |
| 06-09 | Water beading off surface, wiping away - hands inside stay DRY | WATERPROOF PROOF |
| 09-12 | Inside view: hand is dry, fleece lining intact | VERIFICATION |
