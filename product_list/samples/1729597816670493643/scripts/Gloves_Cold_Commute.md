## Scripts

**Concept**: "Cold Commute Solver" - target winter cyclists/commuters suffering from frozen hands. Pain point: can't use phone without removing gloves. Solution: touchscreen + warmth.

### Structure (30s)
- **Hook (0-3s):** Cold hands pain
- **Problem (3-10s):** Phone usability frustration
- **Solution (10-23s):** Gloves demo - warmth + touchscreen
- **CTA (23-30s):** Winter essential positioning

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | POV cycling in cold, hands shivering on handlebars, breath visible | COLD PAIN trigger |
| 03-06 | Trying to use phone with bare hands, fumbling, freezing | FRUSTRATION agitation |
| 06-10 | Putting on thick gloves, phone becomes unusable (no touch response) | COMMON PROBLEM |
| 10-13 | Wearing these gloves, smoothly unlocking phone with thumb | TOUCHSCREEN PROOF |
| 13-16 | Texting, swiping, maps working perfectly while wearing gloves | FUNCTIONALITY validation |
| 16-20 | Fleece lining close-up, hands visibly warm and comfortable | WARMTH BENEFIT |
| 20-23 | Water splash test - drops bead off, hands stay dry | WATER RESISTANCE feature |
| 23-27 | Cycling through rain/snow, hands protected and using GPS | REAL-WORLD usage |
| 27-30 | Text: "Nie wieder kalte Hände!" + product shot | WINTER CTA |
