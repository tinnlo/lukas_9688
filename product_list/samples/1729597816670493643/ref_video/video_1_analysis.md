## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Replicate the CTA:** Use the "If the basket is orange..." line in other product videos; it directs attention effectively.
2.  **Keep it Short:** The 10-second format forces tight editing and high information density.
3.  **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Ensure the "waterproof" claim is visually backed by water beading off the glove.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Overcomplicate:** Do not add long intros. The "Every cyclist needs..." hook is perfect.
2.  **Don't Forget Context:** Ensure the first frame clearly screams "Winter/Cold" visually, not just in audio.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **A/B Test Hooks:** Try "Stop freezing hands" vs "Best Winter Hack" to see which resonates more with the German audience.
2.  **Scale:** The 0.13% conversion on low views suggests this could scale with paid spend or better hashtags to reach more cyclists.
3.  **Reply-to-Comment:** Make a version answering a specific question like "How long does the battery last?" to build trust.
