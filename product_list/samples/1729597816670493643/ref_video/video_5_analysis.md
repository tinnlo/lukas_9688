## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** Switch to a **German Voiceover**. Targeting Germany with English audio is a major friction point for trust and mass appeal. | **本地化音频：** 切换到**德语旁白**。用英语音频针对德国市场是建立信任和大众吸引力的主要阻碍点。
2.  **Add a Visual CTA:** Add an on-screen text overlay at 00:07 saying "Shop Now" or "50% Off" (if applicable) to prompt action. | **增加视觉CTA：** 在00:07处添加屏幕文字叠加“立即购买”或“5折优惠”（如适用）以提示行动。
3.  **Show the "Problem":** Start with a shot of freezing/red hands *before* showing the warm gloves to heighten the relief/satisfaction. | **展示“问题”：** 在展示温暖手套*之前*，先展示冻僵/发红的手，以增强缓解/满足感。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely only on Audio for Context:** Don't assume users have sound on. Add text captions (in German) matching the key benefits (e.g., "Winddicht", "Warm"). | **仅依赖音频提供语境：** 不要假设用户开启声音。添加与主要利益点匹配的（德语）字幕（例如，“Winddicht”，“Warm”）。
2.  **Be Boring:** Don't just show the glove sitting on a table. Ensure there is movement in every frame. | **枯燥乏味：** 不要只展示手套放在桌子上。确保每一帧都有动态。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Waterproof" Test:** The product title mentions "Waterproof" but the transcript misses it. A visual of pouring water on the glove would be a high-converting "Oddly Satisfying" hook. | **“防水”测试：** 产品标题提到了“防水”，但脚本忽略了这一点。在手套上倒水的视觉画面将是一个高转化的“解压”钩子。
2.  **Screen Compatibility:** Demonstrate the "Touchscreen Compatible" feature (mentioned in title) by using a phone while wearing them. This is a huge selling point for runners/cyclists using GPS. | **屏幕兼容性：** 通过戴着手套使用手机来演示“触屏兼容”功能（标题中提到）。对于使用GPS的跑步者/骑行者来说，这是一个巨大的卖点。
