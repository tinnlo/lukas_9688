## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Voiceover:** Explain the benefits (e.g., "Best 10€ I spent", "Watch movies in the shower"). German audiences respond well to clear value propositions.
2. **Shorten the Loop:** Cut to 15-20s. 27s is too long for a simple feature demo without dialogue.
3. **Enhance the Hook:** Start with a question text overlay: "Bored in the shower?" or "Never wet your phone again."

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** Silent reviews rarely go viral unless the visual is mind-blowing. This product is a utility, it needs explanation.
2. **Bad Lighting:** Avoid filming in a dark shower; use a ring light or open the bathroom door for better exposure.
3. **Forget CTA:** Always point to the yellow basket at the end.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Use the sound of the shower + the clear sound of the music coming from the box to prove audio quality (which is a common doubt for this product).
2. **Gift Angle:** Frame it as "The perfect gift for your boyfriend/girlfriend who takes long showers."
3. **Scarcity:** Mention "Selling out fast" or "50% off" in text overlays to drive the 1 sale up.
