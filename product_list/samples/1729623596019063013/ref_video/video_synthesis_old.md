## 12. Risk / Compliance Notes | é£é™©ä¸åˆè§„æé†’

1.  **Waterproof Claims:** Do NOT claim it is "IP68" or "Submersible" unless verified. Use "SpritzwassergeschÃ¼tzt" (Splash-proof) or "Dusche-Geeignet" (Suitable for shower) to be safe, though "Wasserdicht" (Waterproof) is generally accepted for these boxes if they seal. 5.  **Music Rights:** Use TikTok Commercial Music Library only for ads. Do not use trending pop songs without license.
