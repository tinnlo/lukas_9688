## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** MUST use German (TTS or Native) or English for the German market.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Use German text overlays ("Wasserdicht!", "Handy in der Dusche") to communicate value even with sound off.
3.  **Show "POV":** Film from the perspective of the person in the shower to increase immersion.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Mismatch Language:** Never run a Farsi voiceover ad in a DACH (Germany/Austria/Switzerland) region unless specifically targeting that niche community (which is too small for scale).
2.  **Hide the Screen:** Ensure reflections don't obscure the phone screen inside the case; distinct visibility is key.
3.  **Forget Sound Test:** Show that sound actually comes through the case clearly (audio demo).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sounds of the shower and the "click" of the case closing for a sensory hook.
2.  **"Couple/Gift" Angle:** Position it as a funny/useful gift for a partner who spends too much time in the bathroom.
3.  **Music Sync:** Edit the cuts to the beat of a trending German song or popular TikTok sound to boost retention.
