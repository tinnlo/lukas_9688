## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Language:** Use German text overlays (e.g., "Holzig & Maskulin", "Das perfekte Sommer-Parfüm") to speak directly to the target market. | **本地化语言：** 使用德语文字叠加（例如 "Holzig & Maskulin"），直接针对目标市场。
2.  **Visualize the Scent:** Use props (wood, spices, summer vibes) or text to describe the smell. You cannot smell video; you must *describe* it. | **视觉化香味：** 使用道具（木头、香料）或文字来描述气味。观众闻不到视频，你必须*描述*它。
3.  **Add a Hook:** Start with a question or statement: "Looking for the best budget summer scent?" | **增加钩子：** 以问题或陈述开场："在寻找性价比最高的夏季香水吗？"

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on English Audio:** Avoid English-only audio for a strictly German shop unless using a viral trend. | **不要依赖纯英语音频：** 除非利用病毒式趋势，否则不要在德国商店仅使用英语音频。
2.  **Be Silent/Vague:** Don't just show the bottle. Explain *why* it's good. | **不要沉默/含糊：** 不要只展示瓶子。解释*为什么*它好。
3.  **Forget the CTA:** Never end without pointing to the shop link. | **不要忘记行动号召：** 结尾一定要指向商店链接。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Dupe" Strategy:** If it smells like a famous brand, imply it: "Smells like [Brand X] but costs 10%." | **"平替"策略：** 如果它闻起来像大牌，暗示它："闻起来像 [某大牌] 但价格只有它的10%。"
2.  **Gift Angle:** Frame it specifically as a gift for a boyfriend/husband ("Geschenkidee für ihn"). | **礼物角度：** 专门将其包装为给男友/丈夫的礼物。
3.  **ASMR Unboxing:** If the voiceover is hard, try a high-quality ASMR unboxing with satisfying sounds. | **ASMR 开箱：** 如果旁白很难做，尝试高质量的 ASMR 开箱，配合令人满足的声音。
