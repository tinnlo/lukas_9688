## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Describe the Scent vividly:** Instead of just "pleasant," use evocative words (e.g., "Smells like a fresh forest after rain," "Sexy wood notes"). translate the invisible scent into visible emotions.
2.  **Improve Audio:** Use a lapel mic or record voiceover in a quiet closet. Clear audio is crucial for retention.
3.  **Show the Spray:** Ensure there is a high-quality, slow-motion shot of the mist spraying. This is a key visual trigger for perfume ads.

**DON'T (不要):**
1.  **Don't Frame as "Retoure" (Returns):** Unless it's a specific "Amazon Returns Pallet" channel, don't associate a new beauty product with "old/returned" goods. It implies it's expired or used.
2.  **Don't Mumble the Hook:** The first 3 seconds must be crisp. "Stop buying expensive designer scents, try this!" is better than "So, let's see what we have."
3.  **Don't hide the bottle:** Get to the product reveal within the first 5-8 seconds. 20+ seconds to open a box is too slow for TikTok.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **"Dupe" Angle:** If the perfume smells like a famous brand (e.g., Dior Sauvage), explicitly compare it (or strongly hint at it). "Riecht wie der 100€ Bestseller, kostet aber nur 20€." (Smells like the 100€ bestseller but costs only 20€.)
2.  **Street Interviews:** Spray it on strangers (friends) and record their genuine "Wow, you smell good" reaction. Social proof sells perfume better than a solo review.
3.  **ASMR Unboxing:** Focus on the sounds of the premium packaging (if it is premium) to signal quality without words.
