## Scripts

**Concept: The "Skeptic Turned Believer" (Credibility Through Honest Critique)**

Starting with criticism builds trust, then the transformation proves genuine quality.

### Structure (45s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** "I have 4 bottles, this one is the winner"
- **Critique (3-10s):** Initial note is sharp, build credibility through honesty
- **Redemption (10-30s):** After 10 minutes, the musk notes emerge, transformation
- **Verdict (30-40s):** Price-to-quality ratio is unbeatable
- **CTA (40-45s):** "Get it now, link below"
