1. **Eau de Parfum Concentration (EDP 浓度)** – Higher oil concentration than EDT. **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Germans know the difference; builds technical credibility

3. **Woody/Musk/Nutmeg Notes (木质/麝香/肉豆蔻香调)** – Specific scent profile. **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Specificity signals authenticity vs. generic claims

4. **Premium Atomizer Quality (优质喷头)** – Fine mist vs. large droplets. **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Visual proof of luxury feel

5. **Designer Dupe Positioning (设计师平替定位)** – Same scent family as €100+ brands. **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Value-conscious buyers get "luxury" at budget price

## Production Notes | 制作说明

*From image_analysis.md Section 10 (来自图片分析第10节 - Visual Hooks | 视觉钩子):*

- **Primary Visual (主要视觉):** Bottle with amber-colored liquid, minimalist design – Why it works (为什么有效): Signals premium quality without appearing cheap

- **Lighting (灯光):** Warm tones for woody scent association, avoid harsh white (cold = feminine)

- **Props Needed (所需道具):**

  - Test strips (纸质试香条) – for Script 1 scent development
  - Timer or clock (计时器或时钟) – for longevity claims in all scripts

- **Audio (音频):** Keep natural spray sound (ASMR quality) to emphasize atomizer fineness (保持自然喷雾声/ASMR质量以强调喷头细腻度)

**Filming Tips from synthesis (来自综合分析的拍摄技巧):**
- Show mist against dark background (显示深色背景下的雾化) – Purpose (目的): Visual proof of fine atomizer
- Heavy cap click sound (瓶盖重击声) – Purpose (目的): Psychological signal of build quality
- Spray on skin not air (喷在皮肤上而非空气中) – Purpose (目的): Proves confidence + safety
