## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Strategic Pillars (from video_synthesis.md):

1. **Honest Warning (Reverse Psychology)**
   - DE: "Kauf das NICHT, wenn du Aufmerksamkeit hasst" – Umgekehrte Psychologie löst Neugier aus.
   - ZH: "如果你讨厌被关注，别买这个" —— 逆向心理引发好奇心。
   - **Why it works:** Germans distrust hyperbole; reverse psychology feels authentic.

2. **Wait for It (Scent Development)**
   - DE: "Am Anfang riecht es okay... aber warte 5 Minuten" – Ehrlichkeit über Entwicklung baut Vertrauen.
   - ZH: "刚开始闻起来还行...但等 5 分钟" —— 对发展过程的诚实建立信任。
   - **Why it works:** Nuanced reviews signal credibility (not "perfect immediately").

3. **Partner Reaction (Relationship Desire)**
   - DE: "Mein Freund trägt das und ich flippe aus" – Echte Anziehungsreaktion visualisiert.
   - ZH: "我男朋友用这个我快疯了" —— 真实吸引力反应可视化。
   - **Why it works:** Social proof + emotional desire for attractiveness.

### Target Audience (from synthesis §6):
- **Primary (主要受众):** Men 18-40, budget-conscious but style-oriented | 男性 18-40 岁，注重性价比和风格
- **Secondary (次要受众):** Women buying gifts for partners | 女性为伴侣购买礼物
- **Pain Point (痛点):** Designer perfumes too expensive; need affordable masculine scent | 设计师香水太贵；需要实惠的男性香调

## 5. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Visual Requirements (from synthesis §3 Proof Mechanics):
- **Mist Visibility:** Fine mist spray against dark background (luxury signal) | 深色背景下的细腻喷雾（奢华信号）
- **Heavy Cap Sound:** Magnetic cap click (build quality proxy) | 磁性瓶盖咔哒声（质量代理）

- **Skin Test:** Spray on actual skin (safety confidence) | 喷在真实皮肤上（安全信心）
