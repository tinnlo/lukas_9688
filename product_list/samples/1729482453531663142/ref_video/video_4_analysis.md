## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show the Switch:** Explicitly show the hand turning the light ON to dramatize the transformation.
2.  **Zoom In:** Extreme close-up on the petal texture to prove the "realism" claim.
3.  **Use Text Overlay:** Add "Link in Bio" or "50% Off" sticker at the end to boost the weak CTA.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Overspeed:** 9 seconds is very fast. Ensure the product is on screen long enough to be appreciated.
2.  **Bad Lighting:** Avoid grainy footage in the dark scene; use a secondary dim light to outline the vase.
3.  **Generic Audio:** Ensure the music matches the "cozy" vibe; avoid jarring trending sounds if they don't fit the mood.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Gift Angle:** Create a version specifically labeled "The Perfect Gift for Mom/Girlfriend" (text overlay) as this is a strong specific niche mentioned in the product title but only briefly in the VO.
2.  **Unboxing:** Show the packaging to build trust that it arrives safely (fragile item fear).
3.  **Color Options:** If available in pink/white/yellow, show a quick montage of all colors.
