## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** Test a German (DE) TTS or voiceover with the same script to potentially increase conversion in the local market.
2.  **Show Variations:** If the lamp comes in different colors (pink, yellow, purple), show a quick montage of them.
3.  **Social Proof:** Add a small text overlay of a 5-star review or "Viral on TikTok" to boost credibility.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Over-complicate:** Keep the focus on the single best offer (the coupon). Don't confuse with too many features.
2.  **Dark Visuals:** While it's a lamp, ensure the product itself is visible, not just the light.
3.  **Fake Urgency:** Use "ends tonight" sparingly; if users see it tomorrow, it loses trust. (Though effective for short bursts).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Gift Angle:** Frame it explicitly as a "Mother's Day" or "Best Friend" gift in the hook to broaden the reason for purchase.
2.  **Unboxing ASMR:** A version focusing purely on the sound of unboxing and turning it on could capture the "satisfying" niche.
3.  **Day vs Night:** Split screen showing how it looks as a decor piece during the day vs. a lamp at night.
