## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add "Geschenkidee für Paare" (Gift idea for couples) as text on screen immediately. Audio alone is often missed by users watching on mute.
2.  **Stronger CTA:** End with "Link im Profil" or "Jetzt im TikTok Shop" (Link in bio / Now in TikTok Shop) and point to the screen.
3.  **Show Application:** Spray the perfume! Show the mist. Visualizing the scent helps sell it.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't mumble key info:** The end of the transcript ("veräuberlar") was unclear. Ensure the brand name and "Available Now" are crisp.
2.  **Don't rely just on the box:** Make sure the bottles are the hero of the video, not just the cardboard packaging.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Reaction Shot:** Instead of just showing the box, show a partner's reaction to smelling it on you. "Pov: Your boyfriend loves this scent."
2.  **Price Anchor:** Mention the price if it's affordable (e.g., "Only €XX for two!"). Value for money is a huge driver on TikTok Shop.
3.  **Scent Visuals:** Use props (vanilla pods, warm colors) to visually represent the "Vanille" scent mentioned.
