## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add captions for key scent notes ("Holzig", "Nicht Süß", "Für Paare"). Many watch without sound. (添加关键卖点字幕：如“木质调”、“不甜腻”、“情侣适用”)
2.  **Clarify the Product Name:** Ensure the brand name "LASTAR" is mentioned clearly or shown on screen text. (确保品牌名清晰可见或被提及)
3.  **Stronger CTA:** Change "Link is below" to "Grab it for Valentine's Day before it's gone!" (强化行动号召：改为“情人节礼物首选，手慢无！”)

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Mumble:** The transcript errors suggest diction issues. Articulate the specific scent notes clearly. (不要含糊其辞：清晰表达香调细节)
2.  **Don't Rush the Scent Description:** "Woody" is a key selling point; give it a visual cue (wood background or color shift). (不要匆忙带过香调描述：配合视觉元素强化“木质调”概念)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Gift-Giving Angle:** Pivot the script to "The *perfect* gift does exist..." instead of just showing the box. (送礼角度：将脚本重点转为“完美的礼物真的存在……”)
2.  **Reaction Shot:** Show a partner smelling it on the creator and reacting positively. (反应镜头：展示伴侣闻到香味后的积极反应)
3.  **ASMR:** Focus on the sound of the box opening or the spray nozzle for a sensory hook. (ASMR：利用开箱或喷雾的声音作为感官钩子)
