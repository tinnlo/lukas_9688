## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add German Subtitles:** Essential. Even for the Russian niche, text overlays in German allow the algorithm to index the content for the broader DE market.
2.  **Improve Lighting:** The "pearly" texture is a key selling point; use a direct light source to make it sparkle/shimmer more.
3.  **Visual CTA:** Add a sticker pointing to the shopping cart icon at the end.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely solely on audio:** Many users watch with sound off. Without text on screen ("18€", "2 for 1"), silent viewers miss the offer entirely.
2.  **Don't ignore the "Gifting" visual:** Instead of just saying "for Valentine's", show the box being wrapped or placed in a gift bag to visualize the gifting aspect.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The "shimmer" liquid suggests a visual ASMR angle. Slow-motion shots of the liquid swirling could be used as a "Satisfying" hook.
2.  **"His & Hers" Angle:** Since it's a 2-pack and Valentine's is coming, position it as "Keep one, Gift one" or "Scent mixing" strategy.
