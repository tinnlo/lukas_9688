## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Create Actual Video Content:** Extend duration to at least 7-15 seconds.
   *(制作真正的视频内容：将时长延长至至少7-15秒。)*
2. **Show Usage:** Film a person unboxing or spraying the perfume to show the mist and bottle quality.
   *(展示使用过程：拍摄开箱或喷洒香水的画面，以展示喷雾和瓶身质量。)*
3. **Add Text Overlays:** Highlight key benefits like "Langanhaltend" (Long-lasting) or "Perfektes Geschenk" (Perfect Gift) on screen.
   *(添加文字叠加：在屏幕上突出主要卖点，如“持久留香”或“完美礼物”。)*

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Use Static Images as Video:** The algorithm penalizes non-engaging content. Use "Photo Mode" if you only have images, or animate them.
   *(不要将静态图片作为视频发布：算法会惩罚缺乏互动的内容。如果只有图片，请使用“图片模式”或将其动态化。)*
2. **Don't Ignore Audio:** Even a simple voiceover saying "The best vanilla scent for winter" adds massive value.
   *(不要忽视音频：即使是简单的旁白“冬季最佳香草味”也能增加巨大价值。)*

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Unboxing:** The "Geschenkbox" aspect is perfect for a satisfying unwrapping video (rustling paper, opening box).
   *(ASMR开箱：“礼盒”特性非常适合制作令人满足的拆箱视频（纸张沙沙声，打开盒子）。)*
2. **Scent Description:** visually describe the scent (e.g., cutting a vanilla pod, warm sweater vibes) to evoke the smell through the screen.
   *(气味描述：通过视觉描述气味（例如，切开香草荚，温暖毛衣的感觉），透过屏幕唤起嗅觉联想。)*
