## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Core Positioning (核心定位)
**"Riecht wie 100€, kostet unter 20€"** (Smells like €100, costs under €20)

This campaign avoids direct price claims to comply with TikTok DE policies (避免直接价格声明以符合TikTok德国政策). Instead, we use value framing: "erschwinglich" (affordable), "gutes Preis-Leistungs-Verhältnis" (good value), and comparative positioning without exact euros.

### Strategic Pillars (战略支柱)
1. **Trust Through Testing** (通过测试建立信任): Replicate Video 1's time-jump proof structure to demonstrate longevity claims credibly
2. **Gifting Angle** (送礼角度): Position as "looks expensive, affordable gift" for female buyers targeting male recipients
3. **Smart Saving** (聪明消费): Frame purchase as intelligent choice, not cheap compromise ("sei smart, nicht geizig")

### Compliance-First Approach (合规优先)
Per `video_synthesis.md` Section 12 and skill compliance rules:
- **No exact € amounts** in voiceover or captions (无具体欧元金额)
- **No absolute claims** like "100% langanhaltend" - use "hilft bei," "unterstützt" (无绝对声明)
- **No false urgency** ("nur noch 3 Stück") - use "jetzt verfügbar" (无虚假紧迫感)
- All scripts validated against `validate_compliance_flags.py` before publication

## 3. Key Selling Points | 核心卖点

Extracted from `video_synthesis.md` Section 5 (German Copy Bank) and image analysis:

### Primary USPs (主要卖点)
1. **Affordable Luxury** (平价奢华): Designer scent quality at student budget pricing
2. **Long-Lasting EDP** (持久淡香精): Eau de Parfum concentration (not Cologne) = better longevity
3. **Fresh Masculine Scent** (清新阳刚香味): Ocean/citrus notes, not sweet or overpowering
4. **Premium Packaging** (高级包装): Heavy glass bottle, gift-ready presentation
5. **Versatile Usage** (多用途): Office-safe, gym bag portable, date night appropriate

### Proof Mechanisms (证据机制)
Per `video_synthesis.md` Section 3:
- **The "Clock Cut"** (时钟剪辑): Show morning application, then "5 hours later" to prove longevity
- **The "Shake Test"** (摇瓶测试): Demonstrate oil concentration (if bubbles form = quality indicator)
- **Honest Disclaimers** (诚实声明): "Fades after 5 hours" builds more trust than "lasts 24 hours"
- **Heavy Bottle Sound** (重瓶声音): Solid "thud" when placed on table = luxury signal
