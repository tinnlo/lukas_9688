## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议做的):**
1.  **Add Subtitles (添加字幕):** Add German subtitles! This instantly expands the audience from just Pashto/Farsi speakers to *all* Germans, leveraging the "exotic/authentic" vibe.
2.  **Show Application (展示使用):** Actually spray the perfume. Visual sensory cues (mist, wet skin) help sell scent online.
3.  **Improve Lighting (改善灯光):** Ensure the bottle label is readable. If it's dark/blurry, trust decreases.

**DON'T (不要做的):**
1.  **Ignore the German text (不要忽视德语文本):** The product title is German, but the audio is not. Don't leave the visual text disconnect. Match visual text to the audio language or vice versa.
2.  **Ramble (不要啰嗦):** 13s is good, but ensure the first 3s clearly show *why* this perfume is special, not just *that* it is a perfume.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **"Gift for Him" Angle (送男友角度):** Since it's a men's perfume ("Herrenparfum"), explicitly target women in the community looking for gifts for husbands/brothers.
2.  **Price Anchor (价格锚点):** Mention the price. If it's cheap, say "Only €X". If it's luxury, compare it to a big brand ("Smells like Dior but costs half").
3.  **Reply to Comment (评论互动):** Use a video reply to a comment asking "Is it halal?" or "Does it last long?" to build more engagement.
