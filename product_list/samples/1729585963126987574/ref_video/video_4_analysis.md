## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show a Reaction:** Have a person smell the perfume and react (smile, "Wow", lean in). This triggers mirror neurons. | **展示反应**：让人闻香水并做出反应（微笑、“哇”、凑近）。这能触发镜像神经元。
2. **Visualize the Scent:** Use text overlays to describe notes (e.g., "Woody & Fresh" / "Holzig & Frisch"). | **香气可视化**：使用文字贴纸描述香调（如“木质调 & 清新”）。
3. **Add a Hook:** Start with a question like "Searching for the perfect gift?" (Suchst du das perfekte Geschenk?). | **增加钩子**：以问题开场，如“正在寻找完美的礼物？”。

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Static Shots:** Do not make a slideshow of the bottle. TikTok is a video-first platform. | **依赖静态镜头**：不要制作瓶身的幻灯片。TikTok是视频优先的平台。
2. **Forget the CTA:** Always tell the user to check the shop link, especially for gifts. | **忘记行动号召**：总是告诉用户查看店铺链接，尤其是作为礼物推广时。
3. **Leave Audio Empty:** Use trending audio or clear voiceover explaining the benefits. | **音频留白**：使用热门音频或清晰的口播解释产品优势。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Girlfriend POV":** Film a video from a partner's perspective gifting this to their boyfriend. "My boyfriend smells amazing now..." | **“女友视角”**：拍摄一个伴侣视角送礼物给男朋友的视频。“我男朋友现在闻起来太棒了……”
2. **ASMR Unboxing:** Focus on the sound of the box opening and the spray nozzle "pshh" sound. | **ASMR开箱**：专注于打开盒子的声音和喷头的“嘶嘶”声。
3. **Price/Value Contrast:** If the perfume is affordable, compare it to a generic expensive brand (without naming names). "Smells like €100, costs way less." | **价格/价值对比**：如果香水价格实惠，将其与通用昂贵品牌对比（不点名）。“闻起来像100欧，价格却低得多。”
