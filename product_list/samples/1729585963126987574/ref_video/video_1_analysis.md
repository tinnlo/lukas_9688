## 12. Replication Insights | 复制这一策略

- **Production Budget:** **Low (Almost Zero).** Smartphone + Product.
- **Equipment Needed:** Smartphone with decent camera, natural window light or car interior.
- **Key Success Factor:** **The "Time-Travel" Structure.** Don't just say it lasts long. Record the intro, cut, record the outro 5 hours later (wearing different clothes or different lighting helps prove time passed).
- **Reproducible Elements:**
    1.  Hold product -> "This looks expensive."
    2.  Smell it -> "Smells like [Abstract Concept e.g., Beach/Success]."
    3.  Say -> "But check the price."
    4.  Cut -> "Update: It's been 5 hours and I still smell it."

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (务必做):**
1.  **Keep the "Real Test" format:** The "come back later" update is the strongest trust builder for beauty/fragrance products.
2.  **Use Abstract/Synesthetic descriptions:** Describing a scent as "Blue" or "Power" is often more effective on TikTok than technical notes like "Bergamot and Sandalwood."
3.  **Mention Price Early:** For dupes/cheap perfumes, the price is the main hook. Mentioning "Under €20" in the first 5 seconds might hook more viewers.

**DON'T (不要做):**
1.  **Don't hide the bottle:** Ensure the brand name "LASTAR" is legible. In some frames, hand movement might blur it.
2.  **Don't over-promise:** The creator honestly said "after 5 hours it faded." This honesty sells more than lying. Do not change this to "lasts 24 hours."

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **Comparison (Dupe Angle):** Explicitly compare it to a famous "Blue" fragrance (like Dior Sauvage or Bleu de Chanel). "Riecht wie 100€, kostet 16€" (Smells like €100, costs €16).
2.  **Street Interviews:** Spray it on strangers/girls and ask "How much do you think this costs?" Their reaction to the €16 price would be gold.
3.  **ASMR Unboxing:** Focus more on the sound of the spray and the satisfying "click" of the cap to enhance the "Luxury" feel.
