## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Real Audio Comparison:** Ensure the video actually captures the sound difference (Before vs. After) clearly. Use a microphone to pick up the volume boost real-time. (务必展示真实的音量对比，使用麦克风录制“放上去”瞬间的音效变化)。
2.  **Show Use Cases:** Show someone cooking, showering, or working while using it. Contextualize the "convenience". (展示使用场景，如做饭、洗澡时使用，具体化“便利性”)。
3.  **Add Captions:** Ensure German captions are large and readable for sound-off scrollers (though sound is essential for this product). (添加醒目的德语字幕)。

**DON'T (不要):**
1.  **Over-promise Quality:** Don't say it replaces a Hi-Fi system. Stick to "Loud and Convenient". (不要过度承诺音质，重点放在“大声”和“方便”上)。
2.  **Delay the Demo:** Don't wait until 15s to show the phone being placed on the stand. Do it in the first 5 seconds. (不要拖延演示，前5秒内必须展示“手机放上去”的动作)。

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR/Sound Test Angle:** A video purely focused on the "Click & Boom" satisfaction of placing the phone down. (ASMR或纯音效测试角度，强调放手机瞬间的满足感)。
2.  **Gift Angle:** "Perfect gift for Dad/Mom who hates Bluetooth." (礼品角度：“送给讨厌蓝牙配对的长辈的完美礼物”)。
