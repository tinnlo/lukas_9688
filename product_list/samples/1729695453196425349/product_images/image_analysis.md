## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

**Angle 1: The "Hassle-Free" Approach (Problem/Solution)**
*   **Concept:** Focus entirely on the pain of Bluetooth pairing (menus, failing to connect, battery drain).
*   **Hook:** "Stop wasting time with Bluetooth settings!"
*   **Flow:** Show user struggling to connect to a standard speaker. Then show PAXA-C-280: Drop phone -> Music plays. "It's that simple."
*   **Visual Focus:** Split screen comparison (`product_image_5.webp`).
*   **Call to Action:** "Make your life easier. Link below."

**Angle 2: The "Magic Trick" (Entertainment/Wow Factor)**
*   **Concept:** Frame the technology as magic. Don't explain *how* it works initially, just show *that* it works.
*   **Hook:** "I bet you didn't know your phone could do THIS."
*   **Flow:** Drop phone on stand -> Loud music. Lift phone -> Silence. Repeat rapidly (funny editing). Ask audience: "How does it work? Magic?" Then reveal: "Induction Audio."
*   **Visual Focus:** The drop action (`product_image_2.webp`) and reaction shots.
*   **Call to Action:** "Grab this magic block before it's gone."

**Angle 3: The "Kitchen/Home Companion" (Lifestyle/Utility)**
*   **Concept:** Focus on specific use cases where hands-free high volume is needed (cooking, cleaning, exercising).
*   **Hook:** "POV: You're cooking but can't hear your podcast."
*   **Flow:** Show the problem (boiling water noise, loud frying). Show solution: Place phone on PAXA-C-280. Sound cuts through the noise.
*   **Visual Focus:** Lifestyle shots in kitchen/bathroom (`product_image_5.webp`).
*   **Call to Action:** "Upgrade your home routine today."
