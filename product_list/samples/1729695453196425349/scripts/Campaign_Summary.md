## Production Notes | 制作说明

*From image_analysis.md Section 10 (来自图片分析第10节 - Visual Hooks | 视觉钩子):*

- **Primary Visual (主要视觉):** The "Drop" moment - phone placement on stand with exact audio sync (frame-perfect timing). Why it works (为什么有效): Proves induction instantly; creates "magic" illusion

- **Lighting (灯光):** Natural/bright kitchen lighting to show matte finish; avoid yellow artificial light (makes plastic look cheap)

- **Props Needed (所需道具):**
  - Old Bluetooth speaker with cables (旧蓝牙音箱带线缆) – for Script 1 contrast
  - Rice or water in glass (米或水杯) – for Script 2 bass visualization
  - Kitchen scene props (cooking utensils, pot) (厨房道具) – for Script 3 lifestyle
  - Multiple phone brands/cases (多品牌手机/壳) – to prove universal compatibility

- **Audio (音频):** **CRITICAL**: Use CAMERA AUDIO for "after" sound, not studio overlay. Room echo/distortion proves real loudness (使用相机音频而非后期叠加，房间回声/失真证明真实响度)

**Filming Tips from synthesis (来自综合分析的拍摄技巧):**
- **Sync the "Drop"** (同步"放下"动作) – Purpose (目的): Frame where phone touches stand MUST match audio waveform spike (±0.2s mismatch kills illusion)
- **Speed Ramping** (变速处理) – Purpose (目的): Slow-mo during product showcase (appreciation), fast during setup (emphasizes ease)
- **Foley Sound** (拟声效果) – Purpose (目的): Record clear "clack" of phone hitting stand for tactile feel
- **No Over-promising** (不过度承诺) – Purpose (目的): Avoid "Hi-Fi" or "10x louder"; use "deutlich lauter" (clearly louder), "perfekt für Podcasts" (perfect for podcasts)
