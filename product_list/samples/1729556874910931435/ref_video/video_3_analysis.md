## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Improve Audio Clarity:** Use a clear TTS (Text-to-Speech) or a better microphone to explain the features (Wireless, Dimmable, RGB) clearly.
2. **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text like "Kabellos & Wiederaufladbar" (Wireless & Rechargeable) to compensate for the short duration and unclear audio.
3. **Include a CTA:** End with "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) or point to the shop link.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Mumbled Audio:** Do not use unclear voiceovers for technical features like "Bewegungsmelder".
2. **Forget the Context:** Don't just show the light; show it *in context* (e.g., on a dinner table with wine glasses) to sell the lifestyle.
3. **Neglect the Battery Life:** For wireless lamps, battery life is a huge objection. Mention it visually or verbally.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the touch sensor and the bottle placement for a sensory experience.
2. **Gift Angle:** "Das perfekte Geschenk für Weinliebhaber" (The perfect gift for wine lovers).
3. **Restaurant/Bar Pitch:** Target business owners who need table lamps without cables.
