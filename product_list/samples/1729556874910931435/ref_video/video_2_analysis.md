## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add "USB Rechargeable" and "Fits Any Bottle" as text on screen to catch sound-off viewers.
2.  **Stronger CTA:** End with "Get yours below" or "Link in bio" to guide the user.
3.  **Show the 'Click':** Distinctly show the finger tapping the lamp to change colors (demonstrate the 'Touch' feature in title).

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't mumble:** The script had some unclear words ("Liebenölne"). Ensure clear pronunciation or use captions.
2.  **Don't assume B2B only:** While mentioning restaurants is good, emphasize the *Home* use more ("Make your dinner romantic") for a wider audience.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the bottle and the 'click' of the lamp for a sensory hook.
2.  **"Trash to Treasure":** Frame it as a DIY hack: "Don't throw away your empty gin bottles, do this instead!" (High viral potential).
3.  **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for wine lovers" (Excellent for Q4/Christmas or birthdays).
