## 2. Creative Formats & Structures | 创意结构与模板

**Format A: The "Speed-Run" (Winner) | “极速通关”模式（赢家）**
- **0-2s:** "And we're done." (Show result immediately).
- **2-5s:** Quick demo of touch features (Tap, Tap, Tap).
- **5-8s:** Show 1-2 different colors.
- **8-12s:** Wide shot of the room ambience.
- **Audio:** Voiceover matches the speed. Minimal filler.

**Format B: The "Slow Unbox" (Loser) | “慢速开箱”模式（输家）**
- **Avoid this:** 0-10s holding the box, 10-30s peeling plastic. This kills retention (as seen in Video 3).
- **Fix:** If unboxing, jump-cut to the product immediately.

**Format C: The "Contextual Lifestyle" | “场景生活”模式**
- **Context:** Dinner party, romantic date, or camping.
- **Structure:** Problem (Too dark/No atmosphere) -> Solution (Place lamp) -> Benefit (Instant cozy vibes).

## 3. Proof & Trust Mechanics | 证据与信任机制

- **Touch Responsiveness (触控响应):** You *must* show the finger touching the lamp and the light reacting instantly. Latency implies low quality.
- **Fit Security (安装稳固性):** Show that the lamp doesn't wobble on the bottle. A slight shake test (wackeln test) proves stability.
- **Real Environment (真实环境):** Do not use 3D renders. Shoot in a real, slightly messy German apartment or a real balcony.
- **Audio Clarity (音频清晰度):** Bad audio (mumbling, wrong language) destroys trust instantly (see Video 2 & 5). Use clear native German TTS if your accent is strong.

## 6. Visual Editing Playbook | 视觉剪辑打法

- **Pacing (节奏):**
    - **0-3s:** Cut every 0.8 seconds. Visual assault of the transformation.
    - **3-10s:** Slow down to 1.5s per clip to let viewers absorb the details (texture, light quality).
    - **10-15s:** Speed up again for the "Different Colors/Modes" montage.
- **Lighting (灯光):**
    - **Golden Rule:** Film in a dim room (Blue Hour or Night), NOT pitch black. Pitch black creates noise/grain on phone cameras. Dim light allows the bottle silhouette to be seen.
- **Color Grading (调色):**
    - Boost **Contrast** (+10) and **Saturation** (+5) to make the LED colors pop.
    - Slightly lower **Shadows** to hide messy room details.
- **Text Overlays (文字蒙版):**
    - Use "Classic" font in White with Black outline.
    - Position: Center-Top (Header) or Center-Bottom (Subtitles).
    - **NEVER** cover the product with text.

## 7. CTA Patterns & Timing | CTA 节奏与时机

- **Implicit CTA (Early):** At 00:05, show the TikTok Shop price anchor overlay clearly.
- **Verbal CTA (Late):** At the end (last 3 seconds), say "Link ist unten links" (Link is bottom left) while pointing physically or using a sticker arrow.
- **Visual CTA (Loop):** Ensure the video loops perfectly. The end frame should look similar to the start frame (e.g., hand reaching for the lamp).

## 9. Replication Checklist (shooting + edit) | 复刻清单（拍摄+剪辑）

### Pre-Production (前期)
- [ ] **Collect Bottles:** Gather at least 3 distinct bottles (Clear glass, Green wine, Brown whiskey/gin) to show versatility.
- [ ] **Charge Product:** Ensure the lamp is 100% charged for maximum brightness.
- [ ] **Clean Surface:** Wipe the table and bottles. No fingerprints!
- [ ] **Set Ambience:** Close curtains/blinds. Turn off main ceiling lights. Turn on a soft background lamp (warm light) far away for depth.

### Shooting (拍摄)
- [ ] **Shot 1 (Hook):** Place lamp on bottle -> Tap -> ON. (Film 5 times).
- [ ] **Shot 2 (Interaction):** Finger tapping to change colors. Close-up on finger.
- [ ] **Shot 3 (Dimming):** Long press (if supported) to show dimming.
- [ ] **Shot 4 (Installation):** Removing lamp, putting it back. Show the rubber seal fitting the neck.
- [ ] **Shot 5 (Wide):** Step back. Show the whole table/room vibe.
- [ ] **Shot 6 (B-Roll):** Pouring wine next to the lamp (Lifestyle).
- [ ] **Shot 7 (Charging):** Quick clip of plugging in USB-C.
- [ ] **Shot 8 (No Wires):** Pick up the bottle+lamp and walk around. "Wireless" proof.

### Editing (剪辑)
- [ ] **Trim Silence:** Remove all pauses in voiceover.
- [ ] **Speed Ramp:** Speed up the "screwing/placing" action (2x speed). Keep the "light up" action normal speed.

## 11. Risk / Compliance Notes | 风险与合规提醒

- **Misleading Claims:** Do not say "Lasts 1 week" if the battery only lasts 8 hours. Stick to "Hält den ganzen Abend" (Lasts the whole evening).
- **Brand Safety:** Be careful showing recognizable alcohol labels (e.g., Jack Daniels, Absolut) prominently if TikTok has strict alcohol ad policies in DE. Turn the bottle so the label is side-facing or obscured, or use generic wine bottles.
- **Safety Claims:** Do not claim it is "waterproof" (wasserdicht) unless explicitly stated in the manual (IP rating). Usually, these are just "splash resistant". Use "Für Drinnen & Draußen" (For indoors & outdoors) instead of "Waterproof".
- **Music Rights:** Do not use copyrighted radio music (like the Arabic song in Video 5) for commercial posts. Use TikTok Commercial Music Library.
