## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Localize Audio:** Use a German TTS (Text-to-Speech) or trending instrumental audio. Do NOT use Arabic lyrics for a German ad unless there is a specific viral meme context (unlikely here).
2. **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text benefits: "Nie wieder leere Flaschen wegwerfen" (Never throw away empty bottles again) or "Perfekte Stimmung" (Perfect atmosphere).
3. **Show Context:** Show the lamp being used at a dinner party or on a romantic date, not just in isolation.

**DON'T:**
1. **Ignore Audio Mismatch:** The "Tuba Tuba" song likely caused immediate drop-off from German viewers.
2. **Rely on Dark Shots:** While the light needs darkness, the product build quality needs light. Show it in daylight too.
3. **Omit CTA:** Don't assume people know where to buy. Add "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) at the end.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Eco-Friendly Angle:** Position it as "Upcycling" - turning trash (empty bottles) into treasure (lamps). This resonates well in Germany.
2. **Gift Angle:** "Das perfekte Geschenk für Weinliebhaber" (The perfect gift for wine lovers).
3. **ASMR:** Record the *sound* of the bottle clinking and the switch clicking, rather than music.
