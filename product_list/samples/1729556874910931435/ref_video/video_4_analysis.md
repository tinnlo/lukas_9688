## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Cut the duration:** Slash the video from 89 seconds to **15-25 seconds**. Remove the slow unboxing of packaging materials ("Hegemils" part).
2.  **Visual Hook First:** Start with the **glowing lamp** on a beautiful wine bottle in a dark room *immediately* (0:00), then cut to "This cost me 10 Euro". Don't start with a cardboard box.
3.  **Show the Problem:** Briefly show an empty, boring wine bottle -> Snap fingers -> Beautiful Lamp. Transformation sells better than explanation.

**DON'T (避免错误):**
1.  **Don't film lights in bright rooms:** It washes out the LED colors and makes the product look weak.
2.  **Don't ramble:** The transcript has a lot of filler words ("So zock", "Genau", "ehrlich zu sein"). Be concise.
3.  **Don't hide the result:** The viewer had to wait too long to see the actual light function.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the bottle clinking and the "tap" of the light switch without voiceover, just music.
2.  **Gift Angle:** "Best gift for wine lovers under €15."
3.  **Restaurant Vibe at Home:** Pitch it as "Date Night" upgrade for cheap.
