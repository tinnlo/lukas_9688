## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

### Angle 1: The Upcycling Hack (DIY Focus)
- **Concept**: Appeal to the eco-conscious and creative crowd who hate throwing away pretty bottles (Gin, Whiskey, specialized Wine bottles).
- **Narrative**: "I almost threw this Jack Daniels bottle away, but then I found THIS." Show the transformation from 'trash' to 'treasure'. Emphasize how easy it is (just plug it in).
- **Visual Style**: Fast-paced, before/after contrast, satisfying click sounds.
- **Hook Text**: "Altglas? Nein danke!" (Recycling glass? No thanks!)
- **ZH**: 针对环保和创意人群，展示从“垃圾”到“宝藏”的变废为宝过程，强调简单易用。

### Angle 2: The Romantic/Cozy Atmosphere (Vibe Focus)
- **Concept**: Focus on the result—the light quality. Perfect for couples, dinners, or relaxing alone.
- **Narrative**: "Set the perfect mood in seconds." Show a dark room, then the lamp turns on (dimming up). Show a glass of wine next to it. Focus on the warm 3000K light.
- **Visual Style**: Slow, cinematic, warm color grading, soft music.
- **Hook Text**: "Romantik auf Knopfdruck." (Romance at the push of a button.)
- **ZH**: 专注于灯光效果和氛围营造。适合情侣、晚餐或独自放松。展示暖光调光过程，电影感画质。

### Angle 3: The Party/Tech Gadget (Feature Focus)
- **Concept**: Target younger users or gadget lovers. Highlight the RGB features, the touch tech, and the wireless portability.
- **Narrative**: "This isn't just a lamp." Show the RGB modes flashing, take it from the kitchen to the balcony (wireless demo), charge it with USB-C.
- **Visual Style**: High energy, upbeat music, quick cuts between colors.
- **Hook Text**: "Deine Küche ist jetzt eine Bar." (Your kitchen is now a bar.)
- **ZH**: 针对年轻用户或科技迷。强调RGB功能、触摸控制和无线便携性。快节奏，展示多场景切换。
