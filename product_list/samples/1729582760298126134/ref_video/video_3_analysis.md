## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the Product Immediately:** For a perfume ad, the bottle must appear in the first 3 seconds. | **立即展示产品：** 对于香水广告，瓶身必须在前 3 秒内出现。
2. **Speak the Target Language:** Use German (TTS or native) for the German market, or at least provide hardcoded German subtitles. | **说目标语言：** 针对德国市场使用德语（TTS 或母语），或至少提供硬编码的德语字幕。
3. **Focus on Scent Description:** Describe the "Woody" (holzige) notes visibly or verbally. | **专注于香调描述：** 视觉或口头上描述“木质”（holzige）香调。

**DON'T:**
1. **Use Irrelevant Audio:** Avoid using personal story audio that has nothing to do with the item being sold. | **使用无关音频：** 避免使用与所售商品无关的个人故事音频。
2. **Leave Captions to Chance:** Don't rely on auto-captions, especially with accented speech. | **听天由命的字幕：** 不要依赖自动字幕，尤其是带有口音的语音。
3. **Loop Meaningless Phrases:** Repeating "Best show of the world" confuses the buyer about what the "show" is (the perfume?). | **循环无意义的短语：** 重复“Best show of the world”会让买家困惑“Show”到底是指什么（香水吗？）。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Scent Attraction" Angle:** Pivot the "Lovers around the corner" line into a valid script: "Want lovers around the corner? Wear THIS scent." | **“气味吸引”角度：** 将“爱人就在转角处”这句台词转化为有效的脚本：“想要爱人就在转角处吗？喷这与其香水。”
2. **ASMR Unboxing:** A simple, silent ASMR unboxing video would likely outperform this vocal-heavy, confusing clip. | **ASMR 开箱：** 一个简单、安静的 ASMR 开箱视频可能会胜过这个充满嘈杂人声且令人困惑的片段。
3. **German "Dupe" Trend:** Position the scent as a high-end alternative (Dupe) which is huge on German TikTok. | **德国“平替”趋势：** 将香味定位为高端替代品（Dupe），这在德国 TikTok 上非常流行。
