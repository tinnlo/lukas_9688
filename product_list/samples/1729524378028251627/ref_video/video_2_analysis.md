## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a Human Element:** Show a real person *reacting* to how easy it is, or struggling with a manual grinder first (Problem/Agitation).
2.  **Stronger CTA:** End with "Get yours 50% off today - Link in Bio" or "Upgrade your kitchen now."
3.  **ASMR Focus:** Amplify the sound of the grinding and the visuals of the salt falling on the steak.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Generic VO:** Use a trending TikTok voice or a real enthusiastic human voice instead of standard announcer style.
2.  **Forget the Offer:** 0 sales usually means the price isn't clear or the deal isn't compelling enough to trigger an impulse buy.
3.  **End Abruptly:** The ending "Steak or Soup?" is a weak closer. It needs to close the sale.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Gift Idea" Angle:** These sets are perfect gifts. Frame it as "The perfect gift for the home chef."
2.  **Comparison:** Split screen: "Old Manual Grinder (Hard/Slow)" vs. "New Electric Grinder (Fast/Fun)."
3.  **Recipe Integration:** Show the grinder finishing a delicious-looking dish to increase desire (Appetite Appeal).
