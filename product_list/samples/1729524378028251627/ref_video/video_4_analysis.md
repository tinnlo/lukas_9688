## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **ASMR Hook:** Start with the *sound* of the grinder working and a close-up of fresh pepper falling on a juicy steak.
2. **Problem/Solution Voiceover:** "Stop using two hands to grind pepper. This gravity-sensing grinder does it automatically." (German: "Hör auf, beide Hände zu benutzen...")
3. **Clear CTA:** End with "Link in bio for 50% off" or "Get the set on TikTok Shop."

**DON'T:**
1. **Empty Audio:** Never leave 90% of the video silent or relying on low-quality background noise.
2. **Generic Openers:** Avoid "Thank you" or "Unboxing" without context. Start with the *action*.
3. **Language Mismatch:** Ensure the voiceover or text overlays are in German for TikTok Shop DE to maximize local relevance.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Dinner Party" Angle:** Show how elegant the grinders look on a dining table to appeal to the "hosting" demographic.
2. **Comparison:** Split screen showing a struggle with a manual grinder vs. the ease of the electric one.
3. **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for the chef in your life" – great for holiday/birthday marketing.
