## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add ASMR Audio:** The sound of grinding pepper is satisfying; amplify it over the voiceover.
2. **Show Comparison:** Visually split-screen "Old Grinder" (struggling) vs "New Electric Grinder" (effortless).
3. **Add a CTA:** End with "50% Off Link in Bio" or "Get yours now".

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely solely on TTS:** Use a real human voice or trending audio to feel more native to TikTok.
2. **Forget the 'Why':** Don't just show it grinding; show it seasoning a beautiful steak or salad to stimulate appetite.
3. **End abruptly:** 10 seconds is efficient, but ensure the last second drives action.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **One-Handed Operation:** Highlight that you can stir with one hand and grind with the other—a huge pain point solver for cooks.
2. **Light Feature:** If the product has a light (common in these models), show grinding in a dim romantic dinner setting.
3. **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for the chef in your life" works well for kitchen gadgets.
