**DO:**
1.  **Fix the Audio:** Hire a native German speaker or use a high-end AI voice (ElevenLabs) that pronounces "Mühlen" and "rieselt" correctly.
2.  **Add a CTA:** End with "Jetzt 50% Rabatt sichern" (Secure 50% off now) or "Link unten klicken" (Click link below).
3.  **Show the Outcome:** Don't just show the grinder; show the *perfectly seasoned steak* being eaten. Sell the *taste*, not the tool.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on "Translation-ese":** Phrases like "Handgewirzt" (Hand-spiced?) sound unnatural. Use "Genug vom Kurbeln?" (Tired of cranking?).
2.  **Ignore Social Proof:** The current video lacks reviews or a human face vouching for quality.
3.  **End Abruptly:** The video ends on a minor feature (cleaning brush). End on the "Hero Shot" of the grinders looking beautiful on a table.

1.  **ASMR Angle:** Create a version with amplified grinding sounds and no music, just satisfying crunches.
2.  **Gift Angle:** "Das perfekte Geschenk für Hobbyköche" (The perfect gift for home cooks).
3.  **Comparison:** Split screen: "Old Manual Mill (Hard work)" vs "New Electric Mill (Effortless)".
