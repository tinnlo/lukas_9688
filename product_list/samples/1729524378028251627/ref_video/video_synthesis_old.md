## 7. Visual Editing Playbook | ËßÜËßâÂâ™ËæëÊâìÊ≥ï

- **Pacing:** Start fast (0.5s cuts) to match the grinding sound beat. Slow down for the "Food Porn" shot (slow motion sauce/steak seasoning).
- **Text Overlay Strategy:**
    - Top center: "Kitchen Must-Have" (static).
    - Center (pop-up): "One Click" -> "Auto Grind" -> "LED Light".
- **Color Grading:** Increase contrast and saturation. The black pepper needs to look pitch black, and the salt pure white against the food. Do not use flat, grey logs.
- **Sound Design:**
    - Layer 1: Voiceover (Loud, Clear).
    - Layer 2: ASMR Grinding Sound (Boosted 200% during close-ups).
    - Layer 3: Background Music (Low volume, Lo-fi or Upbeat Cooking Jazz).
