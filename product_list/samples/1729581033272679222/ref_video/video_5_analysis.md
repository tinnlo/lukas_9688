## 3. Hook/Opening Strategy (First 3 Seconds) | 开场策略(前3秒)

- **Hook Type | 钩子类型:** Pattern-Interrupt / Skit (Security Check) | 模式中断 / 情景剧 (安检)
- **Hook Description | 策略描述:** The creator simulates a high-tension "security check" or "pocket search" scenario, immediately grabbing attention through conflict and authority-based roleplay. | 创作者模拟了一个高紧张感的“安检”或“搜身”场景，通过冲突和基于权威的角色扮演立即吸引注意力。
- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:** Fast-paced dialogue ("Let me handle it", "Empty the pockets") combined with handheld camera movement to simulate a real-life confrontation. | 快速的对话（“让我来”、“清空口袋”）配合手持摄像机的移动，模拟真实的对峙场景。
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** High. It exploits the "stop-the-scroll" effect by making the viewer wonder what was stolen, before pivoting to a product reveal. | 高。它利用“停止刷屏”效应，让观众好奇到底偷了什么，然后转折到产品展示。

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. Use roleplay to introduce products—it builds much higher retention than simple reviews.
2. Focus on the "Bundle" (Set) aspect to increase perceived value.
3. Keep the German slang natural; it builds trust with the local audience.

**DON'T:**
1. Don't make the skit too long before the first product reveal (the 8-second mark is the limit).
2. Avoid over-scripting; the charm of this video is its semi-improvised feel.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. Add a side-by-side price comparison (TikTok Shop vs. High Street) to boost the "why buy here" logic.
2. Use a "blind scent test" as a follow-up video to prove quality alongside price.
