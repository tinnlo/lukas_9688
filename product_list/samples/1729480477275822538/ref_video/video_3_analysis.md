## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO:**
1.  **Start with the Price:** If the price is a killer deal (€13), say it in second 1. | **从价格开始：** 如果价格是超级优惠（13欧元），在第1秒就说出来。
2.  **Show the App:** Visual proof of the "Smart" features is non-negotiable. Don't just talk about it, scroll through it. | **展示App：** “智能”功能的视觉证据是不可商量的。不要光说，要滑动展示。
3.  **Use "Don't Buy X" framing:** "Don't buy a normal scale when you can get this Smart one for the same price." | **使用“别买X”的框架：** “当你能以同样的价格买到这个智能秤时，别买普通秤。”

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't overcomplicate the health metrics:** Just show the *amount* of data, don't explain every single metric (bone mass details, etc.) in a short ad. | **不要把健康指标复杂化：** 只展示数据的*数量*，不要在短广告中解释每一个指标（骨量细节等）。
2.  **Don't hide the product:** Ensure the scale's design (glass, LED) is clearly visible early on. | **不要隐藏产品：** 确保早期清晰可见秤的设计（玻璃，LED）。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Transformation" Angle:** Briefly show a "Before/After" weight log graph from the app to show progress tracking utility. | **“转变”角度：** 简要展示App中的“前/后”体重记录图表，以显示进度跟踪的实用性。
2.  **Gift Angle:** Mention it as a practical gift (as noted in product tags "Valentinstagsgeschenk"). "Best gift for boyfriend/girlfriend for €13". | **礼物角度：** 提及它作为实用礼物（如产品标签“情人节礼物”中所述）。“13欧元送给男朋友/女朋友的最佳礼物”。
