- **Key Success Factor:** The **Split-Screen or Cut-to-Screen** technique. You MUST show the number changing on the phone as the food hits the scale. This "Cause & Effect" is the money shot.
- **Reproducible Elements:** The script structure: Command -> Action -> Detail -> Benefit -> CTA.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show the "Zeroing" (Tare):** Show placing a bowl, zeroing it, then adding food. This is a satisfying visual mechanism (ASMR-adjacent).
2.  **Clearer Screen Shot:** Ensure the phone screen recording is high contrast. If the text is small, zoom in or add text overlays highlighting "150 KCAL".
3.  **Use Native TTS:** If the voiceover is hard to understand (broken German), use the popular German TikTok Text-to-Speech (e.g., "Katrin" or "Tim") for better clarity and trust.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't hide the App:** The "Smart" part is the app. If you just show the scale, it looks like a normal €10 scale. The value is in the screen connection.
2.  **Avoid Dark Lighting:** Food needs to look appetizing, and screens need to be legible.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"What I Eat in a Day" Angle:** Instead of just selling the scale, frame it as "How I calculate my breakfast macros in 10 seconds."
2.  **Comparison:** "Normal Scale (Confused Math Lady overlay)" vs "Smart Scale (Instant App Result)."
3.  **Gift Angle (Valentines):** "The gift for your gym-rat boyfriend/girlfriend." Explicitly target the partner.
