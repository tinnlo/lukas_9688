## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO (做):**
1.  **Add German Subtitles:** To capture the broader German market while keeping the authentic Arabic audio.
2.  **Show "Before/After" Data:** Use the app to show a weight trend graph (even if simulated) to visualize "Progress".
3.  **Clearer CTA:** End with a visual text overlay pointing to the yellow basket (e.g., "Link hier" or Arabic equivalent).

**DON'T (不做):**
1.  **Don't ignore the packaging:** If the box is nice, show it to reinforce the "Gift" angle.
2.  **Don't mumble:** Ensure the key product benefits (Bluetooth, 12+ metrics) are clearly articulated, not just "Look at this".
3.  **Don't leave dead air:** Ensure the visual cuts match the audio energy.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **Ramadan Prep:** Position the scale for monitoring health during/after Ramadan (upcoming cultural event relevant to audience).
2.  **Family Challenge:** Show multiple profiles on the App (Family use) to increase perceived value.
3.  **Battery Reminder:** The product title says "Battery required, not included". Explicitly mentioning this or showing putting batteries in can manage expectations and reduce returns/bad reviews.
