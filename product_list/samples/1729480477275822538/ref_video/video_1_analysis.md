## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Zoom in on the Sync:** Ensure the frame clearly shows the scale display and phone screen in the same shot or rapid sequence to prove the "smart" feature.
2.  **Speak the Price:** Explicitly mentioning "10 Euro" in German is a strong psychological trigger compared to just showing a sticker.
3.  **Show "Bad" Metrics:** Surprisingly, showing a "red" or "warning" metric (e.g., "Body fat too high") often converts better as it agitates a pain point.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't over-explain the setup:** Skip the Bluetooth pairing process. Just show it working.
2.  **Don't hide the screen:** If the App interface is English, make sure the vital numbers are big enough to be understood universally.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Comparison Video:** "This €10 scale vs my €100 Garmin scale" - showing they give the same number.
2.  **Transformation Arc:** "Day 1 vs Day 30" using the app's graph feature to show a trend line (even if fake/simulated) to sell the *result* (weight loss), not just the *tool*.
