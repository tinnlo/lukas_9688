- **CRITICAL: Show scale display AND phone screen in same shot** (关键：在同一镜头中显示秤显示和手机屏幕) or immediate cut to prove real-time sync (或立即切换以证明实时同步)

- POV foot-on-scale shots work better than third-person (POV脚在秤上的镜头比第三人称效果更好)
- Speed ramps: Normal speed for initial weight display, then 1.5x speed for app scrolling through metrics (初始重量显示正常速度，然后App滚动浏览指标时1.5x速度)
