## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a German Voiceover:** Use a native speaker or high-quality TTS to explain *benefits* (e.g., "Perfekt für das Büro," "In 30 Sekunden fertig").
2.  **Shorten to 15-20 Seconds:** 40 seconds is too long for a simple blender demo. Compress the action.
3.  **Add Text Overlays:** Highlight key features like "USB Aufladbar" (USB Rechargeable) and "BPA-Frei" (BPA Free) on screen.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Silence:** Never post a product ad without a hook (visual or auditory). Silence is a conversion killer.
2.  **Show "Dirty" Work:** Ensure the blender looks pristine. Avoid showing messy spills unless it's a "messy vs. clean" comparison.
3.  **Forget the Offer:** Don't just show the product; show the *deal*. (e.g., "Jetzt 50% Rabatt").

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"What I Eat in a Day":** Frame the blender as part of a lifestyle routine rather than just a gadget demo.
2.  **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the *sound* of the ice crushing (with good audio) to prove motor strength.
3.  **Comparison:** Show this blender vs. a bulky kitchen blender to emphasize portability ("Warum ich meinen großen Mixer verkauft habe...").
