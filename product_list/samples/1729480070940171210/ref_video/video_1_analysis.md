**DO:**
1.  **Add Voiceover (German):** Explain the problem (expensive smoothies, busy mornings) and the solution (this blender) in the local language. | **添加配音（德语）：** 用当地语言解释问题（昂贵的冰沙、忙碌的早晨）和解决方案（这款搅拌机）。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Music Only:** This is the #1 reason for low engagement on TikTok Shop ads. | **仅依赖音乐：** 这是TikTok商店广告互动率低的头号原因。
2.  **Use English Text for DE Market:** It lowers trust and relevance. | **针对德国市场使用英文文字：** 这会降低信任度和相关性。
