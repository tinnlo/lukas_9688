## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Use specific usage sounds:** Start with the *noise* of the blender blending to grab auditory attention immediately.
2.  **Show the "After":** Start with the delicious-looking finished smoothie, then cut back to how you made it.
3.  **Engage with the "Office" angle:** The "using it at the office" angle is strong. Show a shot specifically at a desk to reinforce this use case visually.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely solely on the "dirty" product:** While authentic, ensure the first frame is still appetizing. A "messy" blender might be a turn-off if not framed as "satisfyingly used".
2.  **Avoid generic backgrounds:** If you mention "office" or "sport", try to film in those environments rather than just a kitchen.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The blending sound and the "click" of the bottle could be emphasized for an ASMR hook.
2.  **"What I eat in a day":** Integrate the blender into a broader lifestyle vlog format, which often has higher retention than a direct product review.
3.  **Price Anchor:** Mentioning "Saved me €5 on a store-bought smoothie" adds a strong financial value proposition for the German market.
