## Scripts

A lifestyle/relatability angle targeting office workers during summer. Shows portability and quietness as key benefits. This script taps into the German efficiency mindset and the universal "beat the system" satisfaction of making your own fancy drink at work.

### Structure (35s)
- Hook: POV entering hot office, looking tired (0-3s)
- Problem: Show expensive cafe price (3-6s)
- Solution Reveal: Pull blender from bag (6-10s)
- Demo: Quick assembly and blending at desk (10-25s)
- Payoff: Drinking while colleagues look on (25-30s)
- CTA: Hold bottle to camera (30-35s)
