## Scripts

A value/savings angle that appeals to budget-conscious Germans. Uses clear math to show ROI: store-bought smoothies (teuer) vs homemade (super günstig). This angle works particularly well for students, young families, and anyone looking to eat healthy without overspending.

### Structure (33s)
- Hook: Show expensive supermarket receipt (0-3s)
- Problem Agitation: Calculate monthly waste (3-8s)
- Solution: Introduce blender + show cost breakdown (8-18s)
- Demo: Quick smoothie making with price overlay (18-26s)
- Savings Visual: Side-by-side comparison (26-30s)
- CTA: "Pay once, save forever" message (30-33s)

### Visual Strategy
| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Close-up: Supermarket receipt showing "Smoothie: teuer" circled in red, hand crumples it angrily | Hook: Visceral reaction to price shock |
| 03-08 | Animation: "Teuer x 20 Tage = richtig viel/Monat" with shocked face emoji | Problem amplification: Small expense becomes big |
| 08-12 | Cut to blender on table with price tag "kleiner Betrag" | Contrast: One-time investment |
| 12-18 | Split screen LEFT: Fresh fruits (strawberries, banana, spinach) with text "ein paar Zutaten für mehrere Smoothies" RIGHT: Blender | Education: Ingredient cost transparency |
| 18-22 | Fast-motion: Throwing ingredients into blender, blending (sped up 2x) | Efficiency: Quick process |
| 22-26 | Pour into glass, overlay text "Kosten pro Smoothie: super günstig" | Value: Clear cost comparison |
| 26-30 | Dual screen: LEFT shows "Laden: richtig teuer/Monat" RIGHT shows "Zuhause: viel günstiger/Monat" with arrow pointing right | Decision: Visual savings impact |
| 30-33 | Drinking smoothie contentedly, pointing to shop link with money bag emoji | Conversion: Smart purchase framing |
