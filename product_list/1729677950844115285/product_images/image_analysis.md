## 1. Product Design & Aesthetics | 产品设计与美学

### Overall Style (整体风格)
The jacket embodies a quintessential **"Street & Vintage" (街头复古)** aesthetic, perfectly aligning with current TikTok fashion trends. It moves away from the "technical outdoor" look towards a more "urban cozy" vibe.
*   **Vibe:** Relaxed, effortless, and nostalgic (90s retro influence).
*   **Target Audience:** Gen Z and Millennials looking for comfort without sacrificing style.

### Silhouette & Cut (轮廓与剪裁)
*   **Boxy Fit (箱型剪裁):** The body of the jacket appears wide and slightly cropped or hip-length, creating a modern boxy silhouette. This is crucial for the "oversized" trend.
*   **Dropped Shoulders (落肩设计):** The shoulder seams sit lower on the arm, enhancing the casual, slouchy look. This design feature allows for easy layering over thick hoodies or sweaters without bunching.
*   **Volume Distribution:** The fabric's thickness adds volume to the upper body, contrasting well with slimmer bottoms (leggings, skinny jeans) or complementing baggy cargo pants for a full streetwear look.

### Fabric Texture (面料质感)
*   **The "Popcorn" or "Teddy" Texture:** The visual highlight is the highly textured surface. Unlike flat fleece, this has a 3D structure (possibly boucle, sherpa, or a waffle-knit simulation in fleece).
*   **Visual Softness:** Even through a screen, the fabric communicates "softness" and "warmth." It looks touchable, which is a key selling point for video content (sensory appeal).
*   **Matte Finish:** The fabric absorbs light rather than reflecting it, giving it a premium, deep look compared to shiny, cheap synthetics.

### Key Components (关键部件)
*   **Hood Construction:** The hood appears substantial, not flimsy. It likely provides good coverage and adds to the bulk around the neck when down, acting like a scarf.
*   **Zipper:** A central feature. It allows the jacket to be worn as a cardigan (open) or a pullover substitute (closed).
*   **Cuffs:** Elasticated cuffs gathered at the wrist help create a "balloon" effect on the sleeves, adding to the cute/cozy aesthetic.

*   **Skin Feel:** The inner lining (assuming standard fleece construction) is likely soft against the skin, avoiding the "itchy" feel of wool.
*   **No Irritation:** Ideal for sensitive skin types who want the wool look without the wool scratchiness.

### 5. Durability Features (耐用特性) - *Inferred*
*   **Resilience:** Synthetic fleece is generally resistant to stretching and shrinking.
*   **Quick Dry:** Polyester fibers absorb very little moisture, making it practical for damp European winters.

## 5. Quality Signals | 质量信号

### Visual Indicators of Quality (质量的视觉指标)
1.  **Density of Pile:** The fleece should look dense, not threadbare. You shouldn't see the base fabric mesh through the "fluff".
2.  **Zipper Lay:** When zipped up, the fabric should not pucker or wave excessively. A flat zipper line indicates good sewing tension.
3.  **Hood Shape:** A hood that holds its shape when down (doesn't just hang limp) signals better pattern making and fabric weight.
4.  **Cuff Recovery:** The elastic cuffs should look tight and springy, not loose.

### Potential Quality Concerns to Address (需解决的潜在质量疑虑)
*   **Shedding:** Viewers often ask "Does it shed?" (Fusselt es?). *Script answer: "No, high-quality weave prevents shedding."*
*   **Static:** Synthetic fleece can generate static. *Script tip: Mention using fabric softener.*
