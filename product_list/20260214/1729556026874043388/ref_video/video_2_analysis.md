## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show the Dog Enter:** Instead of just cutting to the dog sleeping, show the dog *choosing* the bed over the floor/couch to prove preference.
2.  **Add Text Overlay:** Ensure "Orthopedic Support" or "Joint Relief" appears as text for sound-off viewers.
3.  **Compare:** Briefly show a "Old/Flat Bed" vs "New Fluffy Bed" side-by-side to heighten the contrast.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Hide the Price:** If the price is a selling point (e.g., under €20), show it. If it's premium, hide it until the click.
2.  **Over-edit:** Keep the colors natural. If the fur looks fake or over-saturated, German buyers will assume it's cheap plastic.
3.  **Forget Dimensions:** Visually show a hand or a common object next to the bed so people gauge the size correctly (avoids "it's smaller than I thought" returns).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Unboxing ASMR:** The sound of the package opening and the bed expanding (vacuum seal pop) is great content for this niche.
2.  **Cleaning Demo:** Show the cover being unzipped or the whole bed going into a washing machine. "Waschbar" (Washable) is a huge keyword for German pet owners.
3.  **Human Test:** Have a human try to use it as a pillow to emphasize the "cloud" claim.
