## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Ensure the "Non-slip" feature is aggressively demonstrated (try to slide it and fail) rather than just shown.
2. **Add Text Hooks:** Add a text overlay at 00:00 saying "Dein Hund wird dich lieben" (Your dog will love you) to boost read-ratio.
3. **Enhance Sound:** Use ASMR sounds of the hand touching the fur to increase sensory immersion.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Use Static Shots:** Avoid photos; use video for all shots to maintain retention.
2. **Don't Ignore Audio Mix:** Ensure music doesn't overpower the voiceover.
3. **Don't Be Boring:** The opening is very standard. Avoid generic "Hello" style openings.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Before/After:** Show a pet sleeping on a hard floor (sad music) vs sleeping on the bed (happy music).
2. **Unboxing:** Show the bed expanding from vacuum seal (satisfying to watch).
3. **German "Gemütlichkeit":** Lean harder into the "Cozy Home" aesthetic which is popular in Germany (Hygge vibe).
