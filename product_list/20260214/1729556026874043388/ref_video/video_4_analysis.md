## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show Motion/Demonstrate Softness (展示动态/演示柔软度):** Use a video of a hand pressing into the bed to show how thick and plush it is ("Squish Test"). | 使用手按压床的视频来展示它有多厚和柔软（“按压测试”）。
2. **Localize for Germany (德国本地化):** Add text overlays in German: "Der gemütlichste Schlafplatz für deinen Liebling" (The coziest sleeping place for your darling). | 添加德语文字叠加：“Der gemütlichste Schlafplatz für deinen Liebling”（为您爱宠准备的最舒适的睡眠之地）。
3. **Extend Duration (延长时长):** Aim for 7-15 seconds to allow time for a hook, problem/solution, and CTA. | 目标时长7-15秒，以便有时间进行钩子、问题/解决方案和号召性用语。

**DON'T:**
1. **Use Static Images as Videos (不要将静态图片用作视频):** TikTok is a video-first platform. Static images have extremely low retention. | TikTok是视频优先的平台。静态图片的留存率极低。
2. **Forget the Audio (不要忘记音频):** Even generic music is better than silence, but ASMR (scratching the fabric) or a voiceover is best. | 即使是通用音乐也比静音好，但ASMR（抓挠布料）或旁白是最好的。
3. **Neglect the CTA (不要忽视CTA):** Tell viewers where to get it (e.g., "Link in Bio" or "Shop Now"). | 告诉观众哪里可以买到（例如，“主页链接”或“立即购买”）。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR "Unboxing" (ASMR“开箱”):** Show the bed coming out of vacuum seal packaging and puffing up. This is very satisfying to watch. | 展示床从真空密封包装中取出并膨胀的过程。这看起来非常解压。
2. **"My Dog Loves This" POV (以“我的狗喜欢这个”为视角的POV):** Film a real dog hesitating, then stepping in and immediately falling asleep. | 拍摄一只真实的狗犹豫，然后走进去立刻睡着的画面。
3. **Comparison (对比):** Show an old, flat dog bed vs. this new fluffy one. "Gönn ihm ein Upgrade" (Give him an upgrade). | 展示旧的、扁平的狗床与这个新的毛绒床的对比。“Gönn ihm ein Upgrade”（给他升级一下）。
