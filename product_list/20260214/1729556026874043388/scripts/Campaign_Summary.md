## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Multi-Angle Approach (3 Scripts)

This campaign addresses three core pet owner pain points:

1. **Wolken Komfort Script** (Visual Satisfaction/ASMR)  
   Targets comfort-seekers with vacuum expansion reveal + cloud sink-in demo. Result-First hook (Type 6).

2. **Gelenkschonung Script** (Health/Wellness)  
   Appeals to owners of senior/large dogs with joint concerns. Pain Point hook (Type 2). Uses wellness language, avoids medical claims.

3. **Sauberes Sofa Script** (Cleanliness/Furniture Protection)  
   Targets fur-frustrated owners with washability + behavior redirect solution. Pain Point hook (Type 2).

**中文：** 三角度策略解决宠物主痛点：视觉满足、健康关怀、清洁家居。

## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Critical Filming Requirements

Per video synthesis proof mechanics and image analysis warnings:

1. **Vacuum Unboxing:**  
   - MUST film cutting vacuum seal and expansion (timelapse or real-time)
   - Include 12-hour wait instruction per image 4 (avoid "defective" complaints)
   - Aggressive shake-up/fluffing demo

2. **Padding Demonstrations:**  
   - Hand sink-in test showing depth
   - Side view showing 5-8cm thickness
   - Fill reveal (white polyester batting)

3. **Non-Slip Proof:**  
   - Flip bed to show black bottom with grip dots
   - Attempt to slide bed on floor (it should stay put)

4. **Washability Demo:**  
   - Remove cover (if removable)
   - Show it going into washing machine (critical trust signal for Germans)
