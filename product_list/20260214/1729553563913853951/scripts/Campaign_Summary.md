## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Multi-Angle Approach (3 Scripts)

This campaign addresses three core sleep pain points with careful compliance positioning:

1. **Hotel Feeling Script** (Aspiration/Luxury)  
   Targets "Comfort Seekers" who associate hotels with better sleep. Result-First hook (Type 6).

2. **Nackenstütze Script** (Wellness/Ergonomics)  
   Appeals to people with morning neck pain using Pain Point hook (Type 2). Uses "unterstützt" not "heilt" per compliance.

3. **Upgrade Script** (Quality/Minimalism)  
   Counter-Intuitive hook (Type 3) targeting pillow-hoarders with German "Qualität" mindset.

**中文：** 三角度策略解决睡眠痛点：奢华感、健康支撑、质量升级。

Per video synthesis analysis (`product_list/20260214/1729553563913853951/ref_video/video_synthesis.md` Section 4-5), all scripts honor:

- **Qualität** (Quality): Emphasize durability over cheap alternatives
- **Sauberkeit** (Cleanliness): Show clean white fill, washable cover
- **Gesundheit** (Health): Neck support framed as wellness, not cure

## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Critical Filming Requirements

Per video synthesis replication checklist and image analysis compliance notes:

1. **Trademark Avoidance:**  
   - Do NOT show "Hilton" branding visible in images 3 and 5
   - Cover logo with hand, crop framing, or blur in post
   - Say "Hotel-Qualität" NOT "Hilton Kissen"

2. **Material Clarity:**  
   - Show soft fiber fill (image 9) if unzipping pillow
   - Do NOT claim "Memory Foam" in voiceover/overlays
   - Use "down-alternative," "fiber pillow," "soft pillow"

3. **Quality Demonstrations:**  
   - Sink-in test (hands pressing deep)
   - Slow rebound test (release, watch pillow re-loft)
   - Quilting close-ups (shows fill won't shift)
   - Clean white fill reveal (trust signal)

4. **Aesthetic Framing:**  
   - Pure white bedding, hotel-style presentation
   - Soft natural light (avoid harsh shadows)
   - Minimalist background (no clutter)

**中文：** 商标规避、材质清晰度、质量演示、美学构图。

Per image analysis risk notes (`image_analysis.md` lines 111-129):

- ✅ DO use "Hotel-Qualität," "Down-Alternative," "weich," "flauschig"
- ✅ DO show clean fill, breathability, ergonomic positioning
