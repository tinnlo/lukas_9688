## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Multi-Angle Approach (3 Scripts)

This campaign deploys three complementary narratives targeting distinct pain points within the German cat owner market:

1. **Platzwunder Script** (Space Optimization)  
   Targets city apartment dwellers frustrated by floor clutter. Pain Point Resonance hook (Type 2).

2. **Naturholz & Stil Script** (Aesthetic/Quality)  
   Appeals to design-conscious owners who reject ugly pet furniture. Result-First hook (Type 6).

3. **Artgerecht Klettern Script** (Cat Welfare/Psychology)  
   Educates on feline enrichment needs, tapping into German Tierwohl values. Emotional Whisper hook (Type 7).

**中文：** 三角度策略覆盖德国猫主的核心痛点：空间、美学、动物福利。

### German Market Psychology Alignment

Per video synthesis analysis (`product_list/20260214/1729480115869948371/ref_video/video_synthesis.md` Section 6), all scripts honor:

- **Ordnung** (Order): Emphasize clean floors, decluttering

- **Tierwohl** (Animal Welfare): Highlight cat happiness, safety

## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Critical Filming Requirements

Per video synthesis replication checklist (`video_synthesis.md` lines 220-242):

1. **Wall Selection:**  
   - White or light grey wall (mandatory for German aesthetic)
   - Use spirit level for straight mounting (Germans notice crooked installations)

2. **Material Close-Ups:**  
   - Pine wood grain texture (ASMR stroking shot)
   - Double-stitched canvas seams
   - Felt pad grip test (fingers pressing)

3. **Safety Proof:**  
   - Hand pressing hard on hammock (no wobble)
   - Cat jumping onto structure (stability test)
   - Weight capacity demonstration (avoid children/humans, cats only)

4. **Aesthetic Framing:**  
   - Include monstera plant or greenery
   - Natural sunlight (Golden Hour preferred)
   - Clean, decluttered background (Ordnung principle)

**中文：** 白墙、材质特写、安全测试、美学构图。

### Compliance Reminders

Per video synthesis risk notes (`video_synthesis.md` lines 302-308):

- ❌ Do NOT show product images with typos ("Wohlbeing") — use own overlays
- ❌ Do NOT claim "indestructible" — use "robust," "stabil"
- ❌ Do NOT promise health cures — focus on "Bereicherung," "Komfort"
- ✅ DO show installation is wall-type agnostic (or mention suitable anchors)
- ✅ DO demonstrate holes are patchable (rental apartment concern)
