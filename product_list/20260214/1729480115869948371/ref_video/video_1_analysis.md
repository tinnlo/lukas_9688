## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a clear CTA:** End with "Link in bio to get yours" or "Treat your cat today - Shop below". | **增加清晰CTA：** 结尾加上“点击下方链接购买”或“今天就给主子下单”。
2.  **Show "Installation Ease":** Many buyers fear drilling walls. A quick clip showing it's easy to mount would remove a barrier. | **展示“安装简便性”：** 许多买家担心钻墙。展示安装简单的片段可以消除购买障碍。
3.  **Use Text Overlays:** Highlight key benefits like "Space Saving" (Platzsparend) or "Washable" (Waschbar) on screen. | **使用文字叠加：** 在屏幕上强调“节省空间”、“可拆洗”等核心卖点。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Leave the ending hanging:** The video just stops after the description. Don't fade out without a command. | **结尾戛然而止：** 视频描述完就停了。不要在没有指令的情况下淡出。
2.  **Rely solely on Voiceover:** Many people watch with sound off. The visual alone must sell the product (needs text). | **仅依赖配音：** 很多人静音观看。画面必须独立完成销售（需要文字配合）。
3.  **Ignore the "Problem":** Don't just show the solution. Show *why* they need it (e.g., "Cat bored?" or "Apartment too small?"). | **忽视“痛点”：** 不要只展示方案。展示*为什么*需要它（如“猫咪无聊？”或“公寓太小？”）。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Emphasize the scratching sounds or the purring of the cat on the hammock for a sensory hook. | **ASMR角度：** 强调抓挠的声音或猫在吊床上的呼噜声，制造感官钩子。
2.  **"POV" Shot:** Film from the cat's perspective looking down from the shelf to show how high/fun it is. | **“主观视角”镜头：** 拍摄猫咪从架子上向下看的视角，展示有多高/多好玩。
3.  **Comparison:** "Old cat tree takes up space" vs "Wall shelf saves space". Visual comparison works well. | **对比：** “旧猫爬架占地” vs “墙面架省空间”。视觉对比效果通常很好。
