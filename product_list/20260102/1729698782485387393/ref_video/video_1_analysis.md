## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show "Steam/Frost":** If possible, film on a cold morning where you can see breath or frost melting on the windshield to visually prove the "cold".
2.  **Sound Design:** Add a shivering sound effect at the start and a "warm/cozy" sigh or fire crackling sound when the heater turns on.
3.  **Show the Cable:** Briefly show that the cable is long enough to reach the dashboard to answer potential customer questions about installation.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Fake the Heat:** Don't make unrealistic claims like "heats entire bus in 1 minute". Keep it believable ("warm cabin in 5 mins").
2.  **Ignore Audio:** Don't rely on music only. The voiceover explaining *why* (saving engine gas) is crucial for the German market which values efficiency.
3.  **Cluttered Overlay:** Avoid covering the product with too much text. Let the demo speak.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Defrosting Angle:** Specifically target the pain point of "scraping ice". Show the heater melting ice from the *inside*.
2.  **Gift Angle:** "Perfect gift for your husband/dad who drives a truck."
3.  **Cost Comparison:** Briefly mention "Saves gas vs. idling engine" to appeal to the cost-conscious German consumer (Benzinpreise).
