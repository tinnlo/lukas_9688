## 13. Claims & Compliance | 合规与风险提示
- **Avoid "Instant Defrost" Absolute Claims | 避免“瞬间除霜”的绝对宣称**:
  - Do not claim it clears an entire iced windshield in 1 second. Use "Fast" or "Rapid".
  - 不要宣称它能在1秒内清除整个结冰的挡风玻璃。使用“快速”或“迅速”。
- **Battery Safety Warning | 电池安全警告**:
  - Always advise users to unplug when the car is turned off to prevent battery drain.
  - 始终建议用户在熄火时拔掉插头，以防电池耗尽。
- **Temperature Limits | 温度限制**:
  - Avoid claims that it can heat the entire cabin to tropical temperatures. Focus on "defrosting" and "hand warming".
  - 避免宣称它可以将整个车厢加热到热带温度。专注于“除霜”和“暖手”。
- **CE/RoHS Marking | CE/RoHS 标志**:
  - Ensure the product (if visible in detail) shows necessary compliance marks for EU markets.
  - 确保产品（如果细节可见）显示欧盟市场必要的合规标志。
- **Do not obstruct view | 不要阻挡视线**:
  - Demonstrate installation that does not block the driver's line of sight.
  - 演示不阻挡驾驶员视线的安装方式。
