   - **Why it works:** Shows unique feature, interactive/fun content
   - **Script hook:** "Das hier ist kein normales Katzenbett - es ist ein Spielplatz mit dem Train-Tunnel!" (This isn't a normal cat bed - it's a playground with the train tunnel!)

3. **"The Cushy Test" (舒适测试)**
   - **How to film:** Close-up of pressing a hand into the thick top cushion to show softness (7cm thick rim)
   - **Why it works:** Proves quality, tactile proof
   - **Script hook:** "7 cm dickes Kissen - so weich wie eine Wolke." (7cm thick cushion - as soft as a cloud.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 按压厚顶部垫子的特写镜头，展示柔软度（7厘米厚边缘）

4. **"Sleep Mode" (睡眠模式)**
   - **How to film:** A cat sinking deep into the bottom hammock (it looks very cozy)
   - **Why it works:** Emotional appeal, comfort signal
   - **Script hook:** "Nach dem Spielen? Ab in die Hängematte." (After playing? Into the hammock.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 一只猫深深沉入底部吊床（看起来非常舒适）

5. **"Sound Check" (声音检查)**

   - **Why it works:** Addresses specific pain point (noise), shows customization
   - **Script hook:** "Für die 3-Uhr-Morgens-Zoomies? Einfach den lautlosen Ball verwenden." (For the 3 AM zoomies? Just use the silent ball.)

6. **"Shake Test" (摇晃测试)**
