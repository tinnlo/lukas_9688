## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Target Sub-Communities:** If selling in Germany, try making videos in Turkish, Arabic, or Polish to target specific immigrant communities who might be underserved by German-only ads.
2. **Focus on Packaging:** For "Gift" items, the *box* is as important as the product. Ensure the unboxing looks premium.
3. **Use Price Anchoring:** Always mention what the product *looks* like it costs (e.g., "Looks like €80") before revealing the actual price.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Over-Explain Tech:** The video didn't mention "AI translation accuracy" or "battery life." It focused on "It looks fancy." Don't bore gift-buyers with specs.
2. **Don't Forget the Occasion:** This video leaned on Black Friday. Don't run generic ads; tie them to a reason (Valentine's, Eid, Christmas).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Gift Bundles:** Suggest buying two (one for you, one for her) to double the AOV (Average Order Value).
2. **Reaction Videos:** Show a girl actually receiving the gift and being happy. This validates the "She will love it" claim.
3. **Valentine's Pivot:** This exact script can be reposted in February with "Black Friday" changed to "Valentine's Deal."
