## Compliance Notes for Scripts | 脚本合规注意事项

### Must-Include Compliance Elements (必须包含的合规元素)

**Category: Health Supplement (Nahrungsergänzungsmittel) (类别：健康补充剂)**

**⚠️ CRITICAL COMPLIANCE WARNINGS (⚠️ 关键合规警告):**

1. **No Medical Claims (不做医疗声明):**
   - DO NOT claim: "cures infections," "treats bacterial vaginosis," "prevents yeast infections"
   - CAN say: "supports," "helps maintain," "promotes," "contributes to"
   - **不允许声称:** "治愈感染"、"治疗细菌性阴道炎"、"预防酵母感染"
   - **可以说:** "支持"、"帮助维持"、"促进"、"有助于"

2. **Dosage Accuracy (剂量准确):**
   - Always specify: "2 gummies daily" (not "take as needed")
   - 始终指定："每日2粒"（而非"按需服用"）

3. **Target Audience Restriction (目标受众限制):**
   - Product for adult women (18+)
   - Not for children, pregnant/breastfeeding without medical consultation
   - 成人女性产品（18+），不适用于儿童、孕妇/哺乳期（无医疗咨询）

4. **Supplement Disclaimer (补充剂免责声明):**
   - "Nahrungsergänzungsmittel ersetzen keine ausgewogene Ernährung"
   - Supplements do not replace a balanced diet
   - 补充剂不能替代均衡饮食
