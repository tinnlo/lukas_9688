## Compliance Notes | 合规说明

✅ Compliant language used:
- "Ergänzt dein Schönheitsritual" (Supplements your beauty ritual)
- "Mit allem, was deine Haare, Haut und Nägel brauchen" (With everything your hair, skin and nails need)
- "Unterstützt" (implied through routine integration)

❌ Avoided:
- "Heilt" (Cures)
- "Garantierte Ergebnisse" (Guaranteed results)
- "In X Tagen sichtbar" (Visible in X days)
- Disease treatment claims

Focus on lifestyle integration and nutritional support rather than medical promises.
