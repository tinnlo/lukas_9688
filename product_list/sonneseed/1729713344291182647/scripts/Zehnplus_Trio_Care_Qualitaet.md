## Compliance Notes | 合规说明

✅ Compliant language used:
- "Mit Zink für starke Nägel und gesunde Haut" (With zinc for strong nails and healthy skin)
- "Vitamin C für strahlenden Teint" (Vitamin C for radiant complexion)
- "D3 und B6 für volles, kräftiges Haar" (D3 and B6 for full, strong hair)
- "Alle 100% deines Tagesbedarfs" (All 100% of your daily requirement)

❌ Avoided:
- "Heilt Haarausfall" (Cures hair loss)
- "Beseitigt Hautprobleme" (Eliminates skin problems)
- "Garantierte Ergebnisse" (Guaranteed results)
- Specific timeframe promises

All benefit claims use permitted "support" language per German supplement regulations.
