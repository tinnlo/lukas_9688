## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Market Insight: German Rental Market (市场洞察：德国租赁市场)
**Key Problem:**
- 58% of Germans rent homes, cannot drill holes (58%德国人租房，不能打孔)
- Lease contracts explicitly forbid drilling ("Bohren verboten") (租约明确禁止钻孔)
- Small kitchens (average 6-8m²) create storage crises (小厨房造成收纳危机)
- Fear of losing deposit (€1,000-€3,000) drives non-destructive solutions (担心失去押金促使寻找非破坏性方案)

**Solution Positioning:**
This rack as the "renter's best friend" — organizes kitchen without risking deposit, uses vertical "dead space" most people ignore.

### Three-Pronged Approach (三管齐下策略)

1. **Script 1: Renter's Dilemma (Problem/Solution)**
   - Hook: "Bohren verboten?" relatable restriction
   - Demo: Adhesive installation without tools
   - Target: Renters (primary German market segment)

2. **Script 2: ASMR & Aesthetic (Sensory)**
   - Hook: Satisfying installation sounds
   - Experience: Clean Girl aesthetic appeal
   - Target: ASMR fans, "CleanTok" enthusiasts

3. **Script 3: Dead Space Hack (Educational)**
   - Hook: "You're wasting 20% of your kitchen"
   - Insight: Utilizing unused fridge-side gap
   - Target: Small apartment dwellers, efficiency seekers

✅ **Proper CTA** ("Link ist unten" for TikTok cart)

### Product-Specific Warnings (产品特定警告)
⚠️ **Surface Compatibility:** ONLY works on smooth surfaces (fridge paint, tiles, glass) - NOT on wallpaper or rough plaster (仅适用于光滑表面 - 不可用于壁纸或粗糙灰泥)
⚠️ **Weight Limit:** Do not overload (realistic 2-3kg demonstration) - avoid "unbreakable" claims (承重限制：不要超载，现实演示2-3公斤)
⚠️ **Heat Proximity:** Do not install next to oven (adhesive may soften from heat) (不要安装在烤箱旁边)
⚠️ **Removal:** Warn that adhesive may remove paint on painted walls (警告：在涂漆墙面上移除可能剥落油漆)
