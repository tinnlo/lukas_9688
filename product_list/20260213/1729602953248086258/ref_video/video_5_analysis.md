## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1. **Start with Chaos:** Show the mess on the floor or counter first.
2. **Stronger CTA:** Use "Nur heute" (Only today) or "Klicke auf den Link" (Click the link).
3. **Show Variety:** Flash different colors (Grey, Black, Beige) quickly.

**DON'T:**
1. **Use Weak Sign-offs:** Avoid "See you next time" unless it's a series; focus on the "Buy" trigger.
2. **Generic Music:** Try using trending German audio or a voiceover explaining the "Platzsparender" (space-saving) benefit.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Focus on Renters:** Use text like "Ideal für Mietwohnungen" (Ideal for rentals).
2. **Holiday/Seasonal Angles:** "Get ready for Spring Cleaning" (Frühjahrsputz).
3. **Weight Test:** Explicitly show that it can hold heavy bathroom bottles.
