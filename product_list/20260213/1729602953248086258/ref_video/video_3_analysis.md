## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add German Text Overlays:** Add "Platzsparendes Wunder" (Space-saving wonder) or "Ordnung in 5 Sekunden" (Order in 5 seconds) to bridge the language gap.
2.  **Show the "Before":** Start with a messy floor/room, then snap to the organized door. The contrast sells the *solution*, not just the product.
3.  **Include a CTA:** Even a simple text sticker "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) at the end.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely solely on English Audio:** For the German market, if you use English audio, you *must* use German subtitles/captions.
2.  **Leave it at 7 Seconds:** Extend to 10-12s to allow a "packing" demo. 7s is too fast to build desire for a utility item.
3.  **Film in Bad Lighting:** Ensure the bathroom/room is bright; dark visuals make the product look cheap.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Packing:** Record the sound of placing items into the pockets (zippers, fabric sounds) overlayed with the music.
2.  **"What fits inside?":** Challenge video – "Can I fit my whole makeup routine in this?"
3.  **German TTS Voiceover:** Re-edit the same footage but use a German AI voice saying "Das brauchst du für dein Badezimmer!" (You need this for your bathroom!).
