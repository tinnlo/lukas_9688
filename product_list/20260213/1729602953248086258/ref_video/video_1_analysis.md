## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1. **Add a "Problem" Hook:** Start with a messy bathroom counter or a overflowing cabinet.
2. **Use German TTS/Voiceover:** A friendly AI voice explaining the benefits would increase retention.
3. **Show Weight Test:** Show the bag holding heavy bottles to prove it won't tear or fall.

**DON'T:**
1. **Forget the CTA:** Don't just show the product; tell them *exactly* where to click (e.g., "Check the yellow basket below").
2. **Use Generic Music Only:** Pure music ads often have lower conversion than narrated ads on TikTok Shop.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Address Rental Concerns:** Explicitly mention "Perfect for rentals - no damage to doors" in the text overlay.
2. **Bilingual Target:** Create versions with German text but English-style "aesthetic" editing to capture a wider expat audience in Germany.
3. **Unboxing Element:** Show how flat the product comes in the mail to emphasize easy shipping.
