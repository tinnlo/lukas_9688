## Scripts

**Concept:** The "Dead Space Activation" angle - use German efficiency psychology to trigger a "waste aversion" response by showing that renters are paying for unused vertical space behind their doors.

### Structure (40s)
- **Hook:** Pointing to empty door space (0-3s)
- **Logic Bomb:** "You're paying rent for this space" (3-10s)
- **Solution:** Door transforms into vertical storage (10-24s)
- **Comparison:** Floor chaos vs. door solution (24-34s)
- **CTA:** Value justification (34-40s)
