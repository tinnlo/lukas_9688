## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Speed it up:** Compress the middle assembly section. 4 minutes is too long. Use timelapse (4x speed) for the screwing parts. | **加速：** 压缩中间的组装部分。4分钟太长了。拧螺丝的部分使用延时摄影（4倍速）。
2. **Show "Before" state:** Clearly show the messy counter *before* the shelf to heighten the contrast. | **展示“使用前”：** 清晰展示架子放上去*之前*凌乱的台面，以加强对比。
3. **Add Text Overlays:** Add benefit callouts on screen (e.g., "Breite verstellbar 40-60cm") for viewers watching without sound. | **添加文字贴纸：** 在屏幕上添加利益点标注（如“宽度可调 40-60cm”），方便静音观看的用户。

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't film in bad light:** The "back of the kitchen" can be dark. Use a simple ring light to make the black product visible against the background. | **别在暗处拍摄：** 厨房角落可能很暗。用个简单的环形灯，确保黑色产品在背景中清晰可见。
2. **Don't leave dead air:** Some assembly parts might be silent or repetitive. Cut them. | **别留死寂时间：** 有些组装部分可能无声或重复。剪掉它们。

3. **Compare with competitors:** "Why this metal one is better than plastic ones." | **竞品对比：** “为什么这款金属的比塑料的好。”
