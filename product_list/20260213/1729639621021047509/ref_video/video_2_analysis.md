## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO | 建议做:**
1.  **Emphasize "No Drilling":** Ensure text overlays explicitly state "Kein Bohren" (No drilling) – huge for German renters. | 务必在文字中强调“Kein Bohren”（免打孔）——这对德国租房者非常重要。
2.  **Show Weight Capacity:** Explicitly place something heavy (like a 5L water bottle) to prove sturdiness. | 放一些重物（如5升水）来证明坚固性。
3.  **Add ASMR Sounds:** If re-editing, include the "click" of assembly or the "thud" of placing items to increase sensory satisfaction. | 如果重新剪辑，加入组装的“咔哒”声或放置物品的“砰”声，增加感官满足感。

**DON'T | 建议不做:**
1.  **Don't Hide the Product:** Avoid long intros with just the creator's face. Show the shelf immediately. | 避免只有创作者脸部的长开场。立即展示架子。
2.  **Avoid Cluttered Backgrounds:** Ensure the "After" shot is pristine. Any mess in the final shot ruins the "organization" promise. | 确保“使用后”的镜头整洁。最终镜头中的任何混乱都会破坏“整理”的承诺。
