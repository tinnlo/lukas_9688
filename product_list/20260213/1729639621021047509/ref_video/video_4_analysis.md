## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show the "After" State Earlier:** Don't leave the "chic" result to the very end or just words; show a split-screen of the mess vs. the organized shelf.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Use captions like "Assembly Struggle?" or "Worth the Hype?" to hook sound-off users.
3.  **Improve Lighting:** Ensure the "mess" is clearly visible and the final product shot is bright and appealing.

**DON'T:**
1.  **End on Confusion:** Ensure the "resolution" (it looks great) is stronger than the "problem" (it's hard to build).
2.  **Film in the Dark:** Poor lighting reduces perceived value of the furniture.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Part 2" Reply Video:** Use the comments from this video to make a "Solved!" follow-up showing the easy assembly hack, driving traffic back to the product.
2.  **ASMR Assembly:** A fast-forward timelapse of the assembly (even the struggle) set to trending audio could be more engaging visually.
