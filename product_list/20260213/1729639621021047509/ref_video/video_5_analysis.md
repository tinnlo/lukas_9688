**DO:**
1.  **Add German Subtitles**: Since the shop is in Germany, you are losing 95% of the audience by only speaking Farsi without subtitles. Hardcoded German captions are essential. | **务必添加德语字幕**：既然面向德国市场，仅讲波斯语会流失95%的受众。必须加上德语硬字幕。
2.  **Stronger CTA**: Explicitly point to the yellow basket at the end. "Link is here" or "Get yours below". | **加强号召**：结尾明确指向黄色购物篮，口头引导“点击下方链接购买”。
3.  **Brighten the Footage**: Ensure the kitchen is well-lit, especially the "After" shot, to make the product look premium. | **提亮画面**：确保厨房光线充足，尤其是“整理后”的镜头，让产品看起来更有质感。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Hide the Product**: Ensure the shelf is the clear focus in the center of the frame, not obscured by the user's back or hands for too long. | **不要遮挡产品**：确保置物架始终是画面焦点，不要被身体或手遮挡太久。
2.  **Don't Rush the Reveal**: Hold the final "After" shot for at least 3 seconds so viewers can admire the organization. | **不要匆忙结束展示**：“整理后”的定格镜头至少保留3秒，让观众充分欣赏整洁的效果。

1.  **ASMR Version**: Repurpose the footage with just the sounds of clicking parts together and placing jars (tapping sounds), removing the language barrier entirely. | **ASMR版本**：重新剪辑素材，只保留组装和放瓶子的声音（敲击声），完全消除语言障碍。
