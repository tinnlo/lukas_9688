## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Market Insight: German Rental Market (市场洞察：德国租赁市场)
**Key Problem:**
- 58% of Germans rent (not own) homes (58%德国人租房)
- Lease contracts strictly prohibit drilling ("Bohren verboten") (租约严格禁止打孔)
- Small kitchen spaces (typical 6-8m²) create storage crises (小厨房空间造成收纳危机)
- Fear of losing deposit (€1,000-€3,000) drives non-destructive solutions (担心失去押金促使寻找非破坏性方案)

**Solution Positioning:**
This rack as the "renter's best friend" — organizes kitchen without risking deposit, portable for frequent moves.

### Three-Pronged Approach (三管齐下策略)

1. **Script 1: Space Multiplier (Functional Value)**
   - Hook: Cluttered small kitchen frustration
   - Demo: Visual math (vertical = double space)
   - Target: Small apartment dwellers, families

2. **Script 2: Heavy Duty Test (Quality Proof)**
   - Hook: Skepticism about stability
   - Proof: Aggressive weight testing with dumbbells
   - Target: Quality-conscious buyers, male demographic

3. **Script 3: Rental Friendly (Niche Targeting)**
   - Hook: "Drilling forbidden" lease clause
   - Benefit: Deposit-safe, portable solution
   - Target: Renters (primary German market segment)
