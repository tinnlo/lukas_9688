## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

**Key Problem:**
- German apartments average 70-90m², bathrooms especially cramped (德国公寓平均70-90平米，浴室尤其狭窄)
- Under-sink cabinets become "black holes" where items get lost (水槽下柜子成为物品遗失的"黑洞")
- Dark, deep cabinets require awkward reaching and searching (黑暗深处的柜子需要费力伸手翻找)

**Solution Positioning:**
This organizer as the "invisible space multiplier" — uses vertical height to double capacity while maintaining easy access through pull-out design.

1. **Script 1: ASMR & Aesthetic (Sensory Satisfaction)**

   - Hook: Relatable frustration with limited bathroom space

   - Target: Renters, students, small apartment dwellers

3. **Script 3: Hidden Dirt (Hygiene Focus)**
   - Hook: Shock value of hidden dust and grime
   - Benefit: Elevated clean storage with full visibility
   - Target: Hygiene-conscious buyers, clean aesthetic fans
