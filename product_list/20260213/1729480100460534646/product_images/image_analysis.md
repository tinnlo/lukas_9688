## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

**Angle 1: The "Small Kitchen Saviour" (Problem/Solution)**
*   **Concept:** Focus purely on the lack of counter space in typical apartments.
*   **Flow:** Show a cluttered counter where you can't even chop vegetables. Then show the assembly of the tower. Move all the clutter onto the tower.
*   **Payoff:** Show the empty, clean counter space revealed underneath/around it.
*   **Audio:** "Hast du auch nie Platz in der Küche? Das hier ändert alles."

**Angle 2: The "Aesthetic Restock" (ASMR/Visuals)**
*   **Concept:** Pure visual satisfaction. Silent or calm music + ASMR sounds.
*   **Flow:** Start with empty clean basket. Place fruits one by one (rhythmically). Mix colors (Red apple, Yellow banana, Green grapes). Use the top tier for something special like a small plant or avocado.
*   **Payoff:** Final slow pan of the beautiful setup.
*   **Audio:** Satisfying clicks, thuds of fruit, ambient jazz.

**Angle 3: The "Health Motivator" (Psychological)**
*   **Concept:** "Out of sight, out of mind."
*   **Flow:** Show fruit rotting in a fridge drawer or an opaque bowl. Explain that you forget to eat it. Then show the open 3-tier stand.
*   **Payoff:** "Seit ich das habe, esse ich doppelt so viele Vitamine." (Since I have this, I eat twice as many vitamins.)
*   **Audio:** Voiceover explaining the psychology of seeing healthy food.
