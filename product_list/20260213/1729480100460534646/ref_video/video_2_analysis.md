## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add German TTS/Voiceover:** "Endlich Platz in der Küche!" (Finally space in the kitchen!) to grab attention immediately.
2.  **Show "Before & After":** Start with a messy counter full of fruit, then snap fingers to show the organized basket.
3.  **Speed Up Pacing:** Cut every 0.5-1s to match a trending beat.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Post Silent Videos:** Especially for "Problem/Solution" products like organizers. You need to verbalize the benefit.
2.  **Don't Use Static Shots:** Move the camera or the product. Show a hand placing fruit into the basket to prove sturdiness.
3.  **Don't Ignore Text Overlays:** Add "100% Bamboo" or "Platzsparend" (Space-saving) text on screen.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Restock:** Fill the basket with crisp sounds (apples thumping, nuts pouring). "Restock with me" videos perform very well in Germany.
2.  **Eco-Friendly Angle:** Focus heavily on "Plastikfrei" (Plastic-free) - the Bamboo aspect is a strong selling point in the DACH region.
3.  **Gift Angle:** "Die perfekte Geschenkidee" (The perfect gift idea) for housewarmings.
