## Scripts

**Concept:** The "Chaos Panic" → Solution reveal. Leverage German "Ordnung muss sein" psychology with dramatic before/after contrast.

### Structure (42s)
- **Hook:** POV tripping over massive shoe pile (0-3s)
- **Problem:** Show the frustration and embarrassment (3-8s)
- **Solution:** Reveal the rack with zippered cover (8-20s)
- **Proof:** Load it up, show stability and capacity (20-35s)
