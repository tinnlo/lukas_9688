## 1. Product Overview | 产品概述

**Category:** Home Organization / Shoe Storage (家居收纳/鞋架)

**Core Value Proposition (核心价值主张):**
Vertical space-saving shoe rack with dustproof zipper cover. Solves the German apartment hallway chaos problem while addressing skepticism about low-price stability.

**Key Features (关键功能):**
- 4-tier (12 pairs) or 7-tier (21 pairs) capacity (4层12双或7层21双容量)
- Dustproof zippered fabric cover (防尘拉链布套)
- Tool-free steel tube plug-in system (免工具钢管插接系统)
- Water-resistant fabric, easy to clean (防水布料，易清洁)
- Side pockets for accessories (侧袋放配件)

**Price Point:** Approximately €10-€20 (budget-friendly positioning)

## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Market Psychology (市场心理)
**German "Ordnung muss sein" (Order is essential) culture:**
- Messy hallways are embarrassing, not just annoying (走廊凌乱是丢脸的，不仅仅是烦人)
- Skepticism of cheap goods ("Billigware") requires stability proof (对廉价商品的怀疑需要稳定性证明)
- Specific numbers valued over vague claims (具体数字比模糊声明更有价值)

**Primary Objection (主要异议):**
"Is a €15 rack stable enough?" → Addressed with aggressive testing footage

**Unique Selling Point (独特卖点):**
Dustproof cover differentiates from open racks, appeals to German cleanliness standards

### Three-Pronged Approach (三管齐下策略)

1. **Script 1: Problem/Solution (Chaos → Order)**
   - Leverage relatable pain (tripping over shoes)
   - Dramatic before/after visual contrast
   - Target: Families, small apartment dwellers

2. **Script 2: Skeptic Conversion (Stability Test)**
   - Address low-price objection head-on
   - Aggressive testing (shake, stomp, heavy boots)
   - Target: Budget-conscious buyers, male demographic

3. **Script 3: Feature-Led ASMR (Dustproof Focus)**
   - ASMR zipping sound for scroll-stop
   - Cleanliness and organization angle
   - Target: "Ordnungsfreaks," women 25-45

## 3. Key Selling Points | 核心卖点

From `video_synthesis.md` Section 5:

1. **Space Efficiency (空间效率):** Vertical storage uses height, not width — critical for narrow German hallways
2. **Dustproof Design (防尘设计):** Zipper cover keeps shoes clean and hallway visually calm
3. **Stability (稳定性):** Steel tubes + reinforced connectors hold heavy boots without wobbling
4. **Easy Assembly (易安装):** Tool-free plug-in system, assembled in under 10 minutes
5. **Versatility (多功能):** Adjustable tiers for boots, side pockets for keys/polish

## 4. Target Audience | 目标受众

**Primary (主要):**
- **Students & WG-Bewohner:** Need cheap, portable furniture for frequent moves
- **Ordnungsliebhaber:** People who hate visual clutter and want hidden shoe storage

- Embarrassment when guests visit (访客来时的尴尬)
- Desire for "spa-like" visual calm at home (家中像水疗中心般视觉平静的渴望)

## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Props Needed (所需道具)
- 15-20 pairs of mixed shoes (sneakers, heels, boots) (15-20双混合鞋类)
- Unboxing-ready product package (开箱就绪的产品包装)
- Dust/flour for comparison demo (灰尘/面粉用于对比演示)
- Water spray bottle for fabric test (喷水瓶用于布料测试)

### Filming Setup (拍摄设置)
- **Location:** Clear hallway corner or bedroom, natural daylight preferred
- **Lighting:** Ring light for close-ups, avoid dark corners
- **Audio:** Clean microphone for ASMR zip sounds, record 10s room tone
- **Pacing:** Cuts every 1.5-2 seconds, speed ramp assembly sequences

### Key Visual Techniques (关键视觉技巧)
- **Speed Ramping:** Fast-forward boring assembly, slow-motion zipping
- **Split Screen:** Before/After chaos contrast
- **Macro Close-Ups:** Steel connectors, zipper mechanism (trust signals)
- **Sound Design:** Amplify natural sounds (click, zip, thud) for realism

## 7. Compliance & Risk Mitigation | 合规与风险缓解

### TikTok Policy Adherence (TikTok政策遵守)
✅ **No exact prices in voiceover/captions** ("günstig" instead of "15€")
✅ **Qualified stability claims** ("stabil" not "unzerstörbar")
✅ **No medical claims** (dustproof for cleanliness, not allergy cure)
✅ **Proper CTA** ("Link ist unten" for TikTok shopping cart)

### Product-Specific Warnings (产品特定警告)
⚠️ Fabric cover is NOT machine washable (只能湿擦，不可机洗)
⚠️ 7-tier version has tip-over risk if climbed by children (7层版儿童攀爬有倾倒风险)
⚠️ "Waterproof" refers to fabric only, steel tubes can rust if wet (防水仅指布料，钢管遇水会生锈)
