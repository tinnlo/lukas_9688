## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** Re-record the voiceover in German (or use a high-quality German TTS) to match the target market.
2.  **Add Subtitles:** If keeping the Arabic audio for authenticity with a specific niche, MUST add large, clear German subtitles (e.g., "Platz für 30 Paar Schuhe!", "Einfache Montage").
3.  **Visual CTA:** Add a text overlay at the end pointing to the shopping cart anchor, not just a verbal "Link in bio".

**DON'T:**
1.  **Assume Language Competency:** Do not run non-German audio ads in Germany without subtitles; users will scroll past immediately.
2.  **Hide the Assembly:** If "easy assembly" is a claim, show a 2-second speed-up clip of the pieces clicking together.
3.  **Bury the Price:** If it's "super cheap", display the price overlay (e.g., "Nur 15€!") to trigger impulse buys.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Assembly:** Create a version focused purely on the sounds of assembly (clicking, snapping) which transcends language barriers.
2.  **"Before & After" Chaos:** Start with a messy pile of shoes (The "Pain"), then snap to the organized rack (The "Solution"). This visual story needs no words.
3.  **German "Ordnung":** Lean into the German cultural value of orderliness. Use captions like "Endlich Ordnung im Flur" (Finally order in the hallway).
