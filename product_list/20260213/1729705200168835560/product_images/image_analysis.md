## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

*   **Angle 1: The Space Saver (Problem/Solution)**
    *   **Concept:** Focus on small apartments where scratching posts take up too much room.
    *   **Visual:** Show a cluttered room vs. the clean, floating hammock.
    *   **Audio:** "Hast du auch keinen Platz für riesige Kratzbäume? Das hier ist die Lösung!"
    *   **Why:** Solves a specific human pain point (space) while benefitting the pet.

*   **Angle 2: The "Sunbath" Wellness Spa**
    *   **Concept:** Treat the hammock as a luxury spa treatment for the cat. Focus on warmth, sun, and relaxation.
    *   **Visual:** Slow pans of the sleeping cats (`product_image_1.webp`), peaceful music, sun rays hitting the fur.
    *   **Audio:** "Gönn deiner Katze den ultimativen Sonnenplatz. Wellness pur für deinen Liebling."
    *   **Why:** Emotional appeal to owners who want to pamper their pets.

*   **Angle 3: The Sturdiness Challenge (Skeptical)**
    *   **Concept:** Address the #1 fear: "Will it fall?" forcefully.
    *   **Visual:** Heavy items being put on it (books, dumbells - carefully), or aggressive shaking by hand before the cat jumps on.
    *   **Audio:** "Ich dachte, das fällt sofort ab... aber schau dir das an! Es hält bombenfest."
    *   **Why:** Overcomes the biggest objection immediately creating trust.
