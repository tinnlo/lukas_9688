## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

*   **Angle 1: The "Renter Friendly" Hack (Problem/Solution)**
    *   **Concept:** Focus entirely on the "No Drilling" aspect. Start with a skit of looking scared holding a drill, then throwing it away and using the adhesive.
    *   **Why:** High percentage of renters in Germany who fear losing their deposit ("Kaution").
    *   **Structure:** Fear of drilling -> Discovery of product -> Easy install -> Happy landlord/tenant.
*   **Angle 2: The "Expensive Tool Protector" (Value Based)**
    *   **Concept:** "You spent €500 on a Dyson/Shark, why do you treat it like trash?" Show the dryer lying in a puddle on the sink vs. safely mounted.
    *   **Why:** Increases the perceived value of the organizer by anchoring it to the expensive appliance it protects.
    *   **Structure:** Shocking visual of mistreated tool -> The Upgrade -> Safe & Stylish home -> "Respect your investment".
*   **Angle 3: The "ASMR / Aesthetic" (Visual/Sensory)**
    *   **Concept:** No talking, just sounds. Cleaning the tile (swish), peeling the sticker (crinkle), sticking it on (thud), clicking the dryer in (click). Relaxing music.
    *   **Why:** TikTok loves "Clean Girl" and "Organization Porn" content. It sells the *feeling* of having one's life together.
    *   **Structure:** Messy start (fast cut) -> Slow, deliberate installation sounds -> Perfect final shot with lighting flare.
