## 7. Compliance & Risk Mitigation | 合规与风险缓解

### TikTok Policy Adherence (TikTok政策遵守)
✅ **No exact prices in voiceover** (use "viel Geld" or "teure Investition" instead of "€400")
✅ **Qualified claims** ("stabil" not "unzerstörbar", "rostbeständig" not "100% rostfrei")
✅ **Surface warning** (only for smooth surfaces - tiles, glass, fridge paint)
✅ **Proper CTA** ("Link ist unten" for TikTok cart)

### Product-Specific Warnings (产品特定警告)
⚠️ **Surface Compatibility:** ONLY works on smooth surfaces (tiles, glass) - NOT on wallpaper or rough plaster (仅适用于光滑表面 - 不可用于壁纸或粗糙灰泥)
⚠️ **Weight Limit:** Realistic demonstration (2-3 tools max) - avoid overloading claims (重量限制：现实演示，最多2-3件工具)
⚠️ **Cure Time:** Advise "wait 24-48 hours before loading" to prevent falls and bad reviews (固化时间：建议"负重前等24-48小时"以防掉落和差评)
⚠️ **Product Not Included:** Clarify hair dryer NOT included in package (明确说明不包含吹风机)
⚠️ **Heat:** Do not install directly above oven or heating elements (adhesive may soften) (不要直接安装在烤箱或加热元件上方)
