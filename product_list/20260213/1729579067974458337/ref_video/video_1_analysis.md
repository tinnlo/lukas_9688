## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add localized Text-to-Speech:** Use a German AI voice saying "Mietwohnung? Kein Problem!" (Rental? No problem!).
   *添加本地化语音：使用德语AI语音说“租房？没问题！”*
2. **Show a "Before" disaster:** Start with bottles falling over to heighten the pain point.
   *展示“使用前”的混乱：以瓶瓶罐罐倒下的混乱场景开场，以此增强痛点。*
3. **Load Test Extreme:** Put something unreasonably heavy (like a dumbbell or 5L water) to prove strength.
   *极限承重测试：放上极其重的东西（如哑铃或5升水）来证明牢固度。*

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't show dark footage:** Bathrooms must look bright and clean.
   *不要展示昏暗的画面：浴室必须看起来明亮洁净。*
2. **Don't drag out the cleaning:** Keep the prep phase under 2 seconds.
   *不要拖延清洁过程：将准备阶段控制在2秒以内。*
3. **Don't ignore audio:** Even without talking, use sound effects (swish, click, pop).
   *不要忽视音频：即使没有说话，也要使用音效（嗖、咔嚓、砰）。*

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Rental Hack" Angle:** Explicitly frame it as "The #1 Gadget for German Renters".
   *“租房神器”角度：明确将其定位为“德国租房者的第一神器”。*
2. **Comparison:** Split screen with a drill (Red X) vs this product (Green Check).
   *对比展示：分屏展示电钻（红叉）与该产品（绿勾）。*
3. **ASMR Version:** Focus entirely on the sounds of peeling, sticking, and placing bottles.
   *ASMR版本：完全专注于撕开、粘贴和放置瓶子的声音。*
