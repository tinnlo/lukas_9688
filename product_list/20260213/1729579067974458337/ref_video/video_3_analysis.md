## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Extend Duration:** Create a video at least 7-15 seconds long to demonstrate the "No-drill" installation process. (延长视频时长至7-15秒，展示“免打孔”安装过程)
2. **Show "Before & After":** Show a messy bathroom corner, then the organized shelf. (展示“使用前后”对比，突出整洁效果)
3. **Add German Voiceover/Text:** Use TTS or voiceover saying "Mietwohnung? Keine Löcher!" (Renter? No holes!) to target the specific pain point. (添加德语旁白/文字，针对租房人群痛点)

**DON'T:**
1. **Use 2-second clips:** The TikTok algorithm penalizes ultra-short content that doesn't loop perfectly or offer high density value. (不要使用2秒的短片，算法会惩罚此类低信息量内容)
2. **Rely on Static Images:** TikTok is a video platform; movement is essential to stop the scroll. (不要依赖静态图片，动态内容才能留住观众)
3. **Ignore Trust Building:** For adhesive products, you MUST show it holding weight (e.g., putting a heavy bottle on it) to prove it won't fall. (不要忽视信任建立，必须展示承重能力)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Installation:** The sound of peeling the sticker, sticking it to the wall, and placing bottles can be satisfying. (ASMR安装过程：撕胶带、粘贴、放置物品的声音很解压)
2. **"Renter Friendly" Angle:** Explicitly market this as "The #1 Gadget for German Renters" who fear losing their deposit (Kaution). (主打“租房神器”角度，针对德国租户担心扣押金的心理)
3. **Stress Test:** Show someone pulling on the shelf to demonstrate extreme adhesion strength. (压力测试：展示用力拉扯架子，证明其超强粘性)
