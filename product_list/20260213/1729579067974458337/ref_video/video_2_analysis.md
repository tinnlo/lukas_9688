## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Enhance the CTA:** Add a clear text overlay at the end like "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) or "50% Rabatt" (50% Off) to drive action.
2.  **Show "No-Drill" Feature:** Explicitly show the installation process (peeling the sticker, sticking it on) as this is a huge selling point for renters in Germany.
3.  **Add Social Proof:** Include a quick user review snippet or star rating overlay if possible.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely solely on the voiceover:** Ensure text overlays support the key words (e.g., "Schimmelfrei", "Ohne Bohren") for users watching without sound.
2.  **Make the "mess" too disgusting:** Keep it relatable clutter, not repulsive filth, to maintain brand safety.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Create a version focused on the *sounds* of organizing – bottles clicking into place, water draining satisfyingly.
2.  **"Renter Friendly" Angle:** Explicitly target the "Mietwohnung" (Rental apartment) keyword in the text overlay and caption, as drilling holes is often forbidden or hassle-prone in German rentals.
