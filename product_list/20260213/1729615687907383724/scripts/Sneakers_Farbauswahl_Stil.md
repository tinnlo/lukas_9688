## Scripts

**Concept:** The "Multi-Color Personality" angle - showcase 6 color options as a lifestyle choice, encouraging multiple purchases through emotional styling.

### Structure (40s)
- **Hook:** "I ordered them in 3 colors" admission (0-3s)
- **Color Showcase:** Rapid flash through all 6 colors (3-15s)
- **Styling Tips:** Match colors to moods/outfits (15-28s)
