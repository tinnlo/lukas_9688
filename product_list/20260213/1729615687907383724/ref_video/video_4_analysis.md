## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio (本地化音频):** Immediately replace the voiceover with a native German speaker or high-quality German AI TTS. The script itself is decent (UGC style).
2.  **Add Subtitles (添加字幕):** If keeping the original audio for authenticity, HARD SUBTITLES in German are mandatory.
3.  **Visual Text Hooks (视觉文字钩子):** Add German text overlays like "Super bequem!" (Super comfortable) or "2 für 1 Deal?" to convey value without sound.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Mix Languages (不要语言错配):** Running a purely Romanian ad to a broad German audience wastes ad spend.
2.  **Don't Hide the Offer (不要隐藏优惠):** If the "buy 2" is a bundle deal, make it explicit visually (e.g., "Kaufe 2, Spare 10%").

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Nurse/Worker" Angle (职业切入):** These shoes look like nursing/service industry shoes. Target people who stand all day (Waitresses, Nurses) with a "Pain Free Feet" angle in German.
2.  **Color Variation (多色展示):** Create a fast cut showing all 6 colors in 5 seconds to appeal to different tastes quickly.
