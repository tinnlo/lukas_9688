## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the Sole:** The comfort aspect is the #1 selling point; keep the "flexing" shot. (展示鞋底：舒适度是第一卖点，保留“弯曲鞋身”的镜头。)
2. **Multi-color Grid:** Visualizing all 6 colors in one frame increases choice-confidence. (多色矩阵：在一帧内展示所有6种颜色，增强消费者的选择信心。)
3. **Keep it UGC:** Avoid overly professional editing to maintain the "friend recommendation" feel. (保持 UGC 风格：避免过度专业化的剪辑，以维持“朋友推荐”的感觉。)

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Hide the Price:** While "pay less" is mentioned, a "From €XX" overlay would boost conversion. (不要隐藏价格：虽然提到了“便宜”，但加上“XX欧元起”的文字叠加会更有利于转化。)
2. **Avoid shaky cam:** Use a simple tripod or software stabilization for better visual trust. (避免镜头过于晃动：使用简易支架或软件防抖，提升视觉信任度。)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Bilingual Captions:** Adding German + Chinese/Turkish subtitles could capture the diverse German expat market. (双语字幕：添加德语+中文/土耳其语字幕，可以捕获多元化的德国外籍市场。)
2. **ASMR Version:** A version focusing on the sound of unboxing and the "squish" of the sole could go viral. (ASMR版本：专注拆箱声和鞋底挤压声的版本可能会引发病毒式传播。)
