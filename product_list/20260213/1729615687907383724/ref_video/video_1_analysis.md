## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Use Colloquialisms:** Use local slang (like "Geil", "Hammer") instead of formal textbook German.
2.  **POV Feet Shots:** Always show the shoes *on feet* walking, not just in the box.
3.  **Address the Weather:** Germany has variable weather. Mentioning "Autumn" (Herbst) handling is a major selling point.

**DON'T (避免错误):**
1.  **Don't Over-polish:** Do not use studio lighting or professional camera movements. It will look like an ad and be skipped.
2.  **Don't Forget the Cart:** Ensure the verbal CTA matches the visual pointing or sticker.
3.  **Don't be Robot:** Avoid AI-sounding TTS. The "mistakes" in speech here helped authenticity.

**OPPORTUNITY (潜在机会):**
1.  **Response Reply:** Create a "Part 2" answering a comment like "Are they waterproof?" to drive more engagement.
2.  **Color Styling:** A video dedicated solely to "How to style the [Color] version".
3.  **Discount Hook:** Re-edit the first 3 seconds with a text overlay "Only €XX today" to test price sensitivity.
