## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1. Emphasize "Weightless" and "Comfort" in the first 3 seconds. (在前3秒强调“无感重量”和“舒适度”。)
2. Use multiple color variations in the background to show variety. (在背景中展示多种颜色选项以体现多样性。)
3. Keep the duration under 15s for high completion rates. (时长保持在15秒以内以获得高完播率。)

**DON'T:**
1. Avoid long static shots; keep the product moving or the camera panning. (避免长时间的静态画面；保持产品移动或相机平移。)
2. Don't hide the "white stripes" if that is a key stylizing feature. (如果白条纹是核心修饰特征，不要遮挡它。)
3. Avoid over-editing; keep the "authentic friend" vibe. (避免过度剪辑；保持“真实朋友”的氛围。)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. Add German text overlays to bridge the language gap with the Spanish VO. (增加德语文字叠加，以弥合西班牙语旁白与德国市场的语言差距。)
2. Side-by-side comparison with a "heavy" old sneaker to prove the lightweight claim. (与“沉重”的旧鞋进行左右对比，证明轻盈的卖点。)
3. Feature the "6 colors available" more prominently as a visual grid. (以视觉网格的形式更突出地展示“6色可选”。)
