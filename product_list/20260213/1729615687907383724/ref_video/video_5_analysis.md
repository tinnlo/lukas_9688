## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO:**
1. **Localize for Germany!** Use ElevenLabs or a native speaker to re-dub this script into German. "Wo hast du die her?" hits harder than "Where did you get it". | **针对德国进行本地化！** 使用ElevenLabs或母语人士将此脚本重新配音为德语。“Wo hast du die her?”比英文更有冲击力。
2. **Add German Subtitles.** If keeping English audio, hard-coded German captions are non-negotiable for retention. | **添加德语字幕。** 如果保留英语音频，硬编码的德语字幕对于保持率是没得商量的。
3. **Show specific price comparison.** "Looks like €100, costs only €20" creates a stronger anchor. | **展示具体的价格对比。** “看起来像100欧，实际只要20欧”能建立更强的锚点。

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't rely on broken English.** Phrases like "help or meet" confuse the buyer and reduce trust in the seller's logistics/support. | **不要依赖蹩脚的英语。** 像“help or meet”这样的短语会让买家困惑，并降低对卖家物流/客服的信任。
2. **Don't repeat "affordable" vaguely.** Show *why* it's affordable or show the actual price tag. | **不要含糊地重复“实惠”。** 展示*为什么*实惠，或者展示实际价格标签。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"German Weather" Angle.** Position the shoe as perfect for German walking/weather conditions (if waterproof/durable). | **“德国天气”角度。** 将鞋子定位为非常适合德国步行/天气条件（如果防水/耐用）。
2. **Bundle Offer.** "Grab two colors" since the script mentions "any colour". | **捆绑销售。** 既然脚本提到了“任何颜色”，可以推“买两双不同色”。
3. **Comment Reply Strategy.** Reply to a (fake or real) comment asking "Link?" with a video response. | **评论回复策略。** 用视频回复一个（真或假）问“链接？”的评论。
