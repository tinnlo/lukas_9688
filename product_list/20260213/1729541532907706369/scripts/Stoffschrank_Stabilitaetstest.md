## Scripts

**Concept:** The "Skeptic" format — Directly address German skepticism about fabric wardrobe stability with aggressive load testing.

### Structure (40s)
- **Hook:** Skeptical question about fabric vs. wood (0-3s)
- **Setup:** Show the lightweight frame (3-8s)
- **Test 1:** Load with heavy winter coats (8-18s)
- **Test 2:** Shake test for stability (18-28s)
- **Result:** Surprised validation (28-35s)
- **CTA:** Trust established, purchase prompt (35-40s)
