## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Cultural Insight: The "Chair of Shame" (文化洞察："羞耻椅")
**Universal German Problem:**
- Clothes pile on chair instead of being hung properly (衣服堆在椅子上而不是好好挂起来)
- Creates visual stress and embarrassment when guests visit (访客来时造成视觉压力和尴尬)
- Wooden wardrobes are expensive (€500+) and hard to move (木衣柜昂贵且难搬运)

**Solution Positioning:**
Foldable wardrobe as the "guilt-free" organizer — cheap enough not to feel wasteful, portable enough for student life.

### Three-Pronged Approach (三管齐下策略)

1. **Script 1: Emotional Transformation (Chaos → Order)**
   - Hook: The "Chair of Shame" relatable frustration
   - Journey: From daily chaos to organized calm
   - Target: Students, WG residents, young professionals

2. **Script 2: Skeptic Conversion (Stability Proof)**
   - Hook: "Can fabric really hold winter coats?"
   - Evidence: Aggressive shake test + heavy load
   - Target: Budget-conscious buyers questioning quality

3. **Script 3: ASMR Efficiency (Speed Build)**
   - Hook: Unboxing surprise (small package → full wardrobe)
   - Satisfaction: ASMR assembly clicks + organization ritual
   - Target: "Ordnung" enthusiasts, ASMR fans

## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Props Needed (所需道具)
- Messy pile of clothes (15-20 items for visual impact) (15-20件衣物营造视觉冲击)
- Chair for "Chair of Shame" setup (椅子用于"羞耻椅"设置)
- Heavy winter coats for stability test (重型冬季外套用于稳定性测试)
- Product packaging box for unboxing shot (产品包装盒用于开箱镜头)

### Filming Setup (拍摄设置)
- **Location:** Bedroom or dorm room with natural mess, clean corner for "after" shots
- **Lighting:** Bright natural light to show fabric texture, ring light for close-ups
- **Audio:** High-quality microphone for ASMR clicking sounds (steel tube connections, zipper)
- **Pacing:** Fast cuts (1.5-2s), speed ramp assembly sequences

### Key Sound Design (关键音效设计)
- **ASMR Clicks:** Amplify steel tube connector snap sounds (satisfying rhythm)
- **Zipper Sound:** Feature the zip close/open for dustproof function proof
- **Ambient:** Keep room tone clean for professional feel despite "messy" setup

## 7. Compliance & Risk Mitigation | 合规与风险缓解

### TikTok Policy Adherence (TikTok政策遵守)
✅ **No exact prices in voiceover** ("günstiger als" instead of specific euros)
✅ **Qualified stability claims** ("stabil" not "unzerstörbar")
✅ **No medical claims** (dust protection for cleanliness, not allergy cure)
✅ **Proper CTA** ("Link ist unten" for TikTok cart)

### Product-Specific Warnings (产品特定警告)
⚠️ Not suitable for very heavy items (weight limit ~50kg total) (不适合超重物品)
⚠️ Fabric cover not machine washable (wipe clean only) (布套不可机洗)
⚠️ Requires level floor to prevent tipping when fully loaded (需平整地面防止倾倒)
