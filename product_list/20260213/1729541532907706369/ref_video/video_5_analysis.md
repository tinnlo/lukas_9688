## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Fix the Ending:** Ensure the video doesn't cut off mid-sentence. Add a clear visual CTA (e.g., "Link in Bio" or "Jetzt bestellen") in the last 2-3 seconds.
2. **Add Text Overlays:** Reinforce the "5 Minutes" and "No Tools" claims with on-screen text for viewers watching without sound.
3. **Show Capacity:** Briefly show the wardrobe *full* of clothes to prove it's sturdy, addressing a common concern with portable wardrobes.

**DON'T:**
1. **Leave the "Before" state too long:** 3 seconds is good, but don't linger on the mess; pivot to the solution quickly (as done here).
2. **Ignore Sound Design:** Ensure the "click" sound of the assembly is audible (ASMR effect) to reinforce sturdiness.
3. **Forget Dimensions:** For German buyers, knowing it fits in a specific corner is key. Mention dimensions or show it in a tight space.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Student/Dorm" Angle:** Explicitly target students moving into dorms (Schlafsaal) or temporary housing, as this is a perfect "temporary furniture" solution.
2. **Load Test:** Film a "How much can it hold?" challenge where you pile heavy coats on it to prove durability.
3. **Travel Angle:** Emphasize that it fits in a suitcase/handbag for long-term stays (AirBnB hopping).
