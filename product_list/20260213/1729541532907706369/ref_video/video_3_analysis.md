## 13. Recommendations | 建议

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Add a "Before/After" Hook:** Show a messy pile of clothes, then snap fingers to show them neatly organized in the wardrobe. (展示衣服乱堆的痛点，然后变魔术般展示整齐收纳).
2.  **Add German Voiceover:** Use TTS or human voice to ask: "Zu viele Klamotten und kein Platz?" (Too many clothes and no space? / 衣服太多没地方放？).
3.  **Demonstrate Assembly:** Show how fast it is to set up (Speed-build). Germans love efficiency. (展示快速组装过程，德国人喜欢效率).

**DON'T (避免错误):**
1.  **Don't rely on silence:** 10s of silence guarantees a scroll-away. (不要依赖静音视频).
2.  **Don't just show the closed wardrobe:** Open it! Show how much fits inside. (不要只展示关着的柜子，打开它展示容量).
3.  **Don't ignore the "Zipper" feature:** Show the zipper closing to emphasize protection from dust. (不要忽略拉链功能，展示防尘效果).

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **ASMR Packing:** Record the sound of zipping/unzipping and stacking clothes. (ASMR收纳/拉链声音).
2.  **"Dorm Room Essential":** Position specifically for students moving into new dorms. (定位为"宿舍必备").
3.  **Load Test:** Put heavy items in to prove sturdiness (often a concern for fabric wardrobes). (承重测试，消除对布衣柜不结实的顾虑).
