## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** **CRITICAL.** Use German TTS (Text-to-Speech) or a native German speaker. Even AI dubbing would be better than the current state.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text benefits on screen: "Einfacher Aufbau" (Easy setup), "Viel Platz" (Lots of space), "Nur X€" (Price).
3.  **Show Weight Test:** Demonstrate durability by putting something heavy (like a stack of jeans or a heavy coat) to prove it won't collapse.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on foreign audio:** Never run a Romanian voiceover ad in the German TikTok Shop feed without at least hard-coded German subtitles.
2.  **Don't hide the result:** Don't spend too long on the empty skeleton; show the *organized* result sooner (within first 5 seconds).
3.  **Don't ignore lighting:** The video seems dim. Brighter lighting makes the fabric look cleaner and higher quality.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Chaos vs. Order" Angle:** Split screen showing a pile of clothes on a chair vs. the neat wardrobe. Germans love order ("Ordnung muss sein").
2.  **ASMR Assembly:** Remove the voiceover entirely and use satisfying "click" sounds of the tubes connecting + upbeat trending music. This transcends language barriers.
3.  **Student/Dorm Angle:** Specifically market it as "The perfect wardrobe for your first WG room" (Das perfekte Schrank für dein erstes WG-Zimmer).
