## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Voiceover:** "Nie wieder Chaos! Dieser faltbare Schrank rettet dein Zimmer." (No more chaos! This foldable wardrobe saves your room.) | **添加德语旁白：**“再也不乱了！这个折叠衣柜拯救你的房间。”
2. **Show "Before & After":** Start with a messy pile of clothes, then snap to the organized wardrobe. | **展示“使用前/使用后”：** 以一堆乱衣服开始，然后切换到整齐的衣柜。
3. **Demonstrate Strength:** Put something heavy on top or fill it completely to prove it's sturdy (Trust Signal). | **展示强度：** 在上面放重物或装满它，以证明它很结实（信任信号）。

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** Never post a silent product video on TikTok. Use trending audio + VO. | **依赖静音：** 永远不要在TikTok上发布静音产品视频。使用流行音频 + 旁白。
2. **Be Generic:** Avoid simple panning shots. Make the product *do* something (fold, zip, hold weight). | **过于平庸：** 避免简单的平移镜头。让产品*动起来*（折叠，拉拉链，承重）。
3. **Ignore Lighting:** Ensure the product looks clean and the environment looks bright. | **忽视灯光：** 确保产品看起来干净，环境看起来明亮。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Unboxing:** The sound of the zipper and the "snap" of unfolding could be satisfying. | **ASMR开箱：** 拉链的声音和展开时的“咔哒”声可能很解压。
2. **"Dorm Room Hack":** Target students specifically with a "University Essentials" angle. | **“宿舍神器”：** 专门针对学生，采用“大学必备品”的角度。
3. **Speed Challenge:** "How fast can I assemble this?" (Timer on screen). | **速度挑战：** “我能多快组装好它？”（屏幕上有计时器）。
