## 7. Recommendations for New Scripts | 新脚本建议

### MUST-HAVE Elements (Non-negotiable) | 必须包含的元素（不可协商）

**1. Opening Hook (0-3 seconds) - Choose One:**
- **Option A (Emotional):** "Mir zu Hause lebt einfach ein Messi" (A hoarder lives at my house)
- **Option B (Humor):** "Das ist kein Beutel, das ist ein Zelt" (This isn't a bag, it's a tent)
- **Option C (Relatable):** "Kennst du das? Chaos überall" (You know this? Chaos everywhere)

**2. Problem Agitation (3-8 seconds):**
- Show the mess (visual proof)
- Express frustration ("Ich kann nicht mehr!")
- Build tension ("Was mach ich da?")

**3. Product Introduction (8-15 seconds):**
- TikTok Shop discovery moment ("Dann hab ich das gefunden")
- Capacity reveal ("Guckt euch das Fassungsvermögen an")

**4. Proof Demo (15-30 seconds):**
- Fast packing timelapse
- Handle strength test (lift heavy bag)
- Zipper closure proof

**5. CTA (30-35 seconds):**
- Clear verbal instruction ("Link unten" / "In Bio")
- Use case reminder ("Für Umzug, Wäsche, alles")

- **Hook:** "Messi lives in my house"

### Script Angle 2: Humor/Hyperbole (Entertainment) | 脚本角度2：幽默/夸张（娱乐）

### DO/DON'T for Production | 制作建议

**DO:**
1. Use German voiceover (native speaker quality)
2. Start with mess/chaos visual (critical for contrast)
3. Speed up packing sequence (timelapse or fast cuts)
4. Show zipper closing on FULL bag (ultimate proof)
5. Include cultural slang ("Messi," "Ciao Kakao")
6. Test handle strength on camera (build trust)

**DON'T:**
1. Never upload silent videos (auto-fail in DE market)
2. Don't show empty bag (looks like generic ad)
3. Don't use English-only audio (no localization)
4. Don't fake the mess (inauthentic = low trust)
5. Don't skip the CTA (clear "Link unten" required)
