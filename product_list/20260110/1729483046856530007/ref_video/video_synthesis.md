## 7. Visual Editing Playbook | è§†è§‰å‰ªè¾‘æ‰“æ³•

**A. The "Glow Up" Transition**
*   **Shot 1:** Lamp OFF in a bright, messy room.
*   **Transition:** Finger snap or hand wave over the speaker.
*   **Shot 2:** Lamp ON (Blue/Purple) in a dark, clean room with audio playing.
*   **Why:** Shows immediate atmospheric transformation.

**B. The "Feature Tap"**
*   Cut to the beat of the recitation.
*   Every time the reciter takes a breath -> Visual cut to a new color (Red -> Green -> Blue).
*   Shows RGB variety without boring the viewer.

**C. The "Unbox & Touch"**
*   Speed up the unboxing (2x speed).
*   Slow down to 0.5x speed when touching the speaker mesh to change light.
*   Emphasize the *tactile* control.
