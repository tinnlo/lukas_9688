## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Match Audio to Action:** Use ASMR sounds of the fan blowing (whoosh noises) or high-energy phonk music which trends well with "tech gadgets" in Germany.
2.  **Add German Text Overlay:** Add "Das stärkste Gebläse?" (The strongest blower?) or "Auto trocknen in Sekunden" (Dry car in seconds) to bridge the language gap.
3.  **Show "Before/After":** Make the cleaning result more explicit.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Alienate the Broad Market:** Using religious spoken word limits the audience strictly to those who understand/respect it, and might confuse the algorithm or other users.
2.  **Leave the Ending Flat:** End with a clear reason to buy *now* (e.g., "Link unten links").
3.  **Hide the Nozzle:** Ensure the specific "Jet" nozzle is visible, as that is the visual identifier for this trending product class.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Route:** This product makes a specific loud noise. An "Unboxing + Sound Test" video often performs well.
2.  **"Vs. Compressed Air":** Compare it to a can of compressed air (which runs out/freezes) to show the value of a rechargeable device.
3.  **Outdoor/Winter Use:** Show it blowing snow off a car windshield (seasonal relevance for Germany in winter).
