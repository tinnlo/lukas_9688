## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Text Overlays:** Add "Link in Bio" or "Best Car Gadget" (in German: "Bestes Auto-Gadget") to bridge the gap between entertainment and purchase.
2. **Show Use Case:** Instead of just "shaking", show a specific problem being solved (e.g., drying a car mirror, cleaning a keyboard) to build value utility.
3. **Reply to Comments:** With 1.5M views, the comment section is a goldmine. Reply with video responses to drive traffic to the product page.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Entertainment Alone:** Do not assume a viral video sells itself. The 0.0015% conversion rate proves views $\neq$ cash.
2. **Forget the Offer:** Don't leave the price or discount a mystery. Add a sticker "50% RABATT" (50% Off).
3. **Leave Audio Unmatched:** Don't use this audio if the visual doesn't actually "shake". The mismatch causes drop-off.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Remix with CTA:** Repost the exact same video but add a TTS voiceover at the end saying "Grab yours now!" or a text overlay "Sale Ends Tonight".
2. **Localize:** Use a German Trending Audio or German TTS voiceover explaining "Endlich staubfrei!" (Finally dust-free).
3. **Loop Strategy:** Edit the video so the end matches the beginning perfectly, encouraging infinite loops which boosts algorithm ranking.
