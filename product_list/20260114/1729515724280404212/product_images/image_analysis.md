## 10. Visual Hooks for Scripts | 脚本视觉钩子

### Visual Hook Ideas (Priority Ranking)

**1. The "Magnet Snap" (High Energy Start)**
-   **Action**: Show the light being thrown/slapped onto a car hood and sticking instantly and firmly.
-   **Filming**: Audio ON. Capture the satisfying "Clack" sound of the magnet hitting metal. Shake it to prove it holds.
-   **German Hook**: "Hält bombenfest an jeder Motorhaube!" (Holds tight to every hood!)
-   **ZH**: 强力磁吸，稳如泰山！

**2. The "360 Spin" (Versatility)**
-   **Action**: Mount the light, then spin the light bar rapidly 360 degrees to show it lights up every corner.
-   **Filming**: Fast speed. Use a dark environment so the light beam movement is dramatic.
-   **German Hook**: "Licht in jedem Winkel – 360 Grad drehbar." (Light in every angle – 360 degree rotatable.)
-   **ZH**: 360度旋转，照明无死角。

**3. The "Red Alert" (Safety/Fear of Missing Out)**
-   **Action**: Cut to a dark road scene (simulated), turn on the flashing red emergency mode.
-   **Filming**: High contrast. Dark background, bright red strobing light.
-   **German Hook**: "Deine Lebensversicherung bei einer Panne." (Your life insurance during a breakdown.)
-   **ZH**: 你的夜间故障"救命灯"。

**4. The "Percentage Reveal" (Feature Focus)**
-   **Action**: Close up on the "88" digital display while plugging in the charger. Watch it go to "89".
-   **Filming**: Macro shot. Blur background. Focus on the sharp LED numbers.
-   **German Hook**: "Nie wieder raten, wie viel Akku noch da ist." (Never guess how much battery is left again.)
-   **ZH**: 电量精确显示，告别电量焦虑。

**5. The "Hidden Transformer" (Curiosity)**
-   **Action**: Start with it looking like a simple stick. Flip out the hooks, rotate the ends, snap it to metal – show it transforming.
-   **Filming**: Fast cuts between the different configurations.
-   **German Hook**: "Das Schweizer Taschenmesser unter den Lampen." (The Swiss Army Knife of lights.)
-   **ZH**: 灯具界的"瑞士军刀"。

### Filming Instructions Summary
-   **Lighting**: ALWAYS film in slightly dim conditions to exaggerate the brightness of the COB LED.
-   **Sound**: Emphasize mechanical clicks (hooks, rotation) and magnetic snaps.
-   **Hands**: Use dirty/greasy hands (gloves) for the car repair shots to look authentic.
-   **Contrast**: Black light on white car paint or silver engine parts looks best.
