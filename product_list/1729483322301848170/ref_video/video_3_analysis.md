## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a "Live Test" Hook:** Start with someone speaking German and the earbuds immediately speaking the translation. *("Hör mal zu..." -> "Listen to this...")*.
2.  **Use Text Overlays (German):** Clearly label features: *"120 Sprachen"* (120 Languages), *"Live Übersetzung"* (Live Translation).
3.  **Show the App:** The hardware is just plastic; the value is in the software. Show the phone screen translating text.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Silence:** For a product about *language and listening*, a silent video is counter-intuitive.
2.  **Hide the Brand:** Ensure MINISO branding is visible if it carries weight (value/trust).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Travel Fail" Angle:** Show a confusing situation (ordering food, asking directions) solved instantly by the earbuds. This resonates highly with German travelers.
2.  **ASMR Unboxing:** Focus on the sound of the case and the texture for a sensory hook.
