## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Use Real Footage:** Film the actual physical product to show material quality (velvet, gold texture) and scale.
2. **Localize Audio:** Use a German voiceover (or clear English) explaining features: "Perfekt für dein Zuhause im Ramadan" (Perfect for your home this Ramadan).
3. **Respectful Atmosphere:** Ensure background audio is high-quality Nasheed without clashing voiceovers.

**DON'T:**
1. **Avoid "AI Host" for Religious Goods:** It feels commercial and soulless. Real human hands or a respectful faceless showcase is better.
2. **Don't use Broken Audio:** Ensure the hook sentence is complete. "Oh, a good one can be..." is a cut-off thought that confuses the viewer.
3. **Don't Rely on Static Images:** If the video is just a slideshow of the product image, it will have low retention.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Unboxing Experience:** Show the packaging and the moment of placing the Kaaba on a shelf to evoke the feeling of preparation for Ramadan.
2. **Lighting Reveal:** Use cinematic lighting to make the gold details "glow" in the dark, emphasizing the "Heavenly" (Himmelskaaba) aspect.
3. **Gift Angle:** Position it specifically as "The best gift for your parents this Eid" to tap into emotional gifting.
