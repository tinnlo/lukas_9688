## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO (做):**
1.  **Add Subtitles (German & English):** This is critical. Many Muslims in Germany speak German or Turkish primarily. Arabic-only audio severely limits the addressable market (TAM).
2.  **Show it "In Situ":** Instead of just holding it, show it actually placed on a decorated Ramadan table with lights/lanterns to sell the "vibe."
3.  **Improve Lighting:** Use a ring light or window light to make the gold details on the black Kaaba pop.

**DON'T (不做):**
1.  **Ignore the "German" Context:** Even if targeting Arabs, adding German keywords in the description or on-screen text aids SEO within TikTok Germany.
2.  **Drift Focus:** Don't talk about too many things. Keep it focused on "Education for Kids" OR "Beautiful Decor". This video mixes both well, but could be sharper.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **Bundle Gift Angle:** Market it specifically as an "Eid Gift" for nephews/nieces, not just for one's own home.
2.  **Interactive Content:** Ask a question in the sticker: "Are you ready for Ramadan?" to boost engagement.
3.  **Localize:** Remake the exact same video but with a German voiceover (or AI dubbing) to reach the millions of Turkish/German Muslims.
