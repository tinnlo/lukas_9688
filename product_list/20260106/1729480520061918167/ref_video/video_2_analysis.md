## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Text Overlays:** "Ramadan Deko 2026" or "Jetzt bestellen" (Order Now) to guide the German viewer.
2. **Show Human Interaction:** A hand placing the Kaaba on a table or dusting it off would add scale and "ownership" desire (Endowment Effect).
3. **Create Urgency:** Add a text sticker "Begrenzte Stückzahl" (Limited Stock) to drive the 0 sales to 1.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely only on Arabic Audio:** While culturally relevant, the *transaction* happens in a German context (shipping, price). Silence or Arabic-only leaves logistic questions unanswered.
2. **Use Static Shots Only:** Ensure there is movement (rotation or hand movement) to prove it's a 3D object and not just a picture.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Gift Angle:** Frame it explicitly as "The perfect Eid Gift for parents" (Das perfekte Eid-Geschenk für Eltern). This emotional angle drives higher conversion than just "decoration".
2. **Unboxing Experience:** Show the packaging. If it comes in a nice box, showing the unboxing builds higher value perception.
3. **Bundle:** Suggest buying it with a prayer mat or Tasbih for a "Spiritual Corner" makeover.
