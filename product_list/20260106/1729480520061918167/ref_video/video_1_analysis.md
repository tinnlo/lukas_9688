## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add German Overlays:** Add text like "Ein Stück Mekka für dein Zuhause" (A piece of Mecca for your home) to bridge the language gap.
2.  **Show Scale/Weight:** Tap the item to show it's solid (ASMR), or place a hand next to it to clarify size.
3.  **Specific CTA:** End with "Perfektes Eid-Geschenk - Jetzt sichern!" (Perfect Eid Gift - Secure yours now!).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely solely on Music:** Don't let the Nasheed carry the whole message. Explain *why* this decor is special.
2.  **Ignore Seasonality:** Instead of generic #UnboxYourYear, use #RamadanPrep or #EidGeschenk to trigger purchase intent.
3.  **Hide the Material:** Don't use filters that wash out the gold contrast; keep it sharp.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **The "Respect" Angle:** Show the proper way to clean/place the Kaaba with reverence—this educates and builds deep emotional trust.
2.  **Parenting/Education:** Frame it as a way to teach children about the Kaaba visually.
3.  **Gift Unboxing:** Film it specifically as "Packing an order for a customer" or "Wrapping a gift for Mom" to spur gifting ideas.
