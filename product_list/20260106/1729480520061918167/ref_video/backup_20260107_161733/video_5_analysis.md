## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Subtitles:** Add German or English subtitles. While targeting Arabic speakers is smart, many 2nd gen immigrants might prefer reading German/English captions.
2.  **Show "Kids Interaction":** If possible, actually show a child's hand touching the model or looking at it to visually prove the "educational" claim.
3.  **Price Anchor:** Mention the specific price if it's truly cheap (e.g., "Less than 10 Euros") to concrete the value.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Over-polish:** Keep the lighting warm and homey. Studio lighting might make it look like a generic commercial and lose the "authentic advice" feel.
2.  **Don't Ignore Seasonality:** Ensure this ad runs heavily before Ramadan/Eid.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Bundle:** Suggest buying 2-3 as gifts for friends/family (Eid Gift), increasing AOV (Average Order Value).
2.  **German Version:** Re-record the exact same script in German to reach the non-Arabic speaking Muslim demographic in Germany (Turkish, Balkan, South Asian backgrounds).
