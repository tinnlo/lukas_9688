## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show Scale:** Place the item next to a Quran, tea set, or hand to show size (potential reason for 0 sales if size is ambiguous).
2. **Add German Text:** Overlay text like "Perfekt für Ramadan" or "Jetzt bestellen" to guide German viewers explicitly.
3. **Demonstrate Unboxing:** Show the packaging to build trust that it arrives safely (important for fragile decor).

**DON'T:**
1. **Over-edit:** Avoid flashy transitions that clash with the spiritual audio.
2. **Leave Audio Only:** Don't rely solely on the nasheed; add a text hook for those watching on mute (though unlikely for this niche).
3. **Hide the Price:** If it's a premium item, justify the value with close-ups of craftsmanship.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Gift Angle:** Frame it explicitly as "The perfect Eid gift for parents/spouse" in text overlays.
2. **ASMR Version:** A version with just the sound of unpacking and placing it (tapping sounds) could work.
3. **Styling Tips:** Show 3 different ways to style it on a shelf/table.
