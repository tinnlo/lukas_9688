## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** "Perfektes Eid Geschenk" (Perfect Eid Gift) or "Ramadan Deko" on screen.
2.  **Shorten the Video:** 10-15 seconds is enough for a static item. 27s is too slow.
3.  **Show Scale:** Place it next to a Quran or a hand to show the 11cm size clearly.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't use generic hashtags:** #UnboxYourYear is irrelevant. Use #RamadanDecor #EidGeschenk #IslamicArt.
2.  **Don't rely on audio alone:** Many users watch on mute; without text, they just see a black box initially.
3.  **Don't ignore the CTA:** Add a "Jetzt kaufen" (Buy Now) arrow or sticker at the end.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Seasonal Timing:** Promote heavily *before* Ramadan (which starts late Feb/March), not just randomly.
2.  **Emotional Angle:** Show someone *gifting* it to a parent or placing it respectfully on a high shelf.
3.  **Bundle:** Sell with prayer beads (Tasbih) or a prayer mat.
