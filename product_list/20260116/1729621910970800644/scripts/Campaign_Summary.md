## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

*From image_analysis.md Section 10 (Visual Hooks):*

- **Primary Visual:** The "squish test" - hand pressing into velvet
- **Secondary Visual:** Zipper opening/cover removal
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight, window placement optimal
- **Props Needed:** Pet (relaxed, preferably after play), tiny pillow
- **Colors Available:** Cream, Grey, Pink, Light Brown

**DO's:**
- Film pet after exercise (sleepy = more natural relaxation footage)
- Use macro lens for texture shots
- Include real washing machine footage

**DON'Ts:**
- Use English voiceover or text
- Film in dark rooms (hides texture quality)
- Start with "Hello Guys" (boring hook)
