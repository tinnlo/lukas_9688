## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Localize for Germany:** Use **German Voiceover** (TTS or human) and German Text Overlays (e.g., "Super Weich", "Waschbar"). English ads perform poorly in DE TikTok Shop.
2. **Show, Don't Just Say:** Zoom in on the fabric texture and press a hand into it to visually prove "Ultra Soft". Show the "Removable Cover" feature being unzipped (huge selling point for hygiene-conscious Germans).
3. **Add a Visual Hook:** Start with the dog jumping *into* the bed or a funny movement, rather than just sitting there.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't use English VO alone:** Unless you add German subtitles, you alienate the majority of the local audience.
2. **Don't ignore features:** You mentioned "Washable" and "Non-Slip" in the product title but *not* in the video. These are critical practical benefits.
3. **Don't make it a slide show:** Ensure there is video motion, not just static frames.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Texture:** Create a version focused solely on the sound of the fabric and the "squishiness" of the bed.
2. **"Before vs After":** Show the dog sleeping on the hard floor (sad) -> Sleeping on the cloud bed (happy).
3. **Hygiene Angle:** "Endlich ein Bett, das man waschen kann!" (Finally a bed you can wash!) - Target the cleaning pain point.
