## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Feature the Product:** The video MUST show the "All-Season Pet Sofa Bed". At minimum, the "No allowed on the bed" clip should transition to the dog happily sleeping in the *product*.
2.  **Add German Context:** For TikTok Shop DE, use German Text-to-Speech (TTS) or German captions overlaying the English audio.
3.  **Shorten & Focus:** Cut to 15-20 seconds. Use *one* funny clip as a hook, then immediately transition to the product as the solution ("Dog won't get off your bed? Get them their own.").

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on blind compilations:** Random funny clips do not sell furniture. The content must bridge to the product utility.
2.  **Don't ignore the language barrier:** Running raw English audio slang in Germany significantly reduces reach and comprehension.
3.  **Don't exceed 30s without a plot:** A 60-second disjointed compilation loses the viewer before they even realize it's an ad.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"The Solution" Angle:** Re-edit the "I allowed on the bed... No!" clip. Text overlay: "Hund will immer ins Bett?" (Dog always wants in bed?) -> Cut to Product -> Text: "Hier ist die Lösung" (Here is the solution).
2.  **ASMR/Unboxing:** Switch strategy completely to a high-quality, close-up video of the bed's texture, showing a pet stepping into it, emphasizing the "Ultra Soft" and "Warm" features mentioned in the product title.
