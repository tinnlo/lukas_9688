## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the "Before/After":** Show the pet sleeping on the floor vs. sleeping happily in the bed to heighten the problem/solution contrast.
2. **Add Text Hooks:** Place a text overlay in the first 3 seconds like "Must-have for Dog Owners!" or "50% OFF Today".
3. **Visual CTA:** Add a sticker or finger pointing down to the shop link at the end.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely solely on audio:** Many users watch with sound off. Ensure captions or text overlays convey the "Sale" message.
2. **Be too generic:** "Haustier" (Pet) is broad. If the video shows a dog, say "Dog Bed". Specificity converts better.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR:** Capture the sound of the pet settling into the "Ultra Soft" material to create a sensory experience.
2. **Durability Test:** Briefly show the "Washable Removable Cover" or "Non-Slip" feature in action (e.g., trying to push it and it stays put) to address practical buyer concerns.
