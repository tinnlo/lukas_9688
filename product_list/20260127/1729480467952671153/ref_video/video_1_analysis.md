## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Emphasize "Ready-to-Give":** The "No wrapping needed" angle is strong for male demographics. Show the box being placed in a gift bag if one is included.
2.  **Zoom on the Jewelry:** Ensure the camera macro focuses on the necklace to prove it doesn't look like cheap plastic.
3.  **Use "Valentine's" text overlay:** Ensure the first frame has text like "Das perfekte Geschenk" (The perfect gift) to hook users watching without sound.

**DON'T (避免做法):**
1.  **Don't fumble the opening:** The box opening must be smooth. If it gets stuck, retake the shot. Clumsiness kills the "high quality" vibe.
2.  **Don't ignore the music:** Ensure the music swells/peaks at the moment the box opens.
3.  **Don't over-promise:** Don't claim the jewelry is "diamond" or "gold" if it's alloy/crystal. Returns will destroy profit.

**OPPORTUNITY (潜在机会):**
1.  **Reaction Video:** Split-screen or cut to a shot of a woman *actually receiving* the box and looking surprised. This adds emotional social proof.
2.  **Price Anchor:** Mention the price if it's a bargain (e.g., "Unter 20€?"). If it looks expensive but is cheap, say it.
3.  **Bundle Deal:** Suggest buying two (one for Mom, one for GF) to increase Average Order Value (AOV).
