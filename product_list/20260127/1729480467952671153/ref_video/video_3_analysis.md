**DO | 建议:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** Place "Perfektes Geschenk für Sie" (Perfect gift for her) or "Alles Inklusive" (All inclusive) on screen to reinforce the audio.
2.  **Improve the Hook:** Instead of "I found something," start with the *action* of opening the box to catch attention immediately. "Kennt ihr schon diese Geschenkbox?" (Do you know this gift box?).
3.  **Clarify CTA:** Add a sticker pointing to the shopping cart icon at the end to compensate for the garbled audio.

**DON'T | 禁忌:**
1.  **Don't rely on audio alone:** Many watch without sound. Without text, the "necklace included" point might be missed if the shot is brief.
2.  **Avoid "dead air" visuals:** Ensure every second shows product movement. Don't linger on the closed box too long.

**OPPORTUNITY | 机会:**
1.  **Gift Giving POV:** Film from the perspective of *giving* it to someone (even staged). Reaction shots drive higher conversion than just product shots.
2.  **ASMR Angle:** The magnetic closure of these boxes usually makes a satisfying "click". Amplify that sound and cut the talking for a "Satisfying Unboxing" variant.
