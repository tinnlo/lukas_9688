## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO | 建议做:**
1.  **Increase Duration:** Extend video to at least 7-10 seconds.
    **增加时长**：将视频延长至至少7-10秒。
2.  **Show "Unboxing":** Film hands opening the double doors to reveal the rose and drawer. Movement creates engagement.
    **展示“开箱”**：拍摄双手打开双门展示玫瑰和抽屉的过程。动态创造互动。
3.  **Add German Text Overlay:** Use phrases like "Das perfekte Geschenk" (The perfect gift) or "Für deine Liebste" (For your loved one).
    **添加德语文字叠加**：使用诸如“完美礼物”或“送给你的挚爱”等短语。

**DON'T | 建议不做:**
1.  **Post Static Images:** TikTok is a video platform. Slideshows perform poorly compared to video.
    **发布静态图片**：TikTok是视频平台。幻灯片的表现远不如视频。
2.  **Ignore Audio:** Never post without trending audio or a voiceover explanation.
    **忽视音频**：发布时绝不能没有热门音频或旁白解说。
3.  **Rely on "Implied" Value:** Don't assume users know the jewelry is included; show it being taken out of the drawer.
    **依赖“暗示”价值**：不要假设用户知道包含首饰；要展示从抽屉中取出的动作。

**OPPORTUNITY | 市场机会:**
1.  **Emotional Angle:** Film a reaction video (POV of someone receiving/opening it). "POV: Du hast das perfekte Geschenk für sie gefunden."
    **情感角度**：拍摄反应视频（某人收到/打开它的主观视角）。“POV：你为她找到了完美的礼物。”
2.  **ASMR Unboxing:** Focus on the sound of the box opening and the texture of the materials.
    **ASMR开箱**：专注于盒子打开的声音和材质的质感。
3.  **Valentine's/Mother's Day Prep:** Frame it specifically for upcoming holidays with a countdown.
    **情人节/母亲节预热**：针对即将到来的节日，用倒计时的方式进行包装。
