## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show a Human Element:** Have someone *receive* the gift. The reaction (smile, surprise) sells the "Gift" angle better than the product itself. (展示真人互动：展示某人收到礼物的反应——微笑、惊喜，这比产品本身更能推销“礼物”的概念。)
2.  **Add German Context:** Use a text overlay like "Das perfekte Geschenk für Mama" (The perfect gift for Mom). (添加德语语境：使用字幕如“给妈妈的完美礼物”。)
3.  **Extend Duration & Narrative:** Make it 15-20s. Show the box, the unboxing, inserting the batteries (if needed), and turning it on. (延长时长和叙事：增加到15-20秒。展示包装盒、开箱、装电池以及开灯的过程。)

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Silence:** Silence is a conversion killer. Use a trending German audio or a voiceover explaining the features. (不要依赖静音：静音是转化率杀手。使用热门德语音乐或旁白解释功能。)
2.  **Use Static Shots Only:** Don't just stare at the product. Move the camera around to show the 3D depth of the glass dome. (不要只用静止镜头：不要只盯着产品看。移动相机展示玻璃罩的3D深度。)

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"POV: You found the best gift":** Use a POV angle to create a relatable story. (POV视角：“你找到了最好的礼物”。)
2.  **Night Light Angle:** Market it as a romantic night light for the bedroom, not just a shelf ornament. (夜灯角度：将其作为卧室浪漫夜灯进行营销，而不仅仅是架子上的装饰品。)
