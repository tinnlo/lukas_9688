## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

### Angle 1: The "German Efficiency" / Cost Saver | 角度一：“德式高效”/省钱专家
*   **Concept:** Focus purely on the **individual switches** and energy saving. Germans are very conscious of electricity prices (Strompreis).
*   **Visual Flow:** Show a meter spinning fast (stock footage) -> Show messy cables consuming standby power -> Introduce product -> Click switches off one by one -> Text: "Stop Vampire Power".
*   **Audio:** Intense music stopping abruptly when switches are clicked off.
*   **Hook (DE):** "Wusstest du, dass deine Geräte im Standby Geld fressen?" (Did you know standby devices eat money?)

### Angle 2: The Space Saver / Organizer | 角度二：空间节省/收纳达人
*   **Concept:** Before/After transformation of a specific area (Kitchen or Desk). Focus on the **short cable T-shape**.
*   **Visual Flow:** Chaotic floor with dust bunnies and long power strips -> Clean wall setup with this product. Show vacuuming the floor easily because no cables are there.
*   **Audio:** Satisfying cleanup music / ASMR.
*   **Hook (DE):** "Ich habe meine hässlichen Steckdosenleisten endlich weggeworfen." (I finally threw away my ugly power strips.)

### Angle 3: The Tech Hub / Multi-Device User | 角度三：科技中心/多设备用户
*   **Concept:** Stress test the "9-in-1" claim. Plug in EVERYTHING.
*   **Visual Flow:** Fast cuts of plugging in Laptop, iPad, Phone, Watch, Lamp, Monitor, Printer... Counter on screen counts up: 1, 2, 3... 9.
*   **Audio:** Fast paced techno beat or counting voice.
*   **Hook (DE):** "Kann dieses kleine Ding wirklich 9 Geräte gleichzeitig laden?" (Can this little thing really charge 9 devices at once?)
