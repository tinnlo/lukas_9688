## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Zoom in on the "Mess"**: Spend 1 more second clearly showing the ugly cable tangle of the old strip to heighten the pain point before showing the solution.
2.  **Show "All Ports Full"**: Visually demonstrate charging a phone, a laptop, and a lamp simultaneously to prove it handles the load.
3.  **Add Text Overlay**: Add "Vorher" (Before) and "Nachher" (After) text on screen to catch users scrolling without sound.

**DON'T (不要):**
1.  **Don't hide the product**: Ensure the product is not covered by the hand when pointing out features like the switches.
2.  **Don't be too monotone**: The voiceover is good, but a slight lift in excitement when the "perfect fit" happens would help retention.
3.  **Don't forget the price**: If it's a good deal (e.g., under €20), mention "Für unter 20 Euro" in the hook.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR Angle**: The clicking of the individual switches could be used as an ASMR hook ("Click, click, click - Strom sparen").
2.  **Travel Angle**: Pitch it as a "Travel Adapter" for hotels where outlets are scarce (if the plug type allows).
3.  **Gamer Setup**: Target gamers who need to power multiple monitors/consoles without cable drag.
