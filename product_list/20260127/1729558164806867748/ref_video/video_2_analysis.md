## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Zoom in on the USB-C port:** Show a cable actually plugging in. Visual confirmation of "Type-C" is crucial for modern phone users.
2.  **Show "Before & After":** Start with a chaotic mess of cables/power strips, then snap fingers to show the clean single wall adapter.
3.  **Mention specific devices:** Say "Charges your iPhone 15 and Apple Watch at the same time." Specificity drives sales.

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't hide the wall connection:** Users fear it might wobble or fall out. Show it plugging in firmly.
2.  **Don't overlook the "White Noise":** Ensure the audio is crisp. Background hiss lowers perceived value.
3.  **Don't be vague on power:** If it supports 2500W, say "Works for Hairdryers" (if true) or "Kitchen appliances". Give a concrete heavy-duty use case.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **Travel Angle:** Position it as the "Only adapter you need for your hotel room."
2.  **Gaming Setup:** Target gamers who need to power a monitor + charge controllers/headsets.
3.  **Kitchen Organizer:** Show it in a kitchen powering a toaster + coffee maker + charging a recipe tablet.
