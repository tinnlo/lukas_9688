## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Overlay Text:** Add text overlays for key features ("5x AC", "1x USB-C", "Energy Saving") to catch scrollers without sound.
2.  **Show the Mess:** Start with a "Before" shot of a messy cable tangle, then show the "After" with this neat strip. Visual contrast sells better.
3.  **Mention Energy Cost:** Explicitly mention "Save electricity bill" (Stromrechnung sparen) – this is a huge trigger in Germany.

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't Rush the CTA:** The ending transcript seems slightly garbled/rushed. Give the CTA 2 clear seconds of silence or text on screen.
2.  **Avoid Dark Footage:** Ensure the black/white product stands out against the background.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The clicking of the switches could be used for an ASMR-style hook ("Satisfying Click").
2.  **Travel/Desk Setup:** Frame it as a "Must-have for your Gaming Setup" or "Home Office Upgrade" to target specific niches.
