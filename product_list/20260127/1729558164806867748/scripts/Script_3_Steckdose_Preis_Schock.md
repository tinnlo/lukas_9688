## Scripts

Price shock / inflation fighter angle: anchors against retail store prices, then reveals TikTok deal. Germans love "Preis-Leistung" (value for money). Taps into distrust of big retail markup.

### Structure (40s)
- Hook: Retail receipt / price comparison provocation (0-3s)
- Anchor: What you'd pay at MediaMarkt / Saturn (3-8s)
- Reveal: TikTok price for MORE features (8-14s)
- Feature demo: 9-in-1 walkthrough (14-26s)
- Quality proof: Build, safety, fit (26-33s)
- CTA: Grab the deal (33-40s)
