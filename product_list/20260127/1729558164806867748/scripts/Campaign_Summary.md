- **Strom Sparen (省电)** – Germans are hyper-aware of electricity costs (Strompreis). Individual switches eliminate standby drain without unplugging. The click sound is satisfying ASMR content (德国人高度关注电费，独立开关消除待机耗电，咔哒声是ASMR内容).
- **Preis Schock (价格冲击)** – Anchors against €20+ retail prices for inferior products with fewer features. Germans love "Preis-Leistung" (value for money). 9-in-1 vs basic 3-port strips (锚定零售€20+高价，9合1 vs 基础3口插排).
