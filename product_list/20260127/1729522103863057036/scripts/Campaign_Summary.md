**Why these angles work (为什么这些角度有效):**
- **Saisonwechsel (换季收纳)** – January/February is peak seasonal storage time in Germany. Winter clothes need to go somewhere. The 3-pack = one purchase solves the entire wardrobe. Appeals to "Ordnung muss sein" mindset (1-2月是德国换季收纳高峰期，三件套一次解决整个衣柜问题).
- **Kleine Wohnung (小公寓)** – German apartments are notoriously small, especially in cities (Munich, Berlin, Hamburg). Under-bed and on-wardrobe dead space is untapped. Before/after transformation is inherently satisfying (德国公寓出了名的小，床下和衣柜顶部的空间未被利用，前后对比本身就很吸引人).
- **Kapazitäts-Test (容量挑战)** – "Does it really fit?" is the #1 skeptic question for storage products. Counting items as they go in creates tension and engagement. The zipper closing is the payoff moment ("真的装得下吗？"是收纳产品的首要疑问，逐件计数创造悬念，拉链合上是爽点).

3. **Reinforced Handles (加固把手)** – Double-stitched handles support full load. **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Overcomes "Will it rip?" fear with visible proof (用可见的双重缝线克服"会不会撕裂"的恐惧).

4. **Transparent Window (透明视窗)** – See contents without opening. **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Solves "Where did I put it?" — the eternal storage problem (解决"我把它放哪了？"——永恒的收纳难题).

5. **Stackable Design (可堆叠设计)** – Boxes stack securely when filled. **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Maximizes vertical space — critical for small apartments (最大化垂直空间——对小公寓至关重要).

## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

*From image_analysis.md & video_synthesis.md (来自图片分析和视频综合分析):*

- **Primary Visual (主要视觉):** The "messy pile → packed box → clean room" transformation — must be dramatic and authentic (凌乱堆→装箱→干净房间的变身——必须戏剧性且真实)
- **Lighting (灯光):** Bright, natural window light. "Clean = bright." Avoid dark or yellow lighting — it makes rooms feel dirty (明亮自然光。"干净=明亮"。避免暗光或黄光)
- **Props Needed (所需道具):**
  - 10-15 bulky winter items: jackets, sweaters, scarves, duvets (10-15件大件冬季物品)
  - Authentically messy room setup — not staged dirty, but lived-in messy (真实凌乱的房间——不是刻意弄脏，而是生活中的凌乱)
  - Clean floor and bed for "after" shots (干净的地板和床用于"后"镜头)
- **Audio (音频):** Record zipper sound separately with close mic — the smooth zip is a key satisfaction moment (单独用近距离麦克风录制拉链声——顺滑的拉链声是关键满足感时刻)

**Filming Tips from synthesis (来自综合分析的拍摄技巧):**
- Speed ramp: 4x during packing (boring part), 0.5x for zipper closing (satisfying part) (变速：打包时4x加速，拉链合上时0.5x慢放)
- Film in a real German apartment setting — Wooden floors, typical windows. No US-style rooms (在真实德国公寓中拍摄)
- The "handle lift test" must look effortless — if the handle strains, don't use the shot (把手提拉测试必须看起来轻松)

**Compliance Reminders (合规提醒):**
- Do NOT claim "waterproof" — only "moisture-resistant" at best (不要声称"防水"——最多"防潮")
- Do NOT overload with heavy non-textile items (bricks, books) (不要用重物超载)
- Do NOT claim "odorless" unless confirmed (不要声称"无味"除非确认)
- Not machine washable — wipe clean only (不可机洗——仅可擦拭清洁)
