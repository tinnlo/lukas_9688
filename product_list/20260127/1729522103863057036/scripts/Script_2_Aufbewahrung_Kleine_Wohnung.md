## Scripts

Small apartment / space hack angle: targets students, renters, and anyone in a compact German apartment. Focuses on using dead space (under bed, top of wardrobe) to reclaim living area. Before/after room transformation.

### Structure (40s)
- Hook: Messy small room — can't walk (0-3s)
- Problem: No closet space, stuff everywhere (3-7s)
- Solution: Storage box introduction (7-12s)
- Demo: Packing + placing in dead spaces (12-24s)
- Transformation: Room before vs. after (24-32s)
- CTA: Get the 3-pack (32-40s)
