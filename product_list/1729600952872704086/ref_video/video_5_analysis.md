## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO:**
1.  **Test "ASMR" Angles:** Record the *hissing* sound of the steam loudly. CleanTok loves audio satisfaction.
2.  **Focus on "Nicotine/Grease" Tests:** Show yellow grease dripping off an extractor hood. This is visually shocking and high-converting.
3.  **Mention Money Saving:** "Save money on chemical cleaners" (Keine Chemie mehr kaufen).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Over-Polish:** Keep the "messy" before shots realistic. If it looks too staged, trust drops.
2.  **Don't Rush the "Before/After":** Give the viewer 1-2 seconds to really see the difference.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Car Detailing Angle:** Use the same device for car rims or interior seats to target a male demographic.
2.  **Pet Owners:** Market it as "Sanitizing pet beds without chemicals" (poison-free cleaning).
