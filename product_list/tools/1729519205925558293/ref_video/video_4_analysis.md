## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Hire a Native German Voice Actor:** Replace the TTS. The errors ("Compression", "Stolbung") are deal-breakers for German buyers who expect technical accuracy.
2.  **Show "Before/After":** Show a brush-painted surface (streaky) vs. the spray-painted surface (smooth) side-by-side.
3.  **Localize the Battery Brand:** Explicitly text overlay "Passend für Makita 18V" (Suitable for Makita 18V) to avoid trademark issues while confirming compatibility.

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't rely on bad translation:** "Kilderjörpaket" is gibberish. Use "Zubehör-Set" or "Gratis Düsen-Set".
2.  **Don't ignore safety:** Ensure the person in the video is wearing a mask. Germans are safety-conscious; spraying without a mask looks unprofessional/unsafe.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Create a version focused purely on the sounds: Battery click, motor hum, spray hiss. No talking, just satisfying sounds and visual finish.
2.  **"Restoration" Niche:** Target the "Furniture Flipping" community specifically, rather than general "Industrial/Car" use. "Turn this old chair into €100 with this tool."
