## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add German Subtitles (添加德语字幕):** This is critical. You are selling in Germany but speaking Arabic. Captions would instantly expand the audience to the entire country.
2.  **Shorten to 15-20 Seconds (缩短视频):** 84 seconds is too long for a simple paint demo. Speed up the painting process (2x or 4x speed) to keep energy high.
3.  **Visual CTA (视觉引导):** Add a sticker or text overlay at the end pointing to the profile picture to click the link.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on Voiceover alone (不要仅依赖旁白):** Visuals must tell the story independently so users watching on mute (or non-Arabic speakers) still understand.
2.  **Don't hide the "Before" (不要忽略对比):** Ensure the "dirty/old" state is clearly shown for longer than a split second to establish the problem.
3.  **Don't Film in Windy Audio (避免风噪):** The outdoor audio can be distracting. Use a lapel mic or do a voiceover in post-production.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle (ASMR视角):** Remove the talking. Amplify the "Hiss" of the spray gun and the visual satisfaction of the paint covering the surface. This transcends language barriers completely.
2.  **"Makita Hack" (牧田电池Hack):** Explicitly market this as "Don't buy the expensive brand, buy this body and use your existing battery." This appeals to the value-conscious German consumer ("Sparfuchs").
3.  **German Text-to-Speech (德语TTS):** Keep the same video, but dub it with a German AI voiceover explaining the benefits. This allows one video to serve two distinct audiences.
