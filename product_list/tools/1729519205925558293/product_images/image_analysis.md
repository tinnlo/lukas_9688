## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

### Angle 1: The "Makita Owner" Pitch (Targeting Existing Ecosystem)
*   **Concept:** Focus 100% on the battery compatibility.
    *   DE: Hast du diese blauen Akkus zu Hause? Dann brauchst du dieses Tool. Mach aus deinem Bohrer-Akku eine Lackierpistole.
        ZH: 你家里有这种蓝色电池吗？那你需要这个工具。把你的电钻电池变成喷漆枪。
*   **Visual Focus:** Constant shots of swapping batteries between tools (if possible) or just clicking the battery in. `product_image_4.webp` is key here.

### Angle 2: The "Fence Savior" (Seasonal/Task Based)
*   **Concept:** Focus on the pain of painting a long garden fence by hand.
    *   DE: Zeigen Sie einen endlos langen Zaun. Pinsel? Dauert Tage. MAWIRON Sprüher? Fertig in Minuten. Der Frühling kommt!
        ZH: 展示一个无尽的长栅栏。用刷子？要好几天。MAWIRON喷枪？几分钟搞定。春天来了！
*   **Visual Focus:** `product_image_8.webp` (Fence shot), `product_image_5.webp` (1.8mm nozzle selection).

### Angle 3: The "Finish Quality" (Perfectionist)
*   **Concept:** "Why do your painted furniture look homemade? Because of brush strokes."
    *   DE: Pinselstriche ruinieren den Look. Sprühen gibt dieses "Wie neu gekauft" Finish. Glatt, professionell, perfekt.
        ZH: 刷痕毁了外观。喷涂能给出这种“像刚买的一样”的表面。光滑，专业，完美。
*   **Visual Focus:** Macro shots of smooth surfaces (`product_image_8.webp` chair), comparison shots (`product_image_4.webp`).
