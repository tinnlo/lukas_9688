## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

### Angle 1: The "Car Detailer" (Problem/Solution)
*   **Concept:** Focus entirely on the car drying aspect. This is a huge niche on TikTok ("CarTok").
*   **Visuals:** Wet car -> Blast air -> Dry car. No towels, no scratches.
*   **Text:** "Hör auf, dein Auto zu zerkratzen!" (Stop scratching your car!)
*   **Why:** High visual satisfaction, solves a specific pain point (drying is annoying), justifies the purchase for something other than just "leaves".

### Angle 2: The "Budget Makita" (Price/Comparison)
*   **Concept:** Direct comparison to big brands. "You have Makita batteries? Don't buy the €100 tool. Buy this for €XX."
*   **Visuals:** Show the battery connector clearly (`product_image_2.webp`). Show the batteries fitting perfectly.
*   **Text:** "Der beste Hack für Makita Besitzer." (The best hack for Makita owners.)
*   **Why:** Targets a massive existing user base (Makita owners) who love expanding their tool arsenal cheaply.

### Angle 3: The "Lazy Homeowner" (Lifestyle/Comedy)
*   **Concept:** Show someone trying to sweep with a broom and failing/sweating. Then switch to the blower, holding a drink in the other hand.
*   **Visuals:** Exaggerated struggle with broom vs. effortless blowing.
*   **Text:** "Warum machst du es dir so schwer?" (Why make it so hard?)
*   **Why:** Relatable, funny, broad appeal. Shows ease of use and lightweight design (`product_image_6.webp`).
