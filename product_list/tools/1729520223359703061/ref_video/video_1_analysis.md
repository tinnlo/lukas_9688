- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:** Visuals show the batteries while the audio states "comes with two batteries". This addresses a common pain point (battery life/cost).

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO:**
1.  **Start with the Action:** The first 3 seconds MUST show the leaves flying away (the demo). Cut the "batteries on table" intro.
2.  **Shorten Drastically:** Cut the video to 15-25 seconds max.
    *   0-3s: Blower clearing leaves powerfully.
    *   3-8s: "Only €33 and comes with TWO batteries!" (Overlay text).
    *   8-12s: Quick shot of Makita battery compatibility.
    *   12-15s: CTA.
3.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add "€33 | 2x Akkus | Makita Kompatibel" on screen. Many people watch without sound.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't wait 60 seconds to show the product working.** This is the biggest killer of retention.
2.  **Don't talk about specs (Amperes) before showing the result.** Emotion/Result first, logic/specs second.
3.  **Don't film in poor lighting.** Ensure the "after" shot of the terrace looks bright and pristine.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Record the sound of the leaves crunching and then the "whoosh" of the air clearing them.
2.  **Comparison:** "Expensive Makita Blower vs. This €33 Beast" – Split screen comparison.
3.  **Snow Testing:** If it's winter in Germany (Jan 2026), test it on light snow (Pulverschnee). That is a huge seasonal hook.
