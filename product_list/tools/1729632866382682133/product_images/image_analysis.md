- **Concept:** Show someone struggling with a brush painting a complex fence (slats, corners). It takes forever, dripping sweat.
- **Pivot:** Enter MAWIRON Sprayer. Click in battery. WHOOSH. Done in seconds.
- **Key Emotion:** Relief, Satisfaction, Speed.
- **DE:** "Warum hast du dich so lange gequält? Hier ist die Lösung."
- **ZH:** “你为什么折磨自己这么久？这才是解决方案。”

**Angle 2: The "Oddly Satisfying" ASMR (Visual Focus)**
- **Concept:** No talking, just music/sounds. Close-ups of the spray hitting different surfaces (wood, metal). Seeing the color change instantly and smoothly.
- **Visuals:** Slow motion of the mist. Peel reveals (masking tape removal).
- **Key Emotion:** Hypnotic, Aesthetic, Quality.
- **DE:** "Einfach nur befriedigend. Schau dir diese Oberfläche an."
- **ZH:** “简直太解压了。看看这个表面。”

**Angle 3: The Tech/Spec Nerd (Feature Focus)**
- **Concept:** Fast-paced rundown of features. "Brushless Motor? Check. 1000ml Tank? Check. Brass Nozzles? Check."
- **Visuals:** Quick cuts of each part. Exploded view (if possible) or disassembly. Viscosity test demo.
- **Key Emotion:** Smart Purchase, Value for Money, Professionalism.
- **DE:** "Profimodell zum Discounterpreis? Lass uns die Specs checken."
- **ZH:** “折扣价的专业型号？让我们检查一下规格。”
