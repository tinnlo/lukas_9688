## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Localize for German Audience:** Create a version in German language ("Grillsaison geht los!"). The Russian version limits reach to ~5% of the population.
2. **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Ensure the "blowing coals" action is visually explicit (sparks/ash flying) to maximize the "Oddly Satisfying" ASMR factor.
3. **Price Math Visual:** Add a text overlay: "❌ €40 Normal" vs "✅ €12 Now".

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Over-polish:** A studio-quality ad would make the €12 price point seem suspicious (scammy). Keep it raw.
2. **Don't Forget the Link:** Ensure the product card is pinned immediately.
3. **Don't Use Generic Music:** The voiceover is the key here; background music should be very low or non-existent to keep focus on the "Advice".

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Car Drying Niche:** The same product is viral for drying cars after a wash. Test a "Car Detailing" angle ("Nie wieder Wasserflecken" - Never water spots again).
2. **Dusting PC/Tech:** Test an angle for cleaning computer towers ("Besser als Druckluft" - Better than compressed air).
3. **Bundle Deal:** If the shop allows, upsell a spare battery immediately.
