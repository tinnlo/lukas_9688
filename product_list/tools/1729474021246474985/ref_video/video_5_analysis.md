## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Voiceover:** Use a TTS or real voice saying "Endlich Schluss mit dem Laub-Chaos!" (Finally an end to the leaf chaos!).
2. **Shorten Drastically:** Cut the video to 15-20 seconds. 81 seconds is fatal for a simple product.
3. **Start with Action:** The first frame should be a pile of leaves *exploding* away, not the battery.

**DON'T:**
1. **Silence/Music Only:** Never run ads in Germany without a localized audio hook.
2. **Static Shots:** Avoid showing the product sitting on a table; show it solving the problem.
3. **Generic Text:** Don't rely on the caption. Put "50% RABATT" (50% Off) in big text on the video.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the *sound* and *visual satisfaction* of clearing a perfectly square line on a dirty patio.
2. **"Not Just Leaves":** Market it as a "Auto-Trockner" (Car dryer) or "Schnee-Bläser" (Snow blower) for winter, which is more relevant in January/February than leaves.
3. **Price Anchor:** Show a competitor at €100 vs this one at "Half Price" to trigger the bargain hunter instinct.
