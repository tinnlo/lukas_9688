### Angle 1: The Seasonal Tire Change Hero | 季节性换胎英雄

- **Hook**: "Willst du deine Reifen wirklich noch von Hand wechseln?" (Do you really still want to change tires by hand?)
- **Flow**: Show sweating/struggling with a cross wrench -> Cut to MAWIRON zipping nuts off in seconds -> Show the happy user finishing early.
- **Key Feature**: High torque, Auto-stop mode.

### Angle 2: The All-Rounder DIY Kit | 全能DIY套装
- **Concept**: Highlight the "Kit" aspect. Unboxing style showing all the bits, drills, and the battery.
- **Hook**: "Alles was du brauchst in einer Box." (Everything you need in one box.)
- **Flow**: Unbox -> Show the battery -> Show drilling wood -> Show drilling metal -> Show driving screws. Emphasize value for money.
- **Key Feature**: Versatility, included accessories, Makita compatibility.

### Angle 3: The "Pro Features, Budget Price" | 专业功能，亲民价格
- **Concept**: Tech-heavy approach. Focus on the brushless motor, speed settings, and build quality, comparing it (visually) to expensive brands without naming them.
- **Hook**: "Warum 200€ bezahlen, wenn du das hier haben kannst?" (Why pay 200€ when you can have this?)
- **Flow**: Close-ups of the motor (Image 2 style) -> Demonstrate the precision control panel -> Show heavy-duty usage -> Reveal price point.
- **Key Feature**: Cost-performance ratio, technical specs (RPM, cooling).
