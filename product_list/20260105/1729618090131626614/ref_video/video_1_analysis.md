## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** Replace French VO with German TTS/VO: *"Nachbar kratzt noch, ich bin schon weg!"* (Neighbor still scraping, I'm already gone!).
2.  **Enhance Sound:** Add a crisp "ASMR" sound effect when peeling the cover to increase satisfaction.
3.  **Show "Magnetic" Snap:** Briefly show the magnets snapping onto the car frame to prove easy installation.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on French audio** for the German market; it creates a disconnect.
2.  **Don't exceed 15s:** The value prop is simple; don't over-explain.
3.  **Don't show complex setup:** Keep the focus on the *removal* (the result), not the *putting on* (the effort).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"POV" Angle:** Film from *inside* the car—pulling the cover off to reveal the world.
2.  **Gift Angle:** Position as the "Best Winter Gift for Dad/Mom".
3.  **Wind Test:** Show the cover staying put in high wind (blower) to address quality concerns.
