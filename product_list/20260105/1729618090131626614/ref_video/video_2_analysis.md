## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Localize for Germany:** Dub this exact video into German ("Schaut euch das an...", "Der Retter für den Winter").
2. **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text overlays (e.g., "Magnetische Befestigung", "Kein Eiskratzen mehr") to compensate for the French audio if you cannot dub it.
3. **Show the "Peel":** Ensure the "removing the cover to reveal perfectly clear glass" shot is the climax of the video.

**DON'T:**
1. **Run French Audio in Germany:** Unless specifically targeting expats, this will kill engagement. TikTok algorithms penalize language mismatches.
2. **Hide the Magnets:** The "Magnetic" feature is the USP (Unique Selling Proposition) against cheap plastic covers. Show the snap!
3. **Make it too long:** Keep it under 15-20 seconds. This is an impulse buy.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus purely on the sounds – the snow crunching, the cover rustling, the magnets clicking – with no voiceover, just text. This transcends language barriers.
2. **Comparison Split Screen:** Show "Neighbor scraping ice for 20 mins" vs "You removing cover in 2 seconds".
3. **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for your husband/dad" (common angle for car gadgets in Q4).
