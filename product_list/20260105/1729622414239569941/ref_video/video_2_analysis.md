## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Show it in Action:** For car polishers, you MUST show a "Before/After" of a scratched hood becoming shiny. Talking about the box isn't enough.
2.  **Evergreen Hooks:** Instead of "Christmas Gift," use "Stop paying €200 for detailing" or "Fix scratches in 5 minutes."
3.  **ASMR Elements:** The sound of the polisher or the peeling of a protective film adds sensory satisfaction.

**DON'T (不要):**
1.  **Date-Stamp Content:** Don't say "Christmas" unless you plan to delete the ad on Dec 26. It kills the video's shelf life.
2.  **Ignore Lighting:** Detailed tools need good lighting to show build quality.
3.  **Repeat Too Much:** The script said "2 days" and "2 batteries" three times in 20 seconds. Use that time to show features (RPM, weight, noise level).

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **"Satisfying" Niche:** Polishing videos can be oddly satisfying. A timelapse of a dirty car becoming clean can go viral beyond just the sales pitch.
2.  **Price Comparison:** Visually compare this €34 tool vs a €200 brand name tool (Makita) since it uses compatible batteries. "Why pay more?"
3.  **Battery Hack:** Emphasize that the batteries work with other tools (if true) or that the tool works with existing Makita batteries users might own.
