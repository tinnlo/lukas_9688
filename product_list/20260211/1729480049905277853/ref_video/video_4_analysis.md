## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1. **Show the Cat:** Use a cat in the FIRST second to hook pet lovers instantly. | **展示猫咪:** 在第一秒就展示猫咪使用产品的画面，瞬间钩住宠物主。
2. **Text Overlays:** Add "Winter Must-Have" or "2-in-1" text to highlight value without sound. | **文字叠加:** 添加“冬季必备”或“二合一”文字，即便静音也能传达价值。
3. **Dynamic Cuts:** Use faster cuts between the tunnel, the toy, and the bed to increase energy. | **动态剪辑:** 在隧道、玩具和猫窝镜头间切换得更快些，提升视频节奏。

**DON'T:**
1. **Static Angles:** Don't linger too long on the same grey plush texture; it becomes visually boring. | **单一角度:** 不要在一个灰色的毛绒纹理上停留太久，会产生视觉疲劳。
2. **Generic Hook:** Avoid just saying "I got this"; start with the *benefit* (e.g., "Your cat will never be bored again"). | **平淡开场:** 避免只说“我买了这”；应从益处开始（如“让你的猫再也不无聊”）。
3. **Muffled Audio:** Ensure the VO is recorded close to the mic to avoid room echo. | **音频模糊:** 确保旁白录制时靠近麦克风，避免房间回声。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Comparison:** Show how this replaces a separate tunnel and a separate bed to emphasize space-saving. | **对比展示:** 展示此产品如何替代独立的隧道和猫窝，强调节省空间。
2. **ASMR:** Record the "crinkle" sound of the tunnel or the soft "squish" of the bed for sensory appeal. | **感官诱惑:** 录制隧道的“沙沙”声或猫窝的“蓬松”挤压声（ASMR），增加感官吸引力。
3. **The "Foldable" Angle:** Demonstrate how easily it folds away for storage—a key pain point for small apartments. | **“可折叠”卖点:** 演示它如何轻松折叠收纳，直击小公寓住户的痛点。
