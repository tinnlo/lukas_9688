## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add German Text Overlays:** Add text like "Perfekt für den Winter" (Perfect for winter) or "Deine Katze wird es lieben" (Your cat will love it) to contextualize for the German audience.
2.  **Clearer CTA:** End with a text overlay "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) or an arrow pointing to the shop link.
3.  **Show "Before/After":** Show a "bored cat" vs. "happy cat in tunnel" to create a micro-story.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Fragmented Audio:** Use a clear trending song or a proper TTS voiceover (German) explaining the benefits. The current audio ("You're not") is confusing.
2.  **Ignore the "2-in-1" Feature:** Ensure the folding mechanism is clearly demonstrated, not just static shots.
3.  **Leave it uncaptioned:** A 10s video without text is easily scrolled past if the audio isn't engaging.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the crinkly tunnel material (if applicable) or the softness of the plush for a sensory hook.
2.  **"Gift for Your Cat" Angle:** Position it as a necessary winter upgrade or a holiday gift.
3.  **Reply to Comment:** Use this video format to answer a specific question like "Is it washable?" to drive higher engagement.
