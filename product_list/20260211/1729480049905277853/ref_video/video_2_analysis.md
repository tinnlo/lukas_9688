## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a Verbal CTA:** Even in 10s, add a voiceover or text: "Cat loves this!" or "Link in bio" / "Hol dir das" (Get this).
2.  **Localize for Germany:** Change "Thank you" to "Danke" or "Meine Katze liebt es" (My cat loves it).
3.  **Highlight the "Problem":** Show a "Before" (Bored cat) vs "After" (Cat in tunnel) split screen.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on "Thank you":** Unless you are a big brand replying to a specific viral comment, this context is lost on cold traffic.
2.  **Don't leave the product ambiguous:** Ensure the "Tunnel" function is clearly demonstrated, not just the bed.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Speed Up:** A 10s video can include 5-6 fast cuts to high-energy music to make it feel more dynamic.
2.  **Price Anchor:** Add a text overlay "Under €20" (if applicable) to trigger impulse buys for this cheap accessory.
