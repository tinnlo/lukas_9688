## 3. Hook/Opening Strategy (First 3 Seconds) | 开场策略(前3秒)

- **Hook Type | 钩子类型:** Problem-Solution (Question based) | 问题-解决方案 (提问式)
- **Hook Description | 策略描述:** The VO immediately addresses a common pain point ("Do you need a grinder? Is yours broken?") targeting households that cook with spices frequently. | 旁白直接切入痛点（“你需要研磨机吗？你的坏了吗？”），精准定位经常烹饪香料的家庭。
- **Visual + Audio Combination | 视觉+音频组合:** The audio poses a direct question while visuals likely display the shiny new product or raw spices, creating immediate context. | 音频提出直接问题，同时视觉展示崭新的产品或原材料，立即建立场景。
- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** **Moderate/High for Niche.** For a Hindi-speaking audience in Germany, hearing their native language is a massive pattern interrupt. For non-Hindi speakers, it is confusing and likely ineffective. | **针对利基市场有效。** 对于德国的印地语受众，听到母语是巨大的“模式中断”信号。但对于非印地语受众，这会造成困惑，效果极差。
