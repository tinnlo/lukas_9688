## Scripts

**Concept:** Family Protection angle targeting parents and pet owners. Opens with accident scenario, demonstrates protection from spills, proves washability, ends with peace of mind message.

### Structure (40s)
- Hook (0-3s): Simulated spill accident
- Problem (3-8s): Expensive mattress damage concern
- Solution Demo (8-25s): Show protection and easy cleanup
- Washing Proof (25-35s): Machine washable convenience
- CTA (35-40s): Link
