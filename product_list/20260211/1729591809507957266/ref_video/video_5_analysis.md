## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Localize Audio:** Use German (DE) voiceover or high-quality AI Dubbing.
2. **Text Overlays:** Add German captions to highlight "Wasserdicht" and "Atmungsaktiv".
3. **Show the Benefit:** Show the dry mattress *after* the water test to complete the logic.

**DON'T:**
1. **Language Mismatch:** Never run ads in a foreign language without subtitles in the target market.
2. **Generic Hooks:** Avoid being too "salesy" too early; stick to the "Problem" hook used here.
3. **Low Contrast:** Ensure the water used in the test is colored (e.g., blue) for better visibility.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Comparison:** Show a "Standard Cover" vs. "This Waterproof Cover".
2. **Pet Angle:** Create a version specifically for pet owners (muddy paws/accidents).
3. **Bundle Offer:** Highlight the "Pillowcases Included" more prominently in the first 5 seconds.
