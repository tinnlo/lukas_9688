## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1. **Add German Subtitles:** Even if the VO is Arabic, adding German subs would capture the 90%+ of the local market who don't speak Arabic.
2. **Start with the "Water Test":** Pouring a cup of colored juice on the protector in the first 1 second is a proven high-retention hook for this product.
3. **Show the "Underneath":** After pouring water, immediately lift the protector to show the bone-dry mattress beneath.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't wait 72 seconds:** Most TikTok users decide to stay or leave in 3 seconds. The core value must be shown much faster.
2. **Avoid "Talking Head" starts:** Unless you are a major celebrity, start with the product action, not your face.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Comparison Video:** Compare this waterproof version with a "standard" sheet to show the damage a spill does to a normal bed.
2. **Bilingual Strategy:** Use a "German text + Arabic VO" or vice versa to maximize reach in the German market while keeping the niche trust.
