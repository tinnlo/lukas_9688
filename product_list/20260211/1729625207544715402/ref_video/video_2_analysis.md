## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show the "Spin":** Ensure the first second shows the wheel spinning to grab visual attention.
2.  **Sound Design:** Add a satisfying "whirring" or "bell" sound effect if the toy makes noise.
3.  **Localize:** Keep using words like "Wohnung" (apartment) for the German market, as many live in flats.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Fake the cat:** Don't just show the product alone; the cat *must* interact with it.
2.  **Over-clutter:** Keep the background clean (as per the "chic" claim).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** If the ball has a bell or food dispenser sound, amplify it.
2.  **Furniture Contrast:** Show a split screen: "Scratched Sofa" vs "Happy Cat with Wheel".
3.  **Gift Angle:** "Perfect gift for your cat's birthday" (emotional hook).
