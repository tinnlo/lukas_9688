## Scripts

**Concept:** Skeptic/test-driven angle targeting pragmatic German buyers who distrust cheap products. Opens with waterproof claim challenge, demonstrates with aggressive water test (bucket pour), proves interior stays dry with tissue test.

### Structure (35s)
- Hook (0-3s): "Really waterproof?" skeptical question
- Test Setup (3-10s): Place dry tissue inside, close dome
- Aggressive Test (10-22s): Pour bucket of water over house
- Reveal (22-30s): Open dome, show dry tissue
- CTA (30-35s): Link with quality message
