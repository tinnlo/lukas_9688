## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (执行建议):**
1. **Show a Real Cat using it:** If the current video only shows the house, the conversion rate drops. Seeing a cat *inside* is the ultimate social proof. (展示真猫使用的画面)
2. **Add Text Overlays:** Ensure "Wasserdicht" (Waterproof) and "Isoliert" (Insulated) appear as big text on screen during those segments. (添加关键卖点字幕)
3. **Price Anchor:** If the price is good, mention it. "Cheaper than a vet visit for a sick cat." (价格锚定：比带猫看病便宜)

**DON'T (避免事项):**
1. **Don't fake the weather:** Filming a "winter" product on a sunny green lawn kills the vibe. Film on a cloudy day or wet pavement. (不要在晴天拍摄冬日产品)
2. **Don't ignore the assembly:** If it looks hard to build, people won't buy. Show a 1-second "Easy Pop-up" clip if applicable. (不要忽略组装难易度)

**OPPORTUNITY (增长机会):**
1. **"Stray Cat" Angle:** Pivot the messaging to helping neighborhood stray cats ("Streuner") survive winter. This often goes viral for charity/goodwill reasons. (流浪猫救助角度)
2. **Thermal Camera:** Use a thermal camera effect (fake or real) to show the heat retention inside vs. outside. (热成像对比视觉效果)
3. **Sound ASMR:** Emphasize the sound of rain hitting the roof while the inside stays silent and dry. (雨声ASMR对比)
