## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Voiceover (TTS or Human):** Explain the problem ("Is your cat freezing?") and the solution immediately. Use German TTS like "Werner" or "Lisa". | **增加旁白（TTS或人声）：** 立即解释问题（“你的猫冷吗？”）和解决方案。使用德语TTS。
2. **Show the "Underground" Benefit:** Film the digging and placement process specifically. This is a unique selling point (USP). | **展示“地下”优势：** 专门拍摄挖掘和放置过程。这是独特的卖点（USP）。
3. **Use Text Overlays:** Add "100% Wasserdicht" (100% Waterproof) and "-20°C Schutz" (Protection) on screen. | **使用文字叠加：** 在屏幕上添加“100%防水”和“-20°C防护”字样。

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** Silence kills retention on TikTok. | **不要依赖静音：** 静音是TikTok完播率的杀手。
2. **Show Empty Boxes Only:** A cat house without a cat is just a box. Use a treat to lure a cat in if needed. | **不要只展示空盒子：** 没有猫的猫屋只是个盒子。如有需要，用零食引诱猫进去。
3. **Ignore Weather Context:** Don't film on a sunny, warm day if the selling point is "winter protection". Film in rain or add rain effects. | **不要忽视天气背景：** 如果卖点是“冬季防护”，不要在温暖晴朗的日子拍摄。在雨中拍摄或添加雨效。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Stray Cat Story":** Frame it as "I built a home for the neighborhood stray". This emotional angle often goes viral. | **“流浪猫故事”：** 包装成“我为附近的流浪猫建了个家”。这种情感角度通常容易爆火。
2. **ASMR Unboxing/Build:** Focus on the sounds of the sturdy materials and the digging. | **ASMR开箱/搭建：** 专注于坚固材质的声音和挖掘声。
3. **Comparison:** Show a wet cardboard box vs. this dry underground house. | **对比：** 展示湿透的纸箱与这个干燥的地下屋的对比。
