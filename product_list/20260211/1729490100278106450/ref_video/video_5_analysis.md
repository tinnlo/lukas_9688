## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Extend Duration:** Increase video length to at least 7-10 seconds to allow the algorithm to categorize it and users to look at it. | **延长时长：** 将视频长度增加到至少7-10秒，以便算法对其分类且让用户有时间观看。
2. **Show Usage:** A cat scratching post *needs* a cat scratching it. Prove stability and engagement. | **展示使用场景：** 猫抓柱*需要*展示猫咪抓挠的画面。证明其稳定性和吸引力。
3. **Add German Text Overlay:** Use hooks like "Katzen lieben es!" (Cats love it!) or "Endlich stabile Qualität" (Finally stable quality). | **添加德语文字叠加：** 使用如“Katzen lieben es!”（猫咪超爱！）或“Endlich stabile Qualität”（终于有了稳定的质量）等钩子。

**DON'T:**
1. **Upload Static Images:** TikTok is a video platform. Slideshows perform poorly compared to motion video. | **上传静态图片：** TikTok是视频平台。幻灯片的表现远不如动态视频。
2. **Ignore Sound:** Even simple ASMR of scratching sounds would boost engagement significantly. | **忽视声音：** 即使是简单的抓挠ASMR声音也能显著提升互动。
3. **Forget Scale:** Without a cat or furniture nearby, the size of the post is ambiguous. | **忘记比例：** 如果旁边没有猫或家具，抓柱的尺寸不仅模糊不清。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the cat scratching the wood/sisal. Very satisfying and native to TikTok. | **ASMR角度：** 聚焦猫咪抓挠木头/剑麻的声音。非常解压且符合TikTok原生风格。
2. **"Furniture Protection" Angle:** Show a destroyed sofa vs. the new scratching post. "Rette dein Sofa" (Save your sofa). | **“家具保护”角度：** 展示被毁的沙发与新抓柱的对比。“Rette dein Sofa”（拯救你的沙发）。
3. **Assembly Speed:** Show a timelapse of putting it together in 5 seconds. Germans love efficiency. | **组装速度：** 展示5秒内的组装延时摄影。德国人喜欢高效率。
