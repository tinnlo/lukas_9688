## 1. Product Overview | 产品概述

**What we're selling (我们在卖什么):**  
A minimalist wooden cat scratching post designed for German cat owners who value quality (质量), aesthetics (美学), and sustainability (可持续性). Unlike cheap cardboard or plush cat trees, this product uses solid wood construction with a replaceable sisal scratching pad—transforming it from a "disposable toy" to a "long-term furniture investment."

**Core differentiator (核心差异化):**  
The **replaceable scratching pad** is the hero feature. German market psychology shows strong rejection of *Wegwerfgesellschaft* (throwaway culture). This feature alone addresses three pain points: saves money (省钱), reduces waste (减少浪费), and signals quality-conscious design (优质设计).

**Key product specs (关键产品规格):**
- Natural wood base (solid, not particle board) | 天然木材底座（实木）
- Replaceable sisal mat | 可更换剑麻垫
- Space-saving vertical design | 节省空间的垂直设计
- Non-slip base | 防滑底座
- Includes catnip (standard in German market) | 包含猫薄荷

## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

**Campaign thesis (活动论点):**  
German cat owners face a "furniture vs. cat happiness" dilemma. Traditional solutions (ugly plush trees, disposable cardboard) force them to choose between home aesthetics and pet satisfaction. This product resolves the conflict by positioning itself as **furniture that cats love**, not "cat furniture that ruins your living room."

**Three-angle attack (三角度攻击):**

Per `video_synthesis.md` Section 11 ("3 Script Angles Recommended"), we execute:

1. **The Sofa Savior (沙发救星)** – Pain Point Resonance  
   - Target: Frustrated owners with damaged furniture | 目标：家具被破坏的沮丧主人
   - Hook: "Your sofa is begging for mercy?" | 钩子："你的沙发在求饶？"
   - Proof mechanic: Before/after split screen + stability test | 证据机制：前后对比+稳定性测试

2. **The Aesthetic Upgrade (美学升级)** – Status/Identity Appeal  
   - Target: Design-conscious millennials (25-45, IKEA shoppers) | 目标：注重设计的千禧一代
   - Hook: "Ugly cat trees were yesterday." | 钩子："丑陋的猫爬架已成过去。"
   - Proof mechanic: ASMR wood texture + interior matching shots | 证据机制：ASMR木纹质感+室内搭配镜头

3. **The Sustainable Swap (可持续替换)** – Logic/Value  
   - Target: Eco-conscious savers (German *Sparsamkeit*) | 目标：环保节约型消费者
   - Hook: "Never throw away again." | 钩子："再也不用扔掉了。"
   - Proof mechanic: Cost calculation overlay + replacement pad demo | 证据机制：成本计算叠加+更换垫片演示

**Why this multi-angle approach works (为什么多角度有效):**  
German TikTok users are psychologically diverse. Some respond to fear (furniture damage), others to aspiration (stylish home), others to logic (savings). Testing three distinct emotional triggers maximizes reach while maintaining brand consistency through shared visual language (wood texture close-ups, space-saving wide shots, stability tests).

## 6. Production Notes | 制作说明

### Must-Have Shots (每个脚本必备镜头)

Per `video_synthesis.md` Section 10 ("Replication Checklist"):

1. **The Sofa Threat** – Close-up of cat claws near expensive fabric | **沙发威胁** – 猫爪靠近昂贵面料的特写
2. **The Stability Test** – Push product firmly on camera (doesn't tip) | **稳定性测试** – 在镜头前用力推产品（不倾倒）
3. **The Texture Proof** – ASMR close-up of wood grain + sisal mat | **质感证明** – 木纹+剑麻垫的ASMR特写
4. **The Replacement Demo** – Hands peeling off/attaching new pad (satisfying sound) | **更换演示** – 手剥离/附上新垫片（令人满意的声音）
5. **The Space-Saving Shot** – Wide angle showing corner fit in small apartment | **节省空间镜头** – 广角展示小公寓角落的适配性
6. **The Happy Cat** – Cat actively scratching or sleeping on post | **快乐的猫** – 猫在柱子上积极抓挠或睡觉

### Filming Environment (拍摄环境)

- **Location:** Clean, Scandinavian-style living room with natural light | 清洁、斯堪的纳维亚风格的客厅，自然光线
- **Flooring:** Wood laminate or light-colored (matches product) | 木层压板或浅色（与产品匹配）
- **Furniture:** IKEA or minimalist aesthetic for target audience relatability | 宜家或极简美学，适应目标受众
- **Lighting:** Soft, warm natural light (avoid harsh shadows or neon) | 柔和、温暖的自然光（避免强烈阴影或霓虹灯）

### Sound Design (音效设计)

Per `video_synthesis.md` Section 7 ("Visual Editing Playbook"):

- **Layer 1:** ASMR scratching sound (crunchy, satisfying) when cat uses post | 猫使用猫抓柱时的ASMR抓挠声（松脆、令人满意）
- **Layer 2:** Light acoustic guitar or Lo-Fi beat (calm, not chaotic) | 轻声原声吉他或Lo-Fi节拍（平静，不混乱）
- **Layer 3:** Clear German TTS voiceover (ElevenLabs v3 with emotion cues) | 清晰的德语TTS画外音（带情感提示的ElevenLabs v3）
- **Volume hierarchy:** Voiceover loudest, music 30% quieter, sound effects for emphasis | 音量层次：画外音最响，音乐降低30%，音效用于强调

### Editing Style (剪辑风格)

- **Pacing:** Fast cuts for hook (0-3s), slow down for quality proof (mid-section), fast again for CTA | 节奏：钩子快速切换（0-3秒），质量证明慢下来（中间部分），CTA再次加快
- **Text overlays:** Clean sans-serif font (Montserrat/Helvetica), placed in negative space (not covering product) | 文字叠加：干净的无衬线字体（Montserrat/Helvetica），放置在负空间（不覆盖产品）
- **Color grading:** Slightly desaturated or warm/earthy tones (avoid hyper-saturated filters) | 色彩分级：略微去饱和或温暖/土色调（避免过饱和滤镜）
- **German spelling:** Triple-check text overlays for typos (Germans judge harshly) | 德语拼写：三重检查文字叠加是否有拼写错误（德国人严格判断）

### Cat Interaction Tips (猫互动技巧)

- **Catnip:** Rub fresh catnip on sisal pad before filming to induce scratching | **猫薄荷：** 拍摄前在剑麻垫上擦新鲜猫薄荷以诱导抓挠
- **Laser pointer:** Use to guide cat to post if lazy | **激光笔：** 如果猫懒惰，使用激光笔引导猫到猫抓柱
- **Patience:** Schedule 2+ hours for cat footage (cannot force natural behavior) | **耐心：** 安排2小时以上用于猫镜头（无法强制自然行为）
- **Backup plan:** If cat won't cooperate, use toy on string to simulate scratching action | **备用计划：** 如果猫不配合，使用绳子上的玩具模拟抓挠动作
