## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a Strong Visual CTA:** Overlay text "50% OFF Today Only" or "Link in Bio to Buy" during the last 3 seconds. The viewer needs a *reason* to leave TikTok and buy.
2.  **Show the Price/Value:** If the product is affordable (€15-25), mention it ("Kostet weniger als ein Sack Futter" - Costs less than a bag of food).
3.  **Pin the Comment:** Ensure a direct link or instruction is the top comment immediately.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely solely on "Cute":** "Cute" gets likes, "Solve Problem + Urgency" gets sales. Don't just show the cat; show *why* the owner needs to buy it *now*.
2.  **Forget the Link:** Verify the TikTok Shop link is actually working and visible. 0 sales on 35k views is suspicious for technical issues or pricing mismatch.
3.  **Leave the Ending Open:** Don't just end with "sweet home." End with "Get yours before they are gone!"

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Reply to Comments with Video:** If there are comments asking "Where to buy?", reply with a video showing *exactly* how to click the shop link.
2.  **Bundle Offer:** "Buy 2 for Free Shipping" (if applicable) to increase AOV.
3.  **ASMR Angle:** Record the sound of the cat purring inside the cave to boost the sensory appeal of "comfort."
