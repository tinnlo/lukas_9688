## Scripts

**Hook (0-3s):**
- Split-screen grid: 6 different messy surfaces
- Text overlay: "1 Gerät = 12 Funktionen"
- Creator holding steam cleaner in center frame

**Build-Up (3-15s):**
- Rapid-fire surface switching (1-2 seconds each):
  - Kitchen counter (grease stains)
  - Bathroom tiles (mold in grout)
  - Living room sofa (dust/stains)
  - Window (water spots)
  - Oven interior (baked-on grime)
  - Car dashboard (dust)
- Pause at 15s: Full spread of 12 accessories on table

**Payoff (15-40s):**
- Detailed cleaning montage with accessory changes:
  - Nylon brush → Kitchen counter (grease melts away)
  - Small angled nozzle → Grout lines (white restoration)
  - Fabric steamer → Sofa (freshen/sanitize)
  - Window squeegee → Windows (streak-free shine)
  - Brass brush → Oven (stubborn carbon deposits)

- Each transition shows quick accessory swap (twist-off, twist-on)
- Text overlays for each: "Küche ✓", "Bad ✓", "Wohnzimmer ✓", etc.

**CTA (40-48s):**
- Side-by-side comparison: Cabinet full of cleaning products → One steam cleaner
- Text: "Spart Geld | Spart Platz | Spart Zeit"
- Price overlay + finger point to basket
