## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Start with the "After" shot:** Flash the cool lighting effects for 0.5s *before* starting the "1, 2, 3" count to grab attention immediately. (先展示效果再展示组装).
2.  **Sync Music to Color Changes:** When demonstrating the remote, cut or change colors on the music beat. (卡点变色).
3.  **Show "Music Mode":** If the lamp reacts to sound (common feature), demonstrate that specifically as it's a huge selling point for parties. (展示音乐律动模式).

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't linger on the cable:** The transcript mentions the cable/outlet for too long (3s). It's the least sexy part of the product. Keep it under 1s. (不要在电源线上浪费时间).
2.  **Don't use "Mumbled" Audio:** Ensure the CTA "Sichere dir..." is crisp. The transcript analysis suggested some clarity issues. (确保结尾号召语音清晰).

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
