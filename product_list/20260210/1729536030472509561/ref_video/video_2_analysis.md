## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Use the "Price Error" Hook:** It is currently the most effective way to drive CTR in the DACH (Germany/Austria/Switzerland) market.
2. **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Keep the visuals of the lamp changing colors constant throughout the VO.
3. **Highlight the Double Pack:** German consumers are very value-conscious; 2-for-1 messaging works well.

**DON'T:**
1. **Avoid Overly Polished Production:** Keep it looking like a real TikTok post. Too much "studio lighting" can lower trust.
2. **Don't Forget the Music Sync:** This is a major selling point for the younger gaming demographic.
3. **Don't Mumble:** Ensure the German VO is high energy and clear, even if colloquial.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Comparison Angle:** Show this lamp vs. a much more expensive brand (like Philips Hue) to emphasize the value.
2. **Setup Transformation:** Use a "Before vs. After" format showing a boring corner turned into a "Vibe" corner.
3. **Gift Angle:** Frame it as the "Perfect gift for gamers" to capture the birthday/gifting market.
