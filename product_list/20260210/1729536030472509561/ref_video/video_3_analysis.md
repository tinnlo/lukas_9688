## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add ASMR or Voiceover:** Even a simple "Look at this upgrade" increases retention. | **加入ASMR或旁白：** 即使是简单的“看这个升级”也能提高留存率。
2. **Show Setup Process:** A 2-second "peel and stick" or assembly shot reduces purchase friction (fear of complex install). | **展示安装过程：** 2秒钟的“撕开粘贴” or 组装镜头能减少购买阻力（担心安装复杂）。
3. **Use Text Overlays:** Add "50% Off Today" or "Free Shipping" to drive impulse buys. | **使用文字叠加：** 添加“今日半价”或“免运费”以促进冲动消费。

**DON'T:**
1. **Filming in Daylight:** Ruins the saturation and makes the LED look cheap. | **在日光下拍摄：** 会破坏饱和度，让LED显得廉价。
2. **Showing Messy Cables:** Hide the power cord for a premium look. | **露出凌乱线缆：** 隐藏电源线以显高级感。
3. **Overusing Strobe:** Fast flashing can annoy users; use smooth transitions ("Breathing" effect). | **过度使用频闪：** 快速闪烁会惹恼用户；应使用平滑过渡（“呼吸”效果）。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Gamer Setup" Angle:** Specifically target the gaming niche with gaming-related hashtags and audio. | **“电竞房”切入点：** 使用游戏相关标签和音频专门针对游戏细分市场。
2. **Reaction/Duet format:** Have a person "react" to how good the room looks. | **反应/合拍格式：** 让真人“反应”房间看起来有多棒。
3. **Gift Angle:** "Best gift for boyfriend" (highly convertible for this demographic). | **礼物切入点：** “送男友的最佳礼物”（对此人群转化率极高）。
