## Scripts

**Hook (0-3s):**
- Living room with one lamp in corner (asymmetrical)
- Camera pans to show empty opposite corner
- Text overlay: "Something is missing..."

**Build-Up (3-18s):**
- Unboxing second lamp
- Time-lapse of person walking to opposite corner
- Placing second lamp symmetrically
- Plugging in both lamps

**Payoff (18-35s):**
- Both lamps turn on in Lava Mode (warm orange-red)
- Smooth slow pan showing symmetrical glow
- Close-up of TV wall illuminated from both sides
- Cozy atmosphere shot (person sitting on couch with tea)
- Remote control switches to Sunset Mode (soft pink-orange)

**CTA (35-42s):**
- Text overlay: "2 = Kostenloser Versand"
- Split-screen comparison: "1 Lampe: Versandkosten" vs "2 Lampen: Gratis Versand"
- Finger points to basket
