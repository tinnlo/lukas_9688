## Scripts

**Hook (0-3s):**
- Split-screen comparison:
  - Left: Boring white wall (empty, stark)
  - Right: Same wall with mirror + plants
- Text overlay: "Kleines Detail. Großer Effekt."

**Build-Up (3-20s):**
- Show the empty corner problem: plain white walls, no character
- Brainstorming solutions (thinking overlay):
  - Painting? (Too permanent for rental)
  - Big artwork? (Too expensive)
  - Photo wall? (Too cluttered)
- Unboxing mirror from package
- Size check: Hand measurement shows it's compact (22.5cm)
- Hang it on a small nail (easy, non-permanent)

**Payoff (20-35s):**
- Wide shot: Corner transformation complete
  - Mirror as focal point
  - Small plant on shelf below
  - Warm lighting from side
- Close-up: Sunlight catching the mirror's reflection
- Pan across the styled corner (minimalist aesthetic)
- Text overlays cycling: "22cm" → "Kein Bohren" → "Mieter-freundlich"

**CTA (35-40s):**
- Side-by-side before/after one more time
- Text: "Manchmal braucht man nicht viel"
- Price + finger point
