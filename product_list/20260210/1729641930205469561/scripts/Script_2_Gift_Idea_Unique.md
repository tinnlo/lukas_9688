## Scripts

**Hook (0-3s):**
- Text overlay: "Deine Freundin hat schon alles?"
- Creator looking stressed/thinking with hand on chin
- Quick cut to mirror being wrapped in tissue paper

**Build-Up (3-18s):**
- Montage of rejected gift ideas (crossed out):
  - Generic candle (too boring)
  - Jewelry box (she has 3 already)
  - Plant (she kills plants)
- Show mirror unboxing (elegant, protected packaging)
- Hand holding mirror next to face for size comparison
- Close-up of facial sculpting detail

**Payoff (18-30s):**
- Gift wrapping montage: tissue paper, ribbon, small card
- Imagined reaction: Friend's excited face (or creator's own smile as proxy)
- Show mirror on friend's wall in new apartment
- Text overlay: "Einzigartig | Persönlich | Langlebig"

**CTA (30-38s):**
- Split-screen: Generic gift vs. This mirror
- Text: "Für Einzug, Geburtstag oder einfach so"
- Price flash + finger point
