## Scripts

**Angle: The Skeptic Test** - Addresses German skepticism about "TikTok gadgets" head-on. Aggressive testing to prove stability. Honest review format that builds trust.

### Structure (42s)

- **Hook:** Direct skeptical question to camera (0-4s)
- **Setup:** Unbox and quick assembly (4-10s)
- **Test 1:** Shake test - does it wobble? (10-16s)
- **Test 2:** Weight test - heavy bottles (16-24s)
- **Test 3:** Pull-out speed test (24-30s)
- **Verdict:** Honest conclusion + CTA (30-42s)

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-04 | Creator holds product, skeptical face. Text: "Ist das stabil?" | Hook: Addresses objection directly, relatable skepticism |
| 04-08 | Quick unboxing. Parts laid out. Steel tubes visible. | Trust: Shows real components, not fake images |
| 08-10 | Speed assembly time-lapse. Clock graphic "58 Sekunden" | Proof: Assembly claim verified |

| 14-16 | Text overlay: "Wackelt nicht! ✓" with checkmark | Verification: Test result |
| 16-20 | Heavy detergent bottles being loaded. Perwoll, Persil visible. | Test 2: German brands = subconscious credibility |
| 20-24 | Full rack with weight. Creator pushes down on top shelf. | Proof: Weight capacity under stress |
| 24-28 | Aggressive drawer pull-out at full speed. Does base tip? | Test 3: Speed stress test |
| 28-30 | Slow-mo replay of drawer slide. Stays stable. | Proof: Slow-motion verification |
| 30-36 | Creator gives thumbs up. Honest verdict delivered. | Trust: Creator endorsement after testing |
| 36-42 | Product in organized cabinet. Shop prompt. | CTA: Earned after honest review |
