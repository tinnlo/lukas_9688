## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German TTS/Voiceover:** Explain the problem ("Chaos unter der Spüle?") and the solution.
   *(添加德语配音：解释痛点“水槽下乱糟糟？”和解决方案。)*
2. **Show "Before" State:** Start with a messy pile of cleaning supplies to create immediate contrast.
   *(展示“使用前”状态：以杂乱的清洁用品堆积开场，制造强烈对比。)*
3. **Overlay Text Benefits:** Add text like "Ausziehbar" (Pull-out) or "Platzsparend" (Space-saving).
   *(叠加卖点文字：如“可抽拉”或“节省空间”。)*

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** 15 seconds of silence (or generic music) signals "low quality ad" to German users.
   *(不要依赖静音：15秒的静默或通用音乐会让德国用户觉得是“劣质广告”。)*
2. **Hide the Capacity:** Ensure you show *how many* bottles it fits, not just that it fits.
   *(不要隐藏容量：确保展示能装下多少瓶子，而不仅仅是能放进去。)*

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR "Restocking" Angle:** Record the sounds of bottles being placed and the drawer sliding. "Restock with me" videos perform well.
   *(ASMR“补货”视角：录制瓶子放置和抽屉滑动的声音。“沉浸式收纳”视频表现很好。)*
2. **"Renter Friendly" Angle:** Emphasize no drilling/installation required (if true).
   *(“租房党友好”视角：强调无需打孔安装。)*
