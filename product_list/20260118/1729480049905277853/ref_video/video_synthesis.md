## 6. German Market Psychology | Âæ∑ÂõΩÂ∏ÇÂú∫ÂøÉÁêÜ

### Cultural Behaviors & Preferences | ÊñáÂåñË°å‰∏∫‰∏éÂÅèÂ•Ω
1.  **"Sparf√ºchse" (Smart Savers):** Germans hate "cheap" junk but love a "Schn√§ppchen" (high value bargain).
    *   *Creative Choice:* Don't just show the price. Show the *versatility* (Bed + Tunnel + Toy) to justify the price. "3 products in 1" works better than "Cheap product."
2.  **Skepticism of "As Seen on TV" vibes:** Overly polished, American-style infomercials trigger distrust.
    *   *Creative Choice:* Use raw, slightly imperfect lighting (UGC style). The "messy living room corner" background in Video 4 creates more trust than a studio white background.
3.  **Tierwohl (Animal Welfare) Focus:** Germans treat pets with high ethical standards.
    *   *Creative Choice:* Ensure the cat looks safe, not trapped. Avoid aggressive forcing of the cat into the tunnel. Show the cat entering *voluntarily*.
4.  **Practicality & Efficiency:** "Ordnung muss sein" (Order must be).
    *   *Creative Choice:* The "Foldable" feature is huge. Show how it disappears behind a couch or into a drawer. It solves the "clutter" problem.
5.  **Seasonal Sensitivity:** "Winter is coming" is a practical concern, not just a slogan.
    *   *Creative Choice:* Focus on heating costs. "Keep your cat warm without turning up the radiator" is a powerful economic angle in Germany.

### Trust Signals | ‰ø°‰ªª‰ø°Âè∑
-   **Specific Numbers:** "My 13-year-old cat" (Video 2) is infinitely better than "My old cat." Specifics = Truth.
-   **Haptic Details:** Close-ups of the fabric texture. German buyers want to "feel" the quality through the screen.
-   **Local Language Nuances:** Use "Kuschelig" (cuddly/cozy), "Pflegeleicht" (easy care), and "Platzsparend" (space-saving).

## 7. Visual Editing Playbook | ËßÜËßâÂâ™ËæëÊâìÊ≥ï

-   **Pacing:** Start fast (0.5s cuts) for the hook, slow down (2-3s shots) for the texture/sleeping shots to let the "cozy" feeling sink in.
-   **Text Overlays:** Use a clean, sans-serif font (like TikTok Classic or Pro).
    -   *Color:* White text with black background or brand colors. Avoid neon cluttered text which looks "scammy" to Germans.
-   **Audio Mixing:**
    -   *Music:* Lofi Beats or Acoustic Guitar (Warm vibes).
    -   *SFX:* Add a "Whoosh" when unfolding. Add a "Ding" sound when the text bubble pops up.
-   **Split Screen:** Use the "Expectation vs. Reality" format. Left side: "Bored cat on sofa". Right side: "Happy cat in Tunnel".
