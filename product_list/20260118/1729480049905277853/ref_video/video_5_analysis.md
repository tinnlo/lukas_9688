## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text hooks like "Mein Kater liebt es!" (My cat loves it!) or "Perfekt für den Winter" (Perfect for winter) to give context.
2. **Shorten the Loop:** Cut to 15-20 seconds to increase retention and loop rate. 34s is too long for a simple demo.
3. **Voiceover/TTS:** Add a simple TTS voiceover explaining the 2-in-1 feature to reinforce the value proposition audibly.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** Silent videos often get scrolled past if the visual hook isn't explosive. Audio is a key engagement driver.
2. **Hide the Mechanism:** Ensure the "snapping" or "folding" is shown clearly so people understand it's not just a standard mat.
3. **Forget the CTA:** Always end with a visual arrow or text pointing to the shop link ("Jetzt bestellen" - Order now).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the crinkly tunnel material (if it makes noise) or the pet purring.
2. **"POV: Your cat is spoiled":** Frame it as a relatable "pet parent" struggle/indulgence.
3. **Gift Angle:** Position it as a "Gift for your furry friend" (Geschenk für deinen Vierbeiner), especially in winter/holiday seasons.
