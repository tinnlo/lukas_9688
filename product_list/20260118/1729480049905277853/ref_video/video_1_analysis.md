## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Text Overlays:** Include German keywords like "Katzenparadies" (Cat Paradise) or "Platzsparend" (Space-saving) to reinforce benefits.
2. **Test Voiceover:** A simple TTS voiceover asking "Is your cat bored?" could increase engagement.
3. **Show Folding Mechanism:** Explicitly show how small it folds down to emphasize the "Foldable" feature for small apartments.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't use dark lighting:** Ensure the plush texture is visible; dark fur on dark fabric disappears.
2. **Don't delay the action:** Keep the cat interaction in the very first second (as done here).
3. **Don't ignore sound:** Ensure the music matches the energy of the "play" section and the "sleep" section.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Version:** Record the crinkle sound of the tunnel (if it crinkles) or the purring of the cat.
2. **"POV" Angle:** Film from the cat's perspective entering the tunnel.
3. **Comparison:** "Old Bed vs. New Tunnel Bed" split screen.
