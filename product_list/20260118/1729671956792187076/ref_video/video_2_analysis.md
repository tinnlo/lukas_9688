## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German TTS/Voiceover:** Use a native-sounding voice to explain *why* it is "Four Seasons" (e.g., "Im Sommer kühl, im Winter warm" - Cool in summer, warm in winter). | **增加德语TTS/旁白：** 使用地道语音解释*为什么*是“四季通用”（如“冬暖夏凉”）。
2. **Text Overlays:** Add key benefits on screen: "Waschbar" (Washable), "Robust" (Durable), "Für Katzen & Hunde" (For Cats & Dogs). | **添加文字贴纸：** 在屏幕上添加核心利益点：“可水洗”、“耐用”、“猫狗适用”。
3. **Explicit CTA:** End with "Jetzt für deinen Liebling sichern!" (Secure one for your darling now!). | **明确CTA：** 结尾加上“现在就为你的爱宠抢购吧！”

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Music Only:** Passive watching does not convert in the DE market. | **不要仅依赖音乐：** 被动观看在德国市场无法转化。
2. **Hide the "Why":** Don't assume viewers know it's a "multifunctional" nest just by looking at it. | **隐藏“为什么”：** 不要假设观众光看一眼就知道这是“多功能”窝。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Seasonal Angle:** Since it's January (Winter), market it specifically as "The warm spot for cold days" right now. | **季节性切入：** 既然是一月（冬季），现在就专门将其营销为“寒冷日子的温暖小窝”。
2. **Unboxing/Assembly:** Show how easy it is to set up or clean to build trust. | **开箱/组装：** 展示安装或清洁是多么容易，以建立信任。
