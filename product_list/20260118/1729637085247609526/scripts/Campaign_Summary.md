## 3. Key Selling Points | 核心卖点

*Extracted from video_synthesis.md (提取自视频综合分析):*

1. **2-in-1 Space-Saving Design (二合一节省空间设计)** – 360° rotatable base allows L-shape corner placement in small kitchens. Unique for German apartments (360°旋转底座允许在小厨房的角落L型摆放。德国公寓的独特卖点). **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Overcomes "too bulky" objection common in WG/small homes (克服小户型常见的"太大"异议).

2. **Gravity-Fed = No Electricity Needed (重力补给=无需电源)** – Siphon physics means silent operation, no cables, works during power outages. Germans value "Geräuschlos" (quiet) (虹吸物理意味着静音操作，无电线，停电时也能工作。德国人重视"无噪音"). **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Addresses skepticism of cheap electronic feeders that break (解决对廉价电子喂食器易损坏的怀疑).

3. **Stainless Steel Hygiene (不锈钢卫生)** – Food bowl is stainless steel, not plastic. Prevents bacterial buildup and chin acne in cats (食盆是不锈钢而非塑料。防止细菌积聚和猫咪黑下巴). **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Premium material signal combats "Billigware" (cheap goods) perception (高级材料信号对抗"廉价商品"感知).

4. **Massive Capacity: 2.6L Water + 2.4L Food (超大容量：2.6L水+2.4L粮)** – One refill lasts 5-7 days for average cat. Perfect for weekend trips (一次加满可供普通猫使用5-7天。适合周末旅行). **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Removes "who feeds the cat?" barrier for working professionals (消除上班族"谁喂猫？"的障碍).

5. **Easy Clean & Disassembly (易于清洁和拆卸)** – Parts separate in seconds. Stainless bowl is dishwasher-safe (零件几秒内拆开。不锈钢碗可用洗碗机清洗). **Conversion Factor (转化因素):** Answers #1 objection "Is it hard to clean?" (回答#1异议"难清洗吗？").
