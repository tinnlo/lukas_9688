## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add a clear CTA:** Must end with "Jetzt erhältlich im TikTok Shop" (Available now in TikTok Shop) or point to the basket icon.
2. **Show the Pet:** A pet feeder video without a cute cat/dog eating happily is missing the emotional payoff.
3. **Fix the Ending:** The video seems to cut off mid-sentence ("Bis ich..."). Ensure the edit is clean and loops well.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Audio Only:** Add text overlays for key benefits ("2-in-1 Design", "Spülmaschinenfest" - Dishwasher safe) for sound-off viewers.
2. **Ignore SEO:** Ensure the description includes hashtags like #KatzenFutter #HundeLiebe #TikTokMadeMeBuyItDE.
3. **Use ambiguous slang:** Ensure the voiceover is crisp. The transcript confusion suggests the audio might be slightly mumbled or rapid.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the sound of the kibble hitting the bowl or the water glugging – highly popular in pet niches.
2. **Problem/Agitation:** Start with the "Problem" (e.g., "Hate filling bowls every morning?") instead of just "Look what I got".
3. **Comparison:** Briefly show an "ugly/dirty" old bowl next to this chic new one to heighten the contrast.
