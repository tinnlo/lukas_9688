## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **UPDATE THE CREATIVE:** Immediately cut the "Weihnachtsaktion" line. Change it to "Neujahrs-Vorsatz" (New Year's Resolution) or just "Winter Sale".
2.  **Add UGC Element:** Replace the stock footage with a real German person filling the bowl and saying "Endlich kann ich ohne Sorge zur Arbeit" (Finally I can go to work without worry).
3.  **Show the "Mess":** Start the video with a messy water bowl spill or a chaotic feeding scene to contrast with the neat product.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Run Expired Ads:** Never run a holiday-specific ad 3 weeks after the holiday. It signals "Scam Store".
2.  **Use Generic TTS:** If possible, use a real voice or a trending TikTok text-to-speech voice (like the energetic female voice), not the standard documentary style.
3.  **Ignore "Tech Specs":** For Germany, mention "Quiet Pump" (Leise Pumpe) or "Stainless Steel" (Edelstahl) if applicable. Germans love technical specs.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Record the sound of the kibble hitting the bowl or the water trickling. "Pet ASMR" is a huge niche.
2.  **"Day in the Life" POV:** Show the feeder working while the owner is at the office (simulated time lapse).
3.  **Humor:** Show a cat trying to break into the feeder and failing – "Diebstahlsicher" (Theft proof).
