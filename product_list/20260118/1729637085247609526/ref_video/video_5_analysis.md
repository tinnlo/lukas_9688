## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the Product in Action:** Film a cat or dog actually eating/drinking from the unit. Movement is crucial. | **展示产品使用过程**：拍摄猫或狗实际进食/饮水的画面。动态至关重要。
2. **Demonstrate "Easy Clean":** Show the parts being disassembled and washed. German buyers care about hygiene and maintenance. | **演示“易清洗”**：展示拆卸和清洗部件的过程。德国买家关注卫生和维护。
3. **Add Voiceover/Text:** Explain *why* this is better than a normal bowl (e.g., "Keeps water fresh," "No spills"). | **添加旁白/文字**：解释*为什么*这比普通碗好（例如，“保持水质新鲜”，“不溅水”）。

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Static Images:** TikTok is a video-first platform. Static clips signal "low quality" to the algorithm and users. | **依赖静态图片**：TikTok是视频优先的平台。静态片段向算法和用户发出“低质量”的信号。
2. **Ignore Duration:** Extend video to at least 7-15 seconds to allow time for a narrative. | **忽视时长**：将视频延长至至少7-15秒，以便有时间进行叙事。
3. **Forget Local Context:** For Germany, ensure the feeder looks sturdy and safe (BPA-free mentions help). | **忘记本地语境**：针对德国市场，确保喂食器看起来坚固且安全（提及不含BPA会有帮助）。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** The sound of dry food hitting the bowl or water glugging can be satisfying and stop the scroll. | **ASMR角度**：干粮落入碗中或水流动的声音可能令人满足并吸引停留。
2. **"Problem/Solution" Setup:** Show a messy floor with old bowls vs. the clean setup with this unit. | **“问题/解决方案”设定**：展示旧碗造成的脏乱地板对比此单元的整洁设置。
3. **German "Ordnung" (Orderliness):** Appeal to the desire for a tidy home. "Endlich kein Chaos mehr beim Füttern" (Finally, no more feeding chaos). | **德国式的“整洁”**：迎合对整洁家居的渴望。“Endlich kein Chaos mehr beim Füttern”（终于不再有喂食的混乱了）。
