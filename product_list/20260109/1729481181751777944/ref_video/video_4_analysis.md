## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Show Usage:** Film a real hand *cranking* the handle to show mechanical action and sound.
2.  **Demonstrate Light:** Turn the lights off and show the flashlight beam brightness in a dark room.
3.  **Localize:** Use **German TTS** ("Solar, Handkurbel Radio für nur 11 Euro!") and German text overlays.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Static Slideshows:** Avoid using static images for 3 seconds each. TikTok is a video-first platform.
2.  **Short Audio/Long Video:** Don't have 3 seconds of audio for a 21-second video. It creates "dead air" that kills retention.
3.  **Ignore Context:** Don't just show the product on white; show it on grass, in a tent, or in a car to suggest use cases.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of the hand crank or the "click" of the buttons could be satisfying.
2.  **"Prepper" Narrative:** "Is your emergency kit ready? For 11€, you can't afford NOT to have this." (Fear/Safety angle).
3.  **Comparison:** "This 11€ radio vs. a 50€ brand name one" – Show it does the same job for cheaper.
