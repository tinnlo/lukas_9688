## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1.  **Show Product Earlier:** Introduce the radio at 00:05 as the "solution" to the fear, not at 00:30.
2.  **Visual Proof:** When mentioning "Powerbank," show a phone going from black screen to "Charging" icon.
3.  **Localize Harder:** Show a map of Germany or specific local news headlines about recent storms in the background (Green screen).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Mumble:** The hook needs crisp, clear audio. The transcript suggests some slurring.
2.  **Don't Linger on "Face Talking":** Reduce the "talking head" time. Overlay b-roll of storms/dark houses while talking.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Gift Angle:** "The perfect gift for your parents who worry." (Positioning it as peace of mind for elderly relatives).
2.  **Car Emergency:** Position it as a "must-have for your car glovebox" rather than just home use.
3.  **ASMR Elements:** The cranking sound and the click of the flashlight can be satisfying triggers if recorded well.
