## 3. Key Selling Points | 核心卖点

1. **Multi-functional Design:** Replaces 3+ devices (powerbank, flashlight, radio) in one product
2. **Energy Independence:** No batteries or outlets required - solar + hand crank provide infinite power
3. **German Value Psychology:** At €11-15, frames safety/preparedness as affordable and responsible
4. **Outdoor Lifestyle Appeal:** Perfect for camping, hiking, festivals, and emergency situations

---

## 4. Target Audience Segments | 目标受众

1. **The "German Prepper"** (35-60M) – Concerned about infrastructure, seeks self-sufficiency
2. **Budget Shoppers** (Gen Z/Millennial) – Want functional gadgets at low price points
3. **Outdoor Hobbyists** (20-40 M/F) – Hikers, campers, festival-goers seeking lightweight gear
4. **Concerned Parents/Seniors** (60+ M/F) – Value safety, simplicity, and reliability

## 5. Scripts Summary | 脚本摘要

**Script 1 (Logic Angle):** Emphasizes the 3-in-1 value proposition. Shows phone charging as proof of functionality. Best for converting price-conscious buyers with a clear, straightforward benefit.

**Script 2 (Prepper Angle):** Triggers preparedness psychology ("better safe than sorry"). Uses the German cultural mindset of "Vorsorge" (forward planning). Best for fear-aware buyers who value being prepared.

**Script 3 (Survival Angle):** Celebrates independence and outdoor lifestyle. Focuses on the fun/freedom aspects (hand crank action, solar power). Best for adventure-minded buyers seeking camping solutions.
