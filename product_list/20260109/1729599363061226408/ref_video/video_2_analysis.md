## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Voiceover:** Explain *why* steam is better than chemicals (e.g., "Spart Geld für Reiniger" - Saves money on cleaners). | **添加旁白：** 解释*为什么*蒸汽比化学品好（例如，“Spart Geld für Reiniger” - 节省清洁剂费用）。
2.  **Shorten to <20s:** Cut the repetitive cleaning shots. Focus on: Dirty -> Steam -> Clean -> CTA. | **缩短至<20秒：** 剪掉重复的清洁镜头。专注于：脏 -> 蒸汽 -> 干净 -> CTA。
3.  **Stronger Text Hook:** Start with "Haustiere? Dann brauchst du das!" (Pets? Then you need this!). | **更强的文字钩子：** 以“Haustiere? Dann brauchst du das!”（有宠物？那你需要这个！）开头。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Silence/Music Only:** Don't rely solely on music. German audiences appreciate factual info/explanation. | **静音/仅音乐：** 不要仅仅依赖音乐。德国观众欣赏事实信息/解释。
2.  **Dark Lighting:** Avoid cleaning in dark corners without extra light; the steam needs to pop. | **昏暗的灯光：** 避免在没有额外光线的情况下清洁黑暗角落；蒸汽需要突显出来。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"ASMR" Cleaning:** Record the *sound* of the steam hiss (Pschhh) distinctively without music for an ASMR hook. | **"ASMR"清洁：** 录制独特的蒸汽嘶嘶声（Pschhh），不加音乐，作为ASMR钩子。
2.  **Cost Comparison:** Show a pile of expensive chemical cleaners vs. this one machine (Value anchor). | **成本比较：** 展示一堆昂贵的化学清洁剂与这台机器的对比（价值锚点）。
