## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add ASMR Audio:** Amplify the "hiss" of the steam for sensory immersion.
   增加ASMR音效：放大蒸汽的“嘶嘶”声以增强感官沉浸感。
2. **Text-to-Speech Hook:** Add a German TTS voiceover asking "Is your kitchen truly clean?" to boost retention.
   添加TTS钩子：增加德语TTS旁白问“你的厨房真的干净吗？”以提高留存率。
3. **Show "Gross" Zoom-ins:** Use a macro lens to show the dirt *before* it gets blasted.
   展示“恶心”特写：使用微距镜头展示被清理*前*的污垢。

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Over-edit:** Avoid distracting effects that hide the actual cleaning process.
   不要过度剪辑：避免使用掩盖实际清洁过程的分散注意力的特效。
2. **Don't Ignore Audio:** Even without VO, the music must match the energy of the cleaning bursts.
   不要忽视音频：即使没有旁白，音乐也必须与清洁爆发的能量相匹配。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Satisfying Cleaning" Compilation:** Create a longer cut of just cleaning different items (keyboard, car rims, shoes) for a "satisfying" loop.
   “解压清洁”合集：制作一个仅包含清洁不同物品（键盘、车轮毂、鞋子）的长视频，打造“解压”循环。
2. **Pet Safety Angle:** Explicitly show a dog/cat walking on the floor immediately after cleaning to prove safety.
   宠物安全角度：明确展示清洁后宠物立即在地板上行走，以证明安全性。
