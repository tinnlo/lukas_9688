## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Target Niches:** If targeting Germany, consider versions in Turkish, Arabic, or Polish to replicate this "Community Trust" strategy.
2.  **Focus on Grease:** For steam cleaners, oily kitchen stains (hoods, filters) are the highest visual payoff.
3.  **Show "Impossible" Cleans:** Find something that *looks* ruined and restore it.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Over-polish:** Do not use studio lighting. It makes the cleaning look fake.
2.  **Hide the Mess:** The "Before" shot must be disgusting for the "After" to be satisfying.
3.  **Forget Audio:** Even if visual, the "sound" of the steam pressure (Hissing) implies power. Don't drown it out with music.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Edit:** A version with NO talking, just the sound of steam and dirt melting, could go viral beyond the language barrier.
2.  **Car Detailing:** Steam cleaners are huge for car interiors. This is a massive untapped angle for this specific product in the generated report context.
3.  **Localized German Dub:** Use an AI dubbing tool to translate this exact high-converting script into native German to broaden the audience.
