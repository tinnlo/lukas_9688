## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Text Overlays (German):** Add "Ramadan Deko" (Ramadan Decor) and "Jetzt Bestellen" (Order Now) on screen.
2. **Clarify the Offer:** Show a "50% OFF" or "Free Shipping to Germany" badge to trigger impulse buys.
3. **Use Localized Tags:** Drop `#france` if targeting Germany. Use `#RamadanGermany` `#IslamicArtDE` `#MuslimInGermany`.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Visuals Alone:** Don't assume people know where to buy just because they see it.
2. **Mix Regions:** Don't confuse the algorithm with French tags for a German shop.
3. **Ignore CTA:** Don't end the video without a clear visual pointer to the shopping cart icon.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Unboxing Angle:** Film the *opening* of the box to show packaging quality (Trust builder).
2. **Magnetic/Easy Install:** If the product is easy to hang (e.g., magnetic), explicitly demonstrate that in a 2-second clip to solve the "installation pain point."
3. **Gift Angle:** Position it explicitly as "The Perfect Eid Gift" with text overlay.
