## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Text Overlays:** Include "Wunderschöne Islamische Wandkunst" (Beautiful Islamic Wall Art) or "Perfektes Ramadan Geschenk" (Perfect Ramadan Gift) to signal local relevance to German viewers.
2. **Show Human Interaction:** Include a hand touching the metal to prove it's solid/high-quality, or someone hanging it up to show ease of installation.
3. **Include a CTA:** Add a text overlay at the end: "Jetzt kaufen / Shop Now" with an arrow to the shopping cart.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely Solely on Arabic:** Don't assume all potential buyers in Germany speak Arabic.
2. **Leave the End Empty:** Don't let the video just fade out without a prompt.
3. **Use Static Slideshows:** Ensure there is video motion (panning/tilting) rather than just static photo cuts.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Unboxing/Installation Angle:** "How to transform your living room for Ramadan in 30 seconds" – show the unboxing and hanging process.
2. **Gift-Giving Angle:** Show someone wrapping it as a gift for Eid.
3. **Size Comparison:** Show a person standing next to it so viewers understand the scale immediately.
