## 11. Risk / Compliance Notes | È£éÈô©‰∏éÂêàËßÑÊèêÈÜí

*   **Audio Copyright:** Many Nasheeds are copyrighted. Ensure you use "Commercial Sounds" from TikTok's library or royalty-free tracks to avoid muting.
*   **Religious Sensitivity:** Do not place the artwork near the floor, bathrooms, or shoes in the video. This is extremely offensive for Quranic verses ("Bismillah"). Treat the product with high respect.
*   **Material Claims:** If it is steel, say steel. If it is aluminum, say aluminum. Don't say "Gold" if it is just "Gold Color" (Goldfarben). German consumer laws are strict on misleading descriptions.
*   **Shipping:** If shipping takes long (>7 days), be transparent or focus on "Pre-order for Ramadan".
