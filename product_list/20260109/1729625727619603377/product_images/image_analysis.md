## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

### Angle 1: The Spiritual Upgrade (Der spirituelle Aufwertung)
*   **Concept:** Focus on the emotional and spiritual value of bringing the "Bismillah" into the home. It starts everything with the name of Allah.
*   **Visuals:** Warm lighting, family gathering, reading Quran near the art, peaceful atmosphere.
*   **Hook:** "Beginne jeden Tag mit Bismillah." (Start every day with Bismillah.)
*   **Audience:** Practicing Muslims, families focusing on Islamic values.

### Angle 2: The Modern Aesthetic (Die moderne Ästhetik)
*   **Concept:** Focus purely on interior design. How it transforms a room from plain to stylish. Highlighting the 3D shadow effect and material quality.
*   **Visuals:** Clean lines, modern furniture, detailed macro shots of the metal texture, time-lapse of shadows moving.
*   **Hook:** "Das fehlende Puzzleteil für dein Wohnzimmer." (The missing puzzle piece for your living room.)
*   **Audience:** Interior design enthusiasts, young couples, people renovating.

### Angle 3: The Perfect Gift (Das perfekte Geschenk)
*   **Concept:** Positioning the product as the ultimate gift for upcoming occasions (Ramadan, Eid, Weddings). Solves the problem of "What should I gift?".
*   **Visuals:** Unboxing, gift wrapping, reaction of a person receiving it (joy, surprise).
*   **Hook:** "Suchst du noch ein Geschenk für Eid?" (Are you still looking for a gift for Eid?)
*   **Audience:** Gift shoppers, friends and family of Muslims.
