## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Price Anchor" - create immediate cognitive dissonance by comparing high-tech magnetic power bank to cheap street food. Target budget-conscious students and commuters. Hook with value shock, prove quality isn't compromised.

### Structure (28s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** Price comparison shock - cheaper than lunch
- **Investigation (3-15s):** Unboxing quality check + magnetic snap demo
- **Proof (15-23s):** LED display + charging speed
- **CTA (23-28s):** Value emphasis with urgency
