## Scripts

"Regret & Price Anchor" hook - most successful pattern per video synthesis. Creates credibility by admitting previous overpayment, validates current deal as steal. Targets German value consciousness with loss aversion psychology.

### Structure (35s)
- Hook: Frustrated price regret (0-3s)
- Past Pain: Show old expensive product (3-8s)
- Current Deal: Price comparison reveal (8-14s)
- Demo: Waterproof + multi-head test (14-26s)
- CTA: Don't make my mistake (26-35s)

### Visual Strategy
| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Frustrated face holding receipt or pointing at old price tag "viel zu teuer" | Hook: Loss aversion triggers attention |
| 03-08 | Old cheap razor with visible rust/dull blade next to new trimmer | Contrast: Why upgrade matters |
| 08-14 | Split screen: "Früher: zu teuer" vs "Jetzt viel günstiger" with arrow down | Logic: Price anchor establishes value |
| 14-18 | Swapping 3 different heads: trimmer, shaver, detail head | Feature: Versatility proof |
| 18-22 | Running trimmer under shower water, still working | Trust: Waterproof claim validated |
| 22-26 | Shaving arm or kiwi fruit in one smooth stroke, hair/skin cleanly removed | Satisfaction: ASMR visual proof |
| 26-30 | Holding trimmer with LED charge indicator visible | Quality: Premium features at budget price |
| 30-35 | Leaning into camera seriously, pointing down emphatically | CTA: Authentic recommendation |
