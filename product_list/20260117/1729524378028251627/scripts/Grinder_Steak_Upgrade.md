## Scripts

**Concept**: The "Steak Upgrade" - target BBQ enthusiasts and home cooks who want restaurant-quality seasoning. Show dramatic difference between pre-ground pepper dust vs. freshly cracked coarse pepper. Use sensory ASMR to sell freshness and texture.

### Structure (35s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** Comparison - boring steak vs. perfectly seasoned steak
- **Problem (3-10s):** Pre-ground pepper is stale and tasteless
- **Solution (10-25s):** Electric grinder demo with adjustable coarseness
- **Proof (25-30s):** Visual texture and aroma emphasis
- **CTA (30-35s):** BBQ season positioning

### Visual Strategy

| Seconds | Visual | Purpose |
|:--------|:-------|:--------|
| 00-03 | Split screen: Left = bland steak with dust-like pre-ground pepper, Right = gorgeous steak with chunky fresh pepper | IMMEDIATE CONTRAST - taste visualization |
