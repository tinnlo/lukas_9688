## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the Mechanism:** Briefly show *how* the food comes out. 6s is too short to understand the "Freeze-Dried Food Feeding Machine" aspect.
2. **Add German Text Overlay:** e.g., "Dein Hund wird es lieben!" (Your dog will love it!) to anchor the context for the DE market.
3. **Extend Duration:** A 10-12s version showing setup -> play -> reward would build higher value perception.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely Solely on "Cute":** "Cute" gets likes, "Useful" gets sales. Don't hide the utility.
2. **Leave Context to Caption:** Viewers might miss the caption. Put key info (e.g., "Anti-Langeweile" / Anti-boredom) on screen.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Record the sound of the kibble hitting the plastic duck or the dog crunching—pet ASMR is huge.
2. **"POV" Text:** "POV: You finally found a toy that keeps him busy for 30 minutes." (POV: Du hast endlich ein Spielzeug gefunden, das ihn 30 Minuten beschäftigt.)
