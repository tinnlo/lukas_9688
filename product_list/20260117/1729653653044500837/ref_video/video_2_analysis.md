## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Update the Script:** Change the "Christmas" angle to "New Year's Resolution" (e.g., "My resolution is to eat more snacks") or "Winter Boredom Buster" immediately.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add captions like "Der beste 8€ Invest" (The best 8€ investment) to capture sound-off users.
3.  **Show "Before/After":** Show a bored dog vs. a happy dog with the duck to heighten the problem/solution contrast.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Run Expired Angles:** Never use specific holiday scripts ("Christmas gift") after the holiday has passed. It screams "old ad" and lowers trust.
2.  **Bury the Price:** If the price is a key selling point (€8.25), show it visually on screen earlier, not just verbally at the end.
3.  **Passive Filming:** Don't just film the dog eating. Show the *setup* (filling the toy) to prove how easy it is for the owner.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** The sound of dry food hitting the plastic or the dog crunching could be a strong ASMR hook.
2.  **Educational Twist:** "3 ways to keep your dog busy while you work" – position the toy as a productivity tool for the owner.
3.  **Bundle Deal:** If you have other shapes (not just Duck), show them all to encourage multi-buys.
