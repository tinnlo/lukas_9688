## 13. Angle Recommendations (3) | 三个脚本角度建议

### Angle 1: The "Home Office Savior" (Problem/Solution)
*   **Concept:** Focus on the owner needing to work and the dog being annoying/bored. The toy is the babysitter.
    *   DE: Fokus auf den Besitzer, der arbeiten muss, während der Hund nervt/gelangweilt ist. Das Spielzeug ist der Babysitter.
    *   ZH: 侧重于主人需要工作而狗狗烦人/无聊。这个玩具是“保姆”。
*   **Visual Flow:** Owner typing stressed -> Dog barking -> Owner sets up Duck Toy -> Dog plays happily -> Owner sighs with relief -> "Dogs play happily without owners" (`product_image_8.webp`).
*   **Hook Text:** "Wie ich im Homeoffice endlich Ruhe habe." (How I finally get peace in home office.)

### Angle 2: The "Smart Dog Challenge" (Educational/Entertainment)
*   **Concept:** Treat it as an IQ test. Will the dog figure it out? Builds curiosity.
    *   DE: Behandle es als IQ-Test. Wird der Hund es kapieren? Baut Neugier auf.
    *   ZH: 把它当作智商测试。狗狗能搞懂吗？建立好奇心。
*   **Visual Flow:** Dog staring at toy -> Pawing tentatively -> Accidentally hits pedal -> Food flies -> Dog's face lights up -> Dog does it repeatedly (`product_image_5.webp`).
*   **Hook Text:** "Ist dein Hund schlau genug für dieses Spielzeug?" (Is your dog smart enough for this toy?)

### Angle 3: The "Healthy Eater" (Health/Feature)
*   **Concept:** Focus on "Slow Feeder" benefits. Contrast with a dog choking/eating too fast from a bowl.
    *   DE: Fokus auf "Slow Feeder"-Vorteile. Kontrast zu einem Hund, der schlingt/sich verschluckt.
    *   ZH: 侧重“慢食”益处。对比狗狗狼吞虎咽/呛到的情况。
*   **Visual Flow:** Scary fast eating sound/visual -> Cut to Duck Toy dispensing one by one -> "Dogs control their diet" (`product_image_4.webp`) -> Happy, calm dog.
*   **Hook Text:** "Schlingt dein Hund sein Essen? Probier das!" (Does your dog gulp food? Try this!)
