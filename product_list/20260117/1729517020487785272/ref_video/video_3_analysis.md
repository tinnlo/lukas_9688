## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Test Hook Variations:** Try "Why Germans love this..." or "Don't buy toilet paper until you see this."
2.  **Visualize the Savings:** Show a calculator graphic comparing store prices vs. this bulk deal.
3.  **ASMR Elements:** Add the sound of the pack landing with a heavy thud to emphasize density/value.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't be boring:** Never just say "Toilet paper for sale." Always sell the *stockpile/security*.
2.  **Don't ignore specs:** Ensure "3-ply" is highlighted visually; German consumers are picky about quality.
3.  **Don't drag it out:** Keep it under 20s. This 18s duration is perfect.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Prepper" Angle:** Lean harder into "Be prepared for anything" (lighthearted doom-prepping).
2.  **Storage Hack:** Show *where* to store 64 rolls (under bed, top of cupboard) to resolve space objections.
3.  **Subscription:** If possible, mention "Abo" (Subscription) potential for even more "peace of mind."
