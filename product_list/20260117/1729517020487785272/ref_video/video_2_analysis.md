- **Effectiveness | 有效性:** High. It acts as a pattern interrupt by sounding like a genuine recommendation from a friend rather than a polished commercial.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Emphasize Bulk Delivery:** Mention "Free delivery to your door" (Kostenlose Lieferung), as carrying toilet paper is a major pain point in Germany (no cars in cities).
2. **Show Price Math:** Visual overlay: "Only 0.30€ per roll!" (Nur 0,30€ pro Rolle!).
3. **Use "Stock Up" Angle:** "Reicht für 6 Monate" (Lasts for 6 months).

**DON'T:**
1. **Over-edit:** Do not use fancy transitions that look like a TV ad. Keep it raw.
2. **Forget the Sound:** Do not rely on text only; the voiceover's urgency drives the sale.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Crinkling the pack or tapping the rolls to show density/quality.
2. **Storage Hack:** Show how to store 64 rolls in a small German apartment/bathroom.
