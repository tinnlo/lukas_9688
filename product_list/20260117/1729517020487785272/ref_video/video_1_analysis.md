## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Emphasize "Free Delivery":** If applicable, stress that this huge box comes to your door (no carrying).
2.  **Show the math:** Overlay text like "0.40€ per roll" to make the savings explicit.
3.  **Keep the humor:** The "saving the day" hero arc works well for household goods.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't be too serious:** It's toilet paper. Keep it lighthearted.
2.  **Don't hide the price:** The "<30€" is a major trigger; ensure it's visible on screen, not just spoken.
3.  **Don't delay the product reveal:** The reveal at ~15-20s is perfect; any later and viewers might scroll.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Test a version focusing solely on the "Softness" and sound of the pack (satisfying squeeze).
2.  **Space Saving Hack:** Show *how* to store 64 rolls (e.g., "Tetris" stacking) – this addresses the objection "I have no space".
3.  **Subscription Angle:** "Never run out again" – push the mental peace of mind aspect.
