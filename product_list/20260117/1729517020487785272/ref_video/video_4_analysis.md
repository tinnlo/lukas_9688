## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO | 建议做:**
1.  **Emphasize "No Carrying"**: For the German market, the "Schleppen" (hauling heavy bags) pain point is #1. Lean harder into visuals of struggling with bags vs. relaxing at home.
2.  **Answer the Riddle**: Respond to comments guessing the "2.5 reason" to drive engagement further.
3.  **Price Breakdown**: Add a text overlay showing "Price per roll" (€0.XX/Rolle) – Germans are price-sensitive (Sparfüchse).

**DON'T | 建议不做:**
1.  **Don't be too serious**: Toilet paper is a boring product; if you are serious, it's a boring ad. Keep the humor.
2.  **Don't hide the bulk**: Never film just one roll. Always show the massive box to justify the shipping/order.

**OPPORTUNITY | 机会:**
1.  **Subscription Angle**: "Imagine never running out again" – sell the peace of mind.
2.  **Guest Appearance**: Have "Jürgen" actually appear and complain about the toilet paper running out, then getting hit by a flying pack of 72.
3.  **Space Test**: Show where to store 72 rolls (e.g., under bed, top of cupboard) to overcome the objection "I have no space".
