## Production Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge 1: Getting Authentic Reaction

**Problem:** Partner might be aware of camera/setup  
**Solution 1:** Film multiple takes ("I want to capture your reaction for memories")  
**Solution 2:** Use alternative narrative: "I surprised her LAST WEEK, here's the footage"

### Challenge 2: Privacy Concerns

**Problem:** Partner may not want face on TikTok  
**Solution 1:** Blur face but keep body language/audio  
**Solution 2:** Shoot from behind (focus on room, not faces)  
**Solution 3:** Get explicit consent beforehand

### Challenge 3: Lighting for Reaction

**Problem:** Bedroom might be dim (romantic lighting kills camera quality)  
**Solution:** Film reaction in doorway (better lit) THEN enter room
