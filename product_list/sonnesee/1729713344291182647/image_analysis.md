## Compliance Notes for Scripts | 脚本合规注意事项

### Must-Include Compliance Elements (必须包含的合规元素)

**Category: Beauty Supplement (Schönheits-Nahrungsergänzungsmittel) (类别：美容补充剂)**

**⚠️ CRITICAL COMPLIANCE WARNINGS (⚠️ 关键合规警告):**

1. **No Medical Claims (不做医疗声明):**
   - DO NOT claim: "treats hair loss," "cures acne," "prevents nail disease"
   - CAN say: "supports," "contributes to," "helps maintain," "promotes"
   - **不允许声称:** "治疗脱发"、"治愈痤疮"、"预防指甲疾病"
   - **可以说:** "支持"、"有助于"、"帮助维持"、"促进"

2. **Dosage Accuracy (剂量准确):**
   - Always specify: "2 gummies daily" (not "take as needed")
   - 始终指定："每日2粒"（而非"按需服用"）

3. **Timeframe Realism (时间框架现实性):**
   - Avoid specific "results in X days" claims
   - Use: "supports with regular use," "contributes to over time"
   - 避免具体的"X天内见效"声明
   - 使用:"支持定期使用"、"随时间有助于"

4. **Supplement Disclaimer (补充剂免责声明):**
   - "Nahrungsergänzungsmittel ersetzen keine ausgewogene Ernährung"
   - Supplements do not replace a balanced diet
   - 补充剂不能替代均衡饮食

5. **Individual Results Variation (个人结果差异):**
   - Acknowledge that results may vary between individuals
   - 承认个人之间结果可能不同

**Allowed Language (允许的语言):**

✅ **PERMITTED (允许):**
- "Unterstützt gesundes Haarwachstum" (Supports healthy hair growth)
- "Trägt zu strahlender Haut bei" (Contributes to radiant skin)
- "Hilft, starke Nägel zu erhalten" (Helps maintain strong nails)
- "Unterstützt dich bei deinem Glow-up" (Supports your glow-up)
