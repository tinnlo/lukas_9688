### 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize for Germany:** Use German TTS (Text-to-Speech) or voiceover (e.g., "Das perfekte Geschenk für sie"). Add German text overlays.
2.  **Humanize:** Show a person's reaction to receiving the gift, not just the product in isolation.
3.  **Add a specific Offer:** Mention "50% Rabatt" (50% Off) or "Kostenloser Versand" (Free Shipping) to drive immediate clicks.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on English Audio:** In the DACH region, native language ads significantly outperform English ones for mass-market goods.
2.  **Leave the Ending Empty:** Don't just fade out; use a "Jetzt kaufen" (Buy Now) arrow or sticker.
3.  **Use Dark Lighting:** Jewelry needs bright, sparkling light to look valuable.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"POV" Angle:** "POV: You found the perfect gift for Grandma" (localized to German).
2.  **Seasonal Pivot:** If Valentine's is approaching, explicitly mention "Valentinstag" in the text overlays.
3.  **Reply-to-Comment:** Film the video as a reply to a comment asking "What should I get my girlfriend?" to boost engagement.
