## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Show the Screen ON:** If the product has a touchscreen case, **film it lighting up and being swiped**. This is the "Wow" factor.
2.  **Demo the Translation:** Split screen: Show someone speaking German, and the earbuds playing English (or text appearing on the app). Visual proof of the "Translation" claim is mandatory.
3.  **Add Text Overlays:** Key benefits like "Echtzeit-Übersetzung" (Real-time translation) should pop up on screen to reinforce the audio.

**DON'T (不要):**
1.  **Don't use static hands for 26 seconds:** Cut between the product in hand, the product in ear, and the app interface.
2.  **Don't claim features you don't show:** Talking about "taking photos" via the case while showing a turned-off case creates skepticism.
3.  **Don't trail off the CTA:** Make the CTA visual (arrow pointing to link) and verbal ("Order now for 50% off").

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **Travel Scenario:** Film this outside (train station/airport). "Pov: You are in London and don't speak English..." contextualizes the product immediately.
2.  **Live Test:** A "Street Interview" style clip where you use the earbuds to talk to a stranger. This builds massive trust.
3.  **Gift Angle:** "Perfect gift for parents who travel" targets the younger demographic buying for older relatives.
