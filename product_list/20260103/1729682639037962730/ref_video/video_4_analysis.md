1.  **Pin the Comment:** If this is a reply, pin the original question visually in the video. | **置顶评论：** 如果这是回复，在视频中视觉上置顶原始问题。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Mumble:** The "Kiroi" and "Karamektasse" slips are funny but might sound unprofessional to some. | **含糊不清：** "Kiroi"和"Karamektasse"的口误很有趣，但对某些人来说可能听起来不专业。

**OPPORTUNITY:**

2.  **Heat Retention:** Use the scale setup to also show a thermometer test (Keep hot vs Ceramic). | **保温性：**利用秤的设置同时展示温度计测试（保温 vs 陶瓷）。
3.  **"Is it Heavy?" Series:** Make a series weighing different car parts vs the cup. | **“它重吗？”系列：** 制作一个系列，称重不同的汽车零件与杯子进行对比。
