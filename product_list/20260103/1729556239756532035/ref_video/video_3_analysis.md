## 13. Recommendations | 建议

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Overlay the Price Visually:** Ensure "€20.94" is huge text on screen at 00:00.
2.  **Use ASMR Audio:** Amplify the "scratching" sound when the cat scratches the jute to trigger sensory appeal.
3.  **Add "Limited Stock" Badge:** German buyers respond well to "Nur noch wenige verfügbar" (Only a few left) to increase urgency.

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't hide the size:** Ensure the cat provides scale. If the product looks too small, returns will be high.
2.  **Avoid Robot Voice:** If the VO sounds too robotic (mispronouncing "orange"), it hurts trust. Use a native German speaker or high-quality ElevenLabs clone.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **"Unboxing" Angle:** Show the assembly (if easy). "Ready in 2 minutes" is a great hook for furniture.
2.  **Comparison:** "Stop buying cardboard scratchers that break in a week." Contrast durability of Jute vs. Cardboard.
