## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add German Voiceover:** Explicitly state the problem ("Does your cat scratch your sofa?") and solution ("Get this chic lounge instead!").
    **添加德语配音：** 明确陈述问题（“你的猫抓沙发吗？”）和解决方案（“买这个别致的躺椅吧！”）。
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** "Katzen lieben es!" (Cats love it!), "50% Rabatt" (50% Off), "Kratzfest" (Scratch-proof).
    **添加文字叠加：** “Katzen lieben es!”（猫咪喜欢它！），“50% Rabatt”（五折），“Kratzfest”（防抓）。
3.  **Include ASMR:** Record the sound of the cat scratching the jute texture.
    **包含ASMR：** 录制猫抓黄麻纹理的声音。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on silence:** Silent ads rarely perform well on TikTok unless they are oddly satisfying visuals (which this is too static for).
    **不要依赖静音：** 静音广告在TikTok上很少表现良好，除非是奇怪的令人满足的视觉效果（而这个太静态了）。
2.  **Don't ignore the CTA:** Tell people where to click.
    **不要忽略CTA：** 告诉人们点击哪里。
3.  **Don't be too short:** 8s is fine if dense, but this is 8s of "emptiness". Stretch to 15s with a story arc.
    **不要太短：** 如果内容丰富，8秒可以，但这是“空洞”的8秒。延长到15秒并加入故事弧。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"POV" Angle:** "POV: You finally found a cat bed that looks good in your living room."
    **"POV"视角：** “POV：你终于找到了一个放在客厅里很好看的猫窝。”
2.  **Comparison:** Split screen showing a destroyed sofa vs. this scratching bed.
    **对比：** 分屏展示被毁坏的沙发 vs 这个抓挠床。
3.  **Assembly Speed:** Fast-forward showing how easy it is to set up.
    **组装速度：** 快进展示安装是多么容易。
