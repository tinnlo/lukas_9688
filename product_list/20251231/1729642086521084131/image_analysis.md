## 7. Key Differentiators (vs. Competitors) | 关键差异化（与竞争对手相比）

### 1. "Innen- und Außenbereich" Claim (室内外声明)
- **Differentiation:** Most plush beds are strictly indoor. This claims outdoor utility.
- **Why it matters:** Implies superior durability, water resistance, or thermal insulation.
- **Caution:** Likely means "covered porch" or "camping tent" style, not rainproof.
- **差异化:** 大多数毛绒床仅限室内。这款声称可用于室外。意味着卓越的耐用性、防水性或隔热性。

### 2. High-Density Foam Structure (高密度泡沫结构)
- **Differentiation:** Cheap beds use loose poly-fill (stuffing) which lumps together. Foam maintains the "House" shape.
- **Visual Proof:** Sharp corners and upright roof.
- **差异化:** 廉价床使用松散的聚酯填充物，容易结块。泡沫保持"房子"的形状。

### 3. "Ganzjahres" (All-Season) Versatility
- **Differentiation:** Not just a winter bed.
- **How:** Breathable material prevents overheating, or removable cushion allows summer use.
- **全季多功能性:** 不仅仅是冬天的床。透气材料防止过热，或可拆卸垫子允许夏季使用。

2. **"The Safe Haven" (安全港湾)**
   - **How to film:** Throw a treat inside; capture the pet running in and turning around to peek out.
   - **Why it works:** Triggers "Cute" response + shows product function (security/den).
   - **Script hook:** "Der perfekte Rückzugsort für ängstliche Fellnasen." (The perfect retreat for anxious fur-noses.)

3. **"The 2-in-1 Transformation" (2合1变形 - If applicable)**
   - **How to film:** Push the roof down firmly to convert the house into an open sofa bed.
   - **Why it works:** Shows value (2 products in 1), "Magic" moment.
   - **Script hook:** "Im Winter eine Höhle, im Sommer ein Sofa. Genial, oder?" (A cave in winter, a sofa in summer. Genius, right?)
   - **如何拍摄:** 用力向下推屋顶，将房子变成开放式沙发床。

4. **"The Damp Test" (潮湿测试)**
   - **How to film:** Place the bed on a slightly wet patio or wipe the bottom with a wet cloth to show it doesn't soak through.
   - **Why it works:** Validates "Indoor/Outdoor" claim.
   - **Script hook:** "Endlich ein Bett, das auch auf dem Balkon trocken bleibt." (Finally a bed that stays dry even on the balcony.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 将床放在稍微湿润的露台上或用湿布擦拭底部，显示它不会湿透。

5. **"The Washing Machine" (洗衣机)**
   - **How to film:** Unzip/Remove the inner cushion and toss it into the washing machine.
   - **Why it works:** Solves a major pain point (stinky dog beds).
   - **Script hook:** "Und wenn es schmutzig wird? Einfach ab in die Wäsche." (And if it gets dirty? Just into the wash.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 拉开拉链/取出内垫并将其扔进洗衣机。

6. **"The Unboxing Explosion" (开箱爆炸)**
