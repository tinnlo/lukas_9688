## 10. Visual Hooks for TikTok Scripts | TikTok 脚本的视觉钩子

### Priority Visual Elements (按优先级排序)

1. **"The Monster Wake Up" (怪物苏醒)**
   - **How to film:** Start in total darkness. Suddenly plug in the USB/turn on the switch. The mouth glows, illuminating the sharp teeth.
   - **Why it works:** High contrast, sudden visual change, triggers "fight or flight" attention.
   - **Script hook:** "Hast du Angst im Dunkeln? Das solltest du..." (Are you afraid of the dark? You should be...)
   - **如何拍摄:** 在完全黑暗中开始。突然插入 USB/打开开关。嘴巴发光，照亮锋利的牙齿。

2. **"The Biomechanical Pan" (生物机械摇摄)**
   - **How to film:** Macro lens/Close-up. Slow pan from the tail, up the ribbed spine, ending on the face. Use dramatic side lighting.
   - **Why it works:** Showcases the intricate detail and quality (value proposition).
   - **Script hook:** "Jedes Detail ein Meisterwerk. Nicht nur eine Lampe, sondern Kunst." (Every detail a masterpiece. Not just a lamp, but art.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 微距镜头/特写。从尾巴缓慢摇摄，沿着棱纹脊柱向上，结束于脸部。使用戏剧性的侧光。

3. **"The Setup Upgrade" (设置升级)**
   - **How to film:** Show a boring, plain desk. Snap fingers -> Cut to desk with Alien lamp glowing + RGB keyboard.
   - **Why it works:** Transformation/Before-After is a classic satisfying format.
   - **Script hook:** "Dein Gaming-Setup ist langweilig? Hier ist die Lösung." (Your gaming setup is boring? Here is the solution.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 展示一张无聊、普通的桌子。打响指 -> 切换到带有外星人灯发光 + RGB 键盘的桌子。

4. **"Scale Reference" (尺寸参考)**
   - **How to film:** Hand placing a soda can or gaming mouse next to it for scale, then caressing the "head".
   - **Why it works:** Helps viewers understand physical size and material texture.
   - **Script hook:** "Perfekte Größe für deinen Schreibtisch." (Perfect size for your desk.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 手放一罐苏打水或游戏鼠标在它旁边作为比例参考，然后抚摸"头"。

5. **"The Scare Prank" (惊吓恶作剧)**
   - **How to film:** POV of someone walking into a dark room and seeing the silhouette/glowing teeth and jumping back.
   - **Why it works:** Humor/Relatability, entertainment value increases retention.
   - **Script hook:** "Mein Besuch hat sich fast in die Hose gemacht!" (My visitor almost peed their pants!)
   - **如何拍摄:**某人走进黑暗房间并看到剪影/发光的牙齿并向后跳的 POV。

6. **"USB Plug-in Action" (USB 插入动作)**
   - **How to film:** Close up of plugging the USB into a PC port, instant light on.
   - **Why it works:** Demonstrates ease of use and compatibility.
   - **Script hook:** "Keine Batterien nötig. Einfach an den PC anschließen." (No batteries needed. Simply connect to the PC.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 将 USB 插入 PC 端口的特写，灯瞬间亮起。
