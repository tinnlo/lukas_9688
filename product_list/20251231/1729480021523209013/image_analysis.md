## 10. Visual Hooks for TikTok Scripts | TikTok 脚本的视觉钩子

### Priority Visual Elements (按优先级排序)

1. **"The Hand Glider" (滑手动作)**
   - **How to film:** Close up of a hand running AGAINST the grain of the plush. Show the fibers standing up and changing shade.
   - **Why it works:** Visually proves softness and thickness. It's satisfying (ASMR visual).
   - **Script hook:** "Schau dir diese Dichte an. Das ist kein billiges Fleece." (Look at this density. This is not cheap fleece.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 特写镜头，手逆着毛绒纹理滑动。展示纤维竖起并改变色调。

2. **"The Fluff Toss" (蓬松抛掷)**
   - **How to film:** Hold the blanket high and throw it onto the bed. It should land with a heavy, air-cushioned "whump" or settle slowly.
   - **Why it works:** Demonstrates weight and volume.
   - **Script hook:** "Schwer, dick und bereit für den Winter." (Heavy, thick, and ready for winter.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 把毯子举高，扔到床上。它应该伴随着沉重的、空气缓冲的"砰"声落地，或者缓慢沉降。

3. **"The Burrito Wrap" (卷饼包裹)**
   - **How to film:** A person rolling themselves tightly in the blanket until only their face is visible.
   - **Why it works:** Humor + Cozy vibes. Relatable desire for warmth.
   - **Script hook:** "Ich verlasse dieses Bett nie wieder." (I am never leaving this bed again.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 一个人用毯子紧紧裹住自己，只露出脸。

4. **"Double Layer Reveal" (双层揭示)**
   - **How to film:** Pinch the two layers of the blanket separately to show they are distinct, then rub them together.
   - **Why it works:** Educates the viewer on *why* it is warm (insulation).
   - **Script hook:** "Doppellagig für doppelte Wärme." (Double-layered for double warmth.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 分别捏住毯子的两层，显示它们是分开的，然后摩擦在一起。

5. **"The Face Rub" (脸部摩擦)**
   - **How to film:** Model gently rubbing their cheek against the surface with closed eyes.
   - **Why it works:** Pure emotional connection. Simulates the feeling of skin contact.
   - **Script hook:** "Kratzt nicht, fusselt nicht. Einfach nur weich." (Doesn't scratch, doesn't lint. Just pure soft.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 模特闭着眼睛，脸颊轻轻摩擦毯子表面。

6. **"Light Reflection" (光线反射)**
   - **How to film:** Slowly moving a light source or tilting the blanket under a lamp to see the sheen ripple.
   - **Why it works:** Premium aesthetic cue.
   - **Script hook:** "So luxuriös, dass es glänzt." (So luxurious that it shines.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 慢慢移动光源或在灯下倾斜毯子，看光泽像波纹一样流动。
