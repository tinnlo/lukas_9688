## 10. Visual Hooks for TikTok Scripts | TikTok 脚本的视觉钩子

### Priority Visual Elements (按优先级排序)

1. **"The Double Proof" (双重证据)**
   - **How to film:** Split screen effect or pointing to the device screen showing BOTH front road view and inside driver view.
   - **Why it works:** Instantly explains the "Dual Lens" value prop. Perfect evidence.
   - **Script hook:** "Eine Kamera für die Straße, eine für dich. Doppelter Schutz." (One camera for the road, one for you. Double protection.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 分屏效果或指向设备屏幕，同时显示前方道路视图和内部驾驶员视图。

2. **"The Night Watch" (夜视)**
   - **How to film:** Film inside a dark car, then snap fingers and show the bright, clear IR footage on the dashcam screen.
   - **Why it works:** Demonstrates the IR feature visually. "Wow" factor.
   - **Script hook:** "Dunkles Auto? Kein Problem. Die Nachtsicht sieht alles." (Dark car? No problem. The night vision sees everything.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 在黑暗的车内拍摄，然后打响指，展示行车记录仪屏幕上明亮、清晰的红外画面。

3. **"The Freebie Reveal" (免费赠品揭秘)**
   - **How to film:** Hold the dashcam, then dramatically pull the 32GB SD card out of the box/slot and hold it to lens.
   - **Why it works:** Value anchoring. Everyone hates buying separate SD cards.
   - **Script hook:** "Und das Beste? Die 32GB Speicherkarte ist schon dabei!" (And the best part? The 32GB memory card is already included!)
   - **如何拍摄:** 拿着行车记录仪，然后戏剧性地将32GB SD卡从盒子/插槽中拿出并对着镜头展示。

4. **"The Idiot-Proof Setup" (傻瓜式安装)**
   - **How to film:** Fast motion (timelapse) of sticking it to windshield and plugging into cigarette lighter. Done in 3 seconds.
   - **Why it works:** Overcomes "it's too hard to install" objection.
   - **Script hook:** "In 30 Sekunden installiert. Einstecken, fertig." (Installed in 30 seconds. Plug in, done.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 快速动作（延时摄影）将其粘在挡风玻璃上并插入点烟器。3秒完成。

5. **"The Rotator" (旋转器)**
   - **How to film:** Close up of hand physically rotating the inside lens to point at driver, then passenger, then back.
   - **Why it works:** Shows mechanical adjustability and versatility.
   - **Script hook:** "Die Innenkamera ist drehbar – du entscheidest, was sie sieht." (The inside camera is rotatable – you decide what it sees.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 特写手物理旋转内部镜头，指向驾驶员，然后是乘客，然后转回。

6. **"The Insurance Saver" (保险救星)**
   - **How to film:** Point to the dashcam recording, then overlay a text bubble "€€€ Saved".
   - **Why it works:** Greed/Fear appeal. The cam pays for itself.
   - **Script hook:** "Dieser kleine Kasten kann dir Tausende Euro sparen." (This little box can save you thousands of euros.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 指向行车记录仪录像，然后叠加气泡文字"节省€€€"。
