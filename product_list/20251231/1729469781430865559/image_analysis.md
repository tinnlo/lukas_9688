## 10. Visual Hooks for TikTok Scripts | TikTok 脚本的视觉钩子

### Priority Visual Elements (按优先级排序)

1. **"The Light Up" (点亮瞬间)**
   - **How to film:** Close up of finger touching the mirror button -> Light turns on instantly -> Face glows.
   - **Why it works:** Instant gratification, visual "magic", demonstrates core feature.
   - **Script hook:** "Dein Licht ist schlecht? Upgrade auf diesen LED-Spiegel!" (Your light is bad? Upgrade to this LED mirror!)
   - **如何拍摄:** 特写手指触摸镜子按钮 -> 灯瞬间亮起 -> 脸部发光。

2. **"The Power Plug" (电源插头)**
   - **How to film:** Phone showing "Low Battery" (red) -> Hand plugs cable into the table's USB port -> Phone "Charging" (green).
   - **Why it works:** Solves a relatable modern pain point immediately.
   - **Script hook:** "Nie wieder leerer Akku beim Schminken." (Never an empty battery while doing makeup again.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 手机显示"低电量"（红色） -> 手将线插入桌子的 USB 端口 -> 手机"充电中"（绿色）。

3. **"The Spill Test" (溢出测试)**
   - **How to film:** Deliberately spill a bit of coffee/water on the white surface -> Wipe it clean in one swipe with a cloth.
   - **Why it works:** "Oddly satisfying", proves durability/quality claim "Wasserfest".
   - **Script hook:** "Angst vor Flecken? Dieser Tisch ist wasserfest und super leicht zu reinigen." (Afraid of stains? This table is waterproof and super easy to clean.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 故意在白色表面倒一点咖啡/水 -> 用布一擦即净。

4. **"The Organization" (整理过程)**
   - **How to film:** Speed ramp (fast forward) of filling the empty shelves with colorful makeup bottles and perfumes.
   - **Why it works:** ASMR appeal, "Before/After" satisfaction, shows storage capacity.
   - **Script hook:** "Von Chaos zu Ordnung in 6 Regalen." (From chaos to order in 6 shelves.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 变速（快进）将空架子装满彩色化妆瓶和香水。

5. **"The Scratch" (刮擦测试)**
   - **How to film:** Take a key and pretend to scratch the surface (or lightly do it) to show no marks.
   - **Why it works:** Shock value, proves "Kratzfest" claim.
   - **Script hook:** "Kratzfest? Lass es uns testen." (Scratch proof? Let's test it.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 拿一把钥匙假装刮表面（或轻轻刮）以显示没有痕迹。

6. **"The Selfie View" (自拍视角)**
   - **How to film:** Filming the mirror reflection showing the ring light effect in the user's eyes.
   - **Why it works:** Shows the end result (better looking selfies).
   - **Script hook:** "Das perfekte Licht für deine TikToks." (The perfect light for your TikToks.)
   - **如何拍摄:** 拍摄镜子反射，显示用户眼中的环形灯效果。
