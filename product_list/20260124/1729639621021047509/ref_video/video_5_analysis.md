## 12. Replication Insights

- **Equipment Needed:** Smartphone with good camera, tripod (optional but helpful for two-hand demo), natural light.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show "Before" State:** Start with a messy, cluttered counter *before* showing the "Gamechanger" shelf. The contrast creates immediate visual desire.
2. **Text Overlays:** Add dynamic text syncing with key words like "ADJUSTABLE" (Verstellbar) or "MORE SPACE" (Mehr Platz) to catch viewers watching without sound.
3. **Specific Price Anchor:** If it is "very cheap", state the price if it's under €20. "Unter 20€!" is a strong click driver.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Audio Alone:** Don't assume everyone hears "Gamechanger". Show it visually immediately.
2. **Hide the Mechanism:** Don't just show the result. The *act* of extending the shelf is the "satisfying" part that retains attention.
3. **Forget Dimensions:** Don't leave viewers guessing if it will fit *their* microwave. Mentioning "Universal fit" or showing different items helps.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Record the sounds of assembling or placing items on the metal shelf. ASMR organization videos are a huge niche.
2. **"Pack with Me" or "Clean with Me":** Frame the product as part of a larger Sunday cleaning routine, which is very popular on German TikTok (#putzroutine).
3. **Scarcity:** Add a text overlay "Low Stock" or "50% Off ends soon" to improve that 0.03% conversion rate.
