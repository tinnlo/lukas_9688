## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays (German):** Add explicit benefits like "Mehr Platz in 5 Minuten" (More space in 5 mins) or "Rostfreier Stahl" (Stainless steel).
2.  **Speed Up:** Compress the assembly to 5-8 seconds max. Spend more time on the *Transformation*.
3.  **Voiceover:** Add a TTS (Text-to-Speech) or human voiceover explaining *why* this specific shelf is good (e.g., "Adjustable height for any microwave").

**DON'T:**
1.  **Start with Parts:** Don't start the video with screws and bars. Start with the **Messy Kitchen** (Pain) or the **Beautiful Kitchen** (Desire).
2.  **Leave it Silent:** Don't rely on music alone. Algorithm favors videos with spoken keywords (SEO).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"ASMR Organization" Angle:** Focus entirely on the sounds of placing jars and plates (clinking, sliding) for a sensory satisfaction hook.
2.  **"Renter Friendly" Angle:** Emphasize that it requires no drilling into walls (key for German renters). Use text: "Kein Bohren!" (No drilling).
