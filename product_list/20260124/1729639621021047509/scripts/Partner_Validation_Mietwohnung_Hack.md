## Scripts

**Hook (0-3s):** Partner Conflict → Relatability
- Visual: Couple in kitchen, one pointing at cluttered countertop, other looking skeptical
- Text: "Mein Freund wollte es nicht..." (My boyfriend didn't want it...)
- Audio: Brief conversation snippet

**The Problem (3-8s):** Shared Frustration
- Visual: Show the messy reality - no counter space
- VO: Partner 1 explaining the problem
- Partner 2 nodding ("Das stimmt")

**The Constraint (8-12s):** Renter Reality
- Visual: Hold up a drill with a red X overlay
- Show the lease/contract document briefly
- Text: "Mietwohnung = Kein Bohren!" (Rental = No drilling!)

**The Solution (12-20s):** Product Introduction
- Visual: Unpacking and placing the shelf
- Show it standing over microwave - no installation required
- Demonstrate the expansion (38-64cm)
- Text: "Einfach draufstellen - fertig!" (Just place it - done!)

**The Proof (20-30s):** Stability Demonstration
- Visual: Partner 2 (the skeptic) loads the shelf
- Performs shake test
- Reaction: Surprised/impressed face
- Text: "Wow, das hält echt!" (Wow, it really holds!)

**The Transformation (30-37s):** After Shot
- Visual: Clean, organized kitchen
- Couple high-five or satisfied nod
- Text: "Er gibt es zu: Es ist genial" (He admits: It's brilliant)

**CTA (37-40s):** Couple Recommendation
- Visual: Both pointing to basket icon
- Text: "Perfekt für jede Mietwohnung"
