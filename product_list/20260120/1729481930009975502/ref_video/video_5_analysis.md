## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add Text Overlays:** MUST add German text pointing out benefits. e.g., *"Schont den Nacken"* (Protects the neck) or *"Kein Erbrechen mehr"* (No more vomiting).
2.  **Shorten the Video:** Cut to 7-12 seconds if there is no story. 33 seconds of silence is boring.
3.  **Add a Hook:** Start with a "Negative" state (cat struggling with a low bowl) then switch to the "Positive" (this raised bowl).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Rely on Music Only:** Germans read and listen. Silence (conceptually) implies a lack of effort or information.
2.  **Hide the Benefit:** Don't just show the bowl; show *why* the height matters (draw a line showing the straight spine).
3.  **Use "Lazy" Editing:** Avoid 3-second static shots. Use fast cuts on the beat of the music.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Whisker Fatigue" Angle:** Market wide bowls as preventing "Schnurrhaar-Stress" (Whisker stress/fatigue) - a high-converting angle in Germany.
2.  **ASMR:** If the ceramic makes a nice sound or the cat crunching is cute, amplify that natural sound instead of generic music.
3.  **Bilingual/Localize:** Even a TTS voice saying *"Das beste Geschenk für deine Katze"* (The best gift for your cat) would double engagement.
