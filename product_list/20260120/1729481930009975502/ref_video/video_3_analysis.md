- **Authenticity:** **Moderate | 中等**. It shows the real product, but lacks the "social proof" of a cat actually eating from it or an owner's voice.
- **Viral Elements:** **None | 无**. No humor, no cute cat moments, no satisfying ASMR sounds.

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a Cat! | 加一只猫！** A pet product video without the pet using it is a massive missed opportunity. Show a cat eating comfortably without bending its neck.
2.  **Add Text Overlays (German) | 添加德语字幕** Use text to highlight benefits: "Schont den Nacken" (Protects the neck), "Kein Erbrechen mehr" (No more vomiting), "Hochwertige Keramik" (High-quality ceramic).
3.  **Voiceover Hook | 旁白钩子** Start with a problem: "Hsst deine Katze es, sich so tief zu bücken?" (Does your cat hate bending down so low?).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on silence | 不要依赖静音/纯音乐** German audiences respond well to clear, logical explanations of value. Silence feels low-effort.
2.  **Don't make it 22s | 不要拖到22秒** If you have no story, cut it to 7-10 seconds. 22 seconds drags on for a static product.
3.  **Don't forget the 'Why' | 别忘了‘为什么’** Selling a raised bowl is selling *health*, not just a ceramic dish.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle | ASMR视角** The sound of kibble hitting ceramic + a happy cat purring could be a great sensory hook.
2.  **"Before & After" | “使用前/使用后”对比** Split screen: Cat struggling with a floor bowl vs. Cat eating happily from the raised bowl.
3.  **Gift Angle | 礼物视角** "Das perfekte Geschenk für deine Katze" (The perfect gift for your cat) - framing it as an act of love.
