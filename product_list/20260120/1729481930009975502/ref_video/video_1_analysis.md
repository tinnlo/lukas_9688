## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO | 建议做:**
1. **Show the Cat!** Use a clip of a cat actually eating comfortably from the bowl to prove the "raised" benefit (Social Proof).
   **展示猫咪！** 使用猫咪舒适地从碗里进食的片段，以证明“加高”的好处（社会证明）。
2. **Add German Text Overlays.** Keywords like *"Rückenschonend"* (Back-friendly) or *"Leicht zu reinigen"* (Easy to clean) are crucial for the DE market.
   **添加德语文字叠加。** 关键词如 *"Rückenschonend"*（护背）或 *"Leicht zu reinigen"*（易清洗）对于德国市场至关重要。
3. **Include a CTA.** A simple overlay "Jetzt bestellen" (Order now) at the end.
   **包含行动号召。** 在结尾添加简单的叠加文字 "Jetzt bestellen"（立即下单）。

**DON'T | 建议不做:**
1. **Don't rely on silence/music only.** Even a TTS voiceover explaining the benefit increases retention.
   **不要仅依赖静音/音乐。** 即使是解释好处的TTS（文本转语音）旁白也能提高留存率。
2. **Don't use static slides.** Ensure there is movement (camera pan or cat movement) to keep attention.
   **不要使用静态幻灯片。** 确保有动态画面（运镜或猫咪动作）以保持注意力。
3. **Don't ignore the "Why".** Just showing the product doesn't explain *why* a raised bowl is better than a floor bowl.
   **不要忽略“为什么”。** 仅展示产品无法解释*为什么*高脚碗比地板碗更好。

**OPPORTUNITY | 机会点:**
1. **ASMR Eating Sounds:** Cats crunching food is a popular niche trend that stops the scroll.
   **ASMR进食声：** 猫咪咀嚼食物的声音是一个能吸引停留的热门细分趋势。
2. **"Unboxing" Angle:** Show the packaging and easy assembly of the iron stand to build trust in quality.
   **“开箱”角度：** 展示包装和铁架的简易组装，以建立对质量的信任。
3. **Before/After Comparison:** Split screen showing a cat struggling with a low bowl vs. comfortable with this one.
   **使用前/后对比：** 分屏展示猫咪使用低碗时的费力 vs 使用此碗时的舒适。
