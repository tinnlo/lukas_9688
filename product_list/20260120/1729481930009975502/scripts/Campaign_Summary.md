**Visual Requirements (All Scripts):**
- MUST show cat actually eating from the bowl (not just product shots)
- Spine alignment overlay (green line) for health scripts is critical
- Ceramic "ting" sound test builds quality perception instantly

**German Copy Standards:**
- "Schnurrhaar-Stress" (Whisker Stress) is memorable and shareable
- "Ergonomisch" signals quality and science (trust word)
- "Keramik" must be emphasized over "Plastik" (material hierarchy)
- Avoid "heilt" (cures) - use "unterstützt" (supports) or "kann helfen" (can help)
- "Spülmaschinenfest" (dishwasher safe) = hygiene trust signal

**Compliance Warnings:**
- Do NOT claim "prevents all vomiting" or "cures arthritis" (medical claims)
- Do NOT say "unbreakable" (it's ceramic - will break if dropped)
- Dishwasher safe applies to CERAMIC BOWL only, not painted iron stand
- Price claims must reflect current TikTok Shop price (dynamic pricing)

## Risk Factors & Mitigation

**Fragility (Ceramic Breakage):**
- **Risk:** Customer drops bowl, breaks, demands refund
- **Mitigation:** Do NOT claim "unbreakable" or "sturdy" (apply to stand only)
- **Action:** Show careful handling in videos, emphasize ceramic = quality trade-off

**Sizing Perception:**
- **Risk:** 16cm may look small in photos, customers expect larger
- **Mitigation:** Show ruler measurement, demonstrate capacity with food pour
- **Action:** Include size context (next to wet food pouch, coffee mug, etc.)

**Stand Rust (Long-term):**
- **Risk:** Painted iron stand coating scratches → rusts with water exposure
- **Mitigation:** Show stand care (wipe clean, don't submerge in water)
- **Action:** Emphasize "bowl is dishwasher safe, stand hand-wipe only"

**Medical Claim Backlash:**
- **Risk:** German advertising law (UWG) prohibits unproven health claims
- **Mitigation:** Use "kann helfen" (can help), "unterstützt" (supports), never "heilt" (cures)
- **Action:** Frame as ergonomic design benefit, not medical device
