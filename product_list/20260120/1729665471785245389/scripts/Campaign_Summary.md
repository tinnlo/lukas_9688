## Creative Execution Notes

**Visual Requirements (All Scripts):**
- Must show actual wet dog (not just product shots)
- Slow-motion "shake" footage is critical for Scripts 1 & 2
- Macro shots of fabric texture build quality perception
- Clean, bright lighting (avoid dim/gloomy = "dirty dog" association)

**Audio/Music:**
- Script 1: Upbeat, problem-solving energy
- Script 2: Neutral, educational background track
- Script 3: Soft, peaceful ASMR-friendly music

**German Copy Standards:**
- Use "Hund" (dog) not "Haustier" (pet) - more direct
- "Nie wieder" (never again) is strong negative hook
- "Hochwertig" (high-quality) is trust word, use liberally
- Avoid medical claims ("verhindert Erkältung" = forbidden)
- "Maschinenwaschbar" (machine washable) = hygiene reassurance

**Compliance Warnings:**
- Do NOT claim "instantly dry" or "waterproof" (misleading)
- Do NOT show belly band too tight (animal welfare concern)
- Do include "Anzeige/Werbung" disclosure if sponsored
- Ensure 14-day return policy visible per German law

## German Market Psychology Integration

**Ordnung (Order/Cleanliness):**
- Scripts 1 & 2 emphasize protection of home/car environment
- Clean visual results (dry floors, pristine car seats) are hero shots
- "Sauberkeit" (cleanliness) is repeated value proposition

**Effizienz (Efficiency):**
- All scripts highlight time-saving ("5 Sekunden", "kein Kampf")
- Scripts show ease of use (Velcro, one-step process)

**Qualitätsbewusstsein (Quality Consciousness):**
- Script 2 directly addresses durability (lint test, washing)
- Material specifications ("Mikrofaser", "Premium") build trust
- Embroidery detail = craftsmanship signal
- Size chart precision reduces perceived risk of "cheap Chinese junk"

**Tierwohl (Animal Welfare):**
- All scripts show relaxed, happy dogs (never distressed)
- Script 3 emphasizes dog's comfort, not just owner convenience
- No restrictive/uncomfortable visuals (dog runs freely in robe)
