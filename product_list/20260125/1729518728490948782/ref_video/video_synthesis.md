    *   DE: "Das perfekte Lehrergeschenk" (The perfect teacher gift)

## Creative Production Patterns | 创意制作模式

### Recommended Storyboard Structure

*   **Hook (0-3s) (钩子):** Problem statement or eye-catching visual (问题陈述或吸睛视觉)
*   **Product Reveal (3-8s) (产品展示):** Unboxing or close-up of flowers (开箱或花朵特写)
*   **Features (8-25s) (特点):** Show gift box, realism, use cases (展示礼盒、逼真度、用途)
*   **Benefits (25-35s) (优势):** Cost savings, longevity, no maintenance (省钱、持久、免维护)
*   **CTA (35-40s) (行动号召):** Link in bio, limited stock (简介链接、限量库存)

### Visual Strategy (视觉策略)

*   **Lighting (灯光):** Soft, natural light to enhance realism (柔和自然光增强真实感)
*   **Setting (场景):** Clean, minimal background (简洁、极简背景)
*   **Camera (相机):** Close-ups of petals + full bouquet shots (花瓣特写+整束花镜头)

## Compliance & Trust Signals | 合规与信任信号

### DO's (推荐做法):
*   **Price (价格):** Use relative terms like "preiswert" / "günstig" (使用相对术语如"实惠"/"便宜")
*   **Quality (质量):** Emphasize "looks realistic" not "100% real" (强调"看起来很真实"而非"100%真实")
*   **Durability (耐用性):** "Bleibt schön" (Stays beautiful) - safe claim (保持美丽——安全的说法)

### DON'Ts (避免事项):
*   ❌ Avoid exact prices (避免精确价格) – prices change (价格会变化)
*   ❌ Don't claim "identical to real flowers" (不要声称"与真花完全相同") – can't prove (无法证明)
*   ❌ Don't guarantee "never fades" (不要保证"永不褪色") – too absolute (太绝对)
