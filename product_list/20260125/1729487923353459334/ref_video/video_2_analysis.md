## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Extend Duration:** Create a 7-15 second video showing the bouquet being held, arranged, or gifted.
   *(延长时长：制作一个7-15秒的视频，展示手持、摆放或赠送花束的场景。)*
2. **Add German Text:** Use overlays like "Das perfekte Geschenk für die Ewigkeit" (The perfect gift for eternity).
   *(添加德语字幕：使用如“永恒的完美礼物”之类的文字覆盖。)*
3. **Show Texture:** Use a close-up pan to show the crochet stitch details, proving the handmade quality.
   *(展示质感：使用特写镜头展示钩针细节，证明手工品质。)*

**DON'T:**
1. **Use Static Images as Video:** Avoid uploading single photos as 2-second MP4s; use TikTok's Photo Mode (Carousel) instead if you have static assets.
   *(不要将静态图片作为视频发布：避免将单张照片作为2秒MP4上传；如果有静态素材，请使用TikTok的照片模式/轮播。)*
2. **Ignore Audio:** Never post without trending audio or a voiceover explaining the product.
   *(忽视音频：发布时绝不要缺少流行音频或解释产品的旁白。)*
3. **Forget CTA:** Don't assume users know what to do; tell them to "Check the basket" (Warenkorb).
   *(忘记行动号召：不要假设用户知道该做什么；告诉他们“查看购物车”。)*

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Making Of" Timelapse:** Show the crochet process speeded up. This validates the "Handmade" claim and is highly satisfying/viral.
   *(“制作过程”延时摄影：加速展示钩针编织过程。这能验证“手工制作”的主张，且非常令人满足/易于传播。)*
2. **Gift Unboxing:** Film the bouquet coming out of a box to simulate the customer experience.
   *(礼物开箱：拍摄花束从盒子中取出的过程，模拟顾客体验。)*
3. **Comparison:** "Real flowers vs. Crochet flowers" (Dead in 3 days vs. Forever beautiful).
   *(对比：“鲜花 vs 钩针花”【3天枯萎 vs 永远美丽】。)*
