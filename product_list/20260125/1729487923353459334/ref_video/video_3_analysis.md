**DO:**
1. **Add German Text Overlays:** e.g., "Handgemachte Häkelblumen" (Handmade Crochet Flowers) or "Das perfekte Geschenk" (The perfect gift).
2. **Improve Lighting:** Use bright, soft daylight to make the colors pop. The product looks dull in bad light.
3. **Show Packaging:** Germans love gifting. Show the bouquet being wrapped or in a nice box to increase perceived value.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on English Audio Alone:** If using English audio, you MUST provide German captions for context.
2. **Use "Shakey" Cam:** For static decor products, use a tripod or very steady hands.
3. **End Abruptly:** 8 seconds is fine, but it needs a clear ending (e.g., placing it in a vase), not just a cut.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"Nie mehr verwelken" (Never wilt):** Market it as the sustainable flower that lasts forever.
2. **DIY/Process Angle:** Show 1 second of the crocheting process to prove it's handmade (High value signal).
3. **Occasion Targeting:** Tag specifically for anniversaries or birthdays in the text overlay ("Zum Jahrestag").
