## Scripts

**Concept:** Gift reaction storytelling—showing the emotional moment of receiving the bear. Creates FOMO through vicarious happiness.

### Structure (40s)

- **Hook (0-3s):** "Watch her reaction..." teaser text over blurred gift
- **Build-up (3-10s):** Wrapping, anticipation, hand over gift
- **Reaction (10-25s):** Unboxing, gasps, huge smile, "SO süß!"
- **Follow-up (25-33s):** She keeps it on nightstand, looks at it daily
- **CTA (33-40s):** Link to give same happiness, 2-pack = share joy
