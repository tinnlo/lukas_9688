## Creative Production Patterns | 创意制作模式

### Recommended Storyboard Structure

*   **Hook (0-3s) (钩子):** Relatable gift problem or cute reveal (礼物问题或可爱展示)
*   **Unboxing (3-10s) (开箱):** Show the bear + flower reveal (展示小熊+花朵)
*   **Cuteness Factor (10-20s) (可爱因素):** Close-ups, different angles, "aww" moments (特写、不同角度、"好可爱"时刻)
*   **Use Cases (20-30s) (用途):** Desk decoration, gift scenarios (桌面装饰、送礼场景)
*   **CTA (30-40s) (行动号召):** Link in bio, "makes a perfect gift" (简介链接、"完美礼物")

*   **Lighting (灯光):** Soft, warm lighting to enhance plush texture (柔和温暖灯光增强毛绒质感)
*   **Setting (场景):** Bedroom/desk setting for relatability (卧室/桌面场景增强共鸣)
*   **Camera (相机):** Close-ups of bear face + full display shots (小熊脸部特写+全景展示)
*   **Props (道具):** Cute desk items, pastel colors (可爱桌面用品、柔和色彩)

### DO's (推荐做法):

*   **Size (尺寸):** "Perfekt fürs Regal" (Perfect for shelf) - relative sizing (完美的架子尺寸——相对尺寸)

*   ❌ Don't claim "premium quality" without proof (不要无证据声称"高级质量")

*   ❌ Don't make durability guarantees (不要做耐用性保证)
