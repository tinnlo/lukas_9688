## 5. Quality Signals | 质量信号

*   **Seam Integrity:** Check for tight, invisible stitching, especially around the neck and limbs where tension is high. (接缝完整性：检查紧密、隐形的缝线，尤其是在张力较高的颈部和四肢周围。)

*   **Shape Retention:** The bear should hold its shape when squeezed, returning to its original form (good fill quality). (形状保持：挤压时小熊应保持形状，恢复原状（填充质量好）。)

*   **No Glue Residue:** Critical check for no visible glue strands around the flower attachment point. (无胶水残留：关键检查花朵连接点周围是否有可见的胶丝。)
*   **Odor:** Should be odorless; chemical smells indicate poor material processing. (气味：应无味；化学气味表明材料加工不良。)
*   **Uniformity:** Since it's a 2-count, both bears should look identical in size and quality. (一致性：既然是 2 只装，两只小熊在尺寸和质量上看起来应该完全相同。)
