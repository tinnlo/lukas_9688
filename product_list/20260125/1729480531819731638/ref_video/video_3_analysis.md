## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show "Before" State:** Ensure the "Before" mess is relatable but clearly chaotic to maximize the satisfaction of the "After".
2. **Text Overlay:** Add German text "Bad Organisation Hack" (Bathroom Organization Hack) to give context for scrollers with sound off.
3. **Link to Shop:** Ensure the yellow basket/shop link is explicitly pointed to or mentioned.

**DON'T:**
1. **Over-edit:** Keep it looking like a real person's bathroom, not a studio set.
2. **Hide the Product:** Don't cover the rack with *too* many items; let the structure be seen.
3. **Ignore Audio:** Do not use this audio without a "reveal" moment; the lyrics demand a payoff.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Version:** Record the sounds of bottles being placed on the rack (clinking/tapping) for an ASMR angle.
2. **Speed Clean:** Speed up the assembly/filling process to show how fast it is (Time-lapse).
3. **"Husband/Boyfriend" Angle:** "He said we don't need it..." (Conflict hook) -> "Now look" (Resolution).
