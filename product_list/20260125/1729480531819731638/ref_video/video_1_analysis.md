## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO | 建议做:**
1.  **Show Usage (展示用法):** Extend video to 15s+. Show putting items on the rack to demonstrate stability and capacity. | 延长视频至15秒以上。展示放置物品的过程以证明稳定性和容量。
2.  **Demonstrate "Easy Installation" (演示“易安装”):** If this is a key claim, show the assembly process (timelapse) to prove it. | 如果这是主要卖点，请展示组装过程（延时摄影）以证明。
3.  **Add German Voiceover (添加德语旁白):** Use a TTS or real voice saying "Endlich mehr Platz im Bad!" (Finally more space in the bathroom!). | 使用TTS或真人配音说“Endlich mehr Platz im Bad!”（浴室终于有更多空间了！）。

**DON'T | 建议不做:**
1.  **Avoid Static Slides (避免静态幻灯片):** TikTok is a video platform; static images get skipped immediately. | TikTok是视频平台；静态图片会被立即划过。
2.  **Don't Ignore Audio (不要忽视音频):** Use trending sounds or spoken hooks. | 使用流行音乐 or 口语钩子。
3.  **Don't rely on 2 seconds (不要依赖2秒):** The algorithm barely registers a view before the video ends. | 算法在视频结束前几乎无法记录一次观看。

**OPPORTUNITY | 机会:**
1.  **ASMR Restocking (ASMR补货):** Sounds of placing bottles/jars on the rack can be satisfying and viral. | 放置瓶罐的声音可以很解压且具传播性。
2.  **Before/After (使用前/后):** Show a messy counter vs. organized rack. | 展示凌乱的台面 vs 整理后的置物架。
3.  **Weight Test (承重测试):** Put heavy items (shampoo bottles) to prove sturdiness. | 放置重物（洗发水瓶）以证明坚固性。
