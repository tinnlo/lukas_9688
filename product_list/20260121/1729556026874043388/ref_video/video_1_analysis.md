## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Create Video Content (制作视频内容):** Extend duration to at least 7-10 seconds. Show the pet *interacting* with the bed (kneading, sleeping). | 延长时间至至少7-10秒。展示宠物与床的*互动*（踩奶、睡觉）。
2.  **Demonstrate "Non-slip" (演示"防滑"):** Show the bottom of the bed or try to push it to prove it stays in place. | 展示床底或尝试推动它以证明其固定不动。
3.  **Add Text Overlays (添加文字叠加):** Highlight "Washable" (可水洗), "Super Soft" (超柔软) in German ("Waschbar", "Super Weich"). | 用德语强调核心卖点。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Post Static Images as Video (不要把静态图片当视频发):** TikTok is a video-first platform. Slideshows perform poorly without strong editing/music sync. | TikTok是视频优先的平台。没有强剪辑/音乐同步的幻灯片表现极差。
2.  **Ignore Audio (不要忽视音频):** Use trending audio or ASMR (pet sounds) to grab attention. | 使用热门音频或ASMR（宠物声音）来吸引注意。
3.  **Omit CTA (不要省略行动号召):** Tell viewers to "Check the shop" or "Treat your pet". | 告诉观众"查看店铺"或"款待你的宠物"。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Unboxing (ASMR开箱):** Squeeze the faux fur to show density and softness (tactile visualization). | 挤压仿皮草以展示密度和柔软度（触觉可视化）。
2.  **Before/After (使用前/使用后):** Show a pet sleeping on the floor vs. sleeping happily in the bed. | 展示宠物睡在地板上 vs 快乐地睡在床里。
3.  **Localize for Germany (德国本地化):** Mention "Warmth for Winter" (Wärme für den Winter) as heating costs are a concern in DE. | 提及"冬季保暖"，因为取暖成本是德国用户关注的问题。
