## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Focus on Function:** Show that the gel *cleans* or *freshens* the air (visualize scent lines or sparkling clean water), not just that it adds color. German consumers value efficacy.
2. **Shorten to 15-20s:** 51 seconds is far too long. Cut the rambling intro. Hook -> Stamp Action -> Flush (Foam/Clean) -> CTA.
3. **Add Strong CTA:** Explicitly say "Get yours now on TikTok Shop" or point to the yellow basket.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't ignore Audio Quality:** Mumbled or echoey voiceovers reduce retention. Use a crisp TTS or record closer to the mic.
2. **Don't over-clutter:** "Ausgetaubt" (letting off steam) implies a mess. A neat row of stamps is "satisfying"; a messy bowl is just "dirty" to some viewers.
3. **Don't forget the niche:** This is a *cleaning* product. Framing it purely as "decoration" might alienate buyers looking for hygiene.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** The sound of the "click" and the "squish" of the gel stamp can be very satisfying (ASMR). Remove the talking and use satisfying sounds + trending music.
2. **"Satisfying Flush":** Show the water running over the gel and creating foam. This proves the product works.
3. **Gift Angle:** The product title mentions "Valentine's Day Gift". This is a bizarre angle for a toilet cleaner, but could be used ironically/humorously ("The gift for the person who has everything").
