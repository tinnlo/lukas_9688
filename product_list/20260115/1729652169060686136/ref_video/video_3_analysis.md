## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Emphasize the "Before/After":** Visually show someone tossing/turning vs. sleeping deeply.
2. **Zoom in on Texture:** The "1200 GSM" means nothing without visual texture; show the fibers.
3. **Use German Text Overlays:** Ensure key benefits (10cm, Waschbar) appear as text for sound-off users.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Linger:** Avoid shots longer than 3 seconds without a cut or movement.
2. **Don't Forget Dimensions:** Germans are precise; ensure fit/size info is clear in description if not in video.
3. **Don't Ignore Returns:** If possible, mention "Risk-Free Trial" to boost trust further.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Record the sound of the hand pressing into the soft topper for a sensory hook.
2. **"Unboxing" Angle:** Show the package arriving and expanding (compressed foam expansion is satisfying to watch).
3. **Gift Angle:** Frame it explicitly as "The perfect gift for parents" for the Christmas season.
