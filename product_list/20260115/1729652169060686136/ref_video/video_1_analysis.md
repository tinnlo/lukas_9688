## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Emphasize Thickness Visually:** Always use a ruler or a hand comparison in the first 3 seconds to prove the "10cm" claim. | **视觉上强调厚度：** 务必在前3秒内使用尺子或手部对比来证明“10厘米”的主张。
2.  **Use Specific Metrics:** Mention "1200 GSM" or "High Density" to differentiate from cheap competitors. | **使用具体指标：** 提及“1200 GSM”或“高密度”以区别于廉价竞品。
3.  **Seasonal Refresh:** Update the CTA end-card for post-Christmas (e.g., "New Year Refresh" or "Winter Sale"). | **季节性更新：** 更新圣诞节后的CTA尾页（如“新年焕新”或“冬季大促”）。

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't Use Dark Lighting:** Bedding needs to look bright and hygienic. Dark lighting implies dirty. | **不要使用暗淡灯光：** 床上用品需要看起来明亮卫生。暗光暗示脏乱。
2.  **Don't Forget the "Washable" Claim:** For the German market, hygiene/maintenance is a top objection handler. | **不要忘记“可洗”声明：** 对于德国市场，卫生/维护是首要的异议处理点。
3.  **Don't Rush the "Relaxation" Shot:** Give the viewer 1-2 seconds to "feel" the comfort through the screen. | **不要匆忙带过“放松”镜头：** 给观众1-2秒时间通过屏幕“感受”舒适。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Before & After" Angle:** Split screen showing a "Old Flat Mattress" vs. "New Cloud Bed" could boost CTR further. | **“使用前 vs 使用后”角度：** 分屏展示“旧的扁平床垫”与“新云朵床”可以进一步提高点击率。
2.  **Unboxing Experience:** Show the topper expanding from the vacuum seal (visual satisfaction). | **开箱体验：** 展示床垫罩从真空密封中膨胀的过程（视觉满足感）。
3.  **Pet Angle:** Show a dog/cat loving the bed too (high engagement trigger). | **宠物角度：** 展示狗/猫也喜欢这张床（高互动触发点）。
