## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO (建议执行):**
1.  **Highlight Specs:** Continue to use specific numbers (10cm, 1200gsm). German buyers trust data.
2.  **Show "Before/After":** Visually contrast a flat, sad mattress with the new, fluffy one.
3.  **Localize Seasonality:** Update "Weihnachtszeit" to "Neujahrsangebot" (New Year Offer) or "Winterschlussverkauf" (Winter Sale) immediately after Christmas.

**DON'T (避免做法):**
1.  **Don't be Vague:** Avoid generic claims like "good quality." Keep using specific GSM/Thickness metrics.
2.  **Don't Ignore Maintenance:** The "Washable" feature is a key objection handler; do not cut it.
3.  **Don't Oversell:** Ensure the visual thickness matches the claim to avoid returns.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会点):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Create a version focused purely on the *sound* of the packaging opening and the hand pressing into the foam (sensory appeal).
2.  **Unboxing Experience:** Show the package arriving and expanding (satisfying transformation).
3.  **Pet/Kid Test:** Show a pet or child sleeping on it to emphasize safety and comfort (emotional hook).
