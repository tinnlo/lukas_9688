## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the Ruler:** Don't just say 10cm, literally hold a ruler against it in the first 3 seconds.
2. **Add Text Overlay:** "Nie wieder Rückenschmerzen?" (No more back pain?) to target a specific problem.
3. **Comparison:** Briefly show a "Standard Topper" (thin) vs "This Topper" (thick) side-by-side.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't Hide the Price:** If the conversion is low, it might be sticker shock. Mention "Luxury for less" or financing if applicable.
2. **Don't Forget the Sound:** Use ASMR sounds of the hand pressing on the fabric to increase sensory immersion.
3. **Don't use messy backgrounds:** Bedding ads require pristine, hotel-like environments to convert.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Seasonal Angle:** "Perfect for Winter" (cozy) or "Cool for Summer" (breathable) depending on current weather in Germany.
2. **Water/Stain Test:** Pour water to show it doesn't soak in immediately (if hydrophobic) or that the cover is washable (unzip demo).
3. **Pet Angle:** "Even my dog loves it" (Germans love pets) - highly engaging hook variation.
