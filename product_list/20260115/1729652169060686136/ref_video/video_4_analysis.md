## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Visualize the "Before/After":** Show a side-by-side of a restless sleep on a thin mattress vs. deep sleep on the topper to visualize "Schluss mit ungemütlichen Nächten".
2.  **Add Social Proof:** Overlay a user review (e.g., "Best purchase of 2025") to build trust, which is likely missing given the low conversion.
3.  **Localize Urgency:** Ensure the "Seasonal" hook is updated. If running post-Christmas, change "Weihnachtsaktion" to "Winter Sale" or "New Year Reset".

**DON'T:**
1.  **Over-promise without proof:** Do not say "cooling" without showing *how* (e.g., breathable mesh close-up).
2.  **Ignore the seasonal decay:** Do not leave "Christmas" audio running in January/February; it kills conversion immediately.
3.  **Rely solely on specs:** "1200GSM" is good for Germans, but "Feels like a cloud" creates more emotional desire.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Enhance the sound of the hand brushing the fabric or the "poof" of the foam expanding to increase retention.
2.  **Price Anchoring:** Mention "Transforms your old bed for 1/10th the price of a new mattress" to justify the value immediately.
3.  **FAQ Response:** Address *how* it stays in place (does it slide?) visually, as this is a common objection for toppers.
