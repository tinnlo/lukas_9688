## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add Text Overlays:** Highlight key benefits like "No Batteries Needed" (Keine Batterien nötig) and "Easy Cleaning" (Leicht zu reinigen) on screen.
2. **Change Audio:** Use a trending song *or* a German voiceover explaining *why* this dispenser is better (e.g., "My cat drinks more water now").
3. **Include a CTA:** visually point to the basket icon or say "Link in Bio/Basket" at the end.

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on weird audio:** The "Baby... I'm Ross" audio is confusing and distracts from the product.
2. **Forget the 'Problem':** Don't just show the solution. Show the *problem* first (e.g., dirty water bowls, cat ignoring water) to create contrast.
3. **Leave out the human element:** Showing a hand refilling or cleaning it can demonstrate ease of use.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Focus on the soothing sound of the water flowing (if the pump is quiet) combined with cat purring.
2. **"Travel Hack":** Frame it as "Perfect for when I'm at work" (1-10L capacity implication) to target busy owners.
3. **Comparison:** Show a "Old vs New" bowl comparison to visually demonstrate the upgrade.
