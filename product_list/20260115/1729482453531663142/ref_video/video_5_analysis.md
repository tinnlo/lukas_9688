## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** **MANDATORY**. Replace the English VO with German TTS or voiceover (e.g., "Wenn es dunkel wird, zaubert diese Lampe Wärme...").
2.  **Show "Gift" Context:** The title mentions "Muttertag" (Mother's Day). The video should explicitly show someone *giving* or *receiving* it as a gift.
3.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text hooks on screen (e.g., "Das perfekte Geschenk" or "Nie wieder einsam fühlen").

**DON'T:**
1.  **Use English in DACH:** Do not run English-only voiceover ads in Germany/Austria/Switzerland unless the visual hook is universally understood (this one relies on the story).
2.  **Be Vague:** Don't just show "vibes." Show the lamp *turning on* clearly in the first 3 seconds to grab attention.
3.  **Forget CTA:** Don't end without a "Jetzt kaufen" (Buy Now) or arrow to the cart.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Unboxing:** Ditch the story. Do a silent, satisfying ASMR unboxing and assembly of the lamp (if it's the DIY kit version).
2.  **"POV: You found the perfect gift":** Use a text-based POV hook in German.
3.  **Night Routine:** Integrate the lamp into a "German Night Routine" (Abendroutine) video.
