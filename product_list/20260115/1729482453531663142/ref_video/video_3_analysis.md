## 12. Replication Insights | 复制洞察

- **Production Budget:** **Low**. Smartphone + Product sample.
- **Key Success Factor:** The "Day vs Night" contrast. You must show how it looks real in the day and magical at night.
- **Reproducible Elements:**
    *   Script structure: Hook -> Feature (Glow) -> Benefit (Cozy) -> Use Case (Gift) -> CTA.
    *   Visual sequence: Light on -> Light off (Glow).

## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1.  **Fix the Script:** Ensure you call the product "Tulips" consistently. Do not say "Rose".
2.  **Shorten the Video:** Compress this to 15-20 seconds. The current 33s duration likely has 15s of dead air or repetitive footage after the CTA.
3.  **Add a stronger Offer:** "Perfekt für Muttertag" (Perfect for Mother's Day) or "Nur heute 50% Rabatt" (50% off today only).

**DON'T (避免):**
1.  **Don't Trail Off:** Avoid leaving 10+ seconds of music/silence after the main pitch. Retention drops to zero.
2.  **Don't Mix Floral Names:** Calling a tulip a rose confuses the algorithm and the user.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1.  **ASMR Angle:** Record the sound of unpacking or the "click" of turning it on with just the visual of the light warming up.
2.  **Multi-Unit Bundle:** Show 3 different colors together to encourage buying more than one (AOV boost).
3.  **Gift Unboxing:** Film someone *receiving* it and their reaction ("Wow, are they real?").
