## 2. Campaign Strategy

**Overall Strategic Approach:** Multi-angle attack on the language barrier pain point with emphasis on comfort and value

**Psychological Triggers:**
1. **Fear/Urgency** - "You don't speak English" (pain point resonance)
2. **Validation** - "I was skeptical, but..." (social proof journey)
3. **Curiosity** - "These can do more than you think" (pattern interrupt)

**Key Insight Statement:**
> The market data shows that **Video 1 (Russian language, "Better than AirPods" comparison)** is the top performer (2 sales, 3,076 views). This indicates that **competitor comparison + "breaks the internet" positioning** resonates strongly. However, German-language videos underperform due to **script-visual mismatches** and **low production authenticity**. Our campaign addresses these gaps with: (1) authentic problem-solution framing, (2) proven counter-intuitive structure, and (3) documentary-style "first impression" format that builds trust.
