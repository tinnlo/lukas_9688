## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

### Overall Strategic Approach | 整体战略方法

- **Visual Allure | 视觉诱惑:** Leverage the "shine" of the silver watch and the "mist" of the perfume to create desire | 利用银色手表的“光泽”和香水的“喷雾”创造购买欲

2. **Value Contrast | 价值对比:** Looks like €100+ but priced at a fraction of that | 看起来价值100欧元以上，但价格仅为其一小部分
3. **Convenience | 便利性:** Everything needed for a "new look" in one box | 一个盒子即可满足“新造型”的所有需求

Market data shows a 37.50% conversion rate, indicating that when this product is seen as a "ready-made gift," buyers don't hesitate. The black presentation box is what bridges the gap between a "cheap watch" and an "exquisite gift set." All scripts emphasize the unboxing and the "complete box" feel to trigger this impulse.

市场数据显示转化率为37.50%，这表明当该产品被视为“现成的礼品”时，买家不会犹豫。黑色展示盒弥补了“廉价手表”和“精美礼品套装”之间的差距。所有脚本都强调了开箱和“完整包装”的感觉，以触发这种冲动。
