## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** If targeting Germany, use German TTS (Text-to-Speech) or a German voiceover.
2.  **Match Product to Content:** If selling Earbuds, show the *cat* wearing them (humor) or the owner using them to "ignore" the cat's meowing.
3.  **Shorten the Video:** 458 seconds is far too long for a TikTok Shop ad. Cut to 15-30 seconds.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't use Russian Audio for Germany:** Unless targeting a specific niche, this kills conversion.
2.  **Don't Hide the Product:** A 7-minute video without a single product shot is a wasted opportunity.
3.  **Don't Mislead:** The algorithm will eventually penalize the shop for high bounce rates (people clicking the link expecting cat toys and seeing earbuds).

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Translation" Angle:** Since the product is "AI Translator Earbuds", a creative angle would be: *"I wish these earbuds could translate what my cat Pesiosha is saying!"* -> This bridges the viral cat content with the actual product utility.
