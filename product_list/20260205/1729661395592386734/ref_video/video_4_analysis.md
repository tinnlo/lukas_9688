## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Soft Sell Bridge:** Instead of immediately saying "Click the cart", bridge with "Did you know this whole look costs less than €XX?" to ground the emotional value in financial reality.
2.  **Pin Comment:** Ensure a comment is pinned asking "Who would you do this for?" to drive engagement further, and a second comment with "Link in bio to get the set".
3.  **Check Landing Page:** **URGENT:** Verify the product link and stock status. 0 sales on 277k views often means a technical error or the product is sold out.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Abrupt Voiceover Change:** Don't switch from "crying emotional girl" voice to "salesman" voice too harshly. Keep the "excited/happy" tone even for the CTA.
2.  **Over-promise:** Ensure the set in the video *exactly* matches what is sold. If the video shows 500 balloons but the set sends 50, trust is broken instantly at the shop page.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Response/Duet Content:** Create a follow-up video showing the "Making of" (Time-lapse of setting it up) to show how *easy* it is. This addresses the objection "This looks like too much work".
2.  **Price Anchor:** If the set is cheap, overlay the price text "Only €15" during the reveal to trigger impulse buys immediately.
