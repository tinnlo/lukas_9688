## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Audio:** Re-record the voiceover in German ("Alles Gute zum Valentinstag!", "Schaut mal, was ich vorbereitet habe"). This is the #1 reason for 0 sales in DE.
2.  **Add Text Overlays:** Add German text on screen listing what is included (e.g., "Alles in einem Set: Ballons + Rosenblätter").
3.  **Add a CTA:** End with "Jetzt bestellen für Valentinstag" (Order now for Valentine's).

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on English:** Even if the aesthetic is universal, the sales pitch needs to be local.
2.  **Don't hide the "Kit" aspect:** Make sure viewers know this is a single purchase that contains everything shown, not just random items.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Partner Reaction":** Show the partner walking into the room and being surprised. This adds emotional proof.
2.  **Tutorial Angle:** Speed up the setup process (timelapse) to show how easy it is to inflate the balloons and scatter petals.
3.  **Price Anchor:** Mention the price if it's affordable (e.g., "Komplettes Set unter 20€").
