## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO (建议):**
1. **Overlay "Link in Bio" or "Shop Now":** Add a subtle text sticker at the end to guide the user to the shop anchor.
2. **Show Human Element:** A hand placing the final balloon or a couple hugging in the background would increase emotional conversion.
3. **Use German Hashtags:** Ensure #Valentinstag #Geschenkideen are used.

**DON'T (避免):**
1. **Dark Lighting:** Do not film this at night without professional lighting; it looks cheap.
2. **Shaky Cam:** For "still life" products, use a tripod. Handheld shake ruins the "premium" feel.
3. **Mismatched Audio:** Don't use high-energy EDM/Techno. Keep it slow/romantic.

**OPPORTUNITY (机会):**
1. **"Preparation" Angle:** Show the *process* of blowing up the balloons (timelapse). It shows effort and ease of assembly.
2. **"Reaction" Angle:** Film a partner's reaction to walking into the room seeing the decorations.
3. **Bundle Offer:** Mention "Includes pump" or "Includes tape" in text overlay if applicable to solve pain points.
