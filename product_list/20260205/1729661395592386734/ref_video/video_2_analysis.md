## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Add a Voiceover:** Explain the value in German (e.g., "Alles was du für den Valentinstag brauchst in einer Box!" - Everything you need for Valentine's in one box!).
2.  **Show the Result:** Don't just show the *bag* of balloons; show the *inflated* balloons arranged on a bed or in a room.
3.  **Add Music:** Use a trending romantic song to evoke emotion.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't rely on silence:** TikTok is an audio-visual platform. Silence kills retention.
2.  **Don't ignore the "Why":** Why buy this set? (Answer: Convenience). Highlight that.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **"Before & After" Transformation:** Show a boring room $\rightarrow$ Boom! Romantic paradise using the kit.
2.  **ASMR Unboxing:** If sticking to no-talking, use crinkling sounds of the package and pumping up balloons for sensory engagement.
3.  **Price Focus:** If the kit is cheap, overlay the price ("Nur 10€!") to trigger impulse buys.
