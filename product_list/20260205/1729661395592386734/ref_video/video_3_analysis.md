## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Show the "Before" state:** Contrast the boring room with the decorated room more sharply to emphasize the transformation.
2. **Add Price Anchor:** If the set is cheap, mention the price ("Für nur 15€...") to reduce friction.
3. **Use Text-to-Speech (TTS):** Experiment with popular German TTS voices if the current voice is not engaging enough.

**DON'T:**
1. **Fake the reaction too much:** Overacting kills trust. Keep the "Wow" grounded.
2. **Hide the setup:** If it's truly "super easy", show a 2-second speed-build to prove it's not a hassle.
3. **Forget the "Link in Bio" arrow:** Ensure a visual cue points to the purchase link/basket.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **"POV" Angle:** Film strictly from the perspective of the person *giving* the surprise (hands visible setting it up).
2. **Valentine's Specific Countdown:** Add a text overlay "Noch 3 Wochen bis Valentinstag!" (Only 3 weeks to Valentine's!) to drive urgency.
3. **Bundle Offer:** Suggest buying two sets for a "Mega" surprise.
