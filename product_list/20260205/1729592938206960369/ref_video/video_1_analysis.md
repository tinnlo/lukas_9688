## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议 (做/不做/机会)

**DO:**
1.  **Localize Language:** **MUST** record in German (or use AI German TTS) if targeting TikTok Shop DE. "Four Euros" must be clear.
2.  **Fix Product-Market Match:** If selling a "Mom" necklace, pitch it for "Mother's Day" or "Birthday", not primarily "Valentine's" (unless for a wife who is a mother, but clarify that).
3.  **Improve Lighting:** Jewelry requires good lighting to show sparkle/shine.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't use "Yo/Broken English":** Avoid unverified auto-captions that print nonsense like "pink hat and gross" (Pink Heart and Rose).
2.  **Don't delay the reveal:** Show the open box in the first 3 seconds. 14 seconds to see the necklace is too long.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **ASMR Unboxing:** The "drawer" mechanism of these boxes makes a satisfying sound. Use that for a hook.
2.  **Gift Reaction:** Show someone actually *receiving* it to prove the emotional value.
3.  **German TTS:** Use a popular German AI voice (like "Mannerstimme") to dub over this footage for an immediate lift in relevance.
