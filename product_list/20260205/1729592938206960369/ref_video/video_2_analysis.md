## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1.  **Improve Lighting:** Jewelry *must* sparkle. Use a ring light or direct sunlight when showing the pendant close-up.
2.  **Add Captions:** German viewers often watch without sound or need help parsing rapid/colloquial speech. Add on-screen text for key features ("Mit 'MAMA' Gravur", "Nur 11€").
3.  **Cleaner Audio:** Re-record the voiceover to avoid stumbling words ("überwütte"). A confident voice sells better.

**DON'T:**
1.  **Don't hide the jewelry too long:** The teddy is cute, but the necklace is the value. Get to the close-up faster.
2.  **Don't be vague about price:** Put the price ON THE SCREEN as a sticker, not just spoken at the end.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1.  **Emotional Reaction:** Instead of just showing the box, show someone *giving* it to their mom and her reaction (even if staged).
2.  **Projection Demo:** If the necklace has the "I Love You in 100 languages" or "MOM" projection feature, use the phone flashlight to actually *project* it on the wall. This is a huge viral trigger missing here.
3.  **Gift Bundling:** Explicitly say "Mother's Day is coming" (Muttertag steht vor der Tür) to create urgency.
