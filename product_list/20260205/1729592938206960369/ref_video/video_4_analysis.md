2.  **Ignore the Market:** Don't run pure English content in DACH (Germany/Austria/Switzerland) without subtitles.
3.  **Rely Solely on Visuals:** Don't assume the product speaks for itself; explain the *emotion* (Mother's Day, Birthday).
