## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议与机会

**DO | 建议:**
1.  **Add Lighting:** Use a simple ring light or face a window. Jewelry *needs* to sparkle to look valuable. The "Mom" text needs to be instantly readable.
2.  **Stronger CTA:** Add a text overlay at the end: "Link in Bio" or "Grab yours below ↓" (arrow pointing to shop link).
3.  **Text Overlays:** Add key selling points as text on screen (e.g., "Geschenkidee für Mama" at 0:00, "Nur 12€" at 0:28) for silent viewers.

**DON'T | 避免:**
1.  **Don't hide the product:** If the video is too dark, the "Heart in Heart" detail is lost.
2.  **Don't be too passive:** "Here it is" is nice, but "You NEED this for Mother's Day" creates more urgency.
3.  **Don't forget the Shop Link:** Ensure the product link is actually attached via TikTok Shop anchor.

**OPPORTUNITY | 机会:**
1.  **Mother's Day Angle:** Repurpose this exact footage with a voiceover focused specifically on Mother's Day (Muttertag) urgency ("Muttertag kommt bald!").
2.  **Reaction Video:** Film a video of actually *giving* this to a mom and catching her reaction. Emotional reaction videos perform better than simple unboxings.
3.  **Reply-to-Comment:** If anyone comments, reply with a video showing the "Rose" version mentioned to double the content output.
