## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY)

**DO:**
1. **Add German Voiceover:** Explain the benefit: "Endlich Ordnung auf dem Schminktisch!" (Finally order on the vanity!).
2. **Start with 'Before' State:** Show a messy pile of makeup *first* to trigger the "pain point".
3. **Add Text Overlays:** Highlight features like "360° Drehbar" (360° Rotatable) and "Passt überall" (Fits anywhere).

**DON'T:**
1. **Rely on Silence:** Never publish a silent ad for a functional product in DACH (Germany/Austria/Switzerland).
2. **Show Empty Product:** Start with it full or filling it up fast; an empty plastic holder looks cheap.
3. **Ignore Lighting:** Ensure the black product doesn't look like a dark blob; use good lighting to show texture.

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **ASMR Angle:** Record the *sounds* of brushes dropping into the holder. "Satisfying Organization" is a huge trend.
2. **"Gift Idea" Angle:** Pitch it as "Das perfekte Geschenk für deine Freundin" (The perfect gift for your girlfriend).
3. **Speed Clean:** A timelapse of cleaning a messy desk into the organizer in 5 seconds.
