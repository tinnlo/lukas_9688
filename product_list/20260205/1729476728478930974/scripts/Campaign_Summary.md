## 1. Product Overview | 产品概述

**Core Product (核心产品):**  
SONGMIC dual-compartment laundry organizer with metal frame and top shelf. Combines laundry sorting functionality with furniture-grade aesthetics for German households seeking "Ordnung" (order/organization).

SONGMIC 双格脏衣篮，配金属框架和顶部置物架。将脏衣分类功能与家具级美学结合，面向追求"秩序"的德国家庭。

**Key Specifications (关键规格):**
- Dimensions (尺寸): 73 x 33 x 72 cm (compact depth 仅33厘米深度)
- Capacity (容量): 2 x 46L = 92L total (总计92升)
- Materials (材质): Steel frame (钢框架), MDF shelf (MDF置物架), Oxford fabric bags (牛津布袋)
- Load capacity (承重): 20kg top shelf (置物架20公斤)
- Features (功能): Slide-out mechanism (滑出机制), detachable bags (可拆卸袋), water-repellent fabric (拒水面料)

## 2. Campaign Strategy | 活动策略

**Strategic Positioning (战略定位):**  
Position as a **"furniture-grade organizer" (家具级收纳)** rather than a commodity laundry basket. Target German consumer psychology: **"Ordnung muss sein"** (order is essential) + **"Qualität über alles"** (quality over everything).

**Three-Angle Approach (三角度策略):**  
We developed 3 scripts targeting different audience psychographics, all referencing insights from `video_synthesis.md` Section 12 (3 Script Angles Recommended):

1. **Aesthetic Upgrade (美学升级)** - For "Der Ästhet" (The Aesthete 审美派): Singles/students who want room décor, not ugly bins.
2. **Efficiency Expert (效率专家)** - For "Der Ordnungs-Freak" (The Organized Perfectionist 整理狂): Busy families who hate sorting laundry.
3. **Quality Skeptic (质量怀疑论者)** - For skeptics fearing "China-Schrott" (cheap Chinese junk 廉价垃圾): Quality-conscious buyers needing proof.

**Hook Strategy (钩子策略):**  
All scripts use "Winning Hook Patterns" identified in `video_synthesis.md` Section 1:
- Script 1: "Satisfying Order" Visual (视觉解压)
- Script 2: "Family Chaos" Problem (家庭混乱问题)
- Script 3: "Honest Flaw" Confession adapted to skepticism (诚实缺陷告白改编为怀疑态度)

- **Visual Signature (视觉特征):** Physical tests (knock test, load test, water test 物理测试：敲击测试、负载测试、防水测试)

## 7. Compliance \u0026 Risk Mitigation | 合规与风险缓解
