## 13. Recommendations (DO/DON'T/OPPORTUNITY) | 建议

**DO:**
1. Use "Honest Hooks" (mentioning a small flaw like wrinkles that gets fixed). | 使用“诚实钩子”（提到一些可以解决的小缺点，如褶皱）。
2. Show the product in at least 2 different rooms to demonstrate versatility. | 在至少两个不同的房间展示产品，以证明其多功能性。
3. Maintain the "Neighborly Advice" tone for the German market. | 针对德国市场保持“邻里建议”式的语气。

**DON'T:**
1. Don't use overly polished studio lighting for this type of home product; it lowers trust. | 不要为此类家居产品使用过度精修的棚拍灯光，这会降低信任度。
2. Don't wait until the very last second for the CTA; integrate it slightly earlier. | 不要等到最后一秒才出 CTA，稍微提前一点整合进去。

**OPPORTUNITY:**
1. **Overlay Price:** Adding a "Black Friday Special" text overlay would likely double the conversion rate. | **价格叠加:** 添加“黑五特惠”文字叠加可能会使转化率翻倍。
2. **Speed up the Hook:** Reduce the initial "Na..." to 1 second and get straight to the utility. | **加快钩子:** 将开头的“Na...”缩短到1秒，直接进入实用功能。
3. **Bilingual Captions:** Adding German text captions would help retention for users watching on mute. | **双语字幕:** 添加德文字幕将有助于留住静音观看的用户。
