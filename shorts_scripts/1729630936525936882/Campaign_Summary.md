## Compliance Notes

**Category**: Electronics & Tech

**Safe Language Used**:
- "unterstützt bis zu X Stunden" (supports up to X hours) - battery life
- "Echtzeit-Übersetzung" (real-time translation) - not "perfect translation"
- "IPX4 wasserdicht" (IPX4 waterproof) - specific rating, not exaggerated
- "Bluetooth 6.0" (Bluetooth 6.0) - accurate specification

**Avoided Claims**:
- "Beste Kopfhörer" (best earbuds) - subjective claim
- "Perfekte Übersetzung" (perfect translation) - unrealistic promise
- "Garantiert X Stunden" (guaranteed X hours) - battery variance exists
- "Wasserdicht wie Taucherausrüstung" (waterproof like diving equipment) - IPX4 is splash-resistant only

**Important Notes**:
- All technical specifications verified against product images and tabcut data
- HTC brand name used accurately (visible on packaging)
- Translation feature requires app download (mentioned in product images)
