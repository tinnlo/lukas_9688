## Compliance Notes

**Health Claims Used (Safe):**
- "unterstützt den Körper beim natürlichen Muskelaufbau" (supportive language)
- "kann helfen" (modal verb, no guarantees)
- "pflanzlicher Aktivstoff" (factual description)

**Avoided Claims:**
- No anabolic steroid comparisons
- No guaranteed muscle gain amounts
- No performance enhancement guarantees
- Clear distinction: natural plant compound, not synthetic hormone

**Important:**
- This is a fitness supplement marketed to adults
- "Natürlich" positioning differentiates from synthetic alternatives
- Emphasize "unterstützen" (support) not "erzeugen" (create)
